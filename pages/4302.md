## Idea

A __fermion__ is a [[particle]]/[[quantum field]] that obeys [[Fermi-Dirac statistics]].  By the [[spin-statistics theorem]], this is the same thing as a particle whose [[spin]] is half an [[integer]] but not itself an integer.
