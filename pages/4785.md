## Idea

The [[classification of simple Lie groups]] contains four infinite series of "classical" simple Lie groups:

* $A_n = SU(n+1)$, the [[special unitary group]] 

* $B_n = SO(2n+1)$, the [[special orthogonal group]]

* $C_n = Sp(n)$, the [[symplectic group]]

* $D_n = SO(2n)$, the [[special orthogonal group]] 

Apart from this there are five [[exceptional Lie groups]].
