PPS is the acronym for __Preuves, Programmes et Systèmes.__  It is officially forms part of [[IRIF]], that is the Institut de Recherche en Informatique Fondamentale, a mixed research group (UMR 7126) of the CNRS and  the Université Paris Diderot (aka Paris 7). 

It is very well known for the research of its members in Theoretical Computer Science.
