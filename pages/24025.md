## Definition

Given a ring $R$, a **filter** of $R$ is a multiplicative [[submonoid]] $(F, 1, \cdot)$ of $R$ with monoid [[monomorphism]] $i:F \hookrightarrow R$ such that 

* $i(1) = 1$, 
* for all elements $x \in F$ and $y \in F$, $i(x \cdot y) = i(x) \cdot i(y)$
* for all elements $x \in F$ and $y \in R$, if there is an element $z \in R$ such that $i(x) = z \cdot y$, then there exists an element $z^{'} \in F$ such that $i(z^{'}) = z$.
