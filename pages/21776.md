## Idea

The _nanometer_ is a [[physical unit]] of [[length]].

## Definition

A **nanometer** (abbreviated $nm$) is one $10^{9}$th of a [[meter]]:

$$
  nm \coloneqq 10^{-9} m
  \,.
$$

The [[scale]] between about a nanometer and a [[micrometer]] is the scale of [[mesoscopic physics]].
