## Definition

In a [[finitely complete category]] $C$, a **cartesian monoidal preordered object** $X$ is a [[preordered object]] with internal preorder $R\stackrel{(p_1,p_2)}\hookrightarrow X \times X$ and a [[monoid object]] in $C$ with multiplication $\mu:X \times X \to X$ and a [[global element|global]] unit $\eta:* \to X$, where $*$ is the [[terminal object]] in $C$, with

* (internal top/terminal object) a function $\tau:(* \to X) \to (* \to R)$ such that for all [[global elements]] $a:* \to X$, $p_1 \circ \tau(a) = a$ and $p_2 \circ \tau(a) = \eta$. 

* (internal binary meets/binary limits) functions 
$$\lambda_l:((* \to X) \times (* \to X)) \to (* \to R)$$ 
$$\lambda_r:((* \to X) \times (* \to X)) \to (* \to R)$$ 
such that for all [[global elements]] $a:* \to X$ and $b:* \to X$, $p_1 \circ \lambda_l(a,b) = \mu(a,b)$, $p_2 \circ \lambda_l(a,b) = a$, $p_1 \circ \lambda_r(a,b) = \mu(a,b)$, and $p_2 \circ \lambda_r(a,b) = b$.
