## Definition

A [[magma]] $(S,\cdot)$ is called _associative_ if it satisfies the [[associativity]] condition, saying that for all $x,y,z \in S$ then the [[equation]]

$$
  (x \cdot y) \cdot z
  =
  x \cdot (y \cdot z)
$$

holds.

## Examples

Examples include [[semigroups]]/[[monoids]], [[rings]], [[associative algebras]], etc.
