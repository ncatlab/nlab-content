## Idea 

The Segal theorem relates the [[Burnside ring]] of a finite group $G$ to the stable cohomotopy of the classifying space $BG$.

## Historical Remarks

Segal conjectured this in the 1970s, and it was proved by [[Gunnar Carlsson]] in 1984.
