## Idea

In [[harmonic analysis]], the _Fourier inversion theorem_ states that the [[Fourier transform]] is its own [[inverse function|inverse]] (possibly up to multiplication with a constant, depending on conventions).

For Fourier transform over [[Cartesian spaces]], see e.g. [Hörmander 90 ,theorem 7.1.5](#Hoermander90)
