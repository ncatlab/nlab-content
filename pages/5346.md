## Idea

The _model structure on simplicial groups_ is a presentation of the [[∞-group]]s in [[∞Grpd]] $\simeq$ [[Top]]. See [[group object in an (∞,1)-category]].

## Definition

There is a [[model category]] structure on the [[category]] $sGrpd$ of [[simplicial group]]s where a morphism is

* is a weak equivalence if the underlying morphism is a weak equiavalence in the standard [[model structure on simplicial sets]].


## Properties

Forming loop space objects and classifying spaces provides a [[Quillen equivalence]]

$$
  (\Omega \dashv \bar W) : sGrp \stackrel{\overset{}{\leftarrow}}{\to}
  sSet_0
$$

with the [[model structure on connected simplicial sets]].
