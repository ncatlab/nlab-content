* [[Leonardo Castellani]], [[Roberto Catenacci]], [[Pietro Grassi]], _Super Quantum Mechanics in the Integral Form Formalism_, Ann. Henri Poincaré (2018) 19: 1385 ([arXiv:1706.04704](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.04704))
