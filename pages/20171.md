## Idea

For $X$ a [[manifold]] equipped with an [[action]] of a [[group]] $G$, a _$G$-equivariant triangulation_ of $X$ is a [[triangulation]] of $X$ which is compatible with this $G$-[[action]], in that this action restricts to [[bijections]] on sets of $k$-[[cells]] of the triangulation, for each $k \in \mathbb{N}$.

## Properties

The _[[equivariant triangulation theorem]]_ ([Illman 78](#Illman78), [Illman 83](#Illman83)) asserts that for $G$ a [[compact Lie group]] (for instance a [[finite group]]) equivariant triangulations of [[smooth manifolds]] always exist.
