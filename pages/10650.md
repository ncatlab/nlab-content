## Idea

An _arithmetic scheme_ is a [[scheme]] in [[arithmetic geometry]], hence a scheme over [[Isbell duality|formal dual]]/[[spectrum of a commutative ring|spectrum]] $Spec \mathbb{Z}$ of the [[ring]] of  [[integers]].

Typically one takes an arithmetic scheme to be ([[regular scheme|regular]]) [[separated scheme|separated]] and of [[scheme of finite type|of finite type]].
