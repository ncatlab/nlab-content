## Idea

A _locally polypresentable category_ is similar to a [[locally presentable category]], but where we ask only for the existence of [[wide pullbacks]] rather than all [[small limits]].

## Definition


+-- {: .num_defn #PolypresentableCategory}
###### Definition 
**(locally polypresentable category)**

A [[category]] $\mathcal{C}$ is called **locally polypresentable** if

1. it is an [[accessible category]];

1. it has all [[wide pullbacks]].

The second condition is equivalent to:

2'. it has all small [[polycolimits]].
