## Idea

The external definition of [[reflective sub-(∞,1)-category]] via the [universal property of the reflector](http://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/reflective+sub-%28infinity%2C1%29-category#CharacterizationOfReflectors) has an immediate formulation in the [[internal language of an (∞,1)-topos]]. This internal formulation, however, automatically gives a [[reflective product-preserving sub-(∞,1)-category]].
