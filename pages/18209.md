## Statement

Every [[Riemannian manifold]] admits a [[isometry|isometric]] [[embedding of smooth manifolds|embedding]] into a [[Euclidean space]].

## Related concepts

* The analogous statement for plain [[differentiable manifolds]] is the [[Whitney embedding theorem]]
