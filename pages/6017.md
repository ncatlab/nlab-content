## Idea

A _Crane-Yetter model_ is a [[sigma-model]] [[quantum field theory]] whose target space is a [[discrete infinity-groupoid|discrete]] [[2-groupoid]] and whose background [[gauge field]] is a [[circle n-bundle|circle 4-bundle]].

Together with the [[Dijkgraaf-Witten model]] this are the first two steps in filtering of target spaces by [[homotopy type]] [[truncated|truncation]] of [[schreiber:âˆž-Chern-Simons theory]] <a href="http://nlab.mathforge.org/schreiber/show/infinity-Chern-Simons+theory#DiscreteTargets">with discrete target spaces</a>.
