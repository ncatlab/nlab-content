Maria Teresa Rivas Rodriguez  is a Spanish topologist, who has worked on [[proper homotopy theory]], [[shape theory
]] and on adapting ideas from those areas to give new insights into [[dynamical systems]].

She is based at the University of La Rioja, Spain.
