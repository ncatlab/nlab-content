## Idea

In [[perturbative quantum field theory]], _dimensional regularization_ is a method for [[renormalization]]. Here [[Feynman amplitudes]] are expressed via [[Schwinger parameters]] in [[worldline formalism]] making the resulting expression a [[function]] of [[spacetime]] [[dimension]] $d$ which may formally be set to any [[real number]]. Then one regularizes via taking the limit $d \to 4$.
