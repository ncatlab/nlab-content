
__Yuri Vladimirovič Matiyasevich__ (also spelled Jurij/Yurij/Yurii Matijasevič/Matijasevich/Matiasevich, Russian Ю́рий Влади́мирович Матиясе́вич) is a Russian
mathematician who obtained a negative solution to [[Hilbert's problems|Hilbert's tenth problem]].
By a seminal theorem of Matiyasevich, for every statement in mathematics (say [[ZFC|ZF set theory]]) there is a [[Diophantine equation]] whose solvability is equivalent to the validity of the statement. This is not of much practical importance, but of large theoretical importance.
