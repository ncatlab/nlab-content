
For $\{U_i \to X\}$ a [[cover]] of a [[space]] $X$, the corresponding **Čech groupoid** is the [[groupoid]]

$$
  C(U) = (\coprod_{i j} U_i \cap U_j \rightrightarrows \coprod_i U_i)
  \,.
$$

This is the $1$-[[coskeleton]] of the full [[Čech nerve]]. See there for more details.
