## Idea

Consider a finite dimensional semisimple complex [[Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{g}$ with [[Cartan subalgebra]] $\mathfrak{h}$. 

Choose a positive [[root system]]. Let $P^+\subset \mathfrak{h}^*$ be the set of dominant integral weights. For every $\lambda\in P^+$ consider the corresponding finite dimensional left $U(\mathfrak{g})$-[[module]] $L(\lambda)$. Certain [[resolutions]] of $L(\lambda)$ are defined in a series of papers of Bernstein, Gel'fand and Gel'fand ([BBG 7x](#BBG7x)) and are now called _BGG resolutions_. There are also generalizations, e.g. for Kac-Moody algebras.
