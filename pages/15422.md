## Definition

The [[category]] of **$k$-[[affinoid spaces]]** is the [[opposite category]] of the category of $k$-[[affinoid algebras]] and bounded [[homomorphisms]] between them.

[Berkovich 09, def. 2.1.11](#Berkovich09)
