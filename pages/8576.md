## Definition

+-- {: .num_defn }
###### Definition

For $(S, \leq)$ a [[preorder]] and $A \hookrightarrow S$ a [[subset]], 
an **upper bound** of $A$ in $S$ is an [[element]] $x$ of $S$ such that $y \leq x$ whenever $y \in A$.
