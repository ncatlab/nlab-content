## Idea

A _plasma_ is a [[phase of matter|phase]] of [[baryon|baryonic]] [[matter]] at [[temperature]] high enough that [[electrons]] and [[nuclei]] do not form [[bound states]] ([[atoms]]) but each propate freely.
