## Idea

**Heyting arithmetic** is the [[constructive]] counterpart of [[Peano arithmetic]]. It adopts all the axioms of Peano arithmetic, while using [[constructive logic]] as its rules of inference. 

## References

Named after [[Arend Heyting]].
