## References

The original reference that interpreted coherent states in the [[geometric quantization]] over [[Kähler manifold]] is

* J. H. Rawnsley, Coherent states and Kaehler manifolds, Quart. J. Math. Oxford (2), 28 (1977), pp. 403–415

A review and further developments of these consideratiojns are in

* Mauro Spera, _On Kählerian coherent states_ ([djvu](http://www.bio21.bas.bg/proceedings/Proceedings_files/vol1/GEOM19.DJVU))

Generalization to non-Kähler symplectic manifolds is in 

* William D. Kirwin, _Coherent States in Geometric Quantization_ ([arXiv:0502026](http://arxiv.org/abs/math/0502026))

See also 

* Anatol Odzijewicz, _Coherent State Method in Geometric Quantization_, in Twenty Years of Bialowiez: a mathematical anthology, Aspects of Differential Geometric Methods in Physics_ (pp 47-78)
