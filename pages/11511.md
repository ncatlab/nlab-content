## Idea

In [[type II string theory]]: a [[bound state]] of $p$-[[F1-branes]] and $q$ [[D1-branes]].

Under [[S-duality]] the [[modular group]] $SL_2(\mathbb{Z})$ acts on these $(p,q)$-tuples and mixes the $F1$- and the $D1$-brane states.
