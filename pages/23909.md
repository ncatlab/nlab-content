## Idea

A [[A4-spatial groupoid|$A_4$-spatial groupoid]] whose unitors satisfy the [[triangle identities]], or equivalently, a 1-truncated [[A-infinity space|$A_\infty$-space]]/[[En-algebra|$E_1$-space]]. 

## Definitions

A **monoidal groupoid** is an [[A4-spatial groupoid|$A_4$-spatial groupoid]] $G$ such that the [[triangle identity]] is satisfied for all objects $A:G$ and $B:G$: 

$$\tau_{A,B}:(id_A \otimes \lambda_B) \circ \alpha_{A,I,B} = (\rho_A \otimes id_B)$$

## Properties

The [[oidification]] of a monoidal groupoid is a [[(2,1)-category]].
