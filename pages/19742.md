## Idea

Among all [[special orthogonal groups]] $SO(n)$, the case of $SO(8)$ is special, since in the [[ADE classification]] of [[simple Lie groups]] it corresponds to [[D4]], which makes its [[representation theory]] enjoy _[[triality]]_.
