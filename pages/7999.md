## Idea

The refinement of the notion of [[fiber integration]] in [[generalized (Eilenberg-Steenrod) cohomology]] to [[differential cohomology]].

General discussion of fiber integration for [Hopkins-Singer coefficients](differential%20cohomology%20diagram#HopkinsSingerCoefficients) is in ([HopkinsSinger, section 4.10](#HopkinsSinger)) and for integration over [[circle]] fibers in ([Bunke-Nikolaus-Völkl 13, section 4.5](#BunkeNikolausVoelkl13)).

In ([Bunke-Schick 09](#BunkeSchick09)) and precursors, existence of fiber integration in differential cohomology over circle fibers was formalized as a separate [[axiom]] ([Bunke-Schick 09, def. 1.3](#BunkeSchick09)).
