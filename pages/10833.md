
Josu&#233; Remedios is a Spanish mathematican based in the Departamento de Matem&#225;tica Fundamental, Universidad de La Laguna, 38271 La Laguna, Spain.  He has worked with [[Antonio Cegarra]] on the total complex construction for [[bisimplicial sets]].

## Selected writings

* [[Antonio Cegarra]], [[Josué Remedios]], _The behaviour of the $\overline{W}$-construction on the homotopy theory of bisimplicial sets_, Manuscripta Mathematica, volume 124 (4) Springer (2007)
