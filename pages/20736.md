## Idea

The _Bogomolny equations_ are [[differential equations]] re-expressing the [[equations of motion]] of [[self-dual Yang-Mills theory]] on 4-dimensional [[Minkowski spacetime]] for [[time]]-independent solutions in terms of dimensional reductions $D = 3$ [[Euclidean space]].
