John von Neumann (German: Johann von Neumann; Hungarian: Neumann János Lajos) was a Hungarian mathematician.

He defended his PhD thesis in 1925 advised by [[Lipót Fejér]],
with the title _Az általános halmazelmélet axiomatikus felépítése_
(Axiomatic construction of general set theory),
which introduced the [[NBG set theory]],
as well as [[classes]] and von Neumann [[ordinals]].
