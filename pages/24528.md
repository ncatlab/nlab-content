
## Overview

Schanuel's lemma is a simple result in homological algebra useful in the study of projective resolutions.

## Statement

Given two short exact sequences of $R$-modules,

$$
0 \rightarrow P_0\rightarrow P_1\rightarrow M\rightarrow 0
$$
$$
0\rightarrow P_0'\rightarrow P_1'\rightarrow M\rightarrow 0
$$
with $P_0,P_1,P_0',P_1'$ projective, there is an isomorphism of
the direct sums
$$
P_0\oplus P_1' \cong P_0'\oplus P_1
$$
