## Statement

+-- {: .num_prop}
###### Proposition

Let $X$ be a [[topological space]] which is

1. [[Lindelöf space|Lindelöf]] (every [[open cover]] has a [[countable cover|countable]] subcover);

1. [[regular topological space|regular]].

Then $X$ is [[paracompact topological space]].
