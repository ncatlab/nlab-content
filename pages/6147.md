__Tannaka-Krein theorem__ (in the narrow sense) is a particular [[Tannaka duality]] theorem for compact topological groups. 

The original theorem is (in two independent and different formulations) stated and proved in  

* [[Tadao Tannaka]], _Über den Dualitätssatz der nichtkommutativen topologischen Gruppen_, Tohoku Math. J. 45 (1938), n. 1, 1–12 (project euclid has only Tohoku new series!)
* M.G. Krein, _A principle of duality for bicompact groups and quadratic block algebras_, Doklady AN SSSR __69__ (1949), 725–728. 

For a textbook treatment see

* E. Hewitt, K. A. Ross, _Abstract harmonic analysis_, 2 vols.
