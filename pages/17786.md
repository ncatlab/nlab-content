## Idea 

A _super-commutative algebra_ is a [[commutative algebra]] _[[internalization|internal]]_ to the [[symmetric monoidal category]] of [[super vector spaces]], hence a [[superalgebra]] such that for $a, b$ any two elements of homogeneous degree $deg(a), deg(b) \in \mathbb{Z}/2$, then

$$
  a b = (-1)^{deg(a) deg(b)} b a 
  \,.
$$
