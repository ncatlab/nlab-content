__Friedrich Wilhelm Bauer__ (1931-2019) was a German algebraic topologist. His students include Bernd G&#252;nther, Peter Mrozik, Claus Ringel and Wolfgang Metzler. 

His contributions include study of stable homotopy theory, homology theories, bordism, categories of chain complexes, Quillen model categories, an original approach to shape theory and especially to  strong shape theory.
