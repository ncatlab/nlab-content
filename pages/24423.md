## Idea

A **super homotopy type theory** is a [[modal homotopy type theory]] which extends [[differential cohesive homotopy type theory]] with the [[rheonomy modality|rheonomy]], [[bosonic modality|bosonic]], and [[fermionic modality|fermionic]] [[modal operators]] ($\mathrm{Rh}$, $\rightsquigarrow$, $\rightrightarrows$ respectively). 

{#AlsoCalledElastic} (Also called *solid* homotopy theory $[$[[schreiber:Proper Orbifold Cohomology|SS20, ยง3.1.3]]$]$, see at *[[geometry of physics -- categories and toposes]]* the sections on *[solid toposes](geometry+of+physics+--+categories+and+toposes#SolidToposes)* and *[solid $\infty$-toposes](geometry+of+physics+--+categories+and+toposes#SolidInfinityToposes)*.)
