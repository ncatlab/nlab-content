## Idea

A [[compactification]] of [[configuration spaces of points]] was introduced in [Fulton-MacPherson 94](#FultonMacPherson94) and an [[operad]]-[[structure]] defined on it in [Getzler-Jones 94](#GetzlerJones94), now called the _Fulton-MacPherson operad_. This underlies the models of configuration spaces by [[graph complexes]], see there for more.
