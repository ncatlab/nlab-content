**Marc Levine** is an American [[algebraic geometry|algebraic geometer]], a specialist on [[motives]], [[algebraic cycles]] and [[algebraic K-theory]]. He proposed several constructions of the (triangulated) category of [[motive]]s and constructed [[algebraic cobordism]] together with [[Fabien Morel]].
