## Idea

The [[vector]] representing the rate of change of the position a [[point]]/[[particle]] as it moves along a [[curve]]. 

## Definition

Given an $n$-[[dimension|dimensional]] [[Euclidean space]] $\mathbb{R}^n$, 
a curve $\mathcal{C}$ in $\mathbb{R}^n$ could be parameterized by a function $\overrightarrow{r}:\mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}^n$. Then the **velocity** of a particle traveling along $\mathcal{C}$ in $\mathbb{R}^n$ is given by the [[derivative]] of the function $\overrightarrow{v}(t) \coloneqq \partial_t \overrightarrow{r}(t)$.
