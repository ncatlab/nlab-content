
The initials 'SGA I' (or in more modern times 'SGA1') refer to the [[SGA]] seminar notes from [[Grothendieck]]'s seminar (1960-61) at [IH&#201;S](http://www.ihes.fr), Bures-sur-Yvette (see at *[[SGA]]* for more). 

* [[A. Grothendieck]], [[Michèle Raynaud]], _Rev&#234;tements &#233;tales et groupe fondamental_ (SGA I), Lecture Notes in Mathematics __224__, Springer 1971 [doi:10.1007/BFb0058656](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/BFb0058656), retyped as [math.AG/0206203](http://arxiv.org/abs/math/0206203); published  version Documents Math&#233;matiques __3__, Soci&#233;t&#233; Math&#233;matique de France, Paris 2003)


These notes contain:

* [[Grothendieck's Galois theory|Galois categories]]

* [[Galois theory|étale fundamental group]]

* [[Grothendieck's section conjecture]]

* [[descent theory]] (chapter SGA I.6)
