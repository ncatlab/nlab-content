## Idea

## Properties

The [[conformal compactification]], of _complexified_ [[Minkowski spacetime]] $\mathbb{R}^{3,1}$, is given by the Klein quadric. (eg, [Fioresi-Lledo-Varadarajan 07, section 2](#FioresiLledoVaradarajan07)).
