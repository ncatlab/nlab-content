## Idea

A potential [[quantum anomaly]] in [[M-theory]]/[[type IIA string theory]].

## References
 {#References}


### General

The anomaly was found in

* [[Duiliu-Emanuel Diaconescu]], [[Gregory Moore]], [[Edward Witten]], _$E_8$ Gauge Theory, and a Derivation of K-Theory from M-Theory_, Adv.Theor.Math.Phys.6:1031-1134 (2003) &lbrack;[arXiv:hep-th/0005090](http://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0005090)&rbrack;

while mostly assuming vanishing NS-fields (the [[B-field]]). The non-vanishing B-field in this context was considered in 

* [[Gregory Moore]], [[Natalia Saulina]], _T-Duality, and the K-Theoretic Partition Function of TypeIIA Superstring Theory_, Nucl.Phys. B670 (2003) 27-89 ([arXiv:hep-th/0206092](http://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0206092))

where however the anomaly as such is not discussed. This was then put together in 

* [[Varghese Mathai]], [[Hisham Sati]], _Some Relations between Twisted K-theory and E8 Gauge Theory_, JHEP 0403:016 (2004) &lbrack;[arXiv:hep-th/0312033](http://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0312033)&rbrack;

which hence discusses the anomaly for [[twisted K-theory]].
