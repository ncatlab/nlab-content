## Definition

A left (right) ideal $I$ in a ring $R$ is __modular__ if  $\exists e\in R$ such that $\forall r \in R$ $r e - r\in I$ (respectively $e r - r\in I$).

## Examples

Every left ideal containing a modular ideal is modular. In particular, any maximal ideal containing given proper modular ideal is modular.

## Properties

[[Jacobson ideal]] of a ring coincides with the intersection of all maximal modular ideals.
