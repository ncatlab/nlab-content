## Idea

_Proof nets_ are a graphical way to denote [[proofs]] in [[linear logic]].

Under the [[categorical semantics]] of (multiplicative intuitionistic) [[linear logic]] in [[monoidal categories]], proof nets are the [[string diagrams]] "with boxes" in these categories (e.g. [Melli&#232;s 06](#Mellies06)).

Hence in with [[linear logic]]/[[linear type theory]] interpreted as [[quantum logic]]/[[quantum computation]], proof nets correspond to [[quantum circuits]] or [[Feynman diagrams]] ([Blute Panangaden](#BlutePanangaden)).
