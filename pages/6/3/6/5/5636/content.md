## Idea

_Seiberg-Witten theory_ studies the [[moduli space]] of [[vacua]] in [[N=2 D=4 super Yang-Mills theory]], in particular the [[electric-magnetic duality]] ([[Montonen-Olive duality]]) of the theory.
