## Idea

An _11-manifold_ is a [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] 11. (Generally a [[topological manifold]], but it can be specified to a [[PL manifold]] or a [[smooth manifold]].) 11-manifolds are of particular interest as underlying [[spacetime]] in [[M-theory]] and its low energy limit in [[D=11 supergravity]].

## Examples

* 11-[[sphere]]
* [[real projective space]] $\mathbb{R}P^11$

## Properties

\begin{proposition}
For a smooth 11-manifold $M$, its last three [[Stiefel-Whitney classes]] vanish:
$$
w_9(M)
=w_10(M)
=w_11(M)
=0.
$$
\end{proposition}
More generally, for every $4n+3$-dimensional [[smooth manifold]], one has $w_{4n+1}=w_{4n+2}(M)=w_{4n+3}(M)=0$. Hence this property also holds for [[3-manifolds]], [[7-manifolds]] and [[15-manifolds]].
