J. F. Jardine or Rick Jardine is a homotopy theorist at the University of Western Ontario in London, Canada. His work on model category structures on simplicial sheaves and simplicial presheaves is fundamental in the subject. His web pages have many course material in homotopy theory, sheaf theory and related subjects. 

* [webpage](http://www.math.uwo.ca/~jardine)
* P.G. Goerss, J.F. Jardine, _Simplicial homotopy theory_, book (and [errata.pdf](http://www.math.uwo.ca/~jardine/errata-simp/errata.pdf))
