## Definition

The fat Delta category $\underline{\Delta}$ is the category whose [[objects]] are the [[epimorphisms]] $g:X \twoheadrightarrow Y$ in the [[simplex category]] and whose [[morphisms]] are [[commutative squares]] in the [[simplex category]] whose vertical arrows are [[epimorphisms]] and horizontal top arrow is a [[monomorphism]]. 

$$\array{& X & \overset{f}\hookrightarrow & Z & \\
          g & \downarrow &&\downarrow & g'\\
          &Y & \underset{f'}\rightarrow& W & \\
}$$
