## Idea

**Einselection**, short for "**e**nvironment-**in**duced [[superselection sector|superselection]]", is a name coined by [Zurek (2003)](#Zurek03) for a process which explains the emergence of [[classical physics|classical]] behavior in an open [[quantum system]] from its interaction with its environment.
