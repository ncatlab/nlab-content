$A_\infty$-spaces were introduced by J. Stasheff as a refinement of an $H$-[[H-space|group]] taking into account higher coherences.

* Stasheff, _Homotopy associative H-spaces I_, _II_, Trans. Amer. Math. Soc. 108 (1963), 275--312

Compare $E_\infty$-[[E-infinity-space|spaces]].
