## Definition

The _supergravity Lie 6-algebra_ is an extension of the [[supergravity Lie 3-algebra]] by a super-[[Lie algebra cohomology|Lie algebra cocycle]] in degree 7.

In terms of its [[Chevalley-Eilenberg algebra]] $CE(\mathfrak{sugra}_6)$ it is defined by

$$
  d_{CE}  : \omega^{a b} \mapsto \omega^{a c} \wedge \omega_c{}^b
$$

$$
  d_{CE} : e^a = -\omega^{a b} e_b  - \frac{1}{2}i \bar \psi \wedge  \Gamma^a \psi
$$

$$
  d_{CE} : \psi \mapsto - \frac{1}{4}\omega^{a b} \Gamma^{a b} 
$$

$$
  d_{CE} : C \mapsto \frac{1}{2} \bar \psi \wedge \Gamma^{a b} \psi \wedge e_a \wedge e_b
$$

$$
  d_{CE} : D \mapsto - \frac{1}{2} \bar \psi \wedge \Gamma^{a_1 \cdots a_5} \psi \wedge e_{a_1} \wedge \cdots \wedge e_{a_5}
  -
  \frac{13}{2} \bar \psi \Gamma^{a_1 a_2} \psi \wedge e_{a_1} \wedge e_{a_2} \wedge C
  \,.
$$
