
On the *[[Nambu-Poisson M5-brane model]]* --- a [[sigma-model]] for [[M5-branes]] with [[product manifold|product]] [[worldvolume]] $\Sigma^{1,5} \simeq X^{1,2} \times Y^3$ carrying large constant [[self-dual higher gauge field|$H_3$]] [[flux density]] --- obtained via an [[M-theory|M-theoretic]] version the of [[Myers effect]] from the [[BLG model]] for [[coincident brane|coincident]] [[M2-branes]] with [[worldvolume]] $X^{1,2}$, by taking the latter's controlling [[M-brane 3-algebra]] to be the [[Nambu bracket]] on the [[smooth functions]] over the [[3-manifold]] $Y^3$ (globalized via [[VPD]] [[gauge symmetry]]).
