## Idea

[[mirror symmetry|Mirror symmetry]] is in fact a [[duality in string theory|duality]] involving families of varieties. Dependence on the moduli is defined using the mirror map, which can therefore be described using corresponding [[Gauss-Manin connection]] and corresponding  [[Picard-Fuchs equations]].
