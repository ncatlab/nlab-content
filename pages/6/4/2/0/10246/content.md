## Idea


The term _moduli_ refers to

1. generally in [[mathematics]]: [[elements]] of a [[moduli space]]/[[moduli stack]]; more generally: [[generalized elements]], hence [[maps]] to a [[moduli stack]];

1. specifically in [[physics]] and notably in [[string theory]]: parameters of a [[KK compactification]], hence in particular elements of the [[moduli space of Riemannian metrics]] on a given KK-compactification [[fiber]]. See also at _[[moduli stabilization]]_.
