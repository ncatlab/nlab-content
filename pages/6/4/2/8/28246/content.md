## Idea

*D=7 [[Yang-Mills theory]]* (D=7 YM theory) studies the [[Yang-Mills equations]] over a base [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] $D=7$, hence a smooth [[7-manifold]]. A special case is over [[G₂ manifolds]].
