## Definition

A **pivotal category** is an [[autonomous category]] equipped with a [[monoidal natural transformation|monoidal natural isomorphism]] $A \to (A^*)^*$.  Pivotal categories have also been called "sovereign categories."

A right autonomous category with such an isomorphism is automatically left autonomous too, so the right/left distinction does not apply to pivotal categories.
