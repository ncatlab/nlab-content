## Idea

The notion of _homotopy spectral sequence_ adapts the notion of _[[spectral sequence]]_ from [[homological algebra]]/[[stable homotopy theory]] to unstable/[[non-abelian cohomology|non-abelian]] [[homotopy theory]].
