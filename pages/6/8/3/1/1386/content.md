As for ordinary [[sheafification]], _$(\infty,1)$-sheafification_ is the left exact left adjoint to the inclusion of [[(∞,1)-sheaves]] into [[(∞,1)-presheaves]].

For more details see [[(∞,1)-category of (∞,1)-sheaves]].
