## Definition

The **law of non-contradiction** says that given a [[proposition]] $P$, $P$ and its [[denial negation]] $\neg P$ are [[mutually exclusive]] in that $\neg (P \wedge \neg P)$ holds. 

The law of non-contradiction fails in [[paraconsistent logic]].
