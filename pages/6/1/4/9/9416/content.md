The Springer theory is an important approach to the representation theory of [[Weyl group]]s or semisimple algebraic groups. In particular, it features so called Springer representations, which are associated to the unipotent conjugacy classes with an additional parameter, via so called Springer correspondence. 

* wikipedia [Springer correspondence](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Springer_correspondence)
* T. A. Springer, _A construction of representations of Weyl groups_, Invent. Math. __44__ (1978), no. 3, 279&#8211;293 [MR0491988](http://www.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=0491988) [doi](http://dx.doi.org/10.1007%2FBF01403165) 
* N. Chriss, V. Ginzburg, _Representation theory and complex geometry_, Birkh&#228;user 1997. x+495 pp.
