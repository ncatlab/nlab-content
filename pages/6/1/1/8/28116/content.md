## Statement

A [[finitely generated group|finitely generated]] [[linear group]] over a [[field]] has either

* a [[solvable group|solvable]] [[subgroup]] of [[finite number|finite]] [[index of a subgroup|index]],

* a [[subgroup]] which is a [[free group]] on two generators.

(due to [Tits 1972](#Tits1972))

This generalizes to hyperbolic groups and some other prominent classes.
