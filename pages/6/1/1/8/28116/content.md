## Idea

If $X$ is a topological spaces, $A$ and $B$ to isomorphic subspaces of $X$, and one is given a fundamental group of $X$, what is the fundamental group of $X$ with attached handle from $A$ to $B$ ?

## Definition

Given a group $G$, and isomorphism $\phi:A\to B$ of its subgroups, the Higman-Neumann-Neumann extension, or HNN extension is the quotient $G\star \langle t\rangle/I$
where $I$ is the normal subgroup generated by $t^{-1}a t\phi(t^{-1})$ for all $a\in A$.
