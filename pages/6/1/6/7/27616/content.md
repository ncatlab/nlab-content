## Idea

A notion intermediate of those of [[bisimplicial set]] and [[bicubical set]]

## Definition

A _cubical-simplicial set_ is a [[functor]]

$$
  F : \Box^{op} \times \Delta^{op} \to \mathrm{Set}
$$

where $\Box$ is a [[category of cubes]] and $\Delta$ is the [[simplex category]].

This is the same as a [[cubical object]] in the [[category]] of [[simplicial sets]].

## Examples

* [[triangulated type theory]] has [[categorical semantics]] in cubical-simplicial sets where the [[category of cubes]] used is the category of [[finite set|finite]] [[flat distributive lattices]].
