

#References#


The original article is

* Plebanski, _On the separation of Einsteinian substructures_, J. Math. Phys.  18, 2511 (1977)

An account of the general formalism is in

* R. Capovilla, T. Jacobson, J. Dell, L. Mason, _Selfdual 2-forms and gravity_ Classical and Quantum Gravity 8, 41 (1991) .

A discussion of practical computations in this formalism is in

* Kirill Krasnov, _Plebanski Formulation of General Relativity: A Practical Introduction_ ([arXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/0904.0423))
