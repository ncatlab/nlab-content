Nikolai Mishachev (Николай Мишачёв) is a Russian mathematician working on the [[h-principle]] and related topics.

Diploma (1974), PhD (1980), Saint Petersburg State University, Faculty of Mathematics and Mechanics.  From 1982 employed at Lipetsk State Technical University.

[MathNet.ru page](https://www.mathnet.ru/rus/person22699), [university page](https://www.stu.lipetsk.ru/kaf/vm/about/staff.html).
