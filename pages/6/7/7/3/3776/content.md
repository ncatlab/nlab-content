**Shigeru Mukai** is a Japanese algebraic geometer. He discovered a first historical example of a nontrivial [[automorphism]] of the [[derived category of coherent sheaves]] on an [[abelian variety]]. Generlizations of this example are studied under the name of *[[Fourier-Mukai transforms]]*, or *[[integral transforms]]* of [[derived categories]]. 

Mukai's textbook (advanced undergraduate level) on moduli spaces has been translated from  Japanese to English and is one of the masterpieces of exposition in [[algebraic geometry]], in accessible manner but with content to the point.
