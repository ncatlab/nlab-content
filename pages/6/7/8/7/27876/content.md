## Definition

A __compact metrizable space__ is a [[metrizable topological space]] that is also a [[compact space]].

## Terminology

A compact metrizable space is also known as a __[[compactum]]__, and this is by far the most common use of this terminology.  Occasionally, “compactum” may refer to a [[compact Hausdorff space]] instead.
