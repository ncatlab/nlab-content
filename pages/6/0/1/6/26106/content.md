## Idea

The *Nicolai map* is a (non-[[local quantum field theory|local]] and non-linear) transformation of [[boson|bosonic]] [[quantum field theories|quantum fields]] to [[free quantum field|free fields]] whose existence characterizes the original bosonic [[quantum field theory]] as arising by [[fermionic path integral|integrating out the fermions]] of a [[global symmetry|globally]] [[supersymmetry|supersymmetric]] field theory.
