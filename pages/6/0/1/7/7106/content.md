## Idea

Quantum hadrodynamics is (or should be) an [[effective field theory]] derived from [[QCD]] that describes [[confinement|confined]] [[bound states]] in [[nuclear physics]] -- [[mesons]] and [[hadrons]]  -- such as [[protons]] and [[neutrons]].

For more see _[[chiral perturbation theory]]_.
