## Definition

The *empty simplicial set* is the [[simplicial set]] $\varnothing_\bullet$ all whose component sets are [[empty sets]]:

$$
  \underset{n \in \mathbb{N}}{\forall}
  \;\;
  \varnothing_n \;=\; \varnothing.
$$

The face- and degeneracy maps are, accordingly, each the unique [[empty function]] from the [[empty set]] to itself.

## Properties

* The empty simplicial set is a [[strict initial object]] in the [[category]] of [[SimplicialSets]].
