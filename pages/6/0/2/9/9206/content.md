## Idea

[[super Yang-Mills theory]] on [[spacetimes]] of [[dimension]] $D=5$, hence the [[supersymmetry|supersymmetric]] version of [[D=5 Yang-Mills theory]].

## Properties

### As the worldvolume theory of the D4-brane

$D = 5$ SYM may be regarded as the [[worldvolume]] field theory on the [[D4-brane]] in [[type II string theory]].


### Via reduction from the M5-brane / $D=6$ SCFT

For relation to the [[D=6 N=(2,0) SCFT]] via [[KK-compactification]] on a [[circle]] [[fiber]], hence as [[worldvolume]] theory of the [[double dimensional reduction]] of the [[M5-brane]] (see also at [[Perry-Schwarz Lagrangian]]), see the references [below](#ReferencesRelationToD=6SCFT).
