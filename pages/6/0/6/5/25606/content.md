## Idea

For $V$ a [[monoidal model category]], a *$V$-enriched monoidal model category* is a [[model category]] equipped with 

1. the [[structure]] of a [[monoidal model category]],

1. the [[structure]] of a $V$-[[enriched model category]],

1. the compatibility structure that makes the [[underlying]] [[monoidal category|monoidal]] and [[enriched category|$V$-enriched]] structure a 

   $V$-[[enriched monoidal category]].

## Examples

* For $V =$ [[classical model structure on simplicial sets|$Set_{Qu}$]] one typically speaks of *[[simplicial monoidal model categories]]*.
