## Definition ##
A **symmetric monoidal dagger category** is a [[braided monoidal dagger category]] $C$ such that for all objects $A \in Ob(C)$ and $B \in Ob(C)$, $\beta_{A,B} \circ \beta_{B, A} = \Iota_{A,B}$.
