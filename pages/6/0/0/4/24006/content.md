A mathematical journal by Koninlijk Wiskundig Genootschap (KWG; Royal Dutch Mathematical Society), previously by Koninklijke Akademie van Wetenschappen (KNAW; Royal Netherlands Academy).

Prior to 1990, this journal existed under different names (used in parallel) and different volume numbering schemes (also used in parallel).
See the [Wikipedia page](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indagationes_Mathematicae) for more information.

Chronology:

1895: Founded under the title of _Koninklijke Akademie van Wetenschappen: Proceedings of the Section of Sciences_.

1939: Mathematics articles published separately, with alternative title (used in parallel) _Indagationes Mathematicae_, with 41 subtracted from the volume number.

1951: Split into three journals.  Series A (Mathematical Sciences) published by North-Holland under the alternative title (used in parallel) _Indagationes Mathematicae (Proceedings)_.

1990: Published under the sole title of _Indagationes Mathematicae_.  Volume numbering reset to 1.
