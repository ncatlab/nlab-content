## Idea

Dirac manifold is a smooth manifold with a Dirac structure in the sense of Courant 1990. (Beware that sometimes "Dirac structure" is used for Dirac brackets in earlier contexts which are related but not formalized in this way) An almost Dirac structure on a manifold $M$ is a subbundle $L\subset T M \oplus T^*M$ of the tangent [[Courant algebroid]],  which is isotropic under certain symmetric pairing. An almost Dirac structure is a Dirac structure if it satisfies an integrability condition.
