## Idea

The *Wedderburn–Artin theorem* says that any [[semisimple ring]] is a [[finite product]] of [[matrix algebras]] over [[division rings]]. 

### Generalizations

The Wedderburn–Artin theorem can be generalized to the following:

If $R$ is a [[semisimple object]] in a [[semiadditive category]] $\mathsf{A}$, then the [[endomorphism rig]] $End(R)$ is a finite product of matrix rigs over [[division rigs]].
