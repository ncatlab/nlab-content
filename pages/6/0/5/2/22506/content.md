## Idea

*ER = EPR* is  the conjecture in [[theoretical physics]] that [[entangled particles]] (Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen phenomenon) are connected by a [[wormhole]] (Einstein-Rosen bridge). It was proposed by ([MS13](#EREPR)) as a solution for the [[black hole firewall problem]].

See [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ER%3DEPR) for now.
