The **Australian Category Theory Seminar**, previously called the **Sydney Category Theory Seminar**, is a weekly meeting on ([[higher category theory|higher]]) [[category theory]] held by the Centre of Australian Category Theory that has been held since 1970. The first seminar was probably held on the 23rd September, 1970.
