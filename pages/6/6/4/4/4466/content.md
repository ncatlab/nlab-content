__Teimuraz Pirashvili__ is a Georgian mathematician (Razmadze Institute of the Georgian Academy of Sciences and University of Leicester) who has contributed to homotopical and [[homological algebra]], [[algebraic topology]], [[homotopy theory|homotopy]] and [[category theory]].
