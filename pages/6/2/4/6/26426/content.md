## Definition

A **rational dagger category** is a [[dagger category]] with a [[zero object]] and dagger [[biproducts]] such that every [[morphism]] has a [[kernel]] that is a dagger section and every binary [[diagonal morphism]] is a [[normal monomorphism]].
