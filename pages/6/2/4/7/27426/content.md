## Idea

In [[relativistic quantum field theory]] the *Unruh effect* &lbrack;[Fulling 1973](#Fulling73)&rbrack; is the phenomenon that uniformly [[acceleration|accelerated]] [[observers]], even in [[vacuum]], observe a [[thermal field theory|thermal bath]] of [[particles]].

This phenomenon is a close cousin of that of *[[Hawking radiation]]* of [[black holes]]
