## Idea

In the theory of [[quantum entanglement]], *embezzlement* &lbrack;[van Dam & Hayden 2003](#vanDamHayden03)&rbrack; refers to the *approximation* of [[quantum entanglement|entangled]] [[quantum states]] by [[images]] of (local) [[unitary transformations]] on unentangled (tensor product-)states upon [[tensor product|tensoring]] with an ancillary state that is approximately left invariant under the transformation (the "catalyst" of the embezzlement).
