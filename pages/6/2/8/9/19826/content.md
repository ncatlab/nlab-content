_Esquisses Mathématiques_ is a series of Paris-Amiens University reports published from 1970 to 1983 which contains more or less preliminary works, in particular theses of members of the research team _Théorie et Applications des Catégories_ (Paris-Amiens), directed by [[Charles Ehresmann]] and [[Andrée Ehresmann]].

Most of them are still available and can be obtained by sending an e-mail to [[Andrée Charles Ehresmann]].
