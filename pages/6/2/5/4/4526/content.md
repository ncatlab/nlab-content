## Definition
  
An _smooth $\infty$-group_ is a [[group object in an (∞,1)-category|group object]] in the [[cohesive (∞,1)-topos]] [[Smooth∞Grpd]] of [[smooth ∞-groupoid]]s.

## Examples

### Lie groups

An ordinary [[Lie group]] is a 0-[[truncated]] $\infty$-Lie group.

### Lie 2-groups

A [[Lie 2-group]] is a 1-[[truncated]] $\infty$-Lie group.

* [[string 2-group]]

### Lie 6-groups

A Lie 6-group is a 5-[[truncated]] $\infty$-Lie group.

* [[fivebrane 6-group]].
