## Idea

The generalization of [[Teichm√ºller theory]] to [[arithmetic geometry]] has been called _inter-universal Teichm&#252;ller theory_ (often abbreviated _IUTT_) by [[Shinichi Mochizuki]].

The term "inter-universal" refers to the fact that the theory is meant to formulated explicitly in a way that respects [[universe enlargement]] ([Mochizuki12d, remark 3.1.4](#Mochizuki12d)).

It is claimed ([Mochizuki 12d](#Mochizuki12d)) but currently unchecked that a proof of the [[abc conjecture]] can be found in this context.
