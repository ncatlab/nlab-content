## Definition

A **light profinite set** is a [[countable set|countable]] [[limit]] of [[finite sets]].

Light profinite sets are used to construct [[light condensed sets]] in [[condensed mathematics]]

## Category of light profinite sets

The category of light profinite sets is equivalent to the [[opposite category|opposite]] of the category of [[countable set|countably]] presented [[Boolean algebras]].
