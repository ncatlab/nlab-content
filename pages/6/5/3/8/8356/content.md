## Idea

The _Brouwer-Heyting-Kolmogorov_ interpretation of [[intuitionistic logic]] is a description of [[proofs]] of [[propositions]] in [[intuitionistic logic]] as [[functions]], often [[computable function]]s, where it is also called the _realizability interpretation_.
