## Properties

### Relation to standard Young tableaux

The $n$th Motzkin number equals the 
the number $\left\vert sYT_n(3) \right\vert$ of [[standard Young tableaux]] with $n$ boxes and $\leq 3$ rows.

([Regev 81](#Regev81))
