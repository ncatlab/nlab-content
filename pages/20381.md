## Idea

The [[semi-spin group]] in [[dimension]] 16.

## Properties

### As a subgroup of $E_8$

The [[subgroup]] of the [[exceptional Lie group]] [[E8]] which corresponds to the [[Lie algebra]]-inclusion $\mathfrak{so}(16) \hookrightarrow \mathfrak{e}_8$ is the [[semi-spin group]] in that dimension

$$
  SemiSpin(16)
  \;\subset\;
  E_8
$$

On the other hand, the [[special orthogonal group]] $SO(16)$ is _not_ a [[subgroup]] of $E_8$ (e.g. [McInnes 99a, p. 11](#McInnes99a)).

### In heterotic string theory

In [[heterotic string theory]] with [[gauge group]] the [[direct product group]] $E_8 \times E_8$ it is usually only this [[subgroup]] $Semispin(16) \times SemiSpin(16)$ which is considered (but typically denoted $Spin(16)/\mathbb{Z}_2$, see also [Distler-Sharpe 10, Sec. 1](parameterized+WZW+model#DistlerSharpe10)).
