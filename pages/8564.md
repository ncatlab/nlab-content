## Definition

A [[ring]] $R$ is a **principal ideal domain** if

1. it is an [[integral domain]];

1. every [[ideal]] in $R$ is a [[principal ideal]].

## Examples

* any [[field]]

* the ring of [[integers]]

* a [[polynomial ring]] with [[coefficients]] in a field
