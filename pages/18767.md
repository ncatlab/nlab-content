# Right/left fibrations of simplicial spaces

* table of contents
{: toc}

## Idea

The analogue for [[complete Segal space|(complete) Segal spaces]] of the notion of [[right/left Kan fibration]] for [[quasi-categories]].

## References

* P. B de Brito, *Segal Objects and the Grothendieck Construction*, 2016. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1605.00706)

* Nima Rasekh, *Yoneda Lemma for Simplicial Spaces*, 2017. [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.03160)

* Nima Rasekh, *Cartesian Fibrations and Representability*, 2017.  [arxiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.03670)
