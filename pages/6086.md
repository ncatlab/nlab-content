## Idea

[[Tannaka duality]] for [[Lie groupoid]]s.

In ([Trentinaglia](#Trentinaglia)) Tannaka duality for [[proper Lie groupoid]]s is established by using instead of [[vector bundle]]s something more general: not quite [[quasicoherent sheaves]] as in [[Tannaka duality for geometric stacks]], maybe, but something the author calls "smooth Euclidean fields".
