## Idea

An _identiy matrix_ is a [[diagonal matrix]] with all diagonal entries equal to the [[identity element]] $1 \in R$ of the [[ground ring]].

The identiy matrices are the [[identity elements]] in their [[matrix algebra]], whence the name.
