## Idea

The generalization of the concept of _[[nilpotent Lie algebra]]_ from [[Lie algebras]] to [[L-infinity algebras|$L_\infty$-algebras]]..

Under the [[formal duality]] between [[L-∞ algebras|$L_\infty$-algebras]] and their [[Chevalley-Eilenberg algebra|Chevalley-Eilenberg]] [[dgc-algebras]], [[connective]] nilpotent $L_\infty$-algebras correspond [[bijection|bijectively]] to the connected [[Sullivan models]] ([Berglund 2015, Thm. 2.3](#Berglund15)).


## Properties

### Rational homotopy theory

The [[fundamental theorem of dgc-algebraic rational homotopy theory]] says that the [[homotopy theory]] of  nilpotent $L_\infty$-algebras [[of finite type]] over the [[rational numbers]] is equivalently that of [[rational topological spaces|rational]] [[nilpotent topological spaces]] of [[finite type]].
