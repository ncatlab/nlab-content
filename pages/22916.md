The term *self-adjoint* is used in various unrelated senses:

* A [[self-adjoint functor]] is a functor that is [[adjunction|adjoint]] to itself.

* A [[self-adjoint operator]] and [[self-adjoint extension]] on a [[Hilbert space]].

* A [[self-adjoint morphism]] in a [[dagger category]].
