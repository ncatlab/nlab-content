## Definition

A *Gauss-Manin connection* in the strict sense is a [[flat connection]] on bundles of [[ordinary cohomology|ordinary]] [[cohomology groups]].

More generall, under the identification of [[local systems]] -- in the sense of [[locally constant sheaf|locally constant]] and locally [[free module|free]] [[abelian sheaves]] -- with [[flat vector bundles]], the [[flat connection]] induced on these [[vector bundles]] is called the *Gauss-Manin connection* (e.g. [Voisin 2002, Def. 9.13](#Voisin02)).

An important application is to Picard-Fuchs theory.
