## Idea

The operations on an [[H-space]] $X$ equip its [[homology]] with the [[mathematical structure|structure]] of  [[ring]]. At least for [[ordinary homology]] this is known as the _[[Pontrjagin ring]]_ $H_*(X)$ of $X$.
