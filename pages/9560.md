## Definition


The [[category]] whose [[objects]] are [[C-star algebras]] and whose [[morphisms]] are $C^\ast$-[[homomorphisms]].

## Properties

The universal additive split-exact and homotopy-invariant [[localization]] of $C^\ast$ at the $C^\ast$-algebra of [[compact operators]] is [[KK-theory]]. See there for more.
