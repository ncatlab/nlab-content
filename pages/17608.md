## Idea 

The [[KK-compactification]] of [[11-dimensional supergravity]]/[[M-theory]] on [[Calabi-Yau manifold]] fibers. 

### On $CY_3$

For CY fibers of complex dimension 6, this yields the strong coupling version of CY-compactification of [[type II string theory]]/[[type II supergravity]] (see [[supersymmetry and Calabi-Yau manifolds]]).

Leads to [[5d supergravity]] [[effective field theory]].

See also at _[[F/M-theory on elliptically fibered Calabi-Yau 3-folds]]_.
