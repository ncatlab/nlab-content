## Definition ##

A **univalent groupoid** or **saturated groupoid** is a [[univalent dagger category]] $A$ where for all objects $a:A$ and $b:A$ and morphisms $f:Hom(a, b)$, $f \circ f^\dagger = id_B$ and $f^\dagger \circ f = id_A$. Equivalently, it is a univalent dagger category where every morphism is a [[unitary morphism]].
