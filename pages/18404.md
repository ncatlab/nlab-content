## Statement

Let $X$ be a [[differentiable manifold]] ([[paracompact topological space|paracompact]]). If every [[compact topological space|compact]] [[subspace]] of $X$ is contained in an [[open subset]] [[diffeomorphism|diffeomorphic]] to [[Euclidean space]] (with its standard [[smooth structure]]), then all of $X$ is diffeomorphic to Euclidean  space: $X \simeq \mathbb{R}^n$.

See for instance [Milnor 64, lemma 3 (p. 168)](#Milnor64)
