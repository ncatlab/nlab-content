# Groups of Lie type

## Definition

A __group of Lie type__ $G(k)$ is a (not necessarily [[finite group|finite]]) [[group]] of [[rational point]]s of a [[reductive group|reductive]] linear [[algebraic group]] $G$ with values in the [[field]] $k$.

+-- {: .un_remark}
###### Warning

Not to be confused with the notion of _[[Lie group]]_.
