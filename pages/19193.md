## Idea

In the context of [[cosmology]], _structure formation_ refers to the process by which cosmological structures such as [[galaxy clusters]] form under gravitational collapse from a primordial gas.

Various computer simulation test theory again observation ([Millenium II](#MilleniumII), [Illustris](#Illustris))
