## Idea

What has come to be called _Poisson-Lie T-Duality_ (due to [Klimcik-Ševera 95](#KlimcikSevera95), [von Unge 02](#vonUnge02)) is a generalization of [[T-duality]] from [[fiber bundles]] equipped with an [[abelian group]] of [[isometries]] ([[torus]] [[bundles]]) to those with a [[nonabelian group]] of isometries.
