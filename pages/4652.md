A **fork** is a diagram of the form

<center><img src="http://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?
\xymatrix{
A\ar[r]_{e} %26 B \ar@%3C.5ex%3E [r]^{f}\ar@%3C-.5ex%3E[r]_{g} %26 C
}" />
</center>

such that $f e=g e$. A special type of a fork is an [[equalizer]]. 

A dual notion is also called a fork, 
but some people distinguish forks and coforks.
