
I. A. Volodin (И. А. Володин) is a Russian mathematician who in 1971 published an alternative approach to the higher algebraic [[K-theory]] of rings, which was later shown by [[Suslin]] to give equivalent $K$-groups to the theory using the [[Quillen plus construction]].
