**Lagrangean**, or **Lagrangian, Grassmanian** of a (real) [[symplectic vector space]] $V = (V,\omega)$ is the space $LGrass(V)$ whose points are the [[lagrangian submanifold|Lagrangean]] (= maximal isotropic) subspaces in $V$. The group of [[symplectomorphism]]s of $V$ naturally acts on $LGrass(V)$. The unitary group $U_J(V)$ associated to any fixed $\omega$-compatible complex structure $J$ on $V$ acts transitively on $LGrass(V)$. The [[Maslov index|universal Maslov index]] is the only homotopy invariant of loops in $LGrass(V)$. 

* Sean Bates, Alan Weinstein, _Lectures on the geometry of quantization_, [pdf](http://www.math.berkeley.edu/~alanw/GofQ.pdf)
