## Idea

A __boson__ is a [[particle]]/[[quantum field]] that obeys [[Bose-Einstein statistics]].  By the [[spin-statistics theorem]], this is the same thing as a particle whose [[spin]] is an [[integer]].  Compare [[fermion]].
