## Idea

The [[Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{sl}(2,k)$ (over some [[ground field]] $k$) is the [[special linear Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{sl}(n,k)$ for $n  = 2$. 

Over [[ground field]] $k = \mathbb{R}$ ([[real numbers]]) or $k = \mathbb{C}$ ([[complex numbers]]) this is the [[Lie algebra]]  [[Lie differentiation|corresponding to]] the [[Lie group]] which is the [[special linear group]] $SL(2,\mathbb{R})$ or $SL(2,\mathbb{C})$, respectively.
