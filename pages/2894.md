This page describes some nice properties of the category of cocommutative coalgebras (over a ground ring $R$), in particular details of the proof that it is a complete, cocomplete [[lextensive category|lextensive]] [[cartesian closed category]] with a [[generating set]]. 

## References 

Michael Barr, [Coalgebras over a commutative ring](ftp://ftp.math.mcgill.ca/pub/barr/coalgebra.pdf), J. Alg. 32 (1974), 600-610.
