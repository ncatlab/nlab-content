## Idea

A _$\Lambda$-baryon_ is a [[baryon]] which is a [[bound state]] of an [[up quark]], a [[down quark]] and one quark from a higher [[generation of fundamental particles]]: a [[charm quark]] ($\Lambda_c$), a [[bottom quark]] ($\Lambda_b$) or a [[top quark]] ($\Lambda_t$).
