## Properties

### Relation to KK-theory

On K-nuclear $C^\ast$-algebras $C$, the [[KK-theory]] functor $KK(C,-)$ preserves [[short exact sequences]] in the middle (satisfies [[excision]]). ([Kasparov 80](#Kasparov80), [Skandalis 88](#Skandalis88)).

Hence restricted to nuclear $C*$-algebras the canonical functor $KK \to E$ from [[KK-theory]] to [[E-theory]] (see there) is a [[full and faithful functor]].
