## Idea

The term "system L" may refer to (at least) two different concepts in [[formal logic]]:

* there is a [[syntax]] for [[sequent calculus]] introduced in ([Curien-Herbelin 00](#CurienHerbelin00)) under the name _$\lambda \bar \mu \mu$-calculus_, whose name then changed in time to "system L" in reference to the "L" in "LK", "LJ", from [[Gerhard Gentzen]]'s original terminology of sequent calculi;

* there is a [[natural deduction]] system of this name, see _[System L](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/System_L)_.
