## Statement

\begin{proposition}
Let $G, H \,\in\, Grp(SmthMfd) \xrightarrow{\;Grp(undrl)\;} Grp(TopSp)$ be [[Lie groups]] ([[finite number|finite]] [[dimension of a manifold|dimensional]]) with [[underlying]] [[topological groups]] denoted by the same symbol.

Then every [[continuous function|continuous]] [[group homomorphism]] $G \xrightarrow{\;} H$ is [[smooth function|smooth]]. 

Hence the [[function]] of [[hom-sets]]

$$
  Grp(SmthMfd)
  (G,\,H)
  \xrightarrow{undrl_1}
  Grp(TopSp)
  (G,\,H)
$$

is a [[bijection]].
\end{proposition}

(e.g. [Hall 2015, Cor. 3.50](#(Hall15))
