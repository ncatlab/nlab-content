Matroid is one of the basic structures of combinatorics with several different ways of encoding/defining and presenting the generalization of the idea of a linearly independent basis in a vector space.

Mnëv's universality theorem says that any [[semialgebraic set]] in $\mathbb{R}^n$ defined over integers is stably equivalent to the realization space of some oriented matroid.
