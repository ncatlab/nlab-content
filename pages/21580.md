## Idea

A _complex orbifold_  is an [[orbifold]] with [[complex structure]], hence like a [[complex manifold]] but possibly with [[singularities]] modeled on [[fixed points]] of local [[actions]] of [[finite groups]] by [[holomorphic maps]].
