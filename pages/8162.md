
## Idea

For $X$ a [[filtered object]] in an [[abelian category]] $\mathcal{C}$, the _associated graded object_ is the [[graded object]] which in degree $n$ is the [[quotient]] of the $n$th layer of $X$ by the next lower one:

$$
  Gr(X)_n := X_{(n)}/X_{(n+1)}
  \,.
$$
