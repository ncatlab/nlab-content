## Reflection Equation

Given a solution $R$ of the [[quantum Yang-Baxter matrix|Yang-Baxter]] equation, the reflection equation (with additive spectral parameter) reads

$$
R(u-v) K_1 (u) R(u+v) K_2 (v) = K_2 (v) R(u+v) K_1 (u) R(u-v)
$$
