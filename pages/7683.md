## Idea

Cartier [[duality]] is a generalization of [[Portyagin dual|Pontryagin duality]] form topological groups to schemes.

## Definition

+-- {: .num_defn}
###### Definition
Let $G$ be a commutative $k$-group functor. Then the *Cartier dual* $D(G)$ of $G$ is defined by

$$D(G)(R)=Gr_R(G\otimes_k R,\mu_R)$$

Moreover we have

$$hom(G,D(H))=hom(H,D(G))=hom(G\times H,\mu_k)$$
