## Statement

+-- {: .num_defn #ParacompactHausdorffSpacesAreNormal}
###### Proposition
**(Dieudonné's theorem)**

Every [[paracompact Hausdorff space]] is [[normal Hausdorff space|normal]].

In particular [[compact Hausdorff spaces are normal]].
