
The article

* [[Edward Witten]],
  _Conformal field theory in four and six dimensions_,
  in 
  [[Ulrike Tillmann]] (ed.), _Topology, Geometry and Quantum Field Theory_, Cambridge University Press (2002) ([arXiv:0712.0157](http://arxiv.org/abs/0712.0157))

disucsses in a simplified setup the [[Kaluza-Klein mechanism|compactification]] of the [[6d (2,0)-superconformal QFT]] on a [[Riemann surface]] to 4-dimensional ([[super Yang-Mills theory|super]]) [[Yang-Mills theory]], and how this geometrically explains the [[S-duality]]/[[electric-magnetic duality]] of this theory.
