## Idea

The __quaternions__ form the largest [[associative algebra|associative]] [[normed division algebra]], usually denoted  $\mathbb{H}$ after [[William Rowan Hamilton]] (since $\mathbb{Q}$ is taken for the [[rational numbers]]).
