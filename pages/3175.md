Given a [[triangulated category]] $T$ there is an [[abelian category]] $A$ and an [[additive functor]] $T\to A$ satisfying certain universal property; this functor is due Verdier and is called the Verdier's abelianization functor. A readable and comprehensive introduction is in the monograph

* A. Neeman, _Triangulated categories_, Annals of Mathematics Studies __148__,  Princeton University Press 2001, viii+449 pp.
