## Idea

There are $1/4$-[[BPS state]] solutions of [[type IIB supergravity]] which locally exhibit [[black branes]] in the shape of three $(p_i,q_i)$-[[strings]] jointly [[brane intersection|intersecting]]. Globally many of such 3-string junctions may combine to form a more complicated _network_ of [[black brane|black]] string configurations, hence called a _string network_ ([Sen 97](#Sen97)).

## Properties

### M-Theory lift to membrane triple junctions

The lift to [[11d supergravity]]/[[M-theory]] of 3-string junctions is supposed to be given by [[membrane triple junctions]].
