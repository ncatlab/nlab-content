## Idea

While [[quantum chromodynamics]] (QCD) expressed via its [[Lagrangian density]] for [[quark]]-[[field (physics)|physics]] looks [[classical field theory|clasically]] like a [[conformal field theory]], a [[quantum anomaly]] in the [[trace]] of its [[energy momentum tensor]] ([[conformal anomaly]]) breaks [[conformal invariance]] as a  [[quantum field theory]]. This is called the _QCD trace anomaly_.

This is relevant in particular for computation of the [[equation of state]] of the [[quark-gluon plasma]].
