## Idea

The analog in [[2-category theory]] of the notion of _[[reflective subcategory]]_ in [[category theory]].

## Definition

A [[full and faithful 2-functor]] $C \hookrightarrow D$ is said to exhibit $C$ as a **reflective sub-2-category** of $D$ if it has a [[left adjoint|left]] [[adjoint 2-functor]].
