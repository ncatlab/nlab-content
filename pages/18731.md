## Idea

The _Laplace transform_ of a [[function]] $f$ on a suitable [[affine space]] is the result of an [[integration]] of the form

$$
  \hat f(a) \coloneqq \int f(x) e^{- x a} \, d x
  \,,
$$

when defined. 

For [[imaginary number|imaginary]] numbers $a$, this is the _[[Fourier transform]]_. If $a$ is allowed to be a [[complex number]], this is also called the _[[Fourier-Laplace transform]]_.
