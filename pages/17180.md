## Idea

A [[model category]] [[presentable (infinity,1)-category|presentation]] for an [[(∞,1)-category]] of [[n-excisive functors]], in particular of [[excisive functors]] (hence a [[model structure for spectra]]).

## References

Model structure for [[excisive functors]] (hence also a [[model structure for spectra]]) is discussed in:

* {#Lydakis98} Lydakis, _Simplicial functors and stable homotopy theory_ Preprint, available via Hopf archive, 1998 ([pdf](http://hopf.math.purdue.edu/Lydakis/s_functors.pdf))

Further generalization of this, in particular to [[n-excisive functors]] for $n \geq 1$ is discussed in

* [[William Dwyer]], _Localizations_ In _Axiomatic, enriched and motivic homotopy theory_, volume 131 of NATO Sci. Ser. II Math. Phys. Chem., pages 3–28. Kluwer Acad. Publ., Dordrecht, 2004.

* [[Georg Biedermann]], [[Boris Chorny]], Oliver Röndigs, _Calculus of functors and model categories_, Advances in Mathematics 214 (2007) 92-115 ([arXiv:math/0601221](http://arxiv.org/abs/math/0601221))

* [[Georg Biedermann]], Oliver Röndigs, _Calculus of functors and model categories II_ ([arXiv:1305.2834v2](http://arxiv.org/abs/1305.2834v2))
