## Idea

A _Clifford bundle_ is a [[fiber bundle]] of [[Clifford algebras]].

## Examples

Given a [[spin bundle]] $P$, then the [[associated bundle]] $P \underset{Spin(n)}{\times} Cl_n$ is a Clifford bundle.
