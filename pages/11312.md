## Idea

A [[modular form]].

The [[Jacobi theta function]] for special values of its arguments...

## Definition

$$
  \eta(\tau) = q^{1/24} \prod_{n=1}^\infty (1-q^n)
$$

for $q \coloneqq e^{2\pi i \tau}$.
