
## Idea

Let $X$ be some kind of [[space]] with $\mathcal{O}(X)$ some kind of [[ring]] of [[functions]] on $X$. Then for $S \subset X$ a subspace, of kind, its _vanishing ideal_ is the [[ideal]] $I(S) \subset \mathcal{O}(X)$ of all those functions that vanish when restricted to $S$.
