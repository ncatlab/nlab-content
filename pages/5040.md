## Idea

An _inverter_ is a special kind of [[2-limit]] in a [[2-category]].

## Definition

The **inverter** of a [[2-morphism]] $\alpha:f\to g:A\to B$ is a universal object $V$ with a morphism $v:V\to A$ such that $\alpha v$ is invertible.
