## Definition

Given [[categories]] $C,D$ and [[functors]] $F,G\colon C \to D$, an __unnatural transformation__ from $F$ to $G$ consists of, for each object $x$ of $C$, a morphism from $F(x)$ to $G(x)$ in $D$.  

Thus a [[natural transformation]] is an unnatural transformation that satisfies an [[extra property]], namely naturality.
