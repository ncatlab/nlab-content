## Definition

Let $(X, \tau)$ be a [[topological space]]. Then a point $x \in X$ in the underlying [[set]] is called an _open point_ if the [[singleton]] [[subset]] $\{x\} \in X$ is an [[open subset]], i.e. $\{x\} \in \tau$.
