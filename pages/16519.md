## Idea

The content of a [[van Est isomorphism]] is that the canonical comparison map from [[Lie group cohomology]] to [[Lie algebra cohomology]] (by [[differentiation]]) is an [[isomorphism]] whenever the Lie group is sufficiently connected.

In particular when the underlying [[topological space]] of a Lie group is a [[contractible topological space]], then [[Lie group cohomology]] (both the naive and the correct stacky version) coincides with Lie algebra cohomology [vanEst 53, theorem 14.1](#vanEst53) (see e.g. the cocycle which gives the [[Heisenberg group]] extension)
