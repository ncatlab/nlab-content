## Idea

The _Hörmander criterion_ ([Hörmander 90, theorem 8.2.10](#Hoermander90)) says that the [[product of distributions|product of two distributions]] $u_1 \cdot u_2$ (on some [[manifold]] $X$) is well-defined if their [[wave front sets]] $WF(u)$ are such that for $v \in T^\ast_x X$ a [[covector]] contained in one of the two wave front sets then the covector $-v \in T^\ast_x X$ with the opposite [[direction of a vector|direction]] in _not_ contained in the other wave front set:

$$
  \left( WF(u_1) + WF(u_2) \right) \underset{T^\ast X}{\times} X
  \;=\;
  \emptyset
$$
