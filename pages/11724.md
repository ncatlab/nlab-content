## Idea

An [[(∞,1)-monad]] such that applying to its unit its unit yields an [[equivalence]]. Exhibiting a [[reflective sub-(∞,1)-category]].

The [[(∞,1)-category]] refinement of a _[[idempotent monad]]_.
