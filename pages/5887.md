## Definition

Let $C$ be a [[closed monoidal category]]. For $X,Y \in C$ two [[object]]s, the [[adjunct]]

$$
  ev : [X,Y]\otimes X \to Y
$$

of the [[identity]] morphism


$$
  Id : [X,Y] \to [X,Y]
$$

is the **evaluation morphism** for the [[internal hom]] $[X,Y]$.  This is the semantical side of [[function application]] from a categorical perspective.
