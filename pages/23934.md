## Definition 

### In commutative rings

For a [[commutative ring]] $R$, a **depressed cubic function** is a [[function]]  $f \colon R \to R$ with [[elements]] $p \in R$, $q \in R$ such that for all $x \in R$,

$$f(x) = x^3 + p \cdot x + q$$

where $x^3$ is the canonical [[cube function]] of the [[multiplication|multiplicative]] [[monoid]].
