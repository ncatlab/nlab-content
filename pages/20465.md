## Idea

A [[duality in string theory]]: relation between the
[[D=3 N=2 super Yang-Mills theory|D=3 N=2 SYM]]
[[worldvolume]] [[quantum field theory]] on [[M5-brane]] [[wrapped brane|wrapped]] on a ([[hyperbolic 3-manifold|hyperbolic]]) [[3-manifold]] and 3d [[Chern-Simons theory]]/[[analytically continued Chern-Simons theory]]. 

## Properties

### Relation to volume conjecture
 {#RelationToTheVolumeConjecture}

In [Gang-Kim-Lee 14b, 3.2](#GangKimLee14b), [Gang-Kim 18 (21)](#GangKim18) it is argued that the [[volume conjecture]] for [[Chern-Simons theory]] on [[hyperbolic 3-manifolds]] $\Sigma^3$ is the combined statement of two [[dualities in string theory]]

1. [[AdS/CFT duality]]

1. [[3d-3d correspondence]]

for the situation of [[M5-branes]] [[wrapped brane|wrapped on]] $\Sigma^3$ ([DGKV 10](#DGKV10)):

## Related concepts

[[wrapped brane|Wrapping]] the [[M5-brane]] on a [[torsion subgroup|torsion]] 3-cycle yields: [[fractional M2-brane]].
