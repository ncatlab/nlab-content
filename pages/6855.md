## Definition

+-- {: .num_defn }
###### Definition
**(diagonal of a bisimplicial set)**

For $X_{\bullet,\bullet}$ a [[bisimplicial set]], its **diagonal** is the [[simplicial set]] that is the precomposition with the [[diagonal morphism|diagonal]] [[functor]] $(Id, Id) \colon \Delta^{op} \to \Delta^{op} \times \Delta^{op}$ on the [[opposite category|opposite]] of the [[simplex category]], i.e. the simplicial set with components:

$$
  \Delta(X)_n \;\coloneqq\; X_{n,n}
  \,.
$$
=--
