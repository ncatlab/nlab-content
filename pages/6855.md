###### Definition
**(diagonal)**

For $X_{\bullet,\bullet}$ a [[bisimplicial set]], its **diagonal** is the simplicial set that is the precomposition with $(Id, Id) : \Delta^{op} \to \Delta^{op} \times \Delta^{op}$, i.e. the simplicial set with components.

$$
  d(X)_n = X_{n,n}
  \,.
$$
=--

See also [[diagonal]].
