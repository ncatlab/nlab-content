## Idea 

The notion of _enriched Reedy category_ is a combination of that of [[Reedy category]] and [[enriched category]].

The main motivation for studying [[Reedy category|Reedy categories]] is that they induce [[Reedy model structure]]s on [[functor category|functor categories]].

The motivation for studying enriched Reedy categories is that they induced [[Reedy model structure|enriched Reedy model structures]] on [[enriched functor category|enriched functor categories]].

## Properties

Let $\mathcal{V}$ be a [[monoidal model category]]. Let $\mathcal{A}$ be a $\mathcal{V}$-enriched Reedy category and let $\mathcal{E}$ be a $\mathcal{V}$-[[enriched model category]]. Write $[\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{C}]$ for the [[enriched functor category]].

+-- {: .num_prop }
###### Proposition

The enriched Reedy model structure on $[\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{C}]$ exists and is a $\mathcal{V}$-[[enriched model category]].
