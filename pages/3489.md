**Tadao Tannaka** was a Japanese mathematician. 

* wikipedia: [de](http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tadao_Tannaka), [en](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tadao_Tannaka), [ja](http://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E6%B7%A1%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%A0%E9%83%8E)

His early works were related mainly to Galois theory and number theory. 

His famous classical paper on [[Tannaka duality]] proving the original [[Tannaka-Krein theorem]] is 

* _&#220;ber den Dualit&#228;tssatz der nichtkommutativen topologischen Gruppen_, T&#244;hoku Math. J. 45 (1939) 1-12.  [PDF](https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/article/tmj1911/45/0/45_0_1/_pdf/-char/ja).

Note that this article is in the first series of T&#244;hoku.

Tannaka was an editor of T&#244;hoku Math. J. for a longer period.

He also wrote a booklet in Japanese which treats a wider picture of dualities 

* S&#244;tai genri.  [The principle of duality.] Iwanami Shoten, Tokyo, 1951. v+128 pp. 

His biography has been published in T&#244;hoku Math. J. (see project [euclid](http://projecteuclid.org/euclid.tmj/1178228363))
