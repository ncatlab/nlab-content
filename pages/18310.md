## Definition

A [[commutative monoid]] $(A, \cdot)$ is called _cancellative_ if 

$$
  \underset{a,b,z \in A}{\forall}
  \left(
    \left(
      a \cdot z = b \cdot z
   \right)
     \Rightarrow
   \left(
     a = b
   \right)
  \right)
$$
