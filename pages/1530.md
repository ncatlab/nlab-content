The Erlangen program (in German, erlanger Programm) is a project, begun by Felix Klein at Erlangen in the 19th century, to study [[geometry]] from the perspective of [[group theory]].  We now use [[groupoid]]s and even [[category|categories]] or $\infty$-[[infinity-groupoid|groupoids]].
