## Definition

A [[semisimple Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{g}$ is _compact_ if its [[Killing form]]  $\langle -,-\rangle : \mathfrak{g} \otimes \mathfrak{g} \to \mathbb{R}$  is a negative definite [[bilinear form]].

## Properties

+-- {: .num_prop}
###### Proposition

The [[Lie algebra]] of a [[compact space|compact]] [[semisimple Lie group]] is compact.

=--

A proof is spelled out for instance  in ([Woit, theorem 1](#Woit)).
