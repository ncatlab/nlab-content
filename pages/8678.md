Martindale quotient or Martindale localization is a kind of symmetric quotient for some types of rings and algebras. The principal construction is for [[prime ring]]s.

* J. A. Anquela, K. McCrimmon, _Martindale Quotients of Jordan Algebras_, [pdf](http://molle.fernuni-hagen.de/~loos/jordan/archive/martindaleq/martindaleq.pdf)
* J. D. Rosen, M. P. Rosen, _The martindale ring of quotients of a skew polynomial ring of automorphism type_, Communications in Algebra
__21__:11, 4051-4063, 1993 [doi](http://dx.doi.org/10.1080/00927879308824783)
