## Idea

A type of [[meson]] which (in [[Wigner classification]]) is a [[Lorentz group]] [[scalar]] or [[pseudoscalar]] is called a _(pseudo-)scalar meson_, for short. This is in contrast to (pseudo-)[[vector mesons]], which instead are ([[pseudovector representation|pseudo-]])[[vector representations]]

## Examples

Examples of [[pseudo-scalar mesons]]:

* [[pion]]

* [[eta-meson]]

* [[kaon]]

Examples of [[scalar mesons]]:

* [[sigma-meson]]
