A __cover__ $\{U_i\}_{i\in I}$ of a scheme by affine open subsets is __semiseparated__ if $U_i\cap U_j$ is also affine for every pair $(i,j)\in I\times I$. An [[algebraic scheme]] is __semiseparated__ if it has an affine cover which is semiseparated. The notion is introduced in 

* Thomason, Trobaugh, in Grothendieck Festschrift 1989
