## Idea

The _Lorentz Lie algebra_ $\mathfrak{o}(d-1,1)$ is the [[Lie algebra]] of the [[Lorentz group]] $O(d-1,1)$ (the group of [[linear map|linear]] [[isometries]] of [[Minkowski spacetime]]). In particular, it is an [[orthogonal Lie algebra]].

It coincides with the Lie algebras $\mathfrak{so}(d-1,1)$ and $\mathfrak{so}^+(d-1,1)$ corresponding to the [[special orthogonal group]] $SO(d-1,1)$ and the proper orthochronous Lorentz group $SO^+(d-1,1)$, respectively. Indeed, the Lie algebra of any [[Lie group]] remains unchanged after passing to a subgroup of finite [[index of a subgroup|index]].
