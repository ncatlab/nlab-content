__Monsky-Washnitzer cohomology__ is a cohomology theory for [[affine scheme|affine algebraic schemes]] over fields of characteristic $p\gt 0$ introduced in

* P. Monsky, G. Washnitzer, _Formal cohomology. I._
Ann. of Math. (2) 88 1968 181–217, [MR248141](http://www.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=248141), [doi](http://dx.doi.org/10.2307/1970571) 
* P. Monsky, _Formal cohomology. II. The cohomology sequence of a pair_, Ann. of Math. (2) 88 1968 218–238, [MR244272](http://www.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=244272), [doi](http://dx.doi.org/10.2307/1970572)
