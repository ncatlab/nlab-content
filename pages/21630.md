## Statements

+-- {: .num_prop}
###### Proposition
**(Arnold-Kuiper-Massey theorem)**

The [[4-sphere]] is the [[quotient space]] of the [[complex projective plane]] by the [[action]] on homogeneous coordinates of [[complex conjugation]]:

$$
  \mathbb{C}P^2 / (-)^*
  \simeq
  S^4
$$

=--
