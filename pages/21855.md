## Idea

By the _stable tangent bundle_ of a [[smooth manifold]] one means the [[Whitney sum]] of the actual [[tangent bundle]] with any [[trivial vector bundle]].

Specifically, for $\{G_k\}_{k \in \mathbb{N}}$ a sequence of [[Lie groups]], a "G-structure on the stable tangent bundle" is an actual [[G-structure|Gk-structure]] on any such direct sum $T X \oplus \underline{\mathbb{R}}^n$, for some $n \in \mathbb{N}$.

In particular, for $G = 1$ the [[trivial group]], a stable [[frame manifold|framing]] is a [[trivial bundle|trivialization]] of the stable tangent bundle.
