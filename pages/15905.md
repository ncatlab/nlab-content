## Idea

In [[formal logic]] and specifically in [[type theory]], a _resizing rule_ is an [[introduction rule]] which allows, under suitable conditions, to find a [[type]] that is in some [[type universe]] $U_2$  also in a smaller [[type universe]] $U_1$.
