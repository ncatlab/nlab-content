## Idea

Parameterized ([[stable homotopy theory|stable]]) [[homotopy theory]] is ([[stable homotopy theory|stable]]) [[homotopy theory]] of [[bundles]] of [[homotopy types]]/[[stable homotopy types]] over a given base space.
