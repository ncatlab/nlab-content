## Idea

A _super Riemann surface_ is the analog of a [[Riemann surface]] in [[supergeometry]].

## Examples

A [[Riemann surface]] with [[spin structure]] that is also a [[branched cover]] of $\mathbb{C}P^{(1 \vert 1)}$ is canonically endowed with the structure of a super Riemann surface with [[Ramond punctures]] ([Donagi-Witten 15](#DonagiWitten15)). This implies that [[adinkras]] encode certain very special super Riemann surfaces [Doran & Iga & Landweber & Mendez-Diez 13](#DoranIgaLandweberMendez-Diez13).
