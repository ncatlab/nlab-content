## Definition 

### In commutative rings

For a [[commutative ring]] $R$, a **cubic function** is a [[function]]  $f \colon R \to R$ with [[elements]] $a \in R$, $b \in R$, $c \in R$, $d \in R$ such that for all $x \in R$,

$$f(x) = a \cdot x^3 + b \cdot x^2 + c \cdot x + d$$

where $x^2$ and $x^3$ is the canonical [[square function]] and [[cube function]] of the [[multiplication|multiplicative]] [[monoid]].
