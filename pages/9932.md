
## Definition

The [[formal group]]  of the [[multiplicative group]] .

Its formal group law $f(x,y) \in R[ [x,y] ]$ is

$$
  f(x,y) = x + y + \beta x y
  \,,
$$

where $\beta$ is the [[Bott element]].
