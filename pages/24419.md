## Idea

By a *multifunctor* one may mean:

1. a "functor of several variables", namely a [[functor]] out of an iterated [[product category]]: 

   $$
     \mathcal{C}_1 \times \cdots \times \mathcal{C}_n \longrightarrow \mathcal{D}
   \,,
   $$

   (this notion is the [[categorification]] of that of *[[multifunction]]*)

   e.g. if here $n = 2$ one may speak of a *[[bifunctor]]*;

1. a *[[morphism of multicategories]]*.
