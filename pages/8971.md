__Arnaud Beauville__ is an algebraic geometer and Professeur émérite at Université de Nice. In addition to the study of various "classical" problems of algebraic geometry, he applied complex algebraic geometry to [[integrable model]]s, [[conformal field theory]], study of moduli spaces etc.
