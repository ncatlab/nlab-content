## Idea

An [[algebraic equation]] is an [[equation]] of the form

$$
  P(x_1, \cdots, x_n) = 0
$$

where $P(x_1, \cdots, x_n)$ is a [[polynomial]] with [[coefficients]] in some [[field]].
