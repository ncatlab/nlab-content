## Definition

Given an [[affine space]] underlying a [[vector space]] $V$, then its _affine group_ is its group of [[automorphisms]], i.e. the [[semidirect product group]] 

$$
  Aff(V) = V \rtimes GL(V)
$$

of the [[translation group]] of $V$ and the [[general linear group]] of $V$.
