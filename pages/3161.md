Herman Stel got his PhD in Mathematics at Università degli Studi di Firenze under the guidance of Timo Schürg. Herman is now dead, in Erdös' sense, but hopes to be resuscitated later in life. His thesis was entitled "On the road to a category of Kuranishi spaces: k-suicidal modules".

Under the guidance of Urs Schreiber he produced his master's thesis:

* H. Stel, _[[schreiber:master thesis Stel|∞-Stacks and their Function Algebras -- with Applications to ∞-Lie Theory]]_ (2010)
