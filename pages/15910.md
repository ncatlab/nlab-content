## Idea

The real form $EO(2)$ of [[ER-theory]] (i.e. [[real-oriented cohomology theory|real oriented]] [[Morava E-theory]]) is [[orientation in generalized cohomology|oriented]] by [[Spin structure]] combined with [[w4-structure]], hence by trivialization of the [[Stiefel-Whitney classes]] $w_1$, $w_2$ and $w_4$. ([Kriz-Sati 04, section 5.2, esp. p. 29](#KrizSati04)).
