## Idea

_Beilinson's conjectures_ ([Beilinson 85](#Beilinson85)) state [[isomorphisms]] between certain [[motivic cohomology]] groups and certain [[Deligne cohomology]] ([[ordinary differential cohomology]]) groups (recalled as [Brylinski-Zucker 91, conjecture 5.20](#BrylinskiZucker91)). Induced by this in turn are conjectures on [[L-functions]] (recalled as [Brylinski-Zucker 91, conjecture 5.21](#BrylinskiZucker91)).
