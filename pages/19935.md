## Definition

A _wallpaper group_ is a [[crystallographic group]] ([[space group]]) in [[dimension]] 2, hence a [[subgroup]] $G \subset Iso(\mathbb{R}^2)$ of the [[isometry group]] of the [[Euclidean plane]] such that 

1. the part of $G$ inside the [[translation group]] is generated by two [[linearly independent subset|linearly independent]] [[vectors]];

1. the [[point group]] is [[finite group|finite]].
