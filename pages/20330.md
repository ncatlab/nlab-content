## Statement

The [[coset space]] of the [[exceptional Lie group]] [[G2]] by its [[special unitary group|special unitary]] [[subgroup]] [[SU(3)]] is [[diffeomorphism|diffeomorphic]] to the [[6-sphere]]:

$$
  G_2/SU(3)
  \;\simeq\;
  S^6
  \,.
$$
