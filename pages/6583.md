## Idea

__Hofer's metric__ is a [[Riemannian metric]] on the [[group]] of [[Hamiltonian diffeomorphisms]] of a [[symplectic manifold]]. This group is infinite-dimensional, so that the existence of this metric is nontrivial, due noncompactness. 

Hofer's geometry is now a classical subject in [[symplectic geometry]].
