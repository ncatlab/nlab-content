## Definition

+-- {: .num_defn}
###### Definition

An [[∞-group]] $G$ is _braided_ if it is equipped with the following equivalent [[structure]]

1. Regarded as a [[monoidal (∞,1)-category]], $G$ is a [[braided monoidal (∞,1)-category]].

1. The [[delooping]] [[∞-groupoid]] $\mathbf{B}G$ has the structure of an [[∞-group]].

1. The double [[delooping]] [[∞-groupoid]] $\mathbf{B}^2 G$ exists.

1. The groupal [[A-∞ algebra]]/[[E1-algebra]] structure on $G$ refines to an [[E2-algebra]] structure.

1. $G$ is a [[k-tuply groupal n-groupoid|doubly groupal ∞-groupoid]].

1. $G$ is a groupal [[k-tuply monoidal (n,r)-category|doubly monoidal (∞,0)-category]].
