__Spectral category__ is a [[enriched category|category enriched]] in the [[symmetric spectrum|symmetric monoidal category of spectra]]. Hence they are a nonlinear version of [[dg-categories]]. More precisely, pretriangulated idempotent complete spectral categories are a model of [[stable (infinity,1)-category|stable]] [[idempotent complete (infinity,1)-category|idempotent complete]] [[(infinity,1)-categories]]. This equivalence is recorded in

* [[Andrew J. Blumberg]], [[David Gepner]], [[Goncalo Tabuada]], _A universal characterization of higher algebraic K-theory_, [arXiv:1001.2282](http://arxiv.org/abs/1001.2282v4).
