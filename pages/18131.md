## Statetment

Given a [[real polynomial function]] $P \colon \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$, if here $\mathbb{R}$ is regarded as a [[Euclidean space]] (equipped with its [[metric topology]]), then this is a [[pointwise continuous function]].
