## Idea

A _super-orbifold_ is an [[orbifold]] in [[supergeometry]], hence like a [[supermanifold]] but possibly with [[singularities]] of the form of [[fixed points]] of local [[actions]] on the supermanifold by [[finite groups]].

## Examples

### Moduli space of super-Riemann surfaces

Just like the [[moduli space of Riemann surfaces]] is an [[orbifold]], so the [[moduli space of super Riemann surfaces|moduli space of]] [[super-Riemann surfaces]] is a super-orbifold (e.g. [Rabin 87](#Rabin87), [LeBrun-Rothstein 88](#LeBrunRothstein88), [Witten 12](#Witten12), [Codogni-Viviani 17](#CodogniViviani17)).
