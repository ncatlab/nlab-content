## Idea 

A [[free monoid]] object in [[Ab]]

## Definition 

Given an [[abelian group]] $G$, the **tensor ring** is a [[ring]] $T(G)$ with an abelian group homomorphism $g:G \to T(G)$, such that for every other ring $R$ with abelian group homomorphism $h:G \to R$, there is a unique ring homomorphism $i:T(G) \to R$ such that $i \circ g = h$.
