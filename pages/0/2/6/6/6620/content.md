J&#252;rgen Jost is an applied mathematician at the  Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Natural Science in Leipzig, studying [[string theory]], [[geometry]], the [[calculus of variations]] and [[geometric analysis]], [[dynamical systems]], cognitive and neural systems, theoretical and mathematical [[biology]], economic and social systems, history of science and philosophy, graphs and networks and system theory.
