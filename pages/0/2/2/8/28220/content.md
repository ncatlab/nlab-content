## Idea

A *spinʰ bordism* is a [[B-bordism]] for the [[tangential structure]] ([[(B,f)-structure]]) being the [[spinʰ structure]]. Its [[bordism homology theory]] and [[cobordism cohomology theory]] are described by the [[Thom spectrum]] [[MSpinʰ]].

Its definition is fully analogous to that of [[spin bordism]]. Similarily, there are *spinʰ bordism groups* and the *spinʰ bordism ring*:
$$
\Omega_n^{Spin^\mathrm{h}}
\coloneqq\pi_n MSpin^\mathrm{h}
=\lim_{k\rightarrow\infty}\pi_{n+k} MSpin^\mathrm{h}_k;
$$
$$
\Omega^{Spin^\mathrm{h}}
\coloneqq\bigoplus_{n\in\mathbb{N}}\Omega_n^Spin^{\mathrm{h}}.
$$
