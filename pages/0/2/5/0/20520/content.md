## Idea

By analogy with [[graded algebra]], a **$\mathcal{M}$-graded monad** for a [[strict monoidal category]], $(\mathcal{M}, \otimes, I)$, is a lax monoidal functor, $(\mathcal{M}, \otimes, I) \to ([\mathbb{C}, \mathbb{C}], \circ, id_{\mathbb{C}})$.
