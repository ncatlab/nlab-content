A [[bicategory]] **has local reflexive coequalizers** if each of its hom-categories has [[reflexive coequalizers]] and composition preserves them.

Similarly, a [[double category]] has local reflexive coequalizers if its loose bicategory has them.

This condition is crucial for the [[virtual equipment]] of monoids and modules in a [[fibrant double category]] $\mathbb{D}$ to be representable, i.e. to have an associative tensor of bimodules ([Proposition 11.10, Shulman 2008](#Shu08)).
