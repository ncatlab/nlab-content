## Idea

A _quadric_ is an [[algebraic variety]] defined as the [[zero locus]] of a [[quadratic form|quadratic]] [[function]].


## Examples


* The quadrics in the [[Euclidean space|Euclidean]] [[plane]] are the [[conic sections]].
