## Idea

A [[duality in string theory]].

Gopakumar–Vafa duality is a [[duality in string theory]] between [[perturbative quantization of 3d Chern-Simons theory|perturbative]] [[Chern-Simons theory|3d Chern-Simons]] [[Wilson loop observables]] ([[Vassiliev link invariants]]) and relative [[Gromov–Witten invariants]] of a 6–dimensional [[symplectic manifold]] relative to a [[Lagrangian submanifold]].
