## Idea

A [[characteristic class]].

By the [[Hirzebruch-Riemann-Roch theorem]] the [[index]] of the [[Dolbeault operator]] is the Todd genus (e.g. [Gilkey 95, section 5.2](#Gilkey95)). (More generally so for the [[Spin^c Dirac operator]]).
