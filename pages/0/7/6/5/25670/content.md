## Idea

In [[dependent type theory]], a type is **$n$-connected** if its [[n-truncation modality|n-truncation]] is a [[contractible type]]. 

In the [[categorical semantics]] of [[homotopy type theory]] in [[(infinity,1)-toposes|$\infty$-toposes]], $n$-connected types are interpreted as [[n-connected object of an (infinity,1)-topos|$n$-connected objects of the $\infty$-topos]].
