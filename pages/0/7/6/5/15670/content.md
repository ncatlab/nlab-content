## Idea

Just as [[pretriangulated dg-categories]] are a model for [[stable (∞,1)-categories|stable]] [[linear (∞,1)-categories]] in the setting of [[dg-categories]], _pretriangulated $A_\infty$-categories_ are designed to be a model for [[stable (∞,1)-categories|stable]] [[linear (∞,1)-categories]] in the setting of $A_\infty$-[[A-∞-categories|categories]].
