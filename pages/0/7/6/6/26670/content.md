
### Cartan structural equations and Bianchi identities

On [[Cartan structural equations]] and their [[Bianchi identities]] for [[curvature]] and [[torsion]] of [[Cartan moving frames]] and ([[Cartan connection|Cartan]]-)[[connection on a bundle|connections]] on [[tangent bundles]] (especially in [[first-order formulation of gravity]]):

The original account:
