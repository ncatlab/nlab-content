## Idea

An **operadic category** is a structure for describing various category-like structures (**generalised operads**) with different kinds of composition operations, including [[monoids]], ([[symmetric operad|symmetric]] and non-symmetric) [[operads]], $n$-operads, hyperoperads, and charades.

Every generalised operad has a notion of algebra, with which can be captured notions like ([[wheeled graph|wheeled]]) [[properads]], [[PROPs]], [[cyclic operads]], and (twisted) [[modular operads]].

Operadic categories are thus a framework for [[generalised multicategories]], albeit of a different style than approaches based on monads.
