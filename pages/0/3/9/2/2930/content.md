## Selected writings

Introducing the [[nilpotence theorem]] in [[stable homotopy theory]]:

* {#DevinatzHopkinsSmith88} [[Ethan Devinatz]], [[Michael Hopkins]], [[Jeffrey Smith]], _Nilpotence and Stable Homotopy Theory I_, Annals of Mathematics Second Series, Vol. 128, No. 2 (Sep., 1988), pp. 207-241 ([jstor:1971440](https://www.jstor.org/stable/1971440)) 

* {#DevinatzHopkinsSmith98} [[Ethan Devinatz]], [[Michael Hopkins]], [[Jeffrey Smith]], _Nilpotence and Stable Homotopy Theory II_, Annals of Mathematics Second Series, Vol. 148, No. 1 (Jul., 1998), pp. 1-49 ([jstor:120991](https://www.jstor.org/stable/120991)) 

On [[homotopy commutative diagrams]]:

* [[William Dwyer]], [[Dan Kan]], [[Jeff Smith]], _Homotopy commutative diagrams and their realizations_, Journal of Pure and Applied Algebra 57 (1989) 5-24 (<a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/0022-4049(89)90023-6">doi:10.1016/0022-4049(89)90023-6</a>)


Introducing [[Delta-generated topological spaces]]:

* {#Smith} [[Jeff Smith]], _A really convenient category of topological spaces_ (unpublished and possibly non-existent, according to [Dugger 03, p. 4](Delta-generated+topological+space#Dugger03))

After Jeff Smith presented his recognition theorem for [[combinatorial model categories]] at a conference in Barcelona, its first appearance in a publication is apparently  

* {#Beke} [[Tibor Beke]], Lemma 1.8 in: _Sheafifiable homotopy model categories_ , Math. Proc. Cambr. Phil. Soc. 129 (2000), 447&#8211;475 ([arXiv:math/0102087](http://arxiv.org/abs/math/0102087))


On [[generalized cup products]]:

* [[James E. McClure]], [[Jeffrey H. Smith]], _Multivariable cochain operations and little $n$-cubes_ ([arXiv:math/0106024v3](https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0106024v3))



On [[derived functors]] such as [[homotopy limit]]-functors on [[model categories]] and more general [[homotopical categories]]:

* [[William Dwyer]], [[Philip Hirschhorn]], [[Daniel Kan]], [[Jeff Smith]], **[[Homotopy Limit Functors on Model Categories and Homotopical Categories]]**, Mathematical Surveys and Monographs **113**, AMS 2004 ([ISBN: 978-1-4704-1340-8](https://bookstore.ams.org/surv-113-s), [pdf](http://dodo.pdmi.ras.ru/~topology/books/dhks.pdf))
