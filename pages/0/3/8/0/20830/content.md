## Idea

In the [[standard model of particle physics]] the _strange quark_ is the down-type [[quark]] in the second [[generation of fermions]]. 

Regarded as a [[heavy quark]] in 2-flavor [[chiral perturbation theory]], and as a [[light quark]] in 3-flavor [[chiral perturbation theory]].
