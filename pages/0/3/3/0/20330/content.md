## Statement

The [[coset space]] of the [[exceptional Lie group]] [[G₂]] by its [[special unitary group|special unitary]] [[subgroup]] [[SU(3)]] is [[diffeomorphism|diffeomorphic]] to the [[6-sphere]]:

$$
  G_2/SU(3)
  \;\simeq\;
  S^6
  \,.
$$
In particular, there is a [[principal SU(3)-bundle]] $G_2\twoheadrightarrow S^6$. Such principal bundles are classified by $\pi_6 B SU(3)\cong\pi_5 SU(3)\cong\mathbb{Z}$.
