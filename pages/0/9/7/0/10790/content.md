## Definition

An $R$-[[associative algebra|algebra]] $A$ is _&#233;tale_ if the definition [[homomorphism]] $R \to A$ is, dually, an [[étale morphism of schemes]].

A more elementary definition for algebras over a discrete field exists in constructive algebra.
