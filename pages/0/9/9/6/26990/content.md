## Idea

In [[knot theory]], an *oriented link* (in particular: *oriented knot*) is a [[link]] $L \,\colon\, (S^1)^{\sqcup^n} \to X$ (e.g. a [[knot]] $K \colon S^1 \to X$) equipped with an [[orientation]] on all the [[domain]] [[circles]].
