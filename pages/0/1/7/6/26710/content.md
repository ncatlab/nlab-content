## Definition

\begin{definition}
If $\mathcal{C}$ is a [[G-∞-category]], then the $G$-$\infty$-category of **$G$-commutative monoid objects in $\mathcal{C}$** is

$$
  \underline{\mathrm{CMon}}_G := \underline{\mathrm{Fun}}_G(\mathrm{Span}(\mathbb{F}_G),\mathcal{C}),
$$
where $\mathrm{Span}(\mathbb{F}_G)$ is the category of [[(∞,1)-category of correspondences|correspondences]] in finite [[G-sets]].

\end{definition}
