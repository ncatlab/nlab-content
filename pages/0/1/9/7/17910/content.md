In [[linear algebra]] and [[matrix calculus]], a _similarity transformation_ between quadratic [[matrices]] $A$ and $B$ is an [[general linear group|invertible matrix]] $S$, such that

$$
  B = S^{-1} A S
  \,.
$$

This is a special case of the concept of [[conjugation action]].
