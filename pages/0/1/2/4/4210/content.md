# Spherical categories
* table of contents
{:toc}

## Idea

A _spherical category_ is a  [[monoidal category]] with [[dualizable object|duals]] that behaves as if its [[morphisms]] can be drawn and moved around on a [[2-sphere]].


## Definition

A **spherical category** is a [[pivotal category]] $\mathcal{C}$ where the left and right [[trace]] operations coincide on all objects.

If $\mathcal{C}$ is in addition a [[tensor category]] so that this trace may be interpreted as an element of the [[ground field]], then the trace $tr\big( id_X\big)$ is called the *[[quantum dimension]]* of the object $X$ (e.g. [FRS02 (2.17)](#FRS02))
