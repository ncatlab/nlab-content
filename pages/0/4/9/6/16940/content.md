Given a [[field]] $K$ whose [[characteristic]] is not 2, let $p\in K[x]$ be a [[polynomial]] over $K$ with value $p = \tfrac{1}{2} a x^2 - b x$. Then

$$
  \tfrac{1}{2} a x^2 - b x 
  =
  \tfrac{1}{2} a(x - \tfrac{b}{a})^2 - \tfrac{b^2}{2a}
$$
