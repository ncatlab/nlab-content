## Idea

_Hopf invariant one_ refers to maps of [[Hopf invariant]] equal to 1.

## Properties

A theorem due to ([Adams 60](#Adams60)) says that these correspond precisely to the four [[normed division algebras]] (see aslo at [[Hurwitz theorem]]).
