## Definition

A _3-morphism_ in an [[n-category]] is a morphism between [[2-morphism]]s.


## Examples

For $A$ an [[abelian group]], the 3-fold [[delooping]] $\mathbf{B}^3 A$ is a [[3-groupoid]] whose 3-morphisms are given by $A$.
