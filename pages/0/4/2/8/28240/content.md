## Idea

An _11-manifold_ is a [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] 11. (Generally a [[topological manifold]], but it can be specified to a [[PL manifold]] or a [[smooth manifold]].)

## Examples

* [[15-sphere]]
* O(6)
* [[SO(6)]]
* [[Spin(6)]]$\cong$[[SU(4)]]

## Properties

\begin{proposition}
For a smooth 15-manifold $M$, its last three [[Stiefel-Whitney classes]] vanish:
$$
w_13(M)
=w_14(M)
=w_15(M)
=0.
$$
\end{proposition}
More generally, for every $4n+3$-dimensional [[smooth manifold]], one has $w_{4n+1}=w_{4n+2}(M)=w_{4n+3}(M)=0$. Hence this property also holds for [[3-manifolds]], [[7-manifolds]] and [[11-manifolds]].
\begin{proposition}
If for a smooth 15-manifold $M$ the following [[Stiefel-Whitney classes]] vanish:
$$
w_1(M)=w_2(M)=w_4(M)=w_8(M)=0,
$$
then all its [[Stiefel-Whitney classes]] vanish.
\end{proposition}
