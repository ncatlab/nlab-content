## Definition 

The Fibonacci numbers are a [[subset]] of the [[natural numbers]] [[inductive definition|defined inductively]] by the equation 

$$f(x + 2) \coloneqq f(x + 1) + f(x)$$

with [[initial conditions]] $f(0) = 0$ and $f(1) = 1$.
