## Statement

The [[coset space]] of [[Spin(7)]] by the [[exceptional Lie group]] [[G2]] is [[homeomorphism|homeomorphic]] to the [[7-sphere]]:

$$
  Spin(7)/G_2
  \;\simeq\;
  S^7
  \,.
$$
