This page is about the article

* [[Henri Poincaré]]:

  \linebreak

  **Analysis Situs**

  \linebreak

  Journal de l'École Polytechnique **2** 1 (1895) 1–123 

  \linebreak

  original: [gallica:12148/bpt6k4337198/f7](https://gallica.bnf.fr/ark:/12148/bpt6k4337198/f7)

  {#Stillwell} English translation by [[John Stillwell]]: [pdf](https://www.maths.ed.ac.uk/~v1ranick/papers/poincare2009.pdf), [[Stillwell_AnalysisSitus.pdf:file]]

which initiated (cf. [Stillwell](#Stillwell) [p. 6](https://ncatlab.org/nlab/files/Stillwell_AnalysisSitus.pdf#page=10)) the modern fields of [[topology]] (such as in introducing the notion and terminology of *[[homeomorphisms]]*), of  [[algebraic topology]] (introducing [[singular homology]]) and of [[homotopy theory]] (introducing the [[fundamental group]]).
