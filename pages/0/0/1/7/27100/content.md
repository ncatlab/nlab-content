## Idea

A **$\sigma$-complete Heyting algebra** is a [[Heyting algebra]] which is also a [[sigma-complete lattice|$\sigma$-complete lattice]]; that is, it is a [[poset]] with [[countable set|countable]] [[limits]] and [[colimits]] that is also [[cartesian closed]]. 

## Examples

* The [[set of truth values]] is a $\sigma$-complete Heyting algebra

* Assuming the [[limited principle of omniscience]], the [[boolean domain]] is a $\sigma$-complete Heyting algebra.
