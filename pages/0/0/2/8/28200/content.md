## Idea

In [[solid state physics]], *Floquet theory* is concerned with [[quantum systems]] which are driven by [[time]]-periodic external [[forces]]/[[potential energy|potentials]]. The archetypical case is the study of [[electrons]] in a [[crystal]] subjected to a strong and periodically oscillating [[electromagnetic field]].

This way, Floquet theory may be understood as the temporal analog of what for spatial periodicity is the subject of *[[Bloch theory]]*.
