## Definition

A [[homotopy type]] $X$ is _stable_ if the canonical [[morphism]]

$$
  X \longrightarrow \Omega \Sigma X
$$

into the [[loop space object]] of its [[suspension object]] is an [[equivalence in an (infinity,1)-category|equivalence]].

## Examples

For $\mathbf{H}$ an [[(∞,1)-topos]] and $T \mathbf{H}$ its [[tangent (∞,1)-topos]], then all homotopy types in $T_\ast \mathbf{H} \hookrightarrow T \mathbf{H}$ are stable, these are equivalently the [[spectrum objects]] in $\mathbf{H}$.
