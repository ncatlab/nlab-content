## Idea

A [[bound state]] of [[D5-branes]] and [[D9-branes]], a special case of [[Dp-D(p+4)-brane bound states]].

## Properties

### Relation to small instantons

Under [[duality between heterotic and type I string theory]] $D5-D9$ brane bound states translate to [[small instantons]] in [[heterotic string theory]] given by [[wrapped brane|wrapped]] [[NS5-branes]], or rather wrapped [[M5-branes]] in [[heterotic M-theory]] ([Witten 96](#Witten96), [Gimon-Polchinski 96](#GimonPolchinski96))
