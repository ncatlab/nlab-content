## Idea

A *Frobenius manifold* &lbrack;[Dubrovin 1996](#Dubrovin96)&rbrack; is a [[flat connection|flat]] [[Riemannian manifold]] with compatible [[Frobenius algebra]]-[[structure]] on the [[fibers]] of its [[tangent bundle]].
