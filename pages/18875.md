## Idea

In [[perturbative quantum field theory]], the LSZ reduction formula ([Lehmann-Symanzik-Zimmermann 55](#LehmannSymanzikZimmermann55)) is a method relating [[vacuum expectation values]] of [[time-ordered products]] of field [[observables]] to [[S-matrix]] elements
