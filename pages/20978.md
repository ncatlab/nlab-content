## Idea 

A variant of the [[Bernoulli numbers]] introduced in [Zagier 98](#Zagier98).

## Properties

### Periodicity

The modified Bernoulli numbers $B_n$ are 12-periodic for $n$ odd [Zagier, (A.4) (A.18)](#ZagierProperties).
