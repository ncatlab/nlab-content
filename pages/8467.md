## Definition

+-- {: .num_defn }
###### Lemma

For $f : A \to B$ a [[morphism]] in a [[category]] with a [[terminal object]], the **cofiber** of $f$ is the [[cofiber coproduct]] of $f$ with the terminal morphism, hence the [[pushout]]

$$
  \array{
    A &\stackrel{f}{\to}& B
    \\
    \downarrow && \downarrow
    \\
    * &\to& B \coprod_A *
  }
  \,.
$$

=--

## Examples

### Cokernels

In an [[additive category]] cofibers over the [[zero object]] are called _[[cokernels]]_.
