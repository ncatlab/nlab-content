##Definition##

Take a topos $\mathcal{E}$ and a [monic](http://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/monomorphism) $\rho \colon R \hookrightarrow B \times A$ (thought of as a _relation_ between $A$ and $B$). The _name_ of $R$ is then the transpose $r \colon B \rightarrow A$ of the characteristic map $\phi_{\rho} \colon B \times A \rightarrow \Omega$ of $\rho$ in $\mathcal{E}$.
