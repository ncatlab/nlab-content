
## Idea

The [[zero object]] in the [[stable (infinity,1)-category of spectra]]/[[stable homotopy category]].

As a [[sequential spectrum]] this is represented by the sequence of [[pointed topological space]] which consists of the one-point space in each degree (an [[Omega-spectrum]]).
