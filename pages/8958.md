## Definition

A **sylleptic $\infty$-group** is an [[∞-group]] $G$ equipped with the following equivalent [[structure]]

1. a lift of its [[A-∞ algebra|A-∞]]/[[E-n algebra|E1]]-algebra structure to an [[E-n algebra|E3]] algebra structure;

1. the structure of a [[braided ∞-group]] on its [[delooping]] $\mathbf{B}G$

1. the structure of an [[∞-group]] on its double [[delooping]] $\mathbf{B}^2 G$

1. a triple [[delooping]] $\mathbf{B}^3 G$.
