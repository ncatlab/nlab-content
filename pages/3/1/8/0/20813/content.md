## Idea

The [[KK-compactification]] of [[M-theory]]/[[string theory]] on [[spacetime]] [[manifolds]] with [[G-structure]] for $G = $ [[Sp(1)]], notably on the [[product space]] $K3 \times T2$ of a [[K3-surface]] with a [[2-torus]].
