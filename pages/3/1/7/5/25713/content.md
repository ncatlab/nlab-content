__Kurusch Ebrahimi-Fard__ is a German mathematician, currently working in Norway. His main works are mainly concerned with combinatorial and algebraic structures appearing in [[renormalization]] in mathematical physics, in solving differential equations, in Lie theory and so on.
Related entries include: [[dendriform algebra]], [[Rota-Baxter algebra]], [[Birkhoff decomposition]].
