__Kurusch Ebrahimi-Fard__ is a German mathematician, currently working in Norway. His research is mainly concerned with [[combinatorics|combinatorial]] and [[algebra|algebraic]] structures in [[mathematical physics]] (e.g. in [[renormalization]]), in solving [[differential equations]], in [[Lie theory]] and so on.
