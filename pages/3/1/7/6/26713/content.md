## Definition

\begin{definition}

  If $\mathcal{C}$ is a [[(∞,1)-limit|complete (∞,1)-category]], then the **∞-category of $m$-commutative monoids in $\mathcal{C}$** is
  $$
    \mathrm{CMon}_m(\mathcal{C}) := \mathrm{Fun}^{m-\mathrm{fin}}(\mathrm{Span}(\mathcal{S}_m)^{\op},\mathcal{C}),
  $$
  where $\mathrm{Fun}^{m-\mathrm{fin}}$ refers to the category of functors which preserve [[(∞,1)-limits]] indexed by [[m-finite spaces]].

\end{definition}
