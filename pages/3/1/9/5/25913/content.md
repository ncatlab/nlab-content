## Definition

A [[triangulated category]] is $m$-periodic for integer $m\geq 2$, if the $m$-th power of the suspension (translation) functor 
$$
\Sigma: X\mapsto X[1]
$$
is isomorphic to the identity functor. 

## Examples

Triangulated categories of [[matrix factorization]]s are 2-periodic.
