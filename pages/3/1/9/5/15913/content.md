## Idea

[[c-structure]] for $c$ the 1st [[Pontryagin class]] $p_1$, o.e. trivialization of $p_1$.

In the presence of [[Spin structure]] then $p_1$-structures are also called $(w_1, w_2, p_1)$-structures: see [Freed-Teleman](#FreedTeleman), §2.4. In the presence of (just) [[orientation]] (i.e. $w_1$-structure) on [[3-manifolds]] then $p_1$-structures are also called $(w_1, p_1)$-structures (see [Freed-Teleman](#FreedTeleman), §2.3), and are closely related to [[Atiyah 2-framing]] structures.

A [[string structure]] induces a $(w_1, w_2, p_1)$-structure, as it includes data of a [[spin structure]] and a trivialization of $p_1/2$, the first fractional [[Pontryagin class]]. However, $\mathbb{RP}^{11}$ has a $(w_1, w_2, p_1)$-structure but is not string.
