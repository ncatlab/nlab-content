## Idea

In foundations of [[quantum mechanics]], the Pusey-Barrett-Rudolph theorem is a [[no-go theorem]] which puts metaphysical requirements on any ψ-epistemic [[interpretation of quantum mechanics]]. Specifically, models of quantum theory may not simultaneously be ψ-epistemic and also *preparation independent*.
