## Idea

In [[trigonometry]] the arcsine or "inverse sine" is one of the [[inverse trigonometric functions]].

## Definition

The arcsine is a [[section]] (or [[right inverse]]) to the sine function [[sin]], which in turn is one of the [[trigonometric functions]]. More precisely, it is the unique [[continuous function|continuous]] section of the [[epimorphism]] in the [[(epi, mono) factorization]] of the sine such that its value at $0$ is $0$ (see [[pi]]).
