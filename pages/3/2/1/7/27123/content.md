## Definition

The **Kreisel-Lacombe-Shoenfield-Tseitin theorem** states that all [[computable functions]] from a computable [[complete metric space|complete]] [[separable metric space|separable]] [[metric space]] to a [[computable metric space]] are computably [[continuous function|continuous]].
