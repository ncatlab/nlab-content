## Definition

For $\mathbf{H}$ an [[(∞,1)-topos]] and $n \in \mathbb{N}$, the collection $n Cat(\mathbf{H})$ of [[n-category object in an (∞,1)-category|n-category object in]] $\mathbf{H}$, hence of [[(∞,n)-sheaves|(∞,n+1)-sheaves]] on $\mathbf{H}$ is an $(\infty,1)$-localic $(\infty,n+1)$-topos.

See at _[Internal category object in an (∞,1)-category -- Iterated internalization](category+object+in+an+%28infinity%2C1%29-category#InternalnCategories)_.
