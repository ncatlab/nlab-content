## Idea

AUC (ACU in the French language) is a method of naming [[magmoidal categories]]:

* A stands for associative

* U stands for unital

* C stands for commutative

Thus an AU category would be a [[monoidal category]]. The AUC terminology is commonly used in [[rewriting]] theory.
