## Idea

Standard monomials are elements of particular (vector space) bases of homogeneous coordinate rings and of the spaces of sections of [[line bundles]] over [[generalized flag varieties]] and [[Schubert varieties]]. 

Historically first case is the homogeneous coordinate ring of complex [[Grassmannian]]s, where the standard monomials in [[Pl√ºcker coordinate]]s are described by [[William Hodge]] via parametrization by standard [[Young tableaux]]. Hodge also described the related straightening algorithm.  Other cases are also parametrized by combinatorial data.

There are also [[quantum group]] versions of the theory.
