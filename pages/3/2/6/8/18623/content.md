## Idea

In [[perturbative quantum field theory]] the [[algebra of observables|algebra of]] [[quantum observables]] for the [[interaction]]-term included could be called the _algebra of quantum observables on the interacting quantum fields_. Beware that often this is referred to simply as the _interacting field algebra_, by some abuse of notation.


If the [[perturbative S-matrix]] is specified, then the quantum observables on the interacting fields may be obtained by [[Bogoliubov's formula]], see there fore more.

More generally the algebra of observables on the interacting quantum fields should be defined as the [[formal deformation quantization]] (specifically [[Fedosov deformation quantization]]) of the [[interaction]] term ([Collini 16](#Collini16), [Hawkins-Rejzner 16](#HawkinsRejzner16))

## Properties




### Causal locality of interacting field quantum observables


+-- {: .num_prop #CausalLocalityOfBogoliubovFormula}
###### Proposition
**([[causal locality]])**

As the spacetime support varies, the algebras of [[interacting field quantum observables]] spanned via the Bogoliubov formula consistitute a [[causally local net of observables]], hence an instance of [[perturbative AQFT]]. 

=--

([D&#252;tsch-Fredenhagen 00, section 3](#DuetschFredenhagen00), following [Brunetti-Fredenhagen 99, section 8](#BrunettiFredenhagen99),
[Il'in-Slavnov 78](#IlinSlavnov78))

For **[[proof]]** see [this prop.](S-matrix#PerturbativeQuantumObservablesIsLocalnet) at _[[S-matrix]]_.
