## Idea

A [[super Lie algebra]] which is a [polyvector](super+Poincare+Lie+algebra#PolyvectorExtensions) [[Lie algebra extension|extension]] of the [[super Poincar√© Lie algebra]] ([[supersymmetry]]) in $D = 9+1$ for  $N=(2,0)$ or $N = (1,1)$ [[supersymmetry]] by [[charges]] corresponding to the [[type II superstring]], the [[D-branes]] and the [[NS5-brane]] ("[[extended supersymmetry]]").
