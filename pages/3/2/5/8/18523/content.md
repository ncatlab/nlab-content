## Statement

Let $Q$ be a [[pseudo-differential operator]] on some [[smooth manifold]] $X$ with homogeneous [[principal symbol]] $q$ that is properly supported, then for $u \in \mathcal{D}'(X)$ a [[distribution]] with $Q u = f$, then the [[complement]] of the [[wave front set]] of $u$ by that of $f$ is contained in the set of of covectors on which the principal symbol $q$ vanishes:

$$
  WF(u) \setminus WF(f) \;\subset\; q^{-1}(0)
  \,.
$$

([Duistermaat-H&#246;rmander 72, theorem 6.1.1](#DuistermaatHoermander72))
