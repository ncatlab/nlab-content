## Idea

A *fermionic group* in the sense of [Benson 1988](#Benson1988) is a ([[topological group|topological]]) [[group]] $G$ equipped with 

1. a [[center|central]] element $-1 \in Z(G)$ squaring to [[neutral element|$\mathrm{e}$]],

1. a [[group homomorphism]] $\theta \colon G \to \mathbb{Z}_{/2}$ such that $\theta(-1) = [0]$.

This is related to the notion of [[quantum symmetries]] and as such appears in the [[K-theory classification of topological phases of matter]] and the classification of [[invertible field theories]].
