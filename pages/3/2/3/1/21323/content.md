## Idea

In the context of [[quantum hadrodynamics]], the [[decay]]-mode of a [[hadron]] via the [[weak interaction]] (e.g. by exchange of a [[W-boson]] into a [[pair]] of [[leptons]] is called 

* a _leptonic decay_ if no further [[particle]] participates

* a _semileptonic decay_ if in addition another [[meson]] results

if in addition [[electromagnetism|electromagnetic]] interaction is involved there is

* _radiative leptonic decay_ if in addition a [[photon]] results.

All this is in contrast to

* non-leptonic decays
