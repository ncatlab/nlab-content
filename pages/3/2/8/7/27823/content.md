## Idea

The Topos Institute is a research institute for [[applied category theory|applications]] of [[category theory]] to various branches of [[science]]. The Topos Institute is lead by [[Brendan Fong]] and [[David Spivak]]. The [[nLab]] historically was [[funding of the nLab|funded]] by donations through the Topos Institute. 

The Topos Institute runs a series of talks called the **Topos Institute Colloquium**, whose website can be found [here](https://topos.institute/events/topos-colloquium/).
