## Idea

The _de Sitter group_ is the [[isometry group]] of [[de Sitter spacetime]]. In [[spacetime]] dimension $d$ its [[connected component]] is [[isomorphism|isomorphic]] to the [[orthogonal group]] $SO(d,1)$ in Lorentzian signature in one dimension higher. 

This is analgous to the [[Poincare group]], which is the [[isometry group]] of [[Minkowski spacetime]].
