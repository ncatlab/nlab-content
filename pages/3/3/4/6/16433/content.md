## Idea

The _de Sitter group_ is the [[isometry group]] of [[de Sitter spacetime]]. In [[spacetime]] dimension $D = d+1$ its [[connected component]] is [[isomorphism|isomorphic]] to the [[orthogonal group]] $SO(D,1)$ in Lorentzian signature in one dimension higher. 

This is analgous to the [[Poincaré group]], which instead is the [[isometry group]] of [[Minkowski spacetime]].
