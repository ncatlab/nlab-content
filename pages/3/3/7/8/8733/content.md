Graham Ellis works at the National University of Ireland Galway. His recent work is on [[computational homological algebra]], homology and related areas of [[topological data analysis]].  He is also expert in non-abelian homotopy theoretic topics such as [[crossed modules]] and [[Lie crossed modules]]

* [Home Page](http://hamilton.nuigalway.ie/)
