
__John Williford Duskin__ (1937-2020) was a [[category theory|category theorist]] from the [[joyalscatlab:North American School of Category Theory]] in the [Buffalo group](http://ncatlab.org/joyalscatlab/published/North+American+School+of+Category+Theory#BuffaloGroup). He is often refered to as **Jack Duskin**.
