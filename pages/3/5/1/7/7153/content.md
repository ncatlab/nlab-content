## Idea

A special kind of [[gauge fixing]] condition on a [[connection on a bundle]]. 

## Definition

Let $A_0$ and $A$ be two [[connection on a bundle|connections on a bundle]]. Then $A$ is said to be in _Coulomb gauge_ relative to $A_0$ if

$$
  d_{A_0}^*(A-A_0) = 0
  \,.
$$
