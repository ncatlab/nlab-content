
### $G_2$-conifolds

On [[G₂-conifolds]] ([[G₂-manifolds]] with [[conical singularities]]):

Survey:

* [[Spiro Karigiannis]], _$G_2$-conifolds: A survey_, 2014 ([[KarigiannisG2Conifolds.pdf:file]])

* Mark Haskins, _Exotic Einstein metrics on $S^6$ and $S^3 \times S^3$ nearly Kähler 6-manifolds and $G_2$-holonomy cones_, 2016 ([[HaskinsG2Conifolds.pdf:file]])

Three [[simply connected topological space|simply connected]] $G_2$-cones are known: the

1. [[metric cone on complex projective 3-space]]

1. metric cone on $S^3 \times S^3$

1. metric cone on $SU(3) / (U(1) \times U(1))$

* {#BryantSalamon89} [[Robert Bryant]], [[Simon Salamon]], _On the construction of some complete metrics with exceptional holonomy_, Duke Math. J. Volume 58, Number 3 (1989), 829-850 ([euclid:euclid.dmj/1077307681](https://projecteuclid.org/euclid.dmj/1077307681))

* [[Gary Gibbons]], [[Don Page]], [[Christopher Pope]], _Einstein metrics on $S^3$, $\mathbb{R}^3$ and $\mathbb{R}^4$ bundles_, Comm. Math. Phys. Volume 127, Number 3 (1990), 529-553 ([euclid:cmp/1104180218](https://projecteuclid.org/euclid.cmp/1104180218))
