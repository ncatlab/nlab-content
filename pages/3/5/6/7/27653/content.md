## Definition

A **Conway semiring** or **Conway rig** is a [[quasiregular rig]] $(R, 0, 1, +, (-)(-), (-)^*)$ such that 

* for all $x \in R$ and $y \in R$, $(x + y)^* = (x^* y)^* x^*$

* for all $x \in R$ and $y \in R$, $(x y)^* = 1 + x (y x)^* y$
