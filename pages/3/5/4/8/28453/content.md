## Definition

A decorated permutation of [n] is a pair $(\pi, col)$, where $\pi:[n]\to[n]$ is a permutation, and col is a function from fixed points of $\pi$ to the set $\{-1,1\}$.

## Properties

Decorated permutations are in bijection with [[Grassmann necklace]]s.
