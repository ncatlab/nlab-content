__Fernando Zalamea Traba__ is a mathematician at Universidad Nacional de Colombia at Bogot&#225; with main interests in [[categorical logic]], non-classical logic, [[history]] and [[philosophy]] of mathematics. He is also writing essays in cultural studies.
