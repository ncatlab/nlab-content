## Idea

A [[link]] $L \subset S^3$  in the [[3-sphere]] is called a _hyperbolic link_ if its [[knot complement]] $S^3 \setminus L$ admits the [[structure]] of a [[hyperbolic metric]], hence of a [[hyperbolic 3-manifold]], hence if it is [[isometry|isometric]] to a [[quotient space]] of [[hyperbolic 3-space]] by a [[torsion subgroup|torsion-free]] [[discrete group]] $\Gamma$ [[action|acting]] by [[isometries]]:

$$
  S^3 \setminus L
  \;\simeq\;
  \mathbb{H}^3/\Gamma
  \,.
$$

(e.g. [FKP 17, Def. 2.8](#FKP17))

If the hyperbolic link is in fact a [[knot]] (has a single [[connected component]]) it is called a _hyperbolic knot_.
