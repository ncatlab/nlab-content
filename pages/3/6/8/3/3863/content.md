## Idea

The _Witten index_ or _supersymmetric index_ of a [[supersymmetry|supersymmetric]] [[quantum field theory]] is its [[partition function]] with the [[trace]]-operation replaced by the [[super-trace]].

## Examples

The Witten index of the [[superstring]] is the [[Witten genus]] of the corresponding [[target space]].
