Witten gave a nonrigorous proof of a remarkable index theorem concerning the [[Dirac operator]] on an infinite-dimensional [[loop space]]. This discovery is one of the big motivations for introducing the [[String structure]]s and for search for geometric interpretation of [[elliptic cohomology]].

* E. Witten, _Elliptic genera and quantum field theory_, Commun.Math.Phys. __109__:525, 1987
