## Definition

A **sequential colimit** is a [[colimit]] of the diagram 

$$A_0 \overset{f_0}\to A_1 \overset{f_1}\to A_2 \overset{f_2}\to \ldots$$

Thus, a sequential colimit is a special case of a [[directed colimit]]. See there for more details.
