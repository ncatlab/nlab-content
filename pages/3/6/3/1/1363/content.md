#Idea#

A [[simplicial model category]] is a presentation of an [[(infinity,1)-category]]. The _localization_ of a simplicial model category corresponds accordingly to the [[localization of an (infinity,1)-category]] (i.e. the passage to a [[reflective (infinity,1)-subcategory]]).
