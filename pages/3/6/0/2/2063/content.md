Given a [[bundle]] $p: E \to B$ and a [[global element]] $x: 1 \to A$, the __fibre__ or __fiber__ $E_x$ of $(E,p)$ over $x$ is simply the [[pullback]]
$$ \array {
   E_x        & \to             & E \\
   \downarrow &                 & \downarrow_p \\
   1          & \stackrel{x}\to & B
} $$
if it exists.

In a [[fiber bundle]], all fibres are [[isomorphism|isomorphic]] to some standard fibre $F$ in a coherent way.
