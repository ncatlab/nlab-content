## Statement 

+-- {: .num_theorem}
###### Theorem

Every [[finite group]] of [[odd number|odd]] [[order]] is a [[solvable group]].

=--

This is due to ([Feit-Thompson 62](#FeitThompson62)).

A fully formalized proof in [[Coq]] has been announced in ([INRIA 2012](#INRIA))
