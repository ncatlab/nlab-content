## Statement 

+-- {: .num_theorem}
###### Theorem

Every [[finite group]] of [[odd number|odd]] [[order]] is a [[solvable group]].

=--

This is due to [Feit & Thompson 1962](#FeitThompson62).

A fully [[formal proof|formalized proof]] in [[Coq]] has been produced by [Gonthier et al. 2013](#Gonthier13).
