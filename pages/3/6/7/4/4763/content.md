## Overview

The _Feynman transform_ is an operation on the category of twisted [[modular operad]]s. It gives a way to parametrize various versions of the Kontsevich's [[graph complex]], by various [[modular operad]]s. Every modular operad is in particular [[cyclic operad|cyclic]] (some say "symplectic"). The Feynman transform, up to a shift, reduces to the [[cobar operad]] of the underlying cyclic operad. 

The name "Feynman transform" is due to [[Getzler]] and [[Kapranov]].
