## Pin⁺ bordism groups

* $\Omega_0^{Pin^+}\cong 1$
* $\Omega_1^{Pin^+}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_2^{Pin^+}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2^2$
* $\Omega_3^{Pin^+}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_4^{Pin^+}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2^2$
* $\Omega_5^{Pin^+}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_6^{Pin^+}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_7^{Pin^+}\cong 1$

([Kirby & Taylor 90, Crl. 2](#KirbyTaylor90))
