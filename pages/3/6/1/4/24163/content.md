## Definition ##

The analytic Markov's principle states that for all points $x$ in the space of real numbers, if $x \leq 0$ is false, then $x \gt 0$. 

$$\prod_{x:\mathbb{R}} ((x \leq 0) \to \emptyset) \to (x \gt 0)$$
