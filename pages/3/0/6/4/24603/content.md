Ivan T. Todorov (1933-2025) was a Bulgarian [[theoretical physics|theoretical]] and [[mathematical physics|mathematical physicist]], a specialist in [[quantum field theory]], especially [[conformal field theory]] as well as [[representation theory|representation theoretic]] methods in quantum field theory.

* [[Jürg Fröhlich]], Junchen Rong: *[Tribute to Ivan T. Todorov](https://www.ihes.fr/en/todorov-en/)*, IHES News (3 Mar 2025)
