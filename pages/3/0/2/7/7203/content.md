__Donald E. Knuth__ is an American computer scientist and a mathematician, a professor emeritus at Stanford University. Knuth is the author of  TeX,  Metafont, the cweb computer language, of many important algorithms in formal languages, parsing, combinatorics, numerics, [[rewriting system]]s, of the concept of literate programming, of the series of books "The Art of Computer Programming", a book on surreal numbers etc.
