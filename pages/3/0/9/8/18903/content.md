## Idea

In [[Yang-Mills theory]] on 4d [[Minkowski spacetime]] the _theta vacuum_ is a [[vacuum state]] which is a [[superposition]] of each [[Yang-Mills instanton]]-sector weighted by a [[complex phase]] proportional to the [[theta angle]] times the [[instanton number]].
