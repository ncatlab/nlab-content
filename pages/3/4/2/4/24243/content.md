Ivan Vinogradov (Иван Матвеевич Виноградов) was a Russian mathematician working at the Steklov Mathematical Institute in Moscow, known for his numerous contributions to [[analytic number theory]].

He got his PhD degree around 1915 at Saint Petersburg University, supervised by Yakov Uspensky (also published as James Uspensky).

He was the editor-in-chief of the [[Encyclopaedia of Mathematics]].
