## Idea

The recognition principle for relative loop spaces states that, for $1 \leq N \lt \infty$, a pair of pointed toplological spaces $(X_c,X_o)$ is of the homotopy type of an $N$-loop relative space pair $(\Omega^N(Y),\Omega^{N-1}(B\times_Y Y^I))$ for some topological pair $(Y,B)$.

## Finite relative loop spaces
\begin{definition}
For $1 \leq N \lt \infty$ the relative $N$-loop space functor is
$$
  \Omega^N_2:Top_*^{\rightarrow}\rightarrow Top_*^2,\qquad \Omega^N_2(\iota:B\rightarrow Y):=(Y^{\mathbb{S}^N},(B\times_Y Y^I)^{\mathbb{S}^{N-1}}).
$$
\end{definition}
