## Idea

A _differential 2-crossed module_ encodes a _[[semi-strict infinity-category|semistrict]] [[Lie 3-algebra]]_ in terms of a system of ordinary [[Lie algebra]]s and morphisms between them. It generalizes the way a [[differential crossed module]] encodes a strict [[Lie 2-algebra]]
