## Definition

An involutive poset is a [[poset]] $P$ with an [[antitonic]] [[involution]] $\neg:P \to P$. 

## Finite involutive posets

The category of finite involutive posets is the [[opposite category]] of the category of finite [[De Morgan algebras]], with dualising object $\mathbb{2}^\mathbb{2}$. The category of finite involutive posets is also the [[opposite category]] of the category of finite [[Kleene algebras]], with dualising object $\mathbb{3}$.
