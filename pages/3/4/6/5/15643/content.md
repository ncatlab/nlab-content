## Idea

A [[theta function]] for [[number fields]] which generalizes the [[Jacobi theta function]]. As the latter is related to the [[Riemann zeta function]], so Hasse theta functions are related to the [[Dedekind zeta function]].

([Kowalski (2.4)](#Kowalski))

## Properties

### Relation to Dedekind zeta function

A certain [[integral]] over a kernel involving the Hecke theta function gives the [[Dedekind zeta function]]. See at _[Dedekind zeta function -- Relation to Hecke theta function](Dedekind+zeta+function#RelationToThetaFunctions)_.
