## Idea

The [[n-sphere]] for $n = 8$.

## Properties

+-- {: .num_prop} 
###### Proposition

There is a [[homeomorphism]]

$$
  \mathbb{O}P^1 \,\simeq\, S^8
$$

between the [[octonionic projective line]] and the [[8-sphere]].
