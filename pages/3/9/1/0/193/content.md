An _adjunction_ is a pair of [[category|categories]] $C, D$ together with [[functor|functors]] $L: C \to D$, $R : D \to C$ and [[natural transformation|natural transformations]] $i : 1_C \to R L$, $e : L R \to 1_D$ satisfying some equations called the [[zig-zag identities]].  

In this situation we call $L$ and $R$ [[adjoint functor|adjoint functors]] - and more precisely, we call $L$ the [[left adjoint]] and $R$ the [[right adjoint]].  We call $i$ the [[unit]] and $e$ the [[counit]] of the adjunction.

More generally, we can [[internalization|internalize]] the concept of adjunction in any [[2-category]] or [[bicategory]].
