## Idea

The *Bergman kernel* is the [[reproducing kernel Hilbert space|reproducing kernel]] for the [[Hilbert space]] of [[square integrable function|square integrable]] [[holomorphic functions]] in a [[domain of holomorphy|domain]] in $\mathbb{C}^n$.
