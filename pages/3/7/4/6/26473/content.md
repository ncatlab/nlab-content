## Idea

A refinement of the notion of [[homotopy category]] of a [[model category]] (more generally, [[relative category]]) that keeps track of homotopies between morphisms.

Dwyer–Kan implement the notion of a simplicial homotopy category as a simplicial category that is Dwyer–Kan equivalent to the [[hammock localization]].
