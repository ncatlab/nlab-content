__Masoud Khalkhali__ is Iranian-Canadian mathematician with professorship at the University of Western Ontario in London, Ontario. His main focus is on ([[spectral triple|spectral]]) [[noncommutative geometry]] and [[homological algebra]], especially the [[cyclic homology]]. He is one of the contributors to the group blog &lt;http://noncommutativegeometry.blogspot.com>.
