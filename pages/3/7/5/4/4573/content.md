__Masoud Khalkhali__ is Iranian-Canadian mathematician with professorship at the University of Western Ontario in London, Ontario. His main focus is on [[noncommutative geometry]] and homological algebra, especially the [[cyclic homology]]. He is one of the contrubutors to the group blog &lt;http://noncommutativegeometry.blogspot.com>.

* book: _Basic Noncommutative Geometry_, Europ. Math. Soc. 2009, 239 p. [ad](http://www.ems-ph.org/books/book.php?proj_nr=109)
