## Idea

In the [[standard model of particle physics]] a _B-meson_ is a [[meson]] which is a [[bound state]] of two (anti-)[[bottom quarks]] (whence "B").

Currently the major [[experiment]] studying B-meson [[physics]] is the [[LHCb]]. It is increasingly seeing [[flavour anomalies]] (see there) in $B$-meson decays at high [[statistical significance]], pointing to physics beyond the [[standard model of particle physics]].
