## Idea

The definition of semilinearity first appeared in Parikh’s theorem [Parikh (1961)](#Parikh61) and has then gained significance in [[linguistics]].

## Definition

A [[formal grammar]] is semilinear if the languages it generates are [[regular language|regular]] when forgetting the order.
