## Definition

\begin{definition}
A [[G-∞-category]] $\mathcal{C}$ is **G-stable** if

1. $\mathcal{C}$ is [[G-semiadditivity|G-semiadditive]], and

2. For all $H \subset G$, $\mathcal{C}_H$ is a [[stable ∞-category]].

\end{definition}
