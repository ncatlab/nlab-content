## Idea

*Ollivier-Ricci curvature* (ORC) is an analogue of [[Ricci curvature]] applicable also to [[graphs]], based on [[optimal transport]] theory. ORC compares the counting distance between [[vertices]] to the [[Wasserstein metric|Wasserstein distance]] between their neighbourhood [[probability measures]].
