Paul Halmos was a Hungarian-born American-based mathematician.

## Selected writings

* Measure Theory. D. Van Nostrand Company, 1950.

* A Hilbert space problem book. D. Van Nostrand Co., 1967.  Second edition, Graduate Texts in Mathematics, 19, Springer, 1982. ISBN: 0-387-90685-1.

* Lectures on ergodic theory. Chelsea Publishing Co., 1960.

* Introduction to Hilbert Space and the theory of Spectral Multiplicity. Chelsea Publishing Company, 1951.

* Lectures on Boolean algebras. Van Nostrand Mathematical Studies, No. 1.  D. Van Nostrand Co., 1963.

* Algebraic logic. Chelsea Publishing Co., 1962.

* Finite Dimensional Vector Spaces.  Annals of Mathematics Studies, no. 7.  Princeton University Press, Princeton, N.J., 1942.  Second edition, D. Van Nostrand Co., 1958.

* Naive set theory.  D. Van Nostrand Co., 1960.

* Linear algebra problem book. The Dolciani Mathematical Expositions, 16. Mathematical Association of America, 1995.  ISBN: 0-88385-322-1.
