## Idea

Where ordinary 3d [[Chern-Simons theory]] is given by an [[action functional]] with values in the [[circle group]] on a space of [[special unitary group]]-[[principal connections]], its "[[analytic continuation]]"([Witten 10](#Witten10)) instead is defined on complex [[special linear group]]-principal connections and its values are elements in $\mathbb{R}/\mathbb{Z}$ plus an imaginery number.

The [[Wilson line]] [[quantum observables]] of analytically continued Chern-Simons theory are accoridngly [[analytic continuations]] of [[knot invariants]] etc.
