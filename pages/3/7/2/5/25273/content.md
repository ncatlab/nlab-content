## Idea

A __Cantor cube__ is a [[topological space]] of the form  of a [[Cartesian product|Cartesian]] [[product topological space|product space]] $\{0,1\}^K$ where $K$ is some index [[set]], hence a [[Cartesian product]] of $K$ copies of the 2-element set equipped with [[Tihonov topology]].

Sometimes Cantor cubes are understood with their structure of a [[topological group]] being a [[direct product]] of copies of [[cyclic group of order 2|$\mathbb{Z}/2$]].


## Examples

The basic example is the [[Cantor space]] $\{0,1\}^{\aleph_0}$.
