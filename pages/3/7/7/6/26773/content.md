## Definition

A **polynomial comonad** on a category is a [[comonad]] whose underlying [[endofunctor]] is a [[polynomial functor]]. 

## Properties

* A polynomial comonad on [[Set]] is equivalent to a [[small category]] ([Ahman-Uustalu, 2016](#AhmanUustalu2016)).
