Hermann Schwarz was a German mathematician.

He got his PhD degree in 1864 at the University of Berline,
advised by Kummer and Weierstrass.

His PhD students include [[Lipót Fejér]] and [[Ernst Zermelo]].

His name is often attached to the [[Cauchy inequality]],
for which he published a proof in an 1888 paper,
although proofs were previously published by Cauchy in 1821 and Bouniakowsky in 1859.
