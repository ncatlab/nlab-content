## Idea

The [[moduli space]] of algebraic curves/[[Riemann surfaces]] is a sort of space of parameters parametrizing algebraic curves of given [[genus]].

Deligne and Mumford have found a nontrivial [[compactification]] of a moduli space of Riemann surfaces of fixed genus which is a [[Deligne-Mumford stack]], see at _[[Deligne-Mumford compactification]].

There is also a decorated version of curves with marked points, and
of the corresponding compactified moduli space of stable curves of genus $g$ with $n$ marked points $\mathcal{M}_{g,n}$ which plays an important role in the mathematical study of [[Gromov-Witten invariants]] and of [[conformal blocks]].
