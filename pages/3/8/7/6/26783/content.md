*[[Jacobson radicals]]* are named after him.  

\begin{proposition}
**(Jacobson's lemma)** 
If $A$ and $B$ are [[matrices]] over a [[field]] of [[characteristic 0]] and $A$ commutes with $A B - B A$ then $A B - B A$ is [[nilpotent element|nilpotent]].
\end{proposition}
