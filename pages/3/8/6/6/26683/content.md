## Idea

The *Bethe ansatz* in the strict sense of the word is an ansatz for the [[energy]] [[eigenstates]] of the Heisenberg [[spin chain]] model. More generally the term refers to related methods for [[non-perturbative quantum field theory|non-perturbative]] analysis of [[quantum system|quantum]] [[lattice models]].
