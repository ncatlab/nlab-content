## Idea

The _ABJM model_ ([ABJM 08](#ABJM08)) is 3-dimensional [[supersymmetry|supersymmetric]] [[Chern-Simons theory]] with [[gauge group]] [[special unitary group|SU(N)]] and [[minimal coupling|coupled]] to [[matter fields]]. It is supposed to describe the [[worldvolume]] theory of $N$ coincident [[M2-branes]] at an $\mathbb{Z}_k$ [[orbifold]] [[singularity]]. For $N = 2$ it reduces to the [[BLG model]]. Due to the matter coupling, the ABJM model is no longer a [[topological field theory]] as pure Chern-Simons is, but it is still a [[conformal field theory]]. As such it is thought to correspond under [[AdS-CFT]] duality to [[M-theory]] on [[anti-de Sitter spacetime|AdS4]] $\times$ [[7-sphere|S7]]/$\mathbb{Z}_k$ (see also [MFFGME 09](#MFFGME09)).

Notice that the worldvolume $SU(N)$ gauge group enhancement at an $\mathbb{Z}_k$-[[ADE singularity]] matches the [[enhanced gauge symmetry|gauge symmetry enhancement]] of the [[effective field theory]] for [[M-theory on G2-manifolds]] at the same kind of singularities (see at _[M-theory on G2-manifolds -- Nonabelian gauge groups](M-theory+on+G2-manifolds#EnhancedGaugeGroups)_).


## Properties

Under  [[holographic duality]] supposed to be related to [[11-dimensional supergravity|M-theory]] on $AdS_4 \times S^7 / \mathbb{Z}_k$.
