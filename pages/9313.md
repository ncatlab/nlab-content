## Definition

For $G$ a [[Lie group]] and $\mathfrak{g}$ its [[Lie algebra]], and for $P \to X$ a $G$-[[principal bundle]], the corresponding **adjoint bundle** is the [[associated bundle]] $P \times_G \mathfrak{g} \to X$ via the [[adjoint action]]/[[adjoint representation]] of $G$ on $\mathfrak{g}$.
