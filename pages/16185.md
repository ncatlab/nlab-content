## Idea

In [[supergeometry]] the relevant concept of [[vielbein]] is often called the _super-vielbein_.

Given a local model _[[super-Minkowski spacetime]]_ $\mathbb{R}^{p,q|N}$ then with the notation as in the section _[canonical coordinates](super+Minkowski+spacetime#CanonicalCoordinates)_ there, the super-vielbein is written


$$
  (E^A) = (E^a, E^\alpha) = (E^a, \Psi^\alpha)
$$

where the index $a$ ranges from 1 to the bosonic dimension $p+q$ and where $\alpha$ ranges over the components of a [[spinor]] in the given [[spin representation]] $N$ (more on which at _[[super Poincare group]]_).
