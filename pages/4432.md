
Swapneel Mahajan works on categories, higher categorical structures, and their applications to combinatorics.  Together with [[Marcelo Aguiar]] he has written an 836-page book on this subject, which is currently available for free online:

* Marcelo Aguiar and Swapneel Mahajan, _[[Monoidal Functors, Species and Hopf Algebras]]_, to be published by Cambridge U. Press.  ([web](http://www.math.tamu.edu/~maguiar/a.pdf))

See also Mahajan's ([website](http://www.math.iitb.ac.in/~swapneel/)).
