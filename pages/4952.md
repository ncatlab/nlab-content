## Definition

For $(X,g)$ a [[Riemannian manifold]] let $\nabla_{g}$ be the corresponding [[Levi-Civita connection]]. The **Riemann curvature** $R_g$ of $(X,g)$ is the [[curvature]] $F_{\nabla_g}$ of $\nabla_g$:

$$
  R_g := F_{\nabla_g}
  \,.
$$
