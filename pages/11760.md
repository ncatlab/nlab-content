## Idea

On a space with [[Fivebrane structure]] a _ninebrane structure_ is a trivialization of (some fractional multiple of) the 3rd [[Pontryagin class]]. This is supposed to be related to parts of the [[quantum anomaly]] cancellation of the [[M9-brane]].
