## Idea

A _dg-model category is the analogue of a [[simplicial model category]] in the [[dg-category|dg]]-setting. Any dg-model category [[dg-category presented by a dg-model category|presents]] an associated [[dg-category]], as described at [[dg-category presented by a dg-model category]].

## Definition

A **dg-model structure** is a $dgmod_k$-[[enriched model structure]], where $dgmod_k$ denotes the category of [[dg-modules]] over the base [[commutative ring]] $k$.
