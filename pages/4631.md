An _augmented simplcial set_ $X$ is a [[presheaf]] on the augmented [[simplex category]] $\Delta_+$,

$$
  X : \Delta^{op}_+ \to Set
  \,.
$$

So its restriction along $\Delta \hookrightarrow \Delta_+$ is an ordinary [[simplicial set]].
