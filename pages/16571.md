
A category $\pi:E\to B$ over $B$ is a __prefibered category__ if for every morphism
$f:b\to b'$ there is a cartesian morphism $u:e\to e'$ with $\pi(e) = f$.

This differs from a [[fibered category]] by not requiring that a composition of
cartesian morphisms is cartesian. Also the notion of cartesian is in the original
(weaker) sense. If every morphism has a strongly cartesian lift then the prefibered
category is in fact fibered. 

Prefibered (prefibred) category (Rus. предрасслоённая категория, Fr. catégorie préfibrée)
