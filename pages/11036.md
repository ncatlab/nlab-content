## Idea

_Proof nets_ are a graphical way to denote [[proofs]] in [[linear logic]].

Under the [[categorical semantics]] of (multiplicative intuitionistic) [[linear logic]] in [[monoidal categories]], proof nets are the [[string diagrams]] "with boxes" in these categories (e.g. [Melliès 06](#Mellies06)).
