#### Limits and colimits
+--{: .hide}
[[!include infinity-limits - contents]]
=--
=--
=--



A [[diagram]] $D \to C$ in a [[category]] $C$ is called _small_ if $D$ is a [[small category]].

A [[limit]] or [[colimit]] over a small diagram is a **small limit** or **small colimit**.
A [[product]] or [[coproduct]] over a small diagram is a **small product** or **small coproduct**.
