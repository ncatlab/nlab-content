## Idea

The *$\pi_\ast$-Kan condition* ([Bousfield & Friedlander 1978, B.3](#BousfieldFriedlander78)) on a [[bisimplicial set]] guarantees that its [[geometric realization]] behaves well with respect to [[homotopy pullback]] ([Bousfield & Friedlander 1978, Thm. B.4](#BousfieldFriedlander78)).

See also [this Prop.](geometric+realization+of+simplicial+topological+spaces#SufficientConditionsForRealizationToPreserveHomotopyPullback) at *[[geometric realization of simplicial topological spaces]]*.
