## Idea

A _vector meson_ is a [[meson]] which (in [[Wigner classification]]) is in the [[vector representation]] or [[pseudo-vector representation]] of the [[Lorentz group]].

This is in contrast to [[scalar mesons]].

## Examples

Examples of [[vector mesons]]:

* [[omega meson]]

* [[rho meson]]

* [[K* meson]]

Examples of [[pseudo-vector mesons]]:

* [[a1-meson]]

* [[f1-meson]]
