## Idea

By *the modular group* one means alternatively 

* the [[projective special linear group]] [[PSL(2,Z)|$PSL(2,\mathbb{Z})$]] of [integral](Moebius+transformation#ModularGroup) [[Moebius transformations]] 

  (cf. [Serre 1973 §VII.1](#Serre73))

* the [[special linear group]] [[SL(2,Z)|$SL(2,\mathbb{Z})$]], [being](MCGOfClosedOrientedSurfaces) the [[mapping class group]] of the [[torus]]

  (cf. [Stein 2007 Def. 1.1](#Stein07))

hence either the [[domain]] or [[codomain]] of the [[quotient]] map

$$
  SL_2(\mathbb{Z})
  \longrightarrow
  SL_2(\mathbb{Z})/\{\pm 1\}
  \eqqcolon
  PSL_2(\mathbb{Z})
  \,.
$$
