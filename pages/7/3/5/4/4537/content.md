## Idea


The _Lie operad_ is the linear [[operad]] whose [[algebras over an operad]] are [[Lie algebra]]s. 

Its cofibrant [[resolution]] (in the context of [[(∞,1)-operad]]s) is the operad whose algebras are [[L-∞-algebra]]s.
