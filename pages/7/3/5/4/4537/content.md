## Idea

Given an ambient additive [[symmetric monoidal category]], the _Lie operad_ is the [[operad]] whose [[algebras over an operad]] are [[Lie algebra]]s in that category. It is a [[quadratic operad]] whose Koszul dual is the *operad for commutative algebras*. 

In the case of the monoidal category of chain complexes, we also say dg-Lie-operad. Its cofibrant [[resolution]] (in the context of [[(∞,1)-operad]]s) is the operad whose algebras are [[L-∞-algebra]]s.
