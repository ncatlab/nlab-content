## Idea

A _Hopf $C^\ast$-algebra_ according to ([Vaes-VanDale](#VaesDaele)) is a [[C-star algebra]] equipped with [[stuff, structure, property|structure and property]] analogous to that of a [[Hopf algebra]] structure on the underlying [[associative algebra]]. 

A _weak $C^\ast$-Hopf algebra_ according to ([B&#246;hm-Szlachanyi](#BohmSzlachanyi)) is a [[star-algebra|star]]-[[weak Hopf algebra]] such that has a faithful [[star-representation]] on a [[Hilbert space]].

With suitable definitions the central [[Tannaka duality]]-property of [[Hopf algebras]] (that their [[representation category]] is a [[rigid monoidal category]] with [[fiber functor]]) is lifted to the [[operator algebra]] context: the $C^\ast$-[[representation category]] of a (weak) $C^\ast$-Hopf algebra is a rigid monoidal _[[C-star-category]]_ with [[fiber functor]]. ([B&#246;hm-Szlachanyi](#BohmSzlachanyi)).
