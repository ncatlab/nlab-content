## Idea

A _12-manifold_ is a [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] 12. (Generally a [[topological manifold]], but it can be specified to a [[PL manifold]] or a [[smooth manifold]].)

## Properties

\begin{proposition}
**([[Hirzebruch signature theorem]])**
For an [[orientable]] smooth 12-manifold $M$ with [[fundamental class]] $[M]\in H^12(M,\mathbb{Z})\cong\mathbb{Z}$, its [[signature]] is given by [[Pontrjagin numbers]] as:
$$
\sigma(M)
=\frac{1}{945}\langle(62p_3-13p_1p_2+2p_1^3)(M),[M]\rangle
\in\mathbb{Z}.
$$
\end{proposition}
