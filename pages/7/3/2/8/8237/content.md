## Idea

A [[lattice in a vector space|lattice in]] an [[inner product space]] is called _integral_ if the [[inner product]] of [[pairs]] of lattice vectors takes values in the [[integer]] numbers.

Integral lattices may encode [[higher structures]], such as [[braided 2-groups]] ([Baez 2015](#Baez15)) and multiplicative [[bundle gerbes]] ([Ganter 2014](#Gan14)).
