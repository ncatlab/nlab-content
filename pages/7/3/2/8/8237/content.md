## Idea

A _[[lattice in a vector space]]_ is called _integral_ if the canonical [[inner product]] of all pairs of lattice vectors is an [[integer]] number.

Such lattices encode higher categorical information, such as a [[braided 2-group]] ([Baez 2015](#Baez15)) or a multiplicative [[bundle gerbe]] ([Ganter 2014](#Gan14)).
