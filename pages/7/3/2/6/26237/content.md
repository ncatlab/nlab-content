__Kim Lin Chew__ is an algebraist with main interests in [[ring]] theory. Among his main works, he studied quotient/localization constructions, including in an approach via modular closure operators (analogous to [[Lawvere-Tierney topologies]] in the context of the abelian categories of modules).

* _On extensions of rings and modules_, 1965 PhD thesis [pdf](https://open.library.ubc.ca/media/download/pdf/831/1.0080549/1)
