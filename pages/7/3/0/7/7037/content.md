## Idea

A [[field extension]] $\mathbb{F}(\zeta_n)$ by a primitive [[root of unity]] $\zeta_n$. 

The default meaning where $\mathbb{F}$ is left unspecified is $\mathbb{F} = \mathbb{Q}$, the field of [[rational numbers]], where $\zeta_n$ may be taken to be $\exp(2\pi i/n)$ (hence the smallest subfield of the [[complex numbers]] $\mathbb{C}$ that contains this [[exponential function|exponential]] value).
