


## Idea

A [[closed manifold|closed]] [[surface]] that does not admit an [[orientation]].

## Properties

\begin{proposition}
The [[topological complexity]] of the [[Klein bottle]] is $4$ (with convention $TC(*)=0$).
\end{proposition}

([Cohen & Vandembrouq 16, Theorem 1](#CohenVandembroucq16))
