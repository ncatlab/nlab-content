## Idea

The _micrometer_ or _micron_ is a [[physical unit]] of [[length]].

## Definition

A **micrometer** (abbreviated $\mu m$) is one $10^{6}$th of a [[meter]]:

$$
  \mu m \coloneqq 10^{-6} m
  \,.
$$

The [[scale]] between about a [[nanometer]] and a micrometer is the scale of [[mesoscopic physics]].
