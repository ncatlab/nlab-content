## Idea

For $A$ an ordinary [[associative algebra]], its _tangent complex_ is its [[module]] of [[derivation]]s.

For $A$ a [[dg-algebra]], its _tangent complex_ is the essentially the value of the [[derived functor]] of the derivations-assigning functor on $A$. This is closely related to the [[automorphism âˆž-Lie algebra]] of $A$.
