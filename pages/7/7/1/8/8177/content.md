## Idea

The _Eilenberg-Moore spectral sequence_ is a [[spectral sequence]] for the [[singular homology]] of [[product topological spaces]] which generalizes the [[Künneth theorem]] in two ways

1. it applies without assumption on vanishing of [[Tor]]-groups;

1. it generalizes from [[product topological spaces]] to [[fiber products]] of [[fibrations]] and to products over group [[actions]].
