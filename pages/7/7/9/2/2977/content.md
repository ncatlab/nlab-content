**Marc Levine** is an American [[algebraic geometry|algebraic geometer]], a specialist on [[algebraic cycle]]s and [[algebraic K-theory]]. He proposed several constructions of the (triangulated) category of [[motive]]s and constructed [[algebraic cobordism]] together with [[Fabien Morel]]. 

* [Marc's homepage](http://www.math.neu.edu/~levine) at Northeastern University, Boston.
