## Idea

The equations of motion on the [[M5-brane]] (the [[6d (2,0)-superconformal QFT]] in a suitable limit) admits a [[soliton]]/[[black brane]] solution which is a [[string]] charged under the [[self-dual higher gauge field|self-dual]] [[B-field]] &lbrack;[Howe, Lambert & West 1997, §3](#HoweLambertWest97)&rbrack;.

Externally this is the [[boundary field theory|boundary]] of the [[M2-brane]] [[brane intersection|ending]] on the [[M5-brane]]. As such this is "the string in [[M-theory]]" and as such also called the _[[M-string]]_.


## Properties

### Elliptic genus and M5-brane charge

The [[elliptic genus]] of the self-dual string at the boundary of the [[M2-brane]] on the [[M5-brane]] has been suggested to be directly analogous to the [[Todd genus]] of the boundary of the [[open string|open]] [[superstring]] ending on [[D-branes]] and hence has been suggested to be the _[[M5-brane charge]]_ in [Sati 2010](#Sati10). Discussion of the computation of this elliptic genus includes [Hohenegger & Iqbal 2013](#HoheneggerIqbal13).
