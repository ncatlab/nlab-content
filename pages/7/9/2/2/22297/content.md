Igor Tyutin (Russian: И́горь Ви́кторович Тю́тин) is a Russian physicist.
He is one of the originators of the [[BRST]] formalism (the T in BRST).

[Website](http://www.tamm.lpi.ru/staff/tyutin.html)

## Selected works

On what came to be known as the [[BRST complex]]:

 * [[D. M. Gitman]], [[I. V. Tyutin]], _Quantization of Fields with Constraints_, Springer, 1990.  [doi:10.1007/978-3-642-83938-2](http://dx.doi.org/10.1007/978-3-642-83938-2).
