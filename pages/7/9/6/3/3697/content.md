(Solutions of) holonomic systems of differential equations are formalized in the notoin of a holonomic D-module. Holonomicity of D-modules is important also in [[geometric representation theory]]. 

* [[Masaki Kashiwara]], _On the holonomic systems of linear differential equations. II_, Invent. Math. __49__ (1978), no. 2, 121--135, [doi](http://dx.doi.org/10.1007/BF01403082)
