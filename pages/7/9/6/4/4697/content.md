The _kilogram_ (kg) is a [[unit]] of [[weight]].

One kilogram is defined to be the weight of $N$ [[atom]]s of the [[carbon]] [[isotope]] $C^{12}$, where $N$ is $\frac{1000}{12}$ times the [[Avogadro number]].
