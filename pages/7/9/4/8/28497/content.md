## Idea

Notation is the assigning of abstract [[stuff, structure, and properties]] a typographic "symbol". The range of the terms, definitions, and glossaries is intended to be indepth, including various synonyms to ensure coverage.

## Glossary

* $\wedge$ The [[propositional logic|null-ordered logic]] conjunction, or AND [[operator]].

* $\wedge _A$ The [[meet]] [[operator]] in the [[semilattice|meet-semilattice]] A.

* $\vee$ The [[propositional logic|null-ordered logic]] inclusive dysjunction, or OR [[operator]].

* $\vee _A$ The [[join]] [[operator]] in the [[semilattice|join-semilattice]] A.

* $\veebar$ The [[propositional logic|null-ordered logic]] exclusive dysjunction, or XOR [[operator]].

* $\Rightarrow$ The [[propositional logic|null-ordered logic]] implication, conditional, or implies [[operator]]. For propositions $\phi, \psi$, 

\begin{theorem} $\phi \Rightarrow \psi = \neg \phi \vee \psi$ \end{theorem}

* $\forall$ The [[first-order logic]] [[universal quantifier]]. For predicate formula $\phi$, $\forall \alpha \phi (\alpha) = \neg \exists \alpha \neg \phi (\alpha)$.

* $\exists$ The [[first-order logic]] [[existential quantifier]].

* $\exists !$ The [[first-order logic]] uniqueness [[quantifier]].

* $\uparrow _A$ $(${$\alpha$}$)$ The principal up-set, coinitial/final segment generated by the singleton subset {$\alpha$} in the [[partial order]], or [[semilattice|join-semilattice]] A.

* $\uparrow _A (\alpha)$ The up-set, or coinitial/final segment generated by the subset $\alpha$ in the [[partial order]], or [[join-semilattice]] A. 

* $0$ The first [[limit ordinal|limit-ordinal]] in the Von Neumann ordinals, i.e. ∅. The [[initial category]].

* $0_A$ The bottom element, or the zero of complete [[partial order]], or bound [[semilattice|meet-semilattice]] $A$.

* $1$ The first [[successor ordinal|successor-ordinal]] in the Von Neumann ordinals, i.e. ∅. The [[terminal category]].

* $1_A$ The top element, or unit of the complete [[partial order]], or bound [[semilattice|meet-semilattice]] $A$. 

* $Fun_A(B,C), Hom_A(B,C)$ The [[hom-set]], or [[hom-object]] called the [[functor category]] of [[functor|functors]] from [[domain]] B, to [[codomain]] C in [[category]] A.

* $Hom_A(x,y), hom(x,y), A(x,y)$ The [[hom-set]], or [[hom-object]] of [[morphism|morphisms]] from [[domain]] x, and [[codomain]] y in [[category]] A.

* $Nat _A(f,g)$, $Hom_A(f,g)$ The [[hom-set]] or [[hom-object]] of [[natural transformation|natural transformations]] from [[domain]] f, and [[codomain]] g in [[functor category]] A.

* [[ZFC]] The standard [[logic|first-order]] foundational [[material set theory|material]] [[pure set|pure]] [[axiom|axiomatic]] [[set theory|set theoretical]] logic with Axiom of Choice.

**Not at all complete.*
