## Idea

In [[chromatic homotopy theory]] the _redshift conjecture_ is a [[conjecture]] about the nature of the [[iterated algebraic K-theory|iterated]] [[algebraic K-theory]] spectrum $K(R)$ of a [[connective spectrum|connective]] [[E-infinity ring]] $R$. Roughly, its say that $K(R)$ has [[chromatic level]] one higher than $R$ has.

The conjecture was originally formulated by [[John Rognes]] ([Rognes 99](#Rognes99), [Rognes 00](#Rognes00)) and appeared in [Ausoni & Rognes 2008](#AusoniRognes08), review in [Rognes 2014](#Rognes14).
