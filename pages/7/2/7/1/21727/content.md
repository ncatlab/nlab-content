## Idea

Twisted Bredon cohomology, or _equivariant ordinary cohomology with local coefficients_ is the [[twisted cohomology|twisted]] generalization of [[Bredon cohomology]], and the [[equivariant cohomology|equivariant]]-generalization of [[twisted ordinary cohomology]].
