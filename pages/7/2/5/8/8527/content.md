## Idea

The _Teichm&#252;ller space_ $\mathcal{T}_{\Sigma}$ of a  ([[closed manifold|closed]] ) 2-dimenional [[manifold]] $\Sigma$  is the [[moduli stack of complex structures]] on $\Sigma$ (in fact an [[orbifold]]). Closely related to the [[moduli space of conformal structures]] hence of  [[Riemann surfaces]].

Since the [[diffeomorphism]] class of  a [[closed manifold]] of dimension 2 is given by its [[genus]] $g\in \mathbb{Z}$, typically one speaks of the Teichm&#252;ller space $\mathcal{T}_g$ for each $g$. More generally one considers $\Sigma$s equipped with $n$ punctures/[[boundary]] circle and then writes $\mathcal{T}_{g,n}$. Hence $\mathcal{T}_{1,1}$ is the [[moduli stack of elliptic curves]] over the complex numbers.
