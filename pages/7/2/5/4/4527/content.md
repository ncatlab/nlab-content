__Fred van Oystaeyen__ is an [[algebraist]] and one of the pioneers of [[noncommutative algebraic geometry]]; he is a Professor at Antwerp. He is especially well known for his work on graded and filtered rings, localization theory for noncommutative rings and the study of Brauer groups and generalizations.
