## Definition

In [[supergeometry]] a _super-diffeomorphism_ is an [[isomorphism]] in the [[category]] of [[supermanifolds]]. This is in direct [[analogy]] to how an ordinary [[diffeomorphism]] is an [[isomorphism]] in the category of [[smooth manifolds]].
