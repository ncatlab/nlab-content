## Definition

For $(L \dashv R) : C \stackrel{\overset{L}{\leftarrow}}{\underset{R}{\to}} D$ a pair of [[adjoint functor]]s between  [[model categories]] $C$ and $D$, an [[object]] $X \in C$ is an $L$-**cell object** if it is connected to the [[initial object]] by a (possibly [[transfinite composition|transfinite]]) composition of [[pushout]]s of morphisms of the form $L(f)$.
