For $f : A \to C$ and $g : B \to C$ two [[morphism]]s in a [[category]] $C$, the **fiber product** 
$A \times_C B$
of $A$ with $B$ over $C$ is, if it exists, [[generalized the|the]] [[pullback]]

$$
  \array{
     A \times_C B &\to& B
     \\
     \downarrow && \downarrow
     \\
     A &\to& C
  }
  \,.
$$

#Examples#

In $C =$ [[Set]] the fiber product is given by the usual formula
$$
  A \times_C B = 
  \left\{
    (a,b) \in A \times B \;|\;
    f(a) = g(b) 
  \right\}
  \,.
$$
