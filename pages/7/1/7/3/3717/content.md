## Idea

Consider a [[pair]] of [[quantum systems]], $Q$ and $E$, where $Q$ is some system of interest and $E$ is some system that is external to $Q$ and that is in some fixed [[pure state]] $\vert e \rangle$.  Now let us suppose that the two systems [[interaction|interact]] and evolve via some [[unitary operator]] on the [[tensor product of Hilbert spaces|tensor product]] [[Hilbert space]] of each, $\mathscr{Q} \otimes \mathscr{E}$.  If one is interested in the $\mathscr{E}$-[[trace]] of [[density operators]] on this system, then $Q$ is known as an _open system_ and $E$ is the _environment_.
