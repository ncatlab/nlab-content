## Statement

\begin{proposition}
**(Jacobson lemma)** 
If $A$ and $B$ are [[matrices]] over a [[field]] of [[characteristic zero]] and $A$ [[commutator|commutes]] with $A B - B A$ then $A B - B A$ is [[nilpotent element|nilpotent]].
\end{proposition}
