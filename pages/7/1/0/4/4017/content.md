Peter Freyd is a [[category theory|category theorist]], known for work in many areas of category theory, including [[adjoint functors]], [[abelian categories]], [[allegories]], [[homotopy theory]], [[polymorphism]] and [[topos theory]].

* [Website](https://www.math.upenn.edu/people/peter-j-freyd)

* [Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_J._Freyd)
