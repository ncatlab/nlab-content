## Idea

_System F_, also known as the _[[polymorphic]] [[lambda-calculus]]_ or _second-order lambda-calculus_, is an extension of [[simply typed lambda-calculus]] to include a type of [[impredicative]] [[universal quantification]].

## References

The calculus now called System F was developed independently in the following works:

* [[Jean-Yves Girard]], _Interprétation functionelle et élimination des coupures dans l'arithmétique d'ordre supérieure_, Ph.D. thesis, Université Paris VII, 1972

* [[John C. Reynolds]], _Towards a theory of type structure_, Programming Symposium, 1974 ([doi](https://doi.org/10.1007/3-540-06859-7_148))
