## Definition

A **ribbon category** (also called a **tortile category**) is a [[braided monoidal category|braided]] [[pivotal category]], or equivalently a [[balanced monoidal category|balanced]] [[autonomous category]], which satisfyies $\theta^* = \theta$, where $\theta$ is the twist.
