## Idea

The suggestion that, at least after suitable [[topological twist|topological twisting]], [[AdS/CFT duality]] becomes the statement that the [[higher algebra|higher]] [[algebras of observables]] on both sides of the correspondence are [[Koszul duality|Koszul duals]] of each other.
