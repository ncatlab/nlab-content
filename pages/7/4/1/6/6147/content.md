__Tannaka-Krein theorem__ (in the narrow sense) is a particular [[Tannaka duality]] theorem for compact topological groups. 

The original theorem is (in two independent and different formulations) stated and proved in  

* [[Tadao Tannaka]], _&#220;ber den Dualit&#228;tssatz der nichtkommutativen topologischen Gruppen_, Tohoku Math. J. 45 (1938), n. 1, 1&#8211;12 (project euclid has only Tohoku new series!)
* M.G. Krein, _A principle of duality for bicompact groups and quadratic block algebras_, Doklady AN SSSR __69__ (1949), 725&#8211;728. 

For a textbook treatment see

* E. Hewitt, K. A. Ross, _Abstract harmonic analysis_, 2 vols. 

See also

* [[Alexander Rosenberg|A. L. Rosenberg]], _[[Reconstruction of groups|Reconstruction of groups]]_ , Selecta Math. N.S. 9:1 (2003) doi
