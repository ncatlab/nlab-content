## Idea

The __Tannaka-Krein theorem__ (in the narrow sense) is a particular [[Tannaka duality]] theorem for [[compact topological groups]]. 

## References

The original theorem is (in two independent and different formulations) stated and proved in  

* [[Tadao Tannaka]]: _&#220;ber den Dualit&#228;tssatz der nichtkommutativen topologischen Gruppen_, Tohoku Math. J. **45** 1 (1938) 1-12 

* [[Mark G. Krein]]: *A principle of duality for bicompact groups and quadratic block algebras*, Doklady AN SSSR __69__ (1949) 725-728

Textbook account:

* E. Hewitt, K. A. Ross, _Abstract harmonic analysis_, 2 vols. 

See also

* [[Alexander Rosenberg]], _[[Reconstruction of groups|Reconstruction of groups]]_ , Selecta Math. N.S. **9** 1 (2003)
