## Idea

The genuinely $\mathbb{Z}_2$-[[equivariant cohomology]] version of [[ordinary cohomology]], taking into account the $\mathbb{Z}_2$-[[action]] on the [[coefficients]]. A [[real-oriented cohomology theory]]. In degree 3 it serves as a [[twisted cohomology|twist]] for [[KR-theory]].

## Examples

The [[B-field]] over [[orientifold]] background of the [[bosonic string]]  is a cocycle in (twisted) HZR-theory. More generally for the [[type II superstring]] it is a genuinely $\mathbb{Z}_2$-[[equivariant cohomology|equivariant]] [[super line 2-bundle]]
