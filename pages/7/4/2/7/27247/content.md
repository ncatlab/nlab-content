## Idea

$Tr(\phi^3)$ is a variant of [[Yang-Mills theory]] consisting of [[spin]]-zero [[gluons]]. The name of the theory comes from the cubic self-interaction term $Tr(\phi^3)$ which appears in the [[Lagrangian]] of the theory:

$$
  \mathcal{L} 
  \;=\; 
  Tr(\partial \phi)^2 + m^2 Tr(\phi^2) + g Tr(\phi^3)
  \,.
$$

The theory has been used by [[Nima Arkani-Hamed]] and collaborators to extend techniques of [[scattering amplitudes]] to non-[[supersymmetric Yang-Mills theory|supersymmetric]] [[Yang-Mills theory]].
