##Emmanuel Haucourt##

**Emmanuel Haucourt** is a researcher at CEA LIST in Saclay in the team of [[Eric Goubault]]. His work is in [[directed algebraic topology]] and in particular in the study of the behaviour of concurrent programs.  He has good notes on _Topologie Alg&#233;brique Dirig&#233;e et Concurrence_ (in both French and English), prepared for the _Master Parisien de Recherche en Informatique_.  These are available from his [web page](http://www.lix.polytechnique.fr/Labo/Emmanuel.Haucourt/).
