## Idea

*D=8 [[Yang-Mills theory]]* (D=8 YM theory) studies the [[Yang-Mills equations]] over a base [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] $D=8$, hence a smooth [[8-manifold]]. A special case is over [[Spin(7) manifolds]].
