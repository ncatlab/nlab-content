##Idea 

Generalising a [[Klein geometry]], a **locally Klein geometry** is a [[triple]] $(\Gamma, G,H)$  where  $G/H$ is a [[Klein geometry]] (not necessarily [[connected topological space|connected]]) and $\Gamma \subset G$ is a [[discrete group|discrete]] [[subgroup]] [[action|acting]] by left multiplication on the [[coset space]] $G/H$ as a group of [[covering space|covering transformations]] with the [[double coset space]], $\Gamma \backslash G/H$, [[connected]] ([Sharpe 97, Def. 3.10](#Sharpe97), [Sharpe 02, Def. 2.7](#Sharpe02)).
