## Idea

An _equivariant function_ is a [[homomorphism]] between two [[G-sets]]:

$$
  f \;\colon\; X \longrightarrow Y
  \phantom{AAA}
  f(g \cdot x) = g \cdot f(x)
  \phantom{A}
  \forall g \in G
  \phantom{A}
  \forall x \in X
$$

More generally, an _equivariant morphism_ is a [[homomorphism]] of $G$-[[actions]].
