## Idea

Given a [[generalized space]] $X$ conceived as a [[concrete sheaf]] $X \in Sh(Charts)_{\sharp_1} \hookrightarrow Sh(Charts)$ on a [[site]] $Charts$ of local model spaces, a _plot_ of $X$ over a $U \in Charts$ is an [[element]] of $X(U) \in Set$, hence (under the [[Yoneda lemma]]) is a [[morphism]] $U \to X$ from that local model space to $X$.
