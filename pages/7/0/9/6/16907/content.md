__Harold Simmons__ was a mathematician at the University of Manchester. His works include topics as logic, model theory, [[lattice]]s, [[ring]]s, [[semiring]]s, [[localization]], [[pointless topology]], sheaf representations, recursion theory, $\lambda$-calculus, and proof theory.
