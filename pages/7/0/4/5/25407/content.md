## Idea

If $A = (a_{i,j}) \in \mathcal{M}_{n,p}(R)$ where $R$ is a commutative [[semi-ring]], we define:

$$
Perm(A) = \underset{\sigma \in \mathfrak{S}_{n}}{\sum}\underset{1 \le i \le n}{\prod}a_{i,\sigma(i)}
$$
