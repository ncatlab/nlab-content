## Definition

A flagged category consists of a category $C$, a groupoid $X$, and a surjection $p:X\to C$ of groupoids from $X$ to the underlying groupoid of objects of $C$. 

## In type theory

A flagged category is a [[1-truncated]] [[precategory]].
