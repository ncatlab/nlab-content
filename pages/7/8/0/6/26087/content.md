## Idea

The notion of (tensorially) *strong natural transformation* is the compatible notion of [[natural transformation]] on (tensorially) [[strong functors]].

## Properties

### Relation to enriched natural transformations

For [[closed monoidal categories]] $V$ there is a close relation between $V$-strong natural transformations and $V$-[[enriched natural transformations]].

For the moment see at *[enriched monad -- relation to strong monads](enriched+monad#RelationToStrongMonads)* for more
