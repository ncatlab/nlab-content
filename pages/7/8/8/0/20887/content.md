## Idea

In [[knot theory]], a certain [[trace]]-like operation turns [[horizontal chord diagrams]] into [[round chord diagrams]]:

Given a [[horizontal chord diagram]] on $n$ strands and given any choice of [[cyclic permutation]] of $n$ elements, the trace is the [[round chord diagram]] obtained by gluing the ends of the strands according to the cyclic permutation, and retaining the chords in the evident way.
