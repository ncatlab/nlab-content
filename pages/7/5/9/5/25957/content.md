## Idea

Prismatization is a stacky approach to [[prismatic cohomology]] developed independently by Drinfeld in [#Drinfeld20] (#Drinfeld20) and Bhatt-Lurie in [#BhattLurie22](#BhattLurie22) and [#BhattLurie22b](#BhattLurie22b).
