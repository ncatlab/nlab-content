__Jack Segal__ is working in geometric topology and shape theory. With [[Sibe Mardešić]] he developed an ANR-approach to [[shape theory]] and wrote a monograph on shape theory. 

* [webpage](http://www.math.washington.edu/People/fac_individ.php?mathid=segal)
