**Dietmar Salamon** is a German mathematician at ETH, Zurich (earlier at University of Warwick), where he retired as prof. emeritus in 2018. His main directions of research include dynamical systems and symplectic and contact geometry and topology. 

* [homepage](http://www.math.ethz.ch/~salamon/)
