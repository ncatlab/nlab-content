## Definition

A [[site]] is __hypercomplete__ if the [[model structure]] on [[simplicial presheaves]] implementing [[Čech descent]] yields a [[hypercomplete (∞,1)-topos]].

## Examples

* [[Zariski site]], [[Nisnevich site]]

* [[Cartesian site]]

* [[Stein site]]

## Related concepts

* [[Čech descent]], [[hyperdescent]]

* [[hypercomplete (∞,1)-topos]]
