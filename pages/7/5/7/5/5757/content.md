
Philip J. Ehlers was a postgraduate student (1989-1993) at Bangor, working in the team of [[Ronnie Brown]] and [[Tim Porter]], who was Phil's doctoral supervisor.

* [MathGenealogy page](https://www.mathgenealogy.org/id.php?id=64070)
