__Kumar S. Gupta__ is a theoretical physicist from Kolkatta, India. His main interests are [[black hole]]s (especially the study of [[quasinormal mode]]s), field theory on [[noncommutative space]]-times, [[self-adjoint extension]]s in quantum mechanics, [[graphene]]. 

* [web](https://www.saha.ac.in/theory/kumars.gupta)
