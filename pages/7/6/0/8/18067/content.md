## Idea

Given a [[ring spectrum]] $R$ and [[module spectrum]] $E$ over $R$, there is the [[free construction|free]] $R$-[[algebra spectrum]] inuced by $E$, this is the _symmetric algebra_ spectrum $Sym_R E$, in direct analogy to the construction of [[symmetric algebras]] on [[vector spaces]].
