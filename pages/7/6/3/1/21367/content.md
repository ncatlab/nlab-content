## Idea

What came to be called _quark-hadron duality_ is a [[duality in physics|duality]] between [[scattering amplitudes]] of, on the one hand [[quarks]] ([[color charge]] in [[quantum chromodynamics]]) and, on the other hand, [[hadrons]] ([[flavor physics|flavor charge]] in [[quantum hadrodynamics]]), valid in a certain parameter range.
