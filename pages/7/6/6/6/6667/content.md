# Alexandrov spaces
* table of contents

## Idea

In [[differential geometry]], *Alexandrov spaces* are certain [[locally compact topological space|locally compact]] [[complete spaces|complete]] [[length spaces]] which generalize [[Riemannian manifolds]] whose [[sectional curvature]] is bounded below by some [[real number]].
