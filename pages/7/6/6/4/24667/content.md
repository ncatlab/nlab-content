__David Fernández__ is a mathematician whose research interests are [[quantum groups]], [[infinite-dimensional Lie group|infinite-dimensional]] [[Lie algebras]], [[cluster algebras]], [[representation theory]] and its interactions with [[algebraic geometry]] and [[mathematical physics]]. 

* [personal page](https://sites.google.com/view/davidfernandezmaths/home)
