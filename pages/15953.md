## Idea

The **symmetric topos** $M(\mathcal{E})$ of a Grothendieck topos $\mathcal{E}$ is the topos analogue of the [[symmetric algebra]] construction. Its importance stems from the property that for a topos $\mathcal{E}$ the symmetric topos $M(\mathcal{E})$ classifies [[Lawvere distribution|topos distributions]] on $\mathcal{E}$.
