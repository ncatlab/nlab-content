## Idea

In generalization of the notion of [[weak homotopy equivalences]] from plain [[homotopy theory]] to [[equivariant homotopy theory]], a [[map]] of [[G-spaces]] is called an *equivariant weak homotopy equivalence* if it induces [[isomorphisms]] on all [[equivariant homotopy groups]].
