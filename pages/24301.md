## Definition ##

In [[homotopy type theory]], the __axiom of replacement__ states that given a [[type universe]] $\mathcal{U}$, for every [[essentially small type|essentially $\mathcal{U}$-small]] type $A$, every [[locally small type|locally $\mathcal{U}$-small]] type $B$, and every function $f:A \to B$, the [[image]] $\mathrm{im}(f)$ is essentially $\mathcal{U}$-small.
