## Idea

Given a [[function]] which is thought of as assigning to each [[element]] of its [[domain]] the _[[pressure]]_ [[observable|observed]] at that locus, then the [[preimage]] of any element in its [[codomain]] (a value of the function)  is called the _isobar_ for that value.
