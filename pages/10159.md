## Idea

On a [[Kähler manifold]] the [[Kähler form]] $\omega$ can locally be written as 

$$
  \omega = \partial \overline{\partial} K
$$

for some real-valued function $K$, where $\partial$ denotes the [[Dolbeault operator]]. This function $K$ is called the corresponding _Kähler potential_.
