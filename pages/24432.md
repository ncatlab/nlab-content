A **CMon-enriched symmetric monoidal category** is a [[symmetric monoidal category]] such that each [[hom-set]] $\mathcal{C}[A,B]$ is a [[commutative monoid]] (we write $f + g$ for the sum of two morphisms $f,g:A \rightarrow B$ and $0$ for the zero $A \rightarrow B$), such that for every $f,g:A \rightarrow B$, $h,i:C \rightarrow D$ and $j,k:B \rightarrow C$:

* $(f+g)\otimes h = f \otimes h + g \otimes h$
* $f \otimes (h+i) = f \otimes h + f \otimes i$
* $0 \otimes f = f \otimes 0 = 0$
* $(f+g);j = f;j + g;j$
* $f;(j+k) = f;i + f;j$
* $0;f = f;0 = 0$
