__Gersten resolution__ (or Gersten complex) is certain exact sequence of Zariski sheaves important for study of algebraic K-theory. One way of deriving it is from a spectral sequence related to the study of motivic sheaves.

* S. Bloch, _Algebraic cycles and higher K-theory_, Adv. in Math. __61__ (1986), 267–304.
* Thomas Geisser, _Motivic cohomology, K-theory and topological cyclic homology_, chapter II.1 in Handbook of K-theory, [pdf](http://www.math.uiuc.edu/K-theory/handbook/1-193-234.pdf)
* [[D. Quillen]], _Algebraic K-theory, I: Higher K-theories_ (Battelle Memorial Inst., Seattle 1972), Proc. Conf., Lecture Notes in Math. __341__, 1973, pp. 85–147.
* S. O. Gorchinskii, _Adelic resolution for sheaves of K-groups_, Russ. Math. Surv. __62__, p. 184-186 (2007) [pdf](http://iopscience.iop.org/0036-0279/62/1/L07/pdf/RMS_62_1_L07.pdf)
