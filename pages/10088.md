## Idea

The D25 is the space-filling [[D-brane]] in [[open string|open]] [[bosonic string theory]].

The decay of the D25 is exhibited by the [[tachyon]] of the [[perturbative string theory|perturbative]] [[bosonic string]], described by _[[Sen's conjecture]]_.

For the moment see the pointers at [[bosonic string field theory]] in the sections _[achievements](string+field+theory#Achievements)_ and at _[references -- Tachyon dynamics, decaying D-branes and Sen's conjecture](string+field+theory#BosonicStringVacuumAndSenConjecture).
