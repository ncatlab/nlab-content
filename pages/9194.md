__Foliated categories__ (catégories feuilletées) were introduced by [[Jean Benabou]] in an ongoing work which started in 1984 and is yet not published.
They are a weaker structure than a [[fibered category]].

* Jean Bénabou, _Foncteurs cartésiens et catégories feuilletées_ (9 novembre 2012, journée Guitart, Paris) [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p3vcZDyZ4Yo) [slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/17E0epsZWcDTYlwAMZ4PFCLIf4dyigS-WUA0siUtU-wY/edit?hl=fr#slide=id.gce595186_059)
