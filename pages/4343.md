
# $*$-categories
* table of contents
{: toc}

## Idea

The notion of $*$-category combines that of a $\dagger$-[[dagger-category|category]] and of an $\mathbb{C}$-[[algebroid]] (i.e. a $\mathbb{C}$-[[linear category]]).  Thus it might reasonably be called simply a *$\mathbb{C}$-linear $\dagger$-category*.  However, $*$-categories were popular in [[AQFT]] for a while, while the notion of [[dagger-category]] was introduced in the context of [[quantum information theory]], so for historical reasons we have two names.


## Definition

+-- {: .un_defn}
###### Definition
A $*$-category is a [[category]] that is a $\dagger$-[[dagger-category|category]] and a $\mathbb{C}$-[[algebroid]] such that the dagger operation is conjugate linear.
=--

In a $*$-category, the dagger operation $\dagger$ is synonymously denoted $*$.
