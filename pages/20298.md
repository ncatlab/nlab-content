Dana May Latch (1943-2015) was an American algebraic topologist and category theorist who worked in North Carolina State University.

##Selected works

* with [[Rudolf Fritsch]], _Homotopy inverses for nerve_, Bull. Amer. Math. Soc. (N.S.), Volume 1, Number 1 (1979), 258-262.

* with [[Robert Thomason]] and [[W. Stephen Wilson]], _Simplicial sets from categories_, Mathematische Zeitschrift 164(3):195-211, (1979).
