
##Idea
The _Jónsson-Tarski topos_ is 'the topos analogue of the [[Cantor space]]'.[^fine]

[^fine]: Quote from Bunge&Funk (2006, p.183). 

##Definition
The category of [[Jónsson-Tarski algebras]] $\mathcal{J}_2$ is called the **Jónsson-Tarski topos**.

##Properties

* $\mathcal{J}_2=Sh(M_2,J)$ where $M_2$ is the free monoid on two generators $a, b$ and $J$ is the coverage whose only covering family on the unique object $\ast$ of $M_2$ is $\{a:\ast\rightarrow \ast ,b:\ast\rightarrow\ast\}$. So $\mathcal{J}_2$ is in a fact a tops.

* $\mathcal{J}_2$ is an [[étendue]] ([[Peter Freyd]]).
