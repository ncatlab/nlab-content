## Idea

The Mordell conjecture is a statement about the [[finite set|finiteness]] of [[rational points]] on an [[algebraic curve]] of [[genus]] $g \gt 1$.

## Relation to other statements

The Mordell conjecture is implied by the [[abc conjecture]]. (See there.)
