## Idea

The _sign function_ on the [[real numbers]]

## Definition

$$
  \array{
    \mathbb{R} &\overset{sgn}{\longrightarrow}& \mathbb{R}
    \\
    x &\mapsto& \left\{ \array{ 1 &\vert& x \gt 0 \\ 0 &\vert& x = 0 \\ -1 &\vert& x \lt 0 }\right.
  }
$$
