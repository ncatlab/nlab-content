The [[category]] of **species**, $\widehat{\mathbb{P}} = PSh(\mathbb{P})$, is the [[category of presheaves]] on the [[groupoid]] $\mathbb{P}$ of [[finite sets]] and [[bijections]], the [[core]] of [[FinSet]]: 

$$
  \hat \mathbb{P} := PSh(core(FinSet))
  \,.
$$

For more, see [[structure type]].
