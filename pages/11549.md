# Giraud's subcategories and Gabriel-Popescu theorem

The __Gabriel-Popescu theorem__, also quoted as Popesco-Gabriel theorem (cf. the spelling in that order, which is considered wrong when in the order of the authors!) asserts that every [[Grothendieck category]] can be represented as a reflective subcategory of a category ${}_R Mod$ of modules over a unital ring, where the localization functor is exact (one calls such subcategories of module categories Giraud subcategories).

* [[Pierre Gabriel]], [[Nicolae Popescu]], _Caractérisation des catégories abéliennes avec générateurs et limites inductives exactes_, Les Comptes rendus de l'Académie des sciences Paris __258__: 4188–4190 [MR 0166241](http://www.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=0166241)
* wikipedia [Gabriel-Popesco theorem](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gabriel%E2%80%93Popesco_theorem)
* [[Nicolae Popescu]], Abelian categories with applications to rings and modules, London Math. Soc. Monographs 3, Academic Press 1973. xii+467 pp. [MR0340375](http://www.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=0340375)

There are various generalizations, e.g.

* Wendy Lowen, _A generalization of the Gabriel-Popescu theorem_, Journal of Pure and Applied Algebra 190 (1) (2004): 197–211, [doi](http:doi.org/10.1016/j.jpaa.2003.11.016) [MR2043328](http://www.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=2043328)

N. Kuhn had related results in the study of [[Steenrod algebra]], cf. also

* Nicholas J. Kuhn, _Generic representations of the finite general linear groups and the Steenrod algebra. I_, American Journal of Mathematics 116 (2): 327–360 (1994) [doi](http://dx.doi.org/10.2307/2374932) 
* [[Jacob Lurie]], _[A theorem of Gabriel-Kuhn-Popesco](http://www-math.mit.edu/~lurie/917notes/Lecture8.pdf)_, MIT math 917 notes, lecture 8
