## Definition


For $(X,g)$ a [[Lorentzian spacetime]], a [[tangent vector]] $v \in T_x X$ is called

* **timelike** if $g(v,v) \lt 0$;

* **lightlike** if $g(v,v) = 0$;

* **spacelike** if $g(v,v) \gt 0$.
 
A [[curve]] $\gamma : \mathbb{R} \to X$ is called _timelike_  or _lightlike_ or _spacelike_ if all of its tangent vectors $\dot \gamma$ are, respectively.
