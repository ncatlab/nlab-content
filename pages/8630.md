## Idea

The _coherence theorem for [[braided monoidal bicategories]]_, like many [[coherence theorems]], has several forms (or, alternatively, refers to several different theorems):

1. Every diagram of constraint 2-cells in a free [[braided monoidal bicategory]] commutes.

1. Every braided monoidal bicategory is equivalent to a strict braided monoidal bicategory.
