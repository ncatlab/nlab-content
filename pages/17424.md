## Idea

The _real projective space_ $\mathbb{R}P^n$ is the [[projective space]] of the real [[vector space]] $\mathbb{R}^{n+1}$.

Equivalently this is the [[Grassmannian]] $Gr_1(\mathbb{R}^{n+1})$.
