##Idea

**Provability logic** studies the properties of a formal provability predicate  $\Box F$ interpreted as 'F is provable'. This gives rise to [[modal logics]] kindred to **S4** and its variations.

For the best known of these systems, the **Gödel-Loeb logic** of provability $GL$, topological interpretations have been pioneered by H. Simmons and L. Esakia in the 1970s.
