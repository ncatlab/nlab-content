## Definition

A [[category]] is __unital in the sense of Bourn__ if it has a [[zero object]] (is a [[pointed category]]), admits [[finite limits]] and for all objects $X,Y$ the pair of maps $(id_X,0) : X\to X\times Y$, $(0,id_Y): Y\to X\times Y$ is (jointly) [[strong epimorphism|strongly epimorphic]].
