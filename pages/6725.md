## Idea

An observation by [Hořava-Witten](#HoravaWitten) that suggests that quantum [[11-dimensional supergravity]] on an  [[orbifold]] of the form $X_{10} \times S^1/\mathbb{Z}_2$ induces on its boundary [[heterotic string theory]].
