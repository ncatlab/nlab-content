## Statement

The [[Sullivan model]] of [[complex projective space]] $\mathbb{C}P^n$ is

$$
  \mathbb{R}
  [
    f_2, h_{2n+1}
  ]
  \big/
  \left(
  \begin{aligned}
    d\,f_2 & = 0
    \\
    d\,h_{2n+1} & = (f_2)^{n+1}
  \end{aligned}
  \right)
$$

(e.g. [Félix-Halperin-Thomas 00, p. 203](#FelixHalperinThomas00), [Menichi 13, 5.3](#Menichi13))
