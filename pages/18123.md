## Statement

Let $(X,d)$ be a [[metric space]], regarded as a [[topological space]] via its [[metric topology]]. Then the following are equivalent:

1. $(X,d)$ is a [[countably compact topological space]].

1. $(X,d)$ is a [[compact topological space]].
