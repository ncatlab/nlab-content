## Idea

A [[supergeometry|supergeometric]] analog of [[anti-de Sitter spacetime]]. By the discussion at _[supersymmetry -- Classification -- Superconformal and super anti de Sitter symmetry](supersymmetry#ClassificationSuperconformal)_ this includes the following [[coset]] [[superspacetimes]] ([[super Klein geometries]]):

| $\phantom{A}$$d$$\phantom{A}$ | $\phantom{A}$[[super anti de Sitter spacetime]]$\phantom{A}$ |
|-----|---|
| $\phantom{A}$4$\phantom{A}$ | $\;\;\;\;\frac{OSp(8\vert4)}{Spin(3,1) \times SO(7)}\;\;\;\;$ |
| $\phantom{A}$5$\phantom{A}$ | $\;\;\;\;\frac{SU(2,2 \vert 5)}{Spin(4,1)\times SO(5)}\;\;\;\;$ |
| $\phantom{A}$7$\phantom{A}$ | $\;\;\;\;\frac{OSp(6,2 \vert 4)}{Spin(6,1) \times SO(4)}\;\;\;\;$ |

(Here $Osp(-\vert-)$ denotes [[orthosymplectic super Lie groups]].)

Notice that the [[de Sitter spacetime]] (not anti-de Sitter) does not have a standard extension to [[supergeometry]], but see [arXiv:1610.01566](https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.01566).
