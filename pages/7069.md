## Idea

An [[∞-representation]] on objects of [[∞Grpd]]. 

The analog on [[(∞,1)-category theory]] of a [[permutation representation]].

## Properties

### Tannaka duality

See at [[Tannaka duality]] the section [For ∞-permutation representations](http://ncatlab.org/nlab/show
/Tannaka%20duality#ForInfinityPermutations).

### Grothendieck construction

By the [[(∞,1)-Grothendieck construction]] $\infty$-permutation representations

$$
  \mathbf{B}G \to \infty \mathrm{Grpd}
$$

correspond to [[∞-functors]]

$$
  V//G \to \mathbf{B}G
$$

sitting in [[fiber sequences]]

$$
  V \to V//G \to \mathbf{B}G
  \,,
$$

where $V//G$ is the corresponding [[action ∞-groupoid]].
