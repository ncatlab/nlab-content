Co-H-spaces are the [[Eckmann-Hilton duality|Eckmann-Hilton duals]] of [[H-space|H-spaces]]. A co-H-space $(X, \phi)$ is a pointed [[CW-complex]], $X$, together with a map $\phi: X \to X \vee X$, such that $p_i \phi = 1$, where $p_i, i = 1, 2$, are the projections $X \vee X \to X$.
