## Idea

Generally, in a [[category]] $\mathcal{C}$ a _filtered object_ is an [[object]] $X$ equipped with a _filtration_: a sequence of [[monomorphisms]]

$$
  \emptyset \hookrightarrow \cdots \hookrightarrow X_{(2)}  
  \hookrightarrow X_{(1)}
  \hookrightarrow X_{(0)} =: X
  \,.
$$
