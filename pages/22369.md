## Idea

A generalization of [[homotopy groups]].

## Definition

Given a [[pointed space]] $X$ and finitely generated [[abelian group]] $A$ and $n\ge2$,
we set
$$\pi_n(X,A)=[P^n(A),X],$$
where
$P^n(A)$ is the $n$th [[Peterson space]] of $A$
and $[-,-]$ denotes [[pointed homotopy classes]].
