## Idea

A variant of [[algebraic K-theory]] for [[operator algebras]] ([[Banach algebras]] and in particular [[C-star algebras]]) is _operator K-theory_: it generalizes [[topological K-theory]] via the [[Serre-Swan theorem]] from commutative [[C-star algebras]] to general $C^\ast$-algebras.
