## Statement

The [[coset space]] of [[Spin(5)]] by its [[subgroup]] [[SU(2)]] is [[homeomorphism|homeomorphic]] to the [[7-sphere]]:

$$
  Spin(5)/SU(2)
  \;\simeq\;
  S^7
  \,.
$$
