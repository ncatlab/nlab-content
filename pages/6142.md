__Gel'fand-Raikov theorem__ (Гельфанд-Райков) The [[irreducible representation|irreducible]] [[unitary representation]]s of a [[locally compact topological group]] $G$ separates its points. In other words, for any two group elements $g,h\in G$ there exist an irreducible unitary representation $\rho : G\to U(H)$ such that $\rho(g)\neq \rho(h)$.
