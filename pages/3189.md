**Paul Bressler** works mainly in [[geometry]] (in the wide and modern sense of the word) including [[deformation theory]], index theorems, [[algebraic geometry|algebraic]] and [[differential geometry|differential]] geometry, [[algebraic topology]], [[homological algebra]], [[K-theory]]; some of his motivation lies in mathematical [[physics]] (quantization, mirror symmetry, conformal field theory, ...). Bressler's advisor was [[Raoul Bott]].
