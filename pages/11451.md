## Idea

What is called _self-conjugate K-theory_ of spaces $X$ ([Anderson 64](#Anderson64)) is  [[KR-theory]] on [[real spaces]] of the form $X \times S^{0,2}$, where the second factor denotes the [[circle]] equipped with the antipodal $\mathbb{Z}_2$-[[action]] (see at _[[real space]]_ for the notation).

## Applications

In the context of [[type II string theory]] on [[orientifolds]] $KSC$-theory is the [[cohomology theory]] in which the [[RR-fields]] of the $\tilde I$-variant of [[type I superstring theory]] are [[cocycles]] ([Witten 98](Witten98), [DMR 13, section 3.3.](#DMR13))
