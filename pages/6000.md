## Definition

An [[action]]

$$
  \rho : G \times X \to X
$$

of a group $G$ on a set $X$ is _transitive_ 
if for every $x \in X$ the morphisms

$$
  \rho_x : G \to X
$$

is an [[epimorphism]], i.e. for every two points $x,x'$ there exists $g\in G$ such that $x' = g x$.
