## Idea 

In [[quantum electrodynamics]], specifically when viewed in context with [[quantum chromodynamics]]/[[quantum hadrodynamics]], the _lepton current_ refers to the [[current]] of [[leptons]]. If $\psi$ denotes the given [[lepton]] [[spinor field]], this is

$$
  J^\mu 
  \;\coloneqq\;
  \overline{\psi} \!\cdot\! \gamma^\mu \!\cdot\! \psi
  \,.
$$

This is in contrast, for instance, to the _[[hadron current]]_, given by the analogous expression, but with [[quarks]] instead of [[leptons]].
