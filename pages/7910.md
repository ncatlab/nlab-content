## Idea

A [[Lie group]] which [[Lie integration|integrates]] the [[Lie algebra|Lie bracket]] inside a [[Poisson algebra]].

## References

An original account is in 

* [[Bertram Kostant]], _Quantization and unitary representations_, in _Lectures in modern analysis and applications III_. Lecture Notes in Math. 170 (1970), Springer Verlag, 87—208

A textbook account is in section II.4 of 

* [[Jean-Luc Brylinski]], _Loop spaces, characteristic classes and geometric quantization_, Birkhäuser (1993)

See also

* Cornelia Vizman, _Some remarks on the quantomorphism group_ ([[VizmanQuantomorphism.pdf:file]])
