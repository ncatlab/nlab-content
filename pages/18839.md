## Idea

In [[BV-BRST formalism]], the _local antibracket_ of a [[Lagrangian field theory]] is the incarnation [[antibracket]] of [[horizontal differential forms]] on the [[jet bundle]] of the [[field bundle]]. Under [[transgression of variational differential forms]] this yields the ordinary ("global") [[antibracket]] on [[polynomial observables]].

For details see _[[A first idea of quantum field theory]]_ the chapter _[Reduced phase space](A+first+idea+of+quantum+field+theory#ReducedPhaseSpace)_
