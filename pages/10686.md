
There are several theorems by [[Serre]] which deserve to be called "finiteness theorems".

## In stable homotopy theorx

On the [[stable homotopy groups of spheres]]

+-- {: .num_theorem }
###### Theorem

The [[homotopy group]] $\pi_{n+k}(S^k)$ is a [[finite group]] for $k \gt 0$ except when $n = 2m$ and $k = 2m -1$ in which case

$$
  \pi_{4m -1 }(S^{2m}) \simeq \mathbb{Z} \oplus F_m
$$

for $F_m$ a [[finite group]].
