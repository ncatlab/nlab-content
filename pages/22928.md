## Definition

An [[operad]] is a [[monoid]] in the [[monoidal category]]
of [[symmetric sequences]] equipped with the [[substitution product]].

A __module over an operad__ is just a [[right module]] over this [[monoid]].

Right modules are very different from left modules,
the latter are essentially [[algebras over an operad]].
