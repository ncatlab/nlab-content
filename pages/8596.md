Given an associative algebra $A$, with enveloping algebra $A^e = A\otimes A^{op}$, the Takeuchi product is certain product in the category of $A$-rings.
It generalizes a construction of [[M. E. Sweedler]] where $A$ is commutative; Sweedler's article may be itself viewed in a sense a "generalization of the relative Brauer group and the associated theory". 

It is used in the theory of associative [[bialgebroid]]s over noncommutative base.
