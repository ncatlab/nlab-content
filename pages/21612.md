## Idea

Equivariant differential K-theory is an [[equivariant cohomology]]-enhancement of [[differential K-theory]], or conversely a [[differential cohomology]]-enhancement of [[equivariant K-theory]].

This is supposed to coincide with [[orbifold differential K-theory]] on [[global quotient orbifolds]].
