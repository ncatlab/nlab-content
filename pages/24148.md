## Definition ##

Given an [[integral domain]] $R$, the commutative ring of **rational fractions**, **rational expressions**, or **polynomial fractions** $R(z)$ with coefficients in $R$ is the [[field of fractions]] of the [[polynomial ring]] $R[z]$.
