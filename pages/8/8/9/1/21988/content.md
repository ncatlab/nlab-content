
### Finite-dimensional complex orientation and Ravenel's spectra
 {#ReferencesFiniteStageOrientations}

Discussion of [[complex oriented cohomology theory|complex orientation]] (in [[Whitehead generalized cohomology]]) on (only) those [[complex vector bundles]] which are [[pullback bundle|pulled back]] from base spaces of _bounded [[cell complex|cell]]-[[dimension]] ([Hopkins 84, 1.2](#Hopkins84), [Ravenel 86, 6.5.2](#Ravenel86))_ -- or rather, for the most part, of  [[Ravenel's spectrum|Ravenel's]] [[Thom spectra]] $X(n)$ and $T(m)$ ([Ravenel 84, Sec. 3](#Ravenel84))
which [[representable functor|co-represent]] these:
