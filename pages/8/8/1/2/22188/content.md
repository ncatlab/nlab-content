Joost Vercruysse works at the Université Libre de Bruxelles 


His research ares include [[Hopf algebra]]s and [[Hopf algebroid]]s, [[coring]]s and [[coalgebra]]s, [[Hopf-Galois extension|Hopf-Galois]] theory and Galois descent, [[noncommutative geometry]] and quantum groups.
