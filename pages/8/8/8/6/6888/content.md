
Jean-Pierre Marquis is a professor in the D&#233;partement de Philosophie of the Universit&#233; de Montr&#233;al. He has written extensively on the [[philosophy|philosophical]] implications of [[category theory]] and is currently exploring the role of '[[model theory|models]]' in [[homotopy theory]].
