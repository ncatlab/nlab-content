## Idea

The *Stieltjes integral* can refer to two different [[integral|integrals]]: the [[Riemann-Stieltjes integral]] or the [[Lebesgue-Stieltjes integral]].

$$
\big( (x+2) \big)
$$

and

$$
  \int_{c' \in \mathcal{C}} 
  V\big(
    \mathcal{C}(c',c)
    ,\, F(c')
  \big) 
    \;\simeq\; 
  F(c)
  \,.
$$
