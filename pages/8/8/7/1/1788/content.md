For example if $X$ is [[nLab:differentiation|differentiable]], then at each of its points there is a [[nLab:vector space]] of _[[nLab:tangent vectors]]_ called the [[nLab:tangent space]] at that point. The collection of these [[nLab:tangent spaces]] forms a [[nLab:vector bundle]] called the _[[nLab:tangent bundle]]_. The graphics on the right shows one tangent space to the [[nLab:2-sphere]].
