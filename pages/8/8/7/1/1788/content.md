On the *[[Nambu-Poisson M5-brane model]]* --- a [[sigma-model]] for [[M5-branes]] with [[product manifold|product]] [[worldvolume]] $\Sigma^{1,5} \simeq X^{1,2} \times Y^3$ carrying large constant [[self-dual higher gauge field|$H_3$]] [[flux density]] --- obtained via an [[M-theory|M-theoretic]] version the of [[Myers effect]] from the [[BLG model]] for [[coincident brane|coincident]] [[M2-branes]] with [[worldvolume]] $X^{1,2}$, by taking their controlling [[M-brane 3-algebra]] to be the [[Nambu bracket]] on the [[smooth functions]] over the [[3-manifold]] $Y^3$.

The original articles:

* [[Pei-Ming Ho]], Yutaka Matsuo: *M5 from M2* &lbrack;[arXiv:0804.3629](https://arxiv.org/abs/0804.3629)&rbrack;

* [[Paolo Pasti]], Igor Samsonov, [[Dmitri Sorokin]], [[Mario Tonin]]: *BLG-motivated Lagrangian formulation for the chiral two-form gauge field in $D=6$ and M5-branes* &lbrack;[arXiv:0907.4596](https://arxiv.org/abs/0907.4596)&rbrack;
