### Tautological $\mathbb{K}$-line bundle

We discuss the [[tautological line bundle|tautological $\mathbb{K}$-line bundles]] over the [[projective space]] $\mathbb{K}P^1$, for $\mathbb{K} \in \{\mathbb{R}, \mathbb{C}, \mathbb{H}, \mathbb{O}\}$ (the [[real numbers]], [[complex numbers]], [[quaternions]] or [[octonions]], hence one of [[Hurwitz theorem|the four real normed division algebras]]), hence topologically over

* the tautological [[real vector bundle|real line bundle]] over  [[circle]] $\mathbb{R}P^1 \sim S^1$, 

* the tautological [[complex vector bundle|complex line bundle]] over the [[2-sphere]] $\mathbb{C}P^1 \sim S^2$

* the tautological [[quaternionic vector bundle|quaternionic line bundle]] over the [[4-sphere]] $\mathbb{H}P^1 \sim S^4$

* the tautological [[octonionic vector bundle|octonionic line bundle]] over the [[8-sphere]] $\mathbb{O}P^1 \sim S^8$
