  For $H, \langle-\vert-\rangle_H$ a complex [[Hermitian form|Hermitian]] [[inner product space]], its [[direct sum]] with its [[linear dual space]] carries the [[anti-linear map|anti-linear]] [[involution]] induced
(eq:AntiLinearIsomorphismWithDualVectorSpace) by the Hermitian form:

  \mathscr{H}

    H  \oplus H^\ast
