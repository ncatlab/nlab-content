## Definition

A [[monoid]] (or [[semigroup]]) $M$ is a __left Bézout monoid__ if all [[finitely generated]] left [[ideal of a monoid|ideals]] of $M$ are left [[principal ideal of a monoid|principal ideals]] in $M$. Similarily, $M$ is a __right Bézout monoid__ if all [[finitely generated]] right [[ideal of a monoid|ideals]] of $M$ are right [[principal ideal of a monoid|principal ideals]]. Finally, $M$ is a __Bézout monoid__ if it is both a left Bézout monoid and a right Bézout monoid.
