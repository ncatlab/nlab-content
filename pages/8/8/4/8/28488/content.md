## Idea

Given a [[differential graded Lie algebra]] $(V, \mathrm{d}, [-,-], )$, its *derived bracket* ([Kosmann-Schwarzbach 1996](#Kosmann-Schwarzbach1996)) is the [[composition]] 

$$
  \big[
    \mathrm{d}(-)
    ,
    -
  \big]
  \;\colon\;
  V \otimes V \longrightarrow V
$$ 

of the [[Lie bracket]] with the [[differential]] .
