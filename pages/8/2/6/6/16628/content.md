## Idea

The [[quotient]] of the degree-3 homology group of the complex [[projective linear group]] $PSL(2,\mathbb{C})$ (with [[discrete topological space|discrete topology]]) by $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$

## Related concepts

* [[hyperbolic manifold]]

* [[Cheeger-Simons class]], [[complex volume]]

* [[Borel regulator]]
