## Definition

A [[field extension]] $K\subset L$ is a transcendental (field) extension of $K$ if there is $\alpha\in L$ for which there is no nontrivial polynomial equation with coefficients in $K$, which interpreted in $L$ has $\alpha$ among its solutions (in other words, $\alpha$ is transcendental over $K$).
