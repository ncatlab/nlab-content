## Idea

In [[linear algebra]], the [[matrix decomposition|decomposition]] of a [[matrix]] $M$ as the [[matrix product]] $M = Q R$ of 

* an [[orthonormal basis|orthonormal]] [[matrix]]  $Q$

and

* an [[upper triangular matrix]] $R$

is is called *QR decomposition* or *QR factorization*.
