### Idea

A [[physical unit]] of [[energy]]: giga-[[electronvolt]].


A [[proton]] has [[rest mass]] about a GeV:

$$
  m_{proton} \simeq 0.938 GeV
  \,.
$$

The [[Higgs particle]] has [[rest mass]] about $125 GeV$.

The [[Planck mass]] is about $10^{19} GeV$.
