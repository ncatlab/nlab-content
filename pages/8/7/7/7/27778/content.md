## Idea

In the [[S-matrix]] program in [[quantum field theory]], a *Feynman polytope* is a [[polytope]] used to classify the structure of [[ultraviolet divergence|ultraviolet]] and [[infrared divergence|infrared]] [[divergent series|divergences]] of a [[Feynman integral]].
