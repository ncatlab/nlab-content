## Idea

A _semi-algebraic set_ is much like a [[variety]] over the [[real numbers]], a [[subset]] of a [[Cartesian space]] $\mathbb{R}^n$ being the joint [[vanishing locus]] in  of a [[finite set]] of [[polynomials]] [[intersection|intersected]] with [[inequalities]].

A _semi-algebraic manifold_ is much like a corresponding [[scheme]], a space that is locally like a semi-algebraic set.

## Examples

+-- {: .num_example }
###### Example

The [[Fulton-MacPherson compactifications]] $FM_n\left( \mathbb{R}^d\right)$ of ordered [[configuration spaces of points]] are semi-algebraic manifolds ([Lambrechts-Volic 14, Prop. 5.2](#LambrechtsVolic14)).

Moreover, the canonical forgetful functions $FM_{n + k}\left( \mathbb{R}^d\right) \longrightarrow FM_{n}\left( \mathbb{R}^d\right)$ are semi-algebraic [[fiber bundles]] ([Lambrechts-Volic 14, Theorem 5.8](#LambrechtsVolic14)).
