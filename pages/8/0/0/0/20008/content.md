## Idea

$MonCat_{s}$ or $MonCat_\ell$ is the [[2-category]] whose [[objects]] are [[monoidal categories]], [[1-morphisms]] are [[strong monoidal functors]] or [[lax monoidal functors]], respectively, and whose [[2-morphisms]] are [[monoidal natural transformations]].
