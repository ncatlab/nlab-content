## Idea

_HOL_ (short for [[higher-order logic]]) is the main application of the [[proof assistant]] _[[Isabelle]]_. 
It is based on the [[type theory]] of the [[simply typed lambda calculus]].


A remotely related variant is [[HOL Light]].
