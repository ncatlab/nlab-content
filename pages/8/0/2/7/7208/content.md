## Idea

The notion of _Kac-Moody Lie algebra_ is a generalization of that of _[[semisimple Lie algebra]]_ to infinite [[dimension]] of the underlying [[vector space]].

## Definition
 {#Definition}


Recall that the Lie algebra of [[sl(2)|$\mathfrak{sl}_2$]] admits a [[linear basis]] $(h,e,f)$ on which the non-vanishing [[Lie brackets]] are

$$
  [e,f] \;=\; h
  \;\;\;\text{and}\;\;\;
  \begin{array}{rcl}
    [h,e] &=& \phantom{-}2 e
    \\
    [h,f] &=& -2 f
    \mathrlap{\,.}
  \end{array}
$$

A Kac-Moody Lie algebra is generated from a set of $r \in \mathbb{N}$ such $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ generators, $(h_i, e_i, f_i)_{i = 1}^r$, which in addition interact among each other as specified by a prescribed *[[generalized Cartan matrix]]* $A = (A_{i j})_{i,j=1}^r$:

\[
  \label{GeneralizedCartanMatrix}
  \begin{array}{cl}
  \underset{i,j}{\forall} & 
    A_{i j} \,\in\, \mathbb{Z}
  \\
  \underset{i}{\forall} &  
    A_{i i} = 2
  \\
  \underset{i \neq j}{\forall} & 
    A_{i j} \lt 0
  \\
  \underset{i, j}{\forall} & 
     A_{i j} = 0 \;\Leftrightarrow\; A_{j i} = 0
  \\
  \underset{
    { D \in DiagMat_{r \times r}(\mathbb{Z}) }
    \atop
    { S \in SymMat_{r \times r}(\mathbb{Z}) }
  }{\exists} &
    A = D S
  \end{array}
\]

(where in the last line $D$ is a [[diagonal matrix]] and $S$ a [[symmetric matrix]])

via the following *Chevalley relations*:

\[
  \label{ChevalleyRelations}
  \begin{array}{l}
    [h_i, h_j] \,=\, 0
    \\
    [h_i, e_j] \,=\, \phantom{-}A_{i j} e_j
    \\
    [h_i, f_j] \,=\, -A_{i j} f_j
    \\
    [e_i, f_i] \,=\, \delta_{i j} h_j
  \end{array}
\]

and subject to the following *Serre relations* for the [[adjoint action]] $ad(e_i)(-) \,\coloneq\,[e_i,-]$:

\[
  \label{SerreRelations}
  \begin{array}{l}
    ad(e_i)^{1 - A_{ij}}(e_j) \,=\, 0
    \\
    ad(f_i)^{1 - A_{ij}}(e_j) \,=\, 0
    \,.
  \end{array}
\]

\begin{definition}
  A *Kac-Moody algebra* of [[rank of a Lie algebra|rank]] $r \,\in\,\mathbb{N}$ is a [[Lie algebra]] [[isomorphism|isomorphic]] to a [[quotient]] of the [[free Lie algebra]] on generators $(h_i, e_i, f_i)_{i=1}^r$ by the [[Lie ideal]] generated by the Chevalley relations (eq:ChevalleyRelations) and the Serre relations (eq:SerreRelations) for some generalized Cartan matrix (eq:GeneralizedCartanMatrix).
\end{definition}
