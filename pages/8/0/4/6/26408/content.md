## Idea

The **magnitude** of an [[enriched category]] is a canonical measure of its size.

## Examples

- The magnitude of an ordinary category is the [[Euler characteristic]] of its [[nerve]].
- The magnitude of a [[set]] (as a [[discrete category]]) is its [[cardinality]].
