## Statement

The [[coset space]] of [[Spin(9)]] by [[Spin(7)]] is [[homeomorphism|homeomorphic]] to the [[n-sphere|15-sphere]]:

$$
  Spin(9)/Spin(7)
  \;\simeq\;
  S^15
  \,.
$$

This coset realization features in the [[octonionic Hopf fibration]], making manifest its $Spin(9)$-[[equivariance]] ([Ornea-Parton-Piccinni-Vuletescu 12, p. 7](#OrneaPartonPiccinniVuletescu12)).
