David Abraham Blanc is an [[algebraic topology|algebraic topologist]] at the Department of Mathematics, University of Haifa.

He got his PhD degree in 1988 from MIT, advised by [[Daniel M. Kan]].

His interests include (higher order) [[primary homotopy operations|homotopy operations]] and [[Pi-algebras]].

* [webpage](http://math.haifa.ac.il/blanc/).
