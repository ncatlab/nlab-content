## Idea
Every [[monad]] induces an augmented cosimplicial [[endofunctor]]; in an [[additive category]] we can produce a cochain complex out of any such cosimplicial endofunctor when applied to any particular object. Taking the [[cohomology]] of that complex yields **monadic cohomology**, see [here](canonical+resolution#monadiccohomology) at *[[canonical resolution]]*.
