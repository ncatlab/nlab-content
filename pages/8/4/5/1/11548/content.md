__Nicolae Popescu__ (1937-2010) was a Romanian mathematician with focus on [[algebra]], especially study of [[rings]] and [[modules]], [[abelian categories]] and [[localization]] theory. Cf. also the *[[Gabriel-Popescu theorem]]*.
