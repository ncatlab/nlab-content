## Idea

A generalization of the notion of *[[concrete category]]* from [[category theory]] to (n,1)-category theory. 

## Definition 

+-- {: .num_defn}
###### Definition

A __concrete (n,1)-category__ is a [[(n,1)-category]] $C$ equipped with a [[k-surjective functor|(n+1)-surjective functor]]
  
  $$
    U \colon C \to nGrpd
  $$

to the [[large]] [[(n,1)-category]] nGrpd of [[n-groupoids]]. We say a (n,1)-category $C$ is _concretizable_ if and only if it admits a $(n+1)$-surjective functor $U \colon C \to Grpd$.
