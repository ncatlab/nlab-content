__Tarski-Grothendieck set theory__ is an axiomatic set theory whose axioms are all axioms of [[ZFC]] plus the axiom that
every set belongs to some [[Grothendieck universe]]. 

It first appeared in

* [[Alfred Tarski]], _On well-ordered subsets of any set_, Fundamenta Mathematicae 32 (1939) 176--183,[pdf](http://matwbn.icm.edu.pl/ksiazki/fm/fm32/fm32115.pdf)

This framework is standard in the works of Grothendieck and his school.
