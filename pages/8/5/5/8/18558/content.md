##Idea

An **indexed (∞,1)-category** is the [[(∞,1)-category theory|(∞,1)-category theoretic]] analogue of an [[indexed category]]. If $S$ is an (∞,1)-category, then an indexed (∞,1)-category is a functor from $S^{op}$ to [[(∞,1)Cat]].

The [[(∞,1)-Grothendieck construction]] establishes an equivalence between (∞,1)-categories indexed by $S$ and [[Cartesian fibrations]] over $S$.
