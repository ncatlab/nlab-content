Patrick Blackburn is a Directeur de Recherche (Director of Research) for INRIA at LORIA (Nancy).  He works in ([[modal logic|modal]]) [[formal logic]] and its applications in cognitive science, [[computer science]], and ppphilosophy]].  

His website is [here](http://www.loria.fr/~blackbur/).
