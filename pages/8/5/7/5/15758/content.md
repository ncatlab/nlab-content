## Idea

Waldhausen's _A-theory_ ([Waldhausen 1985](#Waldhausen85)) of a [[connected homotopy type|connected]] [[homotopy type]] $X$ is the [[algebraic K-theory]] of the [[suspension spectrum]] $\Sigma^\infty_+ (\Omega X)$ of the [[loop space]] $\Omega X$, hence of the [[∞-group ∞-ring]] $\mathbb{S}[\Omega X]$ of the [[looping]] [[∞-group]] $\Omega X$, hence is the K-theory of the [[parametrized spectra]] over $X$ ([Hess & Shipley 2014](#HessShipley14)).
