
By an *integral form* one many mean:

* in [[differential geometry]]:

  a [[differential form]] whose [[periods]] are [[integers]]; hence a closed differential form that is the image in [[de Rham cohomology]], under the [[de Rham isomorphism]], of a class in [[integral cohomology]].

* in [[supergeometry]]:

  a [[differential form on a supermanifold]] for which there is a notion of [[integration over supermanifolds]].
