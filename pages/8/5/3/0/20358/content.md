## Idea

*Superspheres* are [[supergeometry|supergeometric]] analogs of [[spheres]].

## Coset representations

* The supersphere $S^{2|2}$ is the super [[coset space]] $UOSp(1|2)/U(1)$.

* The supersphere $S^{r-1|2s}$ is the super [[coset space]] $OSp(r|2s)/OSp(r-1|2s)$ of [[orthosymplectic  super Lie groups]] ([GJS 18](#GJS18)).
