## Idea

The concept of [[line n-bundle]] in [[complex analytic geometry]], classified by maps into the $n$-fold [[delooping]] $\mathbf{B}^n \mathbb{G}_m$ of the [[multiplicative group]].

* for $n = 0$ these are [[holomorphic functions]] classified by the [[group of units]];

* for $n= 1$ these are [[holomorphic line bundles]], classified by [[Picard group]], modulated by [[Picard stack]];

* for $n =2$ these are [[holomorphic line 2-bundles]], classified by [[Brauer group]], modulated by [[Brauer stack]]
