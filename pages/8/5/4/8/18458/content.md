## Statement

\begin{proposition}
**Robinson's Joint Consistency Theorem:** If $T_1$ is a consistent $L_1$-theory, and $T_2$ is a consistent $L_2$-theory, then their union $T_1 \cup T_2$ is consistent when their intersection $T_1 \cap T_2$ is complete as an $(L_1 \cap L_2)$-theory.
\end{proposition}

\begin{proof}
Apply the [[Craig interpolation theorem]]. 
\end{proof}

This may be used to demonstrate the [[Beth definability theorem]].
