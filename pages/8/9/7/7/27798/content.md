## Definition

A [[semiring]] is a **Boolean semiring** if:

* the semiring is unitary: there exists an element $1$ such that for all $a$ and $b$, $(a \cdot b) \cdot 1 = a \cdot b$ and $1 \cdot (a \cdot b) = a \cdot b$
