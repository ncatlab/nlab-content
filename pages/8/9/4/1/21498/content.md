
*ItaCa* is a community of Italian researchers in [[category theory]].

* homepage: [progetto-itaca.github.io](https://progetto-itaca.github.io)

The name _ItaCa_ stands for **Ita**lian **Ca**tegory theorists, but it also reminds of the isle that Odysseus strived so hard to come back to.
