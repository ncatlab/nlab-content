## Statement

Let $C$ be a [[stable (∞,1)-category]]. Then the $(\infty,1)$-categories of non-negatively graded [[complexes]] in $C$ is equivalent to the $(\infty,1)$-category of [[simplicial objects in an (infinity,1)-category]] in $C$

$$
  Fun(N(\mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}), C)
  \simeq
  Fun(N(\Delta)^{op}, C)  
  \,.
$$
