
William Schumacher Massey (1920-2017) was an American [[algebraic topology|algebraic topologist]].  He is best known for the development of [[Massey products]] and for contributions to the theory of [[spectral sequences]], via his work on [[exact couples]].  He wrote several influential textbooks.
