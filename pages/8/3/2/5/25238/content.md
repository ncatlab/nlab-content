## Definition 

A [[double category]] $\mathbb{D} = (D_{0}, D_{1})$ is *right-connected* if its identity map $id \colon D_{0} \rightarrow D_{1}$ is right adjoint to its codomain map $cod \colon D_{1} \rightarrow D_{0}$. 

## Examples

\begin{example}
For each category $C$, the double category of squares $\mathbb{S}q(C)$ is right-connected. 
\end{example}
