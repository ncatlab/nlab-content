## Idea

A _13-manifold_ is a [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] 13. (Generally a [[topological manifold]], but it can be specified to a [[PL manifold]] or a [[smooth manifold]].)

## Examples

* [[13-sphere]], which appears in the octonionic [[Arnold-Kuiper-Massey theorem]] $\mathbb{O}P^2/Sp(1)\cong S^{13}$.
* Spinʰ(5)
