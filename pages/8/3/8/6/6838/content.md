## Idea

A **dg-scheme** is a [[scheme]] together with a [[structure sheaf]] of negatively graded [[dg algebras]].  It is historically a precursor to the notion of [[derived scheme]] in the sense of [[Toen]]-[[Vezzosi]] and [[Lurie]].
