## Idea

For $(\mathcal{A}, \ast)$ a [[star-algebra]] and $\mathcal{H}$ a [[Hilbert space]], a _star-representation_ of $(\mathcal{A},\ast)$ on $\mathcal{H}$ is a [[homomorphism]] of star algebras from $\mathcal{A}$ to [[linear operators]] on (a [[dense subspace]] of) $\mathcal{H}$.
