## Idea

_Mochizuki's corollary 3.12_ or _Mochizuki's inequality_ is a conjecture by [[Shinichi Mochizuki]] in [[number theory]] and [[algebraic geometry]] that first appeared in [[Mochizuki's proof of abc|his attemped proof]] of the [[abc conjecture]] from [[inter-universal Teichm端ller theory]]. Mochizuki's proof of Mochizuki's corollary 3.12 from [[inter-universal Teichm端ller theory]] was shown to be incorrect by [[Peter Scholze]] and [[Jakob Stix]]. 

## Statement

Let $E$ be an [[elliptic curve]] over a [[number field]] $F$ with [[initial theta data]] $(\overline{F}/F, E_F, l, \underline{M}, \underline{V}, V_{mod}^{bad}, \underline{\epsilon})$. Then the following inequality holds:

$$
  -\underline{\widehat{deg}}(P_q)\leq\underline{\widehat{deg}}_{lgp}(P_{hull(U_\Theta)})
$$
