
__Shun-ichi Amari__ is, along with N. N. Chentsov, a pioneer of [[information geometry]] and its applications to [[machine learning]].

* Shun-ichi Amari, O. E. Barndorff-Nielsen, R. E. Kass, S. L. Lauritzen, C. R. Rao, _Differential geometry in statistical inference_ ([project Euclid](http://projecteuclid.org/euclid.lnms/1215467056))
* Shun-ichi Amari, _Information geometry and its applications_, Applied Mathematical Sciences 194, Springer 2016
* Shun-ichi Amari, Hiroshi Nagaoka, _Methods of information geometry_, Transactions of mathematical monographs __191__, American Mathematical Society, 2000. 

* Shun-ichi Amari, T. Ozeki, H. Park, _Learning and inference in hierarchical models with singularities_, Syst. Comput. Japan 34:7 (2003) 34--42
