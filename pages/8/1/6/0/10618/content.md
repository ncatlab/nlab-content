## Idea

A _fraction_ (or _quotient_) $\frac{x}{y}$ is the expression for the [[ratio]] of the quantity $x$ over $y$, hence for the [[product]] of a quantity $x$ with the [[multiplication|multiplicative]] [[inverse]] of a quantity $y$.

In $\tfrac{x}{y}$ 

* $x$ is called the _numerator_ and 

* $y$ is called the _denominator_.

If $x = 1$ then one speaks of a *unit fraction*.

If $(x,y)$ are [[coprime integers]] then one speaks of a *reduced fraction* or *irreducible fraction*.
