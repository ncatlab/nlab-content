Sacha Ikonicoff is _maître de conférences_ at the Université de Strasbourg, in France. He is part of the _Algèbre, Représentation, Topologie_ group of the IRMA research unit.

Before that, he was a postdoctoral scholar at the University of Calgary, in Canada, with [[Kristine Bauer]], and at the University of Ottawa, in Canada, with [[Rick Blute]], [[Simon Henry]], and [[Philip Scott]].

His research interests are in algebraic topology, homotopy theory and category theory. He is especially interested in the theory of operads and their divided powers.
