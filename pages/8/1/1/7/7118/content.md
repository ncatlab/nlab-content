## Idea

If $W$ is a finite [[Coxeter group]] then the associated __Solomon's descent algebra__ is certain subalgebra $\Sigma[W]$ of the integral [[group algebra]] $\mathbf{Z}[W]$ discovered/introduced by [Solomon 1976](#Solomon76)
