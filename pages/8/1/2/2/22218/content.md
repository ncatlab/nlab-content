## Properties

### Black ring solutions

While impossible in 4 dimensions, 5-dimensional gravity has [[soliton|solitonic]] solutions with non-[[n-sphere|spherical]] [[horizons]], specifically with horizons [[homeomorphism|homeomorphic]] to a [[torus]]: "[[black rings]]" ([Emparan-Reall 02](#EmparanReall02))
