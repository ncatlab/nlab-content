## Idea

In [[type theory]]: the [[free group]] on a [[set]] as a type. 

## Definitions

Assuming that [[identification types]], [[equivalence types]] and [[dependent product types]] exist in the type theory, the free group type on a set $A$ of [[generators]] is the ([[higher inductive type|higher]]) [[inductive type]] $\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)$ generated by an element and a function from $A$ to the [[equivalence type]] $\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \simeq \mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)$:

Formation rules for the free group type:
$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A)}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \; \mathrm{type}}$$

Introduction rules for the free group type:
$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A)}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{unit}:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)} \qquad \frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A)}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{gen}:A \to (\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \simeq \mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))}$$

Elimination rules for the free group type:
$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{unit}:C(\mathrm{unit}) \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{gen}:\prod_{a:A} \prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)} C(x) \simeq C(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x)) \quad \Gamma \vdash g:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, g):C(g)}$$

Computation rules for the free group type:

* Judgmental computation rules

$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{unit}:C(\mathrm{unit}) \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{gen}:\prod_{a:A} \prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))} C(x) \simeq C(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x))}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, 0) \equiv c_\mathrm{unit}:C(\mathrm{unit})}$$

$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{unit}:C(\mathrm{unit}) \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{gen}:\prod_{a:A} \prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))} C(x) \simeq C(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x)) \quad \Gamma \vdash b:A \quad \Gamma \vdash g:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, \mathrm{gen}(b)(g)) \equiv c_\mathrm{gen}(b)(g)(\mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, g)):C(\mathrm{gen}(b)(g))}$$

* Typal computation rules

$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{unit}:C(\mathrm{unit}) \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{gen}:\prod_{a:A} \prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))} C(x) \simeq C(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x))}{\Gamma \vdash \beta_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^\mathrm{unit}(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}):\mathrm{Id}_{C(\mathrm{unit})}(\mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, 0), c_\mathrm{unit})}$$

$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{unit}:C(\mathrm{unit}) \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{gen}:\prod_{a:A} \prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))} C(x) \simeq C(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x)) \quad \Gamma \vdash b:A \quad \Gamma \vdash g:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))}{\Gamma \vdash \beta_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^\mathrm{gen}(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, b, g):\mathrm{Id}_{C(\mathrm{gen}(b)(g))}(\mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, \mathrm{gen}(b)(g)), c_\mathrm{gen}(b)(g)(\mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}, g)))}$$

Uniqueness rules for the free group type:

* Judgmental uniqueness rules

$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c:\prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)} C(x) \quad \Gamma \vdash g:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c(\mathrm{unit}), \lambda a:A.\lambda x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A).c(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x)), g) \equiv c(g):C(g)}$$

* Typal uniqueness rules

$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c:\prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)} C(x) \quad \Gamma \vdash g:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}{\Gamma \vdash \eta_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}(c, n):\mathrm{Id}_{C(g)}(\mathrm{ind}_{\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)}^C(c(\mathrm{unit}), \lambda a:A.\lambda x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A).c(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x)), g), c(g))}$$

The elimination, typal computation, and typal uniqueness rules for the free group type state that the free group type satisfy the **dependent universal property of the free group**. If the dependent type theory also has [[dependent sum types]] and [[product types]], allowing one to define the [[uniqueness quantifier]], the dependent universal property of the free group could be simplified to the following rule:

$$\frac{\Gamma \vdash A \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash \tau_0:\mathrm{isSet}(A) \quad \Gamma, x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A) \vdash C(x) \; \mathrm{type} \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{unit}:C(\mathrm{unit}) \quad \Gamma \vdash c_\mathrm{gen}:\prod_{a:A} \prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))} C(x) \simeq C(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x))}{\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{up}_\mathbb{Z}^C(c_\mathrm{unit}, c_\mathrm{gen}):\exists!c:\prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A))} C(x).\mathrm{Id}_{C(\mathrm{unit})}(c(\mathrm{unit}), c_\mathrm{unit}) \times \prod_{a:A} \prod_{x:\mathrm{FreeGroup}(A)} \mathrm{Id}_{C(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x))}(c(\mathrm{gen}(a)(x)), c_\mathrm{gen}(a)(c(x)))}$$
