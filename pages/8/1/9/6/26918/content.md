Ken Goodearl is a mathematician working on noncommutative [[ring]] theory, [[operator algebra]]s and [[quantum group]]s.

* [webpage](https://web.math.ucsb.edu/~goodearl)

* K. R. Goodearl, _Notes on real and complex C$^\ast$-algebras_, Shiva Math. Series, No. 5 (1983)
