## Definition

In [[probability theory]], a finite family of [[events]] $E_i$ for $0 \leq i \lt n$ is said to be **mutually exclusive** or **pairwise mutually exclusive** if for all $0 \leq i, j \lt n$ such that $i \neq j$, $E_i \cap E_j = \emptyset$.
