## Idea

*Einstein-Dirac theory* is a [[theory (physics)]] or [[model (in theoretical physics)|model]] in [[physics]], which combines [[general relativity]] ("Einstein theory") with the [[Dirac equation]] ("Dirac theory"). It describes [[gravity]] coupled to [[fermion|fermionic]] [[matter]]. (Additionally coupling [[gravity]] to its [[spin]] is described by [[Einstein-Cartan-Dirac theory]].)
