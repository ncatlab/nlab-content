## Definition

Let $G$ be a [[finite group]].

An _$G$-equivariant chain complex_ is a [[functor]] 

$$
  G Orbits \longrightarrow ChainComplexes
$$

from the [[orbit category]] of $G$ to a [[category of chain complexes]].
