## Idea ##

A morphism similar to how onto relations are defined in [[Rel]]

## Definition ##

A morphism $f \in hom_A(a,b)$ of a [[dagger 2-poset]] $A$ is an **onto morphism** if $1_b \leq f \circ f^\dagger$.
