
__Felix C. Klein__ was a mathematician now famous for his study of [[group theory]] in conjunction with [[geometry]]  ([[Klein geometry]]) as laid out in his _[[Erlanger program]]_ ([Klein 72](#Klein72)).

## Writings

* {#Klein72} [[Felix Klein]], _A comparative review of recent researches in geometry_, 1872 (1892 translation: [arxiv/0807.3161](http://arxiv.org/abs/0807.3161)

  on the _[[Erlangen program]]_

* {#Klein1884} [[Felix Klein]], _Vorlesungen über das Ikosaeder und die Auflösung der Gleichungen vom fünften Grade_, 1884, translated as _Lectures on the Icosahedron and the Resolution of Equations of Degree Five_ by George Morrice 1888, [online version](https://archive.org/details/cu31924059413439)

  on the [[classification of finite rotation groups]] and specifically the [[icosahedron]]
