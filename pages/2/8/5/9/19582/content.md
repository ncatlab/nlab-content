## Idea

[[Supergravity]] theories in dimension greater than 11 in [[Lorentzian signature]] are consider to be unphysical since their reduction to $D = 4$ entails [[supermultiplets]] with [[spin]] greater than 2. However, a twelve dimensional theory with signature $(10,2)$ avoids this difficulty, since there [[fermions]] can be both [[Majorana spinor|Majorana]] and [[Weyl spinor|Weyl]] in $D= 10+2$, with 32 real components.
