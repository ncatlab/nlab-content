## Idea 

_gevolution_ is a [[computer program]] for [[relativistic field theory|relativistic]] simulation of [[gravity]] ([[general relativity]]). It has been used for [[computer experiment]] in [[cosmology]], specifically for discussion of [[cosmic structure formation]] and [[inhomogeneous cosmology]].
