_$SAb$_ is the category of simplicial abelian groups, i.e. of simplicial objects in the category [[Ab]] of abelian groups, i.e. it is the [[functor category]] of functors from the [[opposite category]] $\Delta^{op}$ of the [[simplex category]] $\Delta$ to $Ab$:

$$
  SAb = [\Delta^{op}, Ab]
  \,.
$$
