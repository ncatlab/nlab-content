An **n-polygraph** is a synonym for an $n$-[[computad]]. The term seems to be used more in [[rewriting]] theory, although some work in that area has also used the 'computad' terminology.

The category of [[strict omega-categories]] is [[monadic]] over the category of [[polygraphs]] as shown in:

* [[François Métayer]], _Strict $\omega$-categories are monadic over polygraphs_, Theory and Applications of Categories, Vol. 31, No. 27, 2016, pp. 799-806. &lbrack;[TAC](http://www.tac.mta.ca/tac/volumes/31/27/31-27abs.html)&rbrack;
