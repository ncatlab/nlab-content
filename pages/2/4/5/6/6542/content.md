## Idea

The _Euler class_ is a [[characteristic class]] of the [[special orthogonal group]], hence of [[oriented]] real [[vector bundle]]s. 

The Euler class of the [[tangent bundle]] of a [[manifold]] is its [[Euler characteristic]].
