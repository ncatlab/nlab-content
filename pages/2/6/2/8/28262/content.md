## Idea

A *pin⁻ bordism* is a [[B-bordism]] for the [[tangential structure]] ([[(B,f)-structure]]) being the pin⁻ structure. Its [[bordism homology theory]] and [[cobordism cohomology theory]] are described by the [[Thom spectrum]] MPin⁻.

## Pin⁻ bordism groups

* $\Omega_0^{Pin^-}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2^2$
* $\Omega_1^{Pin^-}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_2^{Pin^-}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_3^{Pin^-}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_4^{Pin^-}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$
* $\Omega_5^{Pin^-}\cong 1$
* $\Omega_6^{Pin^-}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2^2$
* $\Omega_7^{Pin^-}\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$

([Kirby & Taylor 90, Crl. 2](#KirbyTaylor90))
