## Idea

A [[group]] of [[invertible matrix|invertible]] [[matrices]] under [[matrix multiplication]], hence a [[subgroup]] of a [[general linear group]].

Often: _[[matrix Lie group]]_.

For instance: [[orthogonal group]], [[unitary group]].
