## Idea

**Makkai duality** is a kind of [[syntax-semantics duality]], due to [[Michael Makkai]], relating [[pretoposes]] (categories having to do with the [[syntax]] of [[first-order logic]]) and [[ultracategories]] (which are a way of capturing the [[semantics]] of first-order logic).
