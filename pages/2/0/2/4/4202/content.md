_Jean-Pierre Jouanolou_ is working in [[algebraic geometry]], [[commutative algebra]] and [[algebraic K-theory]]; he was a student of [[Alexandre Grothendieck]]. There is a famous [[cover]] of the [[projective space]] by [[quadrics]] called the *[[Jouanolou cover]]*.
