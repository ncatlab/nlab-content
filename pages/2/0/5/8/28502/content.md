## Idea

Mikan is a free and libre interactive [[proof assistant]] for [[cubical type theory]], featuring a computational interpretation of [[univalence axiom|univalence]] among a variety of schemas for [[inductive definition|inductive]] and [[coinductive definition|coinductive definitions]]. Proofs are constructed incrementally in a flexible concrete syntax based on mixfix operators, and organised through an expressive system of parametrised modules. Mikan is maintained on Codeberg as part of the [[1Lab]] project.
