## Idea

The introduction of (a very small number of) impurities into a [[semiconductor|semiconducting]] material to change its ([[electromagnetism|electromagnetic]]) properties is called *doping*. A doped semiconductors is also called an *extrinsic semiconductor* (with an undoped semiconductor then called *intrinsic*).
