### Idea

Dirac cohomology appears in the effort to refine the nilpotent Lie algebra cohomology as it appears in the context of [[representation theory]] (in work of [[Bertram Kostant]] (Lie algebra cohomology and the generalized Borel-Weil theorem, Annals Math. 74 (1961) 329--387), related to [[Borel-Weil-Bott theorem]]).
