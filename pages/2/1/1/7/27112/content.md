S. Paul Smith is a specialist in noncommutative algebra and noncommutative algebraic geometry. He is a professor at the University of Washington at Seattle; he is originally from New Zealand. 

Along with Artin and Zhang, Smith is one of the founders of the main flavour of [[noncommutative projective geometry]]. 

* [homepage](https://sites.math.washington.edu/~smith/personal.html)

Smith's [research page](https://sites.math.washington.edu/~smith/Research/research.html) has a long list of links to the pages of noncommutative geometers.
