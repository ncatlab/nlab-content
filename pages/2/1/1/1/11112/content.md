## Idea

A diagrammatic calculus for [[closed categories]], akin to the [[string diagrams]] for [[monoidal categories]].

## Related concepts

* [[proof net]]

* [[Trimble rewiring]]

## References

The concept was introduced in

* {#KM71} [[Max Kelly]], [[Saunders MacLane]], _Coherence in closed categories_, JPAA 1 (1971), 97-140 ([web](http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0022404971900132)) 

for the [[coherence theorem]] for [[closed categories]].

Reviews include

* [[Dominic Hughes]], _Simple free star-autonomous categories and full coherence_ (2005) ([web](http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/viewdoc/summary?doi=10.1.1.219.4499))

* [[John Baez]], _Classical versus quantum lambda-calculus. From lambda-terms to string diagrams_, [Nov. 2006](http://math.ucr.edu/home/baez/qg-fall2006/index.html#computation) ([pdf](http://math.ucr.edu/home/baez/qg-fall2006/f06week06b.pdf))

* [[Eugenia Cheng]], section 1 of _A relationship between trees and Kelly&#8211;Mac Lane graphs_, Math. Proc. Camb. Phil. Soc. (2006), 141, 33 ([pdf](http://www.sci-prew.inf.ua/v141/1/S0305004105009060.pdf))
