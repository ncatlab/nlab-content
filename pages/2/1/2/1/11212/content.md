## Definition

An _orthomodular lattice_ is an [[orthocomplemented lattice]] in which in addition a weak form of [[modular lattice|modularity]] holds in that

$$
  a \leq c
$$

implies that

$$
  a \vee (a^\perp \wedge c) = c
  \,.
$$

## Examples

The canonical example are the [[Hilbert lattices]] that interpret Birkhoff-vonNeumann [[quantum logic]].
