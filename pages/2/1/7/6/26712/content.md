## Definition

\begin{definition}
A [[connected space]] $X$ is __$m$-finite__ for $m \in \mathbb{N}$ if

* $X$ is [[(n-connected, n-truncated) factorization system|m-truncated]], and

* $\pi_k(X)$ is finite, and for all $k$.

A space is $m$-finite if it's a finite disjoint union of $m$-finite connected spaces;
a space is **$\pi$-finite** if it's $m$-finite for some $m \in \mathbb{N}$.

\end{definition}
