## Definition


\begin{definition}
A [[knot]] $K$ is called *composite* if it is the [[knot sum]] of a [[pair]] of knots $K_1$, $K_2$

$$
  K \,=\, K_1 # K_2
$$

such that neither $K_i$ is the [[unknot]]. 
\end{definition}

If no such $K_i$ exists, hence if $K$ is not composite, then it is also called a *[[prime knot]]*.
