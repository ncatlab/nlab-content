### About Me ###

I am a _f&oslash;rsteamanuensis_ at the [Department of Mathematics](http://www.math.ntnu.no/) at the
_Norwegian University of Science and Technology_, otherwise known as [NTNU](http://www.ntnu.no/).

### My Role on the n-Lab ###

One of my roles here is as the local sysadmin.  Therefore if someone has any technical questions or problems on the n-lab, it's a good idea to ask them somewhere that I'll see them.  The best place is at the [n-Forum](http://www.math.ntnu.no/~stacey/Vanilla/nForum) (which I also maintain) since if I'm online I'm probably keeping an eye on the forum, and also there are other people equally (or better) able to answer technical questions and they tend to keep an eye on the forum as well.

### Other Information ###

My professional homepage is <http://www.math.ntnu.no/~stacey>, which has details of my research, seminars, teaching, and other vaguely work-related matters.
