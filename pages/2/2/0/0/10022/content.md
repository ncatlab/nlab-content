## Idea

The notion of [[entanglement entropy]] for horizons in the context of the [[holographic principle]] and specifically in the [[AdS-CFT correspondence]].

The correct statement of this relation turns out to be that quantum corrections to the [[black hole entropy]] in the presence of matter fields is equal to the entanglement entropy. ([Ryu-Takayanagi 06, p. 13](#RyuTakayanagi06))
