## Idea

The (hypothetical) [[superpartners]] of [[fermions]] are called _sfermions_. 

Generally, for ever fermions species, the corresponding superpartner species name is given by pre-fixing an "s".

For instance "[[squarks]]" is the name for the (hypothetical) superpartners of [[quarks]].
