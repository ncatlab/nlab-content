## Idea ##
Borel sets are the elements of the Borel $\sigma$-algebra of a [[topological space]], they play an important role in [[measure theory]], see [[measurable space]].

## Definition ##
Let $X$ be a [[topological space]], then there is a unique $\sigma$-algebra $\mathcal{B}$ of subsets of $X$ generated by the open subsets of $X$. Elements of $\mathcal{B}$ are called **Borel sets**.
