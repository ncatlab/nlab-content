## Idea 

A $\mathbb{Z}$-[[exterior algebra]]. 

## Definition 

Given an [[abelian group]] $G$, the **exterior ring** $\Lambda(G)$ is the [[quotient ring]] of the [[tensor ring]] $T(G)$ by the [[ideal]] generated by the relations $g \cdot g$ for all $g \in G$.
