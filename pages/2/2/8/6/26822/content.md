## Definition

The *Arens-Fort space* is the set $\mathbb{N}^2$ with all subsets not containing $(0,0)$ being open and all subsets containing $(0,0)$ and containing almost all (all but finitely many) points in almosts all columns being open.

([Joshi 83, Chapter 4, Section 2, Example 10](#Joshi83))
