## Idea

The _Calabi-Penrose fibration_ or _twistor fibration_ is a [[fiber bundle]]-structure on [[complex projective 3-space]] over the [[4-sphere]] with [[2-sphere]] ([[Riemann sphere]]) [[fibers]]:

$$
  \array{
    \mathbb{C}P^1 
      &\longrightarrow&
    \mathbb{C}P^3
    \\
    && \big\downarrow
    \\
    && S^4
  }
$$
