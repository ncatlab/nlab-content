**George Janelidze** is a Georgian-South African category theorist, currently a professor at the University of Capetown. He studied in Tbilisi under Hvedri Inassaridze, and brought to the creation of the strong Georgian school of category theory. 

Janelidze's research includes nonabelian homological algebra, the discovery of categorical Galois theory, semiabelian categories, internal actions and internal crossed modules, categorical approaches to universal algebra etc.
