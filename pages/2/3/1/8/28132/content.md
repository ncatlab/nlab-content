## Idea

In [[theoretical physics]], *unimodular gravity* refers to [[theory (physics)|theories]] of [[gravity]] ([[general relativity]]) where the [[determinant]] of the [[metric tensor]] is [[constrained mechanics|constrained]] to unity.

This constraint restricts the [[diffeomorphism]]-invariance of these theories to *[[volume-preserving diffeomorphisms]]*.
