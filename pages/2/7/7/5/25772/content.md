__Piotr Podleś__ is a Polish mathematical physicist known for early new examples of quantum homogeneous spaces, [[Podleś sphere]]s, from 

* Piotr Podleś, _Symmetries of quantum spaces. Subgroups and quotient spaces of quantum SU(2) and SO(3) groups_, Commun.Math.Phys. 170 (1995) 1-20 [doi](https://doi.org/10.1007/BF02099436) [hep-th/9402069](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9402069) 

Other works include

* [[Piotr Podleś]], [[S. L. Woronowicz]], _Quantum deformation of Lorentz group_, Comm. Math. Phys. __130__(2) (1990) 381--431 [doi](https://doi.org/10.1007/bf02473358)
