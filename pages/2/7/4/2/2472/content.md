
_Rigid analytic geometry_ is an analogue of [[analytic geometry]] over a [[nonarchimedean field]]. The definitions are done by gluing affinoid spaces (see [[affinoid algebra]]) in certain [[Grothendieck topology]]. The idea goes back to [[John Tate]].

See also [[global analytic geometry]].
