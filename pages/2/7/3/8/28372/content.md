## Definition

A (discrete) group $G$ is __fully residually free__ if for any finite subset $S\subset G$ there is a homomorphism of groups $f:G\to F$ into some [[free group]], such that $f|_S$ is injective (images of elements of $S$ under $f$ are pairwise distinct). 

A fully residually free group which is finitely generated is also called a __limit group__.
