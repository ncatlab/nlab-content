## Definition

A ([[discrete group|discrete]]) [[group]] $G$ is calld __fully residually free__ if for each [[finite set|finite]] [[subset]] $S\subset G$ there exists a [[free group]] $F_S$ and a [[homomorphism]] of groups $f \colon G\to F_S$ such that $f|_S$ is [[injective]] (images of elements of $S$ under $f$ are pairwise distinct). 

A fully residually free group which is [[finitely generated group|finitely generated]] is also called a __limit group__.
