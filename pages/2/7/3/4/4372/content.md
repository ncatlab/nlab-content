A [[category]] is __bicartesian__ if it is both cartesian and cocartesian, that is if both it and its [[opposite category|opposite]] may be made into [[cartesian monoidal categories]].

A bicartesian category which is also [[cartesian closed category|cartesian closed]] (but *not* necessarily cocartesian closed) is a __[[bicartesian closed category]]__.
