## Idea

A *dyadic space* &lbrack;[Engelking 1989, p. 231](#Engelking89)&rbrack; is a [[compact topological space]] which is [[homeomorphism|homeomorphic]] to the [[image]] under a [[continuous map]] of a [[Cantor cube]] $\{0,1\}^S$, for $S$ some [[infinite set]].

A *dyadic compactum* &lbrack;[Shapiro 2003](#Shapiro03)&rbrack; is a dyadic space which is also [[Hausdorff space|Hausdorff]] (hence a dyadic [[compact Hausdorff space]] and in this sense a "[[compactum]]"). 


## Examples

* The basic example is the [[Cantor set]] $2^{\aleph_0}$.
