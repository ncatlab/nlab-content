
In a [[monoidal category]] $(C, \otimes, I)$, the **unit object** $I$ is [[generalized the|the]] [[object]] which plays the role of the unit for the [[tensor product]] $\otimes$, in that for any other object $a$ there is an [[isomorphism]] $a \otimes I \simeq a$ and $I \otimes a \simeq a$.
