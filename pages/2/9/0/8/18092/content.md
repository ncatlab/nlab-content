

## Definition

The [[norm]] on the [[real vector space]] $\mathbb{R}^n$ given by

   $$ 
     {\vert \vec x \vert}_p  \coloneqq \root p {\sum_i {|x_i|^p}} 
   $$

making it a _[[normed vector space]]_.
