## Idea

A **weak Kan complex** &lbrack;[Boardman & Vogt (1973)](#BoardmanVogt73)&rbrack; is a [[simplicial set]] for which all _inner_ [[horns]] have a filler.

The notion was later popularized under the name *[[quasi-category]]*. See there for more.
