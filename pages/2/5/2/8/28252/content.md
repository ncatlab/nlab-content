## Idea

A *2-spin structure* is a special [[tangential structure]] in the [[Whitehead tower]] of the [[orthogonal group]], bridging the more important [[fivebrane structure]] and [[ninebrane structure]]. Its name comes from the fact, that it corresponds to a [[spin structure]] under eight-fold [[Bott periodicity]].

## Lift

Let $x_10\in H^10(B2\text{-}Orient,\mathbb{Z}_2)$ be the universal [[characteristic class]] from the [[short exact sequence]] $B2\text{-}Spin\rightarrow B2\text{-}Orient\rightarrow K(\mathbb{Z}_2,10)$. It classifies lifts, meaning that a [[2-orientation]] $f\colon M\rightarrow B2\text{-}Orient$ lifts to a [[2-spin structure]] $f\colon M\rightarrow B2\text{-}Spin$ if and only if:
$$
f^*x_10=0\in H^10(M,\mathbb{Z}_2).
$$

([Sati 14, Def. 2.5](#Sati14))

In this case the [[set]] of all [[2-spin structures]] is a [[torsor]] for $H^9(M,\mathbb{Z}_2)$. ([Sati 14, Prop. 4.1 (ii)](#Sati14))

## Twisted 2-Spin structure

If $i\colon M\hookrightarrow X$ is a [[submanifold]] (seen as a [[brane]] in [[spacetime]]) with a [[2-orientation]] $f\colon M\rightarrow B2\text{-}Orient$, then for a [[singular cohomology]] class $\alpha\in H^10(X,\mathbb{Z}_2)$ with:
$$
f^*x_10
=i^*\alpha
\in H^10(M,\mathbb{Z}_2)
$$
a [[homotopy]] between their classifying maps $M\rightarrow K(\mathbb{Z}_2,10)$ is a *twisted 2-spin structure*. ($\alpha=0$ gives back an ordinary 2-spin structure.)

([Sati 14, Def. 5.2](#Sati14))

([Sati 14](#Sati14)) asks for explicit examples of twisted 2-spin structures.
