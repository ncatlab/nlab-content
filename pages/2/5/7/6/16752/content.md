## Idea

A **Sasakian manifold** is a [[contact manifold]] $(M,\theta)$ equipped with a [[Riemannian manifold|metric]] such that its [[cone (Riemannian geometry)|Riemannian cone]] is a [[Kähler manifold]].

When the Riemannian cone is a [[hyper-Kähler manifold]], then one talks of a **3-Sasakian manifold**.


## Examples
An example is an odd-dimensional [[sphere]] $S^{2n-1} \subset \mathbb{C}^n\setminus\{\mathbf{0}\}$, where the ambient [[complex manifold]] carries its standard K&#228;hler structure.

## Properties

When the Riemannian cone is a [[hyper-Kähler manifold]], then one talks of a **3-Sasakian manifold**.

 Such a manifold is then automatically [[Einstein manifold|Einstein]] and [[spin structure|spin]].
