
A __binary digit__, or __bit__, is either $0$ or $1$.

The [[set]] of binary digits is the __[[boolean domain]]__ $\mathbb{B}$.

As a [[unit]] of [[information]], a __bit__ is the amount of information needed to specify which of the $2$ possibilities a given binary digit is.  In natural units, a bit is $ln 2$.
