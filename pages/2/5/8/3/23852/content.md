## Definition ##

A [[protoring]] $M$ is a **difference protoring** if for all terms $a \in M$, there exists a strictly positive term $b \in M_+$ such that $0 \lt a + b$. 

## Examples ##

* Every [[ordered integral domain]] is a difference protoring.
