
A $*$-monoid is a [[monoid]] with a compatible [[involution]].  The compatibility requirement is this:

$$ (a b)^* = b^* a^* .$$

These generalize to $*$-[[star-ring|rings]], $C^*$-[[C-star-algebra|algebras]], [[dagger categories]], etc.
