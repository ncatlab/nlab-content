## Idea

A **semiring scheme** is like a [[scheme]] but where the base [[commutative ring]] can be any [[commutative semiring]]. Note that we consider the simplest definition of semirings as defined under the name "rig" on the page [[rig]] ie. we have an additive unit 0, a multiplicative unit 1 and $0.x=0$.
