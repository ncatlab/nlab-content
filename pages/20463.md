## Idea

An _S-brane_ is a [[spacelike]] [[brane]], hence a brane [[instanton]].

For instance in [[11-dimensional supergravity]] S2-branes are [[membrane instantons]].

## Properties

### Near horizon geometry

The [[near horizon geometry]] of an S$p$-brane involves [[hyperbolic manifolds]] ([Gutperle-Strominger 02 (5.20)](#GutperleStrominger02), [Chen-Galtsov-Gutperle 02, A.](#ChenGaltsovGutperle02), [Roy 02, p. 3-4](#Roy02)), the [[isometry group]] being $Iso(p+1) \times SO(d-p-2,1)$

## References

The S-brane solutions in [[type II supergravity]] and [[11-dimensional supergravity]] were found in

* {#GutperleStrominger02} Michael Gutperle, [[Andrew Strominger]], _Spacelike Branes_, JHEP 0204:018, 2002  ([arXiv:hep-th/0202210](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0202210))

* {#ChenGaltsovGutperle02} Chiang-Mei Chen, Dmitri V. Gal'tsov, Michael Gutperle, _S-brane Solutions in Supergravity Theories_, Phys. Rev. D66 (2002) 024043 ([arXiv:hep-th/0204071](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0204071))

and by a different method in

* {#KruczenskiMyersPeet02} [[Martin Kruczenski]], [[Robert Myers]], [[Amanda Peet]], _Supergravity S-Branes_, JHEP 0205 (2002) 039 ([arXiv:hep-th/0204144](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0204144))

and the two formulations were shown to be equivalent in 

* {#Roy02} Shibaji Roy, _On supergravity solutions of space-like Dp-branes_, JHEP 0208:025, 2002 ([arXiv:hep-th/0205198](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0205198))
