Some [[Hopf algebra]]s encode relevant combinatorial information and are quite common and useful in [[algebraic combinatorics]] in general. More recently Hopf algebras appeared also in the study of [[renormalization]] of QFT, controlling its combinatorics.  

There is a classical survey of combinatorial Hopf algebras
