## Statement

The **modularity theorem** states that given an [[elliptic curve]] $E$ over the [[rational numbers]] with a [[conductor]] $N_E$, there is a [[cusp form]] $f \in S_2(\Gamma_0(N_E))$ such that $a_1(f) = 1$ and $a_p(f) = a_p(E)$ for all [[primes]] $p$. 

The "semistable" case of the modularity theorem was proven in [Wiles 1995](#Wiles95), [Taylor & Wiles 1995](#TaylorWiles95). The full theorem was proved by [Breuil, Conrad, Diamond & Taylor 2001](#BreuilConradDiamondTaylor2001).
