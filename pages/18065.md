## Definition

Given a $\mathbb{R}$-[[vector space]] $V$, a [[smooth curve]] $\mathcal{C}$ in $V$ could be parameterized by a [[smooth function]] $\overrightarrow{r}:\mathbb{R} \to V$. Then the **acceleration** of $\mathcal{C}$ is given by the [[second derivative]] of $\overrightarrow{r}$: $\overrightarrow{a} \coloneqq \partial_t^2 \overrightarrow{r}$.
