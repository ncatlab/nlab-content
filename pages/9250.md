This entry provides hyperlinks for the text

* [[Kevin Costello]], [[Owen Gwilliam]], 

  _Factorization algebras in perturbative quantum field theory_

  ([Volume 1](http://people.mpim-bonn.mpg.de/gwilliam/vol1may8.pdf), [Volume 2](http://people.mpim-bonn.mpg.de/gwilliam/vol2may8.pdf))

on the description of [[perturbation theory|perturbative]] [[quantum field theory]] via [[factorization algebras of observables]] (see there for more details).

The construction proceeds by a refined (spacetime-local) form of [[deformation quantization]] via [[BV-complexes]]. For [[renormalization]] of [[interaction]] terms it relies on methods in 

* [[Kevin Costello]], _[[Renormalization and Effective Field Theory]]_.

Some of the notions in the text are inspired or influenced by

* [[Alexander Beilinson]], [[Vladimir Drinfeld]], _[[Chiral Algebras]]_

For further related publications see the references at _[[factorization algebra]]_.
