## Definition

The [[Lorentzian manifold]] which is the [[coset space]] of [[Lorentz groups]]

$$
  dS^d \simeq O(d,1)/O(d-1,1)
$$

This models a cosmology [[FRW model]] for positive [[cosmological constant]]/[[dark energy]].

Similarly [[anti de Sitter spacetime]] is

$$
  adS^d \simeq O(d-1,2)/O(d-1,1)
$$

(e.g <a href="https://books.google.de/books?id=vtEkBAAAQBAJ&lpg=PA135&ots=zsId1G2lKU&dq=anti-de%20Sitter%20quotient%20of%20SO(3%2C2)&pg=PA135#v=onepage&q=anti-de%20Sitter%20quotient%20of%20SO(3,2)&f=false">here</a>)

Discussion in the context of [[M-theory on G2-manifolds]] includes

* Beatriz de Carlos, Andre Lukas, Stephen Morris, _Non-perturbative vacua for M-theory on G2 manifolds_, JHEP 0412:018, 2004 ([arxiv:hep-th/0409255](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0409255))

They close by speculating that [[M5-brane]] instantons might yield [[de Sitter spacetime]].
