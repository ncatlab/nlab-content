## Idea

A _real vector bundle_ is a [[vector bundle]] of [[real vector spaces]] [[associated bundle|associated]] to an $O(n)$-[[principal bundle]] for $O(n)$ the [[orthogonal group]].

This is in contrast to [[complex vector bundles]].
