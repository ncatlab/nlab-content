## Definition

A **symmetric 3-group** is the following equivalent [[structure]] 

1. A [[3-group]] whose [[E1-algebra]] structure is equipped with a lift to to an [[E4-algebra]] structure.

1. A [[3-group]] which regarded as an [[∞-group]] is equipped with the 
structure of an [[abelian ∞-group]].

1. A [[3-group]] such that the [[delooping]] $\mathbf{B}G$ is equipped with the structure of a [[braided 4-group]].

1. A [[symmetric monoidal 2-category]] all whose objects are invertible under the tensor product.
