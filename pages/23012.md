## Idea 

In the context of [[higher toposes]], the notion of a topos for [[(1,2)-categories]] or 2-posets.

The $(1,2)$-category of [[posets]] and monotone maps, [[Pos]], is the archetypal $(1,2)$-topos. The poset of [[truth values]] there classifies [[monic]] [[fibrations]] or [[sieves]].
