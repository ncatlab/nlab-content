## Definition

**Cobase change** is another term for [[pushout]] of a morphism:

if
$$
  \array{
    X &\to & U
    \\
    \downarrow && \downarrow
    \\
    Y &\to& Y \sqcup_X U
  }
$$
is a [[pushout]] square in some [[category]] then the [[morphism]] $U \to U \sqcup_X Y$ is the **cobase change** or **cobase extension** of $X \to Y$ along $X \to U$.
