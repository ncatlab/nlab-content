## Idea

The notion of _polyfolds_, due to [[Helmut Hofer]], is a notion of [[generalized smooth spaces]]. The bulk of the theory is a generalized approach to [[Fredholm operator]] [[analysis]] in geometrical situations; which can be considered as an analytic framework designed to systematically resolve the issue of [[transversal map|transversality]]. It gives a unified framework for [[symplectic field theory]], [[Floer homology]], and some constructions in [[contact geometry]] etc.
