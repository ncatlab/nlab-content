

A **super vector space** is an [[object]] in the [[category]] [[SVect]]: as an object it is just a $\mathbb{Z}_2$-[[graded vector space]], but when tensoring it one uses the non-trivial [[symmetric monoidal category|symmetric monoidal structure]] on $\mathbb{Z}_2$-graded algebras.
