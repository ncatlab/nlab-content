**Pregroup grammar** is a mathematical model of natural language grammar introduced by Lambek, it is part of the [[categorial grammar]] tradition. 

## References

* Joachim Lambek, _Type Grammar Revisited_, Logical Aspects of Computational Linguistics (1999) 

* Joachim Lambek, _From Word to Sentence: A Computational Algebraic Approach to Grammar_, Polimetrica (2018) ([pdf](http://www.math.mcgill.ca/barr/lambek/pdffiles/2008lambek.pdf))
