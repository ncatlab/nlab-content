## Statement

+-- {: .num_prop}
###### Proposition

Let $(X,\tau)$ be a [[compact topological space|compact]] [[Hausdorff topological space]] and let $Y \subset X$
be a [[topological subspace]]. Then the following are equivalent:

1. $Y \subset X$ is a [[closed subspace]];

1. $Y$ is a [[compact topological space]].

=--


+-- {: .proof}
###### Proof

The two directions to be proven are 

1. _[[closed subsets of compact spaces are compact]]_

1. _[[compact subspaces of Hausdorff spaces are closed]]_.

See the proofs there.

=--
