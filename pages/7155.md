## Definition

Given indeterminates $t_1,\ldots, t_k$ a _Laurent polynomial_ over a [[field]] or [[ring]] $F$ is a [[polynomial]] in $t_1,\ldots,t_k,t_1^{-1},\ldots,t_k^{-1}$ over $F$.
