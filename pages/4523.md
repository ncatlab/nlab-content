## Definition

A **Lie 3-algebra** or **$3$-Lie algebra** is a 3-[[truncated]] [[L-∞-algebra]]. Under [[Lie integration]] is the [[infinitesimal space|infinitesimal]] approximation to a [[Lie 3-group]].

## Models by 2-crossed modules

A semistrict model for Lie 3-algebras, analogous to that of 3-groupoids that are [[Gray groupoid]]s is given by [[differential 2-crossed module]]s.
