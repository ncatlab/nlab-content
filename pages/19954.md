## Idea

In [[(∞,1)-category theory|(∞,1)-]][[categorification]] of the [[chain rule]] in ordinary [[differential calculus]], in [[Goodwillie calculus]] the **Goodwillie chain rule** is a formula for describing the [[Goodwillie derivatives]] of a composite [[functor]] $F \circ G$ in terms of the [[Goodwillie derivatives]] of $F$ and $G$.
