## Idea

A **spatial topos** is a [[topos]] which is equivalent to the [[category of sheaves]] on (the [[site of opens]] of)  a [[topological space]].

## Examples

* The [[Bohr topos]] of a [[C*-algebra]]
* The category of [[hypergraphs]]
* The [[Sierpinski topos]]
