A [[topological group]] is called **topologically cyclic** or **monothetic** if there exists a [[dense subspace|dense]] [[cyclic group|cyclic]] [[subgroup]].

###Examples

* Any finite cyclic group
* The group of [[p-adic integers]]
* Any torus $(S^1)^n$
