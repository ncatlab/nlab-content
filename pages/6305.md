## Definition

For $(X,g)$ a [[Lorentzian manifold]], a **Cauchy surface** is a [[submanifold]] $\Sigma \hookrightarrow X$ such that every [[timelike]] [[curve]] in $X$ intersects $\Sigma$ precisely in one point.
