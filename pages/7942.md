## Idea

For $(X, \{-,-\})$ a [[Poisson manifold]], a _symplectic leaf_ is a maximal [[submanifold]] $Y \hookrightarrow X$ on which the [[Poisson bracket]] restricts to a [[symplectic manifold]] structure. 

$X$ is [[foliation|foliated]] by its symplectic leaves.

## References

Regular foliations by symplectic leafs have originally been found and studied in 

* F. Bayen, M. Flato, C. Fronsdal, A. Lichnerovicz & D. Sternheimer, _Deformation theory and quantization_, Ann. Phys. I l l (1978) 61-151.

A detailed technical review is in the notes

* Jordan Watts, _An introduction to Poisson manifolds_ (2007) ([pdf](http://www.math.toronto.edu/karshon/grad/2006-07/poisson.pdf))
