## Definition

A [[topological space]] is a _Lindelöf space_ if every [[open cover]] has a [[countable cover|countable]] sub-cover.

## Examples

* Every [[compact topological space]] is Lindelöf.

* Every [[second-countable topological space]] is Lindelöf.

* Every [[sigma-compact topological space]] is Lindelöf.

## Properties

[[regular Lindelöf spaces are paracompact]]
