## Definition

For $X$ a [[scheme]], its **crystalline site** $Cris(X)$ is the [[big site]] of the [[de Rham space]] $X_{dR}$.

## Properties

The [[abelian sheaf cohomology]] over $Cris(X)$ is the [[crystalline cohomology]] of $X$.
