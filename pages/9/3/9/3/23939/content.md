

*[bananaspace](https://www.bananaspace.org/wiki/%E9%A6%96%E9%A1%B5)* (香蕉空间) is a wiki on mathematics in Chinese, somewhat akin in style to (and apparently inspired by) the [[nLab]].

The name "bananaspace" is an inside-joke on a typo for *[[Banach space]]* that occurred in the presentation of a friend of the founders ([here](https://twitter.com/jasonchen0325/status/1526583860191825920)).
