__Marjorie Batchelor__ is an American mathematician known for her work on [[coalgebra]]s ([[measuring coalgebra]]s in particular) and [[supermanifold]]s.

* [wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marjorie_Batchelor)
