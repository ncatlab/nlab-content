## Idea

A _14-manifold_ is a [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] 14. (Generally a [[topological manifold]], but it can be specified to a [[PL manifold]] or a [[smooth manifold]].)

## Examples

* 14-[[sphere]]
* Unique exotic 14-sphere (the [[Kervaire-Milnor group]] is $\Theta_14\cong\mathbb{Z}_2$)
* [[real projective space]] $\mathbb{R}P^14$
* [[complex projective space]] $\mathbb{C}P^7$
