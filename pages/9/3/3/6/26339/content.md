## Idea

A *[[cobordism category]]* $Cob$ typically is canonically a [[dagger-category]] with dagger-[[involution]] given by reversal of the [[orientation]] of [[cobordisms]].

Given a [[symmetric monoidal category|symmetric]] [[monoidal dagger category]] $\mathcal{C}$ as [[coefficients]], a [[1-functor|1-]][[functorial field theory]]

$$
  Cob \longrightarrow \mathcal{C}
$$

is called "unitary" if it is a [[dagger functor]]. 

(This is the "hermitian axiom" of [Atiyah 1989](#Atiyah89).)
