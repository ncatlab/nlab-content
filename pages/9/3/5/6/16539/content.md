## References

Discussion identifying [[twistor spinors]] (hence in particular [[Killing spinors]]) as infinitesimal superisometries is in

* [[Dmitri Alekseevsky]], [[Vicente Cortés]], [[Chandrashekar Devchand]], [[Uwe Semmelmann]], _Killing spinors are Killing vector fields in Riemannian Supergeometry_ ([arXiv:dg-ga/9704002](http://arxiv.org/abs/dg-ga/9704002))

Discussion regarding the conceptualization of Killing spinors in [[super-Cartan geometry]] is in

* {#EgeilehChami13} Michel Egeileh, Fida El Chami, _Some remarks on the geometry of superspace supergravity_, J.Geom.Phys. 62 (2012) 53-60 ([spire](http://inspirehep.net/record/1333125))

Discussion in [[string theory]] includes

* [[Paul Townsend]], _Killing spinors, supersymmetries and rotating intersecting branes_, in _Novelties in string theory_ (G&#246;teborg, 1998), pp. 177&#8211;182. World Sci.
Publishing, River Edge, NJ, 19 ([arXiv:hep-th/9901102](http://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9901102))

and specifically for [[type II supergravity]]:

* [[José Figueroa-O'Farrill]], Emily Hackett-Jones, George Moutsopoulos, _The Killing superalgebra of ten-dimensional supergravity backgrounds_, Class.Quant.Grav.24:3291-3308,2007 ([arXiv:hep-th/0703192](http://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0703192))

and [[11-dimensional supergravity]]

* [[José Figueroa-O'Farrill]], Andrea Santi, _On the algebraic structure of Killing superalgebras_ ([arXiv:1608.05915](http://arxiv.org/abs/1608.05915))

Discussion in the context of [[superconformal group|superconformal symmetry]] is in 

* [[Piet Claus]], [[Renata Kallosh]], _Superisometries of the $adS \times S$ Superspace_, JHEP 9903:014,1999 ([arXiv:hep-th/9812087](http://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9812087))
