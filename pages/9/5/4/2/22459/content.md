## Definition

Given an index [[set]] *I* and a [[matrix]] $A = (A_{i j})_{i,j \in I}$, a *principal submatrix* of $A$ is a matrix of the form $(A_{i j})_{i,j \in I'}$ for $I' \subset I$ some [[subset]].
