## Idea

In [[simplicial type theory]] and [[bicubical type theory]] and related approaches to [[synthetic (infinity,1)-category theory]], the **twisted arrow modality** is a [[modality]] $(-)^\mathrm{tw}$ which is used in defining the [[(infinity,1)-Yoneda embedding]]. Given a simplicial type $A$, the $n$-simplicies in $A^\mathrm{tw}$ are [[equivalence of types|equivalent]] to the $(2n + 1)$-simplicies in $A$.
