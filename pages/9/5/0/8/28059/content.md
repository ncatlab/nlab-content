**Two-dimensional categories** are any 2-dimensional structure that may be viewed as a [[vertical categorification]] of [[categories]]. This includes:

- (strict) [[2-categories]]

- [[bicategories]] (i.e. weak 2-categories)

- [[double categories]] (both strict and weak)

- [[virtual double categories]]
