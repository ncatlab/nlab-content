A school inspired by the traditional Modave school in Belgium takes place on 21-25. June 2010 at [Trpanj](http://hr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trpanj), at Pelje&#353;ac peninsula in southern Croatia. The organizer is [[Jarah Evslin]] and the main topic is the physics of [[black holes]], both theoretical and observational. 

The web page is [here](http://antimodave.jimdo.com).
