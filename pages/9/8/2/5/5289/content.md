## Definition

An **$E_n$-algebra** is an [[algebra over an operad]] over the [[little n-cube operad|little k-cube operad]].

## Examples

### $E_1$-algebras

$E_1$-algebras are often called [[A-∞ algebras]]. See also [[algebra in an (∞,1)-category]].

An $E_1$ algebra in the [[symmetric monoidal (∞,1)-category]] [[Spec]] of [[spectra]] is a [[ring spectrum]]. 

### $E_2$-algebras

The [[homology]] of an $E_2$-algebra in [[chain complex]]es is a [[BV-algebra]].

### $E_\infty$-algebra

See [[E-∞ algebra]].
