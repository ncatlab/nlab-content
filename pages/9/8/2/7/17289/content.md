## Idea

A *chain homotopy equivalence* is a [[homotopy equivalence]] of [[chain complexes]], hence a [[chain map]] that has an [[inverse]] up to [[chain homotopy]].

These are the [[weak equivalences]] in the [[Hurewicz model structure on chain complexes]].
