

### Swampland cobordism conjecture
  {#SwamplandCobordismConjectureReferences}


The _[swampland cobordism conjecture](swampland#SwamplandCobordismConjecture)_ is the hypothesis that consistency of [[string theory vacuum|vacua]] in [[string theory]]/[[M-theory]]/[[F-theory]] (hence their being in the "[[landscape of string theory vacua|landscape]]" instead of the "[[swampland]]") requires (undefined) [[string theory|stringy]]/[[quantum gravity]]-analogs of [[cobordism groups]] to vanish:

* [[Markus Dierigl]], [[Jonathan Heckman]], _On the Swampland Cobordism Conjecture and Non-Abelian Duality Groups_, Physical Review D 2021 \[<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.00013">arXiv:2012.00013</a>\]
