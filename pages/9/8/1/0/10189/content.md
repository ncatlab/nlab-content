## Definition

The [[core]] [[groupoid]] of [[FinSet]], hence the [[category]] of [[finite sets]] and [[bijections]] ([[permutations]]) between them, is sometimes called the _permutation category_ and sometimes denoted $\mathbb{P}$, or the _symmetric groupoid_ (e.g. [BMT 2021](#BMT21)) and denoted $\mathbb{S}$:

Its [[connected components]] are the [[delooping groupoids]] of all the [[symmetric groups]] $Sym(n)$:

$$
  Core(FinSet)
  \;\simeq\;
  \textstyle{
    \underset{n \in \mathbb{N}}
    {\coprod}
  }
  \,
  \mathbf{B} Sym(n)
  \,.
$$
