## Definition

An _EI-category_ is a [[category]] in which every [[endomorphism]] is an [[isomorphism]].

Similarly an _EI $(\infty,1)$-category_ is an [[(∞,1)-category]] in which every [[endomorphism]] is an [[equivalence in an (∞,1)-category]].
