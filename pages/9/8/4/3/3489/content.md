**Tadao Tannaka** ([wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tadao_Tannaka))

His early works were related mainly to Galois theory and number theory. 

His famous classical paper on [[Tannaka duality]] is 

* _&#220;ber den Dualit&#228;tssatz der nichtkommutativen topologischen Gruppen_, T&#244;hoku Math. J. 45 (1939) 1-12.

He also wrote a booklet in Japanese which treats a wider picture of dualities 

* S&#244;tai genri.  [The principle of duality.] Iwanami Shoten, Tokyo, 1951. v+128 pp. 

His biography has been published in Tohoku Math. J. (see project [euclid](http://projecteuclid.org/euclid.tmj/1178228363))
