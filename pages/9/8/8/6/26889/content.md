## Idea

A *von Neumann regular ring* is a [[ring]] $R$ (not necessarily [[commutative ring|commutative]]) such that for every [[element]] $r \in R$ there exists $\tilde r \in R$ satisfying

$$
  r
  \;=\;
  r \, \tilde r \, r
  \,.
$$
