## Idea

... observation &lbrack;[Church & Farb (2013)](#ChuchFarb13)&rbrack; that for various sequences of [[group representations]] which arise in [[topology]] --- notably for  ([[braid representations]] on) [[cohomology groups]] of  [[configuration spaces of points]] and other [[moduli spaces]] --- certain aspects, such as the multiplicities of [[irrep|irreducible]] sub-representations, eventually stabilize. (...)
