## Idea

In [[linear algebra]], the [[matrix decomposition|decomposition]] of a [[matrix]] $M$ as the [[matrix product]] $M = L U$ of 

* a [[lower triangular matrix]]  $L$

and

* an [[upper triangular matrix]] $U$

is is called *LU decomposition* or *LU factorization*.
