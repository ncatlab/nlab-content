##Â Definition ##

Since the [[real numbers]] $\mathbb{R}$ are a [[totally ordered abelian group]], there exists a function $\vert - \vert:\mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ called the **absolute value**, usually defined as 

$$\vert x \vert \coloneqq \max(x, -x)$$
