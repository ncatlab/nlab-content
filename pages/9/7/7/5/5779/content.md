## Idea

__Gel'fand-Fuks__ cohomology is the [[cochain cohomology]] of continuous alternating chains on the topological algebra of smooth [[vector fields]] on [[smooth manifolds]], where the topology on the algebra is given by [[uniform convergence]] of all (higher) [[partial derivative]] on [[compact subsets]] (sometimes called $C^\infty$-topology on that algebra).
