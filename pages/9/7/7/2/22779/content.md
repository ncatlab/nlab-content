When multiplying [[Schur functions]]:

$$s_\lambda\cdot s_\mu= \sum_\nu c^\nu_{\lambda,\mu}s_\nu$$

The coefficients, $c^\nu_{\lambda,\mu}$, are called  the _Littlewood–Richardson coefficients_.

There are various combinatorial models for the [[Littlewood]]–[[Richardson]] coefficients.
