## Idea

### For Lie groups

A [[Riemannian metric]] on a [[Lie group]] $G$ is called 

* _left invariant_ if for each element $g \in G$ the left multiplication [[diffeomorphism]] $g \cdot (-) \;\colon\; G \to G$ is an [[isometry]];

* _right invariant_ if for each element $g \in G$ the right multiplication [[diffeomorphism]] $(-) \cdot g \;\colon\; G \to G$ is an [[isometry]];

* _bi-invariant_ if it is both left- and right-invariant.

## Properties

### Existence on [[Lie groups]]

\begin{prop}
  Every [[compact Lie group]] admits a bi-invariant metric.
\end{prop}

([Milnor 76, Cor. 1.4](#Milnor76), reviewed in [Gallier 18, Prop. 17.6](#Gallier18))
