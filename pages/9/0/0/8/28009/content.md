## Definition

A **2-bicategory** or **bi-2-category** is a [[enriched bicategory|bicategory enriched]] in the [[cartesian monoidal category|cartesian monoidal]] 2-category [[2Cat]] of strict [[2-categories]]. A 2-bicategory is thus a kind of [[semistrict]] [[tricategory]].

By Corollary 8.4 of [GPS 1995](#GPS95), every 2-bicategory is [[equivalence|triequivalent]] (by a 3-functor which is locally a 2-functor) to a [[strict 3-category]].

## Examples

- Categories enriched on two sides (see [KLSS02](#KLSS02)).
- Polynomials in [[Cat]] (see [Weber 2015](#Weber2015)).
