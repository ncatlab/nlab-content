Sam Braunstein is a professor in the department of Computer Science of the University of York.Research interests include: quantum and non-standard computation; algorithm and hardware design; quantum information; quantum communication and foundations. 

* [Webpage](https://www-users.cs.york.ac.uk/~schmuel/)
