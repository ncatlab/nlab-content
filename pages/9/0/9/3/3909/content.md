## Idea

$T = \mathbb{C}^4$

[[flag manifold]]

$$
  F_{d_1, \cdots, d_n}
  \coloneqq
  \left\{
    V_1 \subset \cdots \subset V_n
    |
    V_i \; linear\;subspace\; of\;T\;of\;dimension\; d_n
  \right\}
$$

_twistor [[correspondence]]_

$$
  \array{
    && F_{12}
    \\
    & \swarrow && \searrow
    \\
    F_1 && && F_2
  }
$$

here

* $F_1$ "projective twistor space"

* $F_2$ "compactified complexified [[Minkowski spacetime]]"

* $F_{12}$ twistor [[correspondence]] space

([Ward-Wells 90](#WardWells90))
