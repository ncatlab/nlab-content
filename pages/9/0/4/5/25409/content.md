## Idea

The *knots-quivers correspondence* &lbrack;[KRSS19](#KRSS19)&rbrack; is kind of correspondence between [[knots]] and [[quivers]] conjectured from [[geometric engineering]] via [[intersecting D-brane models]] of both [[knot invariants]] and [[quiver gauge theory]].
