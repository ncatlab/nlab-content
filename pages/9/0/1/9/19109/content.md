## Idea

A type of [[monopole]] solution of [[Yang-Mills theory]] with [[Higgs field]] included.

## Properties

### In string theory

In [[string theory]], in the context of [[intersecting D-brane models]], the 't Hooft-Polyakov monopole has been identified with the [[black brane|black]] "half" [[NS5-brane]] coincident on an [[O8-plane]] ([Hanany-Zaffaroni 99](#HananyZaffaroni99)). See there at _[Intersection of D6s with O8s](intersecting+D-brane+model#IntersectionOfD6WithO8)_.
