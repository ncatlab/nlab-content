**Janez Mr&#269;un** is a mathematician from [Ljubljana](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ljubljana); he was a student of [[Ieke Moerdijk]].

His interests include [[Lie groupoids]], [[Lie algebroids]], Hopf algebroids, [[orbifolds]] and geometry of foliations.
