**Janez Mr&#269;un** is a mathematician from [Ljubljana](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ljubljana); he was a student of [[Ieke Moerdijk]] with whom he wrote the monograph

* *Introduction to foliations and Lie groupoids*, Cambridge Studies in Advanced Mathematics, 91. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 2003. x+173 pp. ISBN: 0-521-83197-0

His interests include [[Lie groupoids]], [[Lie algebroids]], Hopf algebroids, [[orbifolds]] and geometry of foliations.
