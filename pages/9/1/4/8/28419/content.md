## Idea

*Einstein-Dirac-Maxwell theory* is a [[theory (physics)]] or [[model (in theoretical physics)|model]] in [[physics]], which combines [[general relativity]] ("Einstein theory") with the [[Dirac equation]] ("Dirac theory") and [[electrodynamics]] ("Maxwell theory").
