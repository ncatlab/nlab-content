## Idea

In [[perturbative quantum field theory]], _tadpole cancellation_ refers to the condition that, or the mechanism by which, [[tadpoles]] disappear from the [[Feynman perturbation series]].


## Examples

* [[RR-field tadpole cancellation]] in [[type II string theory]]

* [[C-field tadpole cancellation]] in [[M-theory]]

* [[24 branes transverse to K3]] in [[F-theory]] and [[HET-theory]]
