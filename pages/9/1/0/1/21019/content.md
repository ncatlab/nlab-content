## Idea

Under mapping the [[Feynman diagrams]] of [[quantization of 3d Chern-Simons theory|perturbative]] [[Chern-Simons theory]] on a [[3-manifold]] $X^3$ via [['t Hooft double line notation]] to [[open string]] [[worldsheets]], its [[quantum observables]] ([[Wilson loop]] [[Vassiliev knot invariants]]) are equivalent to those of an [[open string|open]] [[topological string theory]] with [[target space]] the [[cotangent bundle]] $T^\ast X^3$ with [[D3-branes]].  ([Witten 92](#Witten92))

For $X^3 = S^3$ the [[3-sphere]], the [[large N limit]] is also [[duality in string theory|dual]], in a [[topological string]]/[[TQFT]]-version of [[AdS/CFT duality]], to a [[closed string|closed]] [[topological string theory]] with [[target space]] the [[blow-up]] of the [[conifold]] ([Gopakumar-Vafa 98](#GopakumarVafa98)).
