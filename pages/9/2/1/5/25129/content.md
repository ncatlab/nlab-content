

### Categorical semantics of $\mathcal{W}$-types
 {#CategoricalSemanticsOfWTypesReferences}

The observation that the [[categorical semantics]] of well-founded [[inductive types]] ([[W-types|$\mathcal{W}$-types]]) is given by [[initial algebra over an endofunctor|initial algebras over]] [[polynomial functor|polynomial]] [[endofunctors]] on the [[type system]]:

* {#Dybjer97} [[Peter Dybjer]], *Representing inductively defined sets by wellorderings in Martin-Löf's type theory*, Theoretical Computer Science **176** 1–2 (1997) 329-335 $[$<a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/S0304-3975(96)00145-4">doi:10.1016/S0304-3975(96)00145-4</a>$]$

* {#MoerdijkPalmgren00} [[Ieke Moerdijk]], [[Erik Palmgren]], *Wellfounded trees in categories*, Annals of Pure and Applied Logic **104** 1-3 (2000) 189-218 $[$<a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/S0168-0072(00)00012-9">doi:10.1016/S0168-0072(00)00012-9</a>$]$

Further discussion:

* [[Michael Abbott]], [[Thorsten Altenkirch]], [[Neil Ghani]], *Containers: Constructing strictly positive types*, Theoretical Computer Science **342** 1 (2005) 3-27 $[$[doi:10.1016/j.tcs.2005.06.002](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.tcs.2005.06.002)$]$
