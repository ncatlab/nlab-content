## Idea

In [[number theory]], the **Thue-Morse sequence** or **fair share sequence** or **parity sequence** is the [[sequence]] of [[booleans]] that is inductively generated by starting with 0 and successively appending the boolean complement of the [[list]] obtained thus far.
