

V&#283;ra Trnkov&#225; (1934 - 2018) was a pure [[category theory|category theorist]]. She was a student of [[Eduard Čech]]. The list of her students includes [[Jiří Adámek]], Jiří Velebil, Libor Barto, Václav Koubek.
