## Ides

In [[operator algebra]], by *spectral flow* one refers to the change of [[spectrum of an operator]] as that operator continuously varies with a parameter.

Concretely, for closed paths (loops) of [[self-adjoint operator|self-adjoint]] [[Fredholm operators]] their spectral flow is essentially the number of [[eigenvalues]] (which are all [[real number|real]], by self-adjointness) that crosses from [[positive number|positive]] to [[negative number|negative]] as one goes around the loop.
