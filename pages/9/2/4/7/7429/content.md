

Michael G. Barratt, Professor of Mathematics from 1974-99 at the Northwestern University, Illinois, and Professor of Pure Mathematics from 1964-74 at the University of Manchester. He was a student of [[Henry Whitehead]].  His research interests were in algebraic topology.


* [Mathematics genealogy page](http://www.genealogy.ams.org/id.php?id=12765) 

* [obituary](https://lists.lehigh.edu/pipermail/algtop-l/2015q4/002298.html) by Bill Richter

His  students included [[Ronnie Brown]] and [[Peter Eccles]].
