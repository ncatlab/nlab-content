## Definition

A _Kleinian group_ is a [[discrete group|discrete]] [[subgroup]] of the [[projective general linear group]] [[PSL(2,C)]] over the [[complex numbers]] $PGL(2,\mathbb{C}) \simeq PSL(2,\mathbb{C})$.
