## Idea

The [[2-category]] $Mnd$ is the free-standing monad ([[walking]] monad).

A [[2-functor]] $Mnd \to K$ is a [[monad]] in the 2-category $K$. These 2-functors form the [2-category of monads](monad#TwoCategoryOfMonads) of $K$.

## Definition

$Mnd$ is the 2-category composed of a single object $\ast$, such that $Hom(\ast, \ast)$ is the [[augmented simplex category]].

It is a one object sub-2-category of [[Adj]], the free-standing adjunction.
