## Overview

Kadomtsev-Petviashvili is a particular nonlinear evolution equation from fluid mechanics which is completely integrable and has soliton solutions. 

KP equation belongs to an infinite hierarchy of completely integrable quadratic partial differential equations for an unknown functions. This is the Kadomtsev-Petviashvili (KP) hierarchy generalizing the [[KdV hierarchy]]. A linear combination of [[Schur function]]s satisfies KP hierarchy iff its coefficients satisfy the Pluecker relations.
