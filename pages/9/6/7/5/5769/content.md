## Definition

An element $a \in A$ in a [[C-star algebra]] is called _normal_ if it commutes with its star-adjoint:

$$
  a \cdot a^* 
  \;=\; a^* \cdot a
  \,.
$$ 


## Properties

The [[spectral theorem]] asserts, roughly, that (bounded) normal operators can be diagonalized.
