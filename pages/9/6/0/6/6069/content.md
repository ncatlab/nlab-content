A category $C$ is __homological__ if it is [[pointed category|pointed]], [[regular category|regular]] and [[protomodular category|protomodular]].

Many of the standard results of classical homological algebra in abelian categories extend to homological categories: the [[five lemma]], the [[nine lemma]], the [[snake lemma]], long exact sequence in homology, the Noether isomorphism theorem. There is also a version of the [[Jordan-HÃ¶lder theorem]].  

A homological category which is [[Barr-exact]] and has finite [[coproducts]] is [[semiabelian category|semiabelian]].
