## Idea

__$A_\infty$-module__ $M$ over an $A_\infty$-algebra $A$ is an object with an $\infty$-[[infinity-representation|representation]] of $A$, modelled in the same formalism as the $A_\infty$-algebras are (usually in $k$-linear context, most often in (âˆž,1)-categories of chain complexes). In particular, one considers $A_\infty$-modules over an associative algebra or a dg-algebra viewed as a special case of an $A_\infty$-algebra.
