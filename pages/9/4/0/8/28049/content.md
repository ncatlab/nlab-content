## Idea 

Being *perfectly normal* is one of the [[separation axioms]] ($T_6$) on [[topological spaces]].


## Definition

A [[Hausdorff space]] $X$ is *perfectly normal* iff for every [[pair]] of disjoint [[closed subsets]] $A_0, A_1 \subset X$ there exists a [[continuous function]] $\phi \,\colon\, X \longrightarrow I$ such that $A_0 = \phi^{-1}(0)$ and $A_1 = \phi^{-1}(1)$.
