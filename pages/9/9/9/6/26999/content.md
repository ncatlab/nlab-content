## Idea

A family of [[axioms]] in [[constructive mathematics]] which are provable from the [[principle of excluded middle]]. They imply their respective versions of the [[fan theorem]]. 

> This page should contain the classical proof of full bar induction, as well as Brouwer’s reasoning for why bar induction should hold.
