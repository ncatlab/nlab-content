## Idea

The concept of a _bimodule category_ is a [[categorification]] of that of [[bimodule]], hence the two-sided version of the notion of a *[[module category]]*.

Where a [[bimodule]] is an [[abelian group]] [[action|acted]] on by two [[rings]], so a bimodule category is a suitable [[category]] suitably acted on by two [[monoidal categories]], hence a 2-sided _[[module category]]_.
