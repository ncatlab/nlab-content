
__Anthony Joseph__ is an algebraist at the Weizmann Institute of Science. His interests include [[universal enveloping algebras]], [[quantum groups]] and [[invariant theory]].

* personal webpages: [newer](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/math/joseph/home), [older](https://www.wisdom.weizmann.ac.il/~/joseph)
