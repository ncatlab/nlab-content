__Anthony Joseph__ is an algebraist at Weizmann Institute of Science. His interests include [[universal enveloping algebra]]s, [[quantum group]]s and [[invariant theory]].

* personal webpages: [newer](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/math/joseph/home), [older](https://www.wisdom.weizmann.ac.il/~/joseph)
* profile [webpage](https://www.wisdom.weizmann.ac.il/profile/scientists/joseph-profile.html)
