\section{Idea}

In [[quantum complexity theory]], "PostBQP" stands for *[[postselected]] bounded-error quantum polynomial time*, denoting a fundamental [[complexity class]] of [[quantum computation]]. 

\section{Properties}

PostBQP is closed under [[unions]] and [[intersections]] of problems in PostBQP. PostBQP is equivalent to the complexity class [[PP]], and includes [[BQP]] and [[QMA]] as subclasses.
