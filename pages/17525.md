## Definition

+-- {: .num_defn #CoreOfARing}
###### Definition

For $R$ a [[ring]], its _[[core of a ring|core]]_ $c R$ is the [[equalizer]] in

$$
  c R 
    \longrightarrow 
  R 
    \stackrel{\longrightarrow}{\longrightarrow}
  R \otimes R
  \,.
$$

=--

([Bousfield 79, 6.4](#Bousfield79))
