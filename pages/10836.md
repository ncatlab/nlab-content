## Idea

Over a [[ring]] of [[characteristic]] a [[prime number]] $p$ it is true that

$$
  (a+b)^p = a^p + b^p
  \,,
$$

because all the non-trivial [[binomial theorem|binomial coefficients]] are multiples of $p$.
