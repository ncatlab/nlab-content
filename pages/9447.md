## Idea

The notion of _Haefliger structure_ is a slight generalization of that of _[[foliation]]_. A _Haefliger structure_ on a [[manifold]] is a [[cocycle]] with coefficients in the [[Haefliger groupoid]], a _[[Haefliger cocycle]]_.
