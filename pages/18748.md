## Definition

For $V$ an [[inner product space]] and $S(V)$ its [[unit sphere]], the _direction_ of an element $v \in V$ with [[norm]] ${\vert v\vert}$

$$
  v/{\vert v\vert} \;\in \; S(V)
  \,.
$$
