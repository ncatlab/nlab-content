## Idea

By $Spectrum$ or $Spectra$ one may denote the [[stable (∞,1)-category]] of [[spectrum|spectra]].  It is also denoted $Sp$, or sometimes $Spec$ although that [[affine scheme|can be confusing]].

Its [[homotopy category of an (infinity,1)-category|homotopy category]] is the classical [[stable homotopy category]].

For more see the entry _[[stable (∞,1)-category of spectra]]_.

### Other abstract characterizations

$Spectra$ is equivalently

* the free stable [[locally presentable (∞,1)-category]] on one [[compact object|compact]] generator, namely the [[sphere spectrum]].

* the [[stable (infinity,1)-category]] of [[quasicoherent infinity-stacks]] on [[Spec(S)]].
