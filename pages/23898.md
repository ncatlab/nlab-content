## Definition ##

An extensional category $C$ is a [[concrete category]] such that for morphisms $f \colon Hom(A,B)$ and $g \colon Hom(A,B)$, if $f(x) = g(x)$ for all [[elements]] $x \colon El(A)$, then $f = g$.
