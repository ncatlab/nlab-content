A **phantom category** is a [[localization of an (infinity,1)-category|localization]] $C \subset D(X)$ of the [[stable (infinity,1)-category]] of [[perfect complexes]] on some [[scheme]] $X$, with trivial [[algebraic K-theory]] $K(C)$.
