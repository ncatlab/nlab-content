## Idea

The [[thick subcategory]] of the [[triangulated category]] $KK$ of [[KK-theory]] generated by the [[tensor unit]] is called the _bootstrap category_. 

## Definition

See ([Blackadar, def. 22.3.4](#Blackadar)).

## Examples of objects in the bootstrap category

+-- {: .num_prop}
###### Proposition

The [[groupoid convolution algebra]] of an [[amenable topological groupoid]] is in the bootstrap category. 

=--

([Tu 99, prop. 10.7](#Tu99)), recalled as ([Uuye 11, example 3.6](#Uuye11)).


## Properties

### Künneth theorem

In the bootstrap category a [[Künneth theorem]] for [[operator K-theory]] is true. ([Rosenberg-Schochet 87](RosenbergSchochet87)).

### Embedding into $KU$-module spectra

On the bootstrap category the operator K-theory spectrum functor $KK \to Ho(KU Mod)$ is 

* a [[fully faithful functor]] ([DEKM 11, section 3](DEKM11)). 

* a [[strong monoidal functor]] ([DEKM 11, prop. 4.2](#DEKM11))

See at _[[KK-theory]]_ the section _[Triangulated and spectrum-enriched structure](KK-theory#TriangulatedAndSpectrumEnrichedStructure)_ for more details.
