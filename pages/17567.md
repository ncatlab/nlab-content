## Definition

A [[smooth function]] $f \;\colon\; X_1 \to X_2$ between two [[Riemannian manifold]] $(X_1,g_1)$, $(X_2,g_2)$ is called _conformal_ if it preserves the [[conformal geometry]] induced by the [[Riemannian metrics]] $g_1$ and $g_2$, hence if there is a [[smooth function]] $r X_1 \to \mathbb{R}$ such that

$$
  f^\ast g_2 = r g_1
  \,.
$$

If $X_1 = X_2$ and $f$ is a [[diffeomorphism]], then this is also called a _[[conformal transformation]]_.
