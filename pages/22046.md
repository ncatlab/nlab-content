## Idea

_Complex conjugation_ is the operation on [[complex numbers]] which reverses the sign of the [[imaginary part]], hence the [[function]]

$$
  \array{
    \mathbb{C} 
    &
      \overset{ 
        \;\;\;
        (-)^\ast 
        \;\;\;
      }{
        \longrightarrow
      }
    &
    \mathbb{C}
    \\
    a + \mathrm{i} b &\mapsto&  a - \mathrm{i} b
  }
  \phantom{AAAAAA}
  for\;\; a,b \in \mathbb{R}
  \,.
$$

More generally, the [[anti-involution]] on any [[star-algebra]] may be referred to as _conjugation_. For instance one speaks of _quaternionc conjugation_ for the analogous operation on [[quaternions]]:

$$
  \array{
    \mathbb{H} 
    &
      \overset{ 
        \;\;\;
        (-)^\ast 
        \;\;\;
      }{
        \longrightarrow
      }
    &
    \mathbb{H}
    \\
    a 
      + \mathrm{i} b 
      + \mathrm{j} c 
      + \mathrm{k} d 
    &\mapsto&  
    a
      - \mathrm{i} b 
      - \mathrm{j} c 
      - \mathrm{k} d 
  }
  \phantom{AAAAAA}
  for\;\; a, b, c, d \in \mathbb{R}
  \,.
$$
