**Fabien Morel** is a French [[algebraic geometry|algebraic geometer]] (currently holding professorship at LMU Munich). His most important work so far is the construction of $\mathbb{A}^1$-[[A1-homotopy theory|homotopy theory]] (two variants, one jointly with [[Vladimir Voevodsky]]) and of [[algebraic cobordism]] with [[Marc Levine]].
