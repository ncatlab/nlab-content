## Idea

Every partition of an integer $n$ induces a [[subgroup]] of $\mathfrak{S}_{n}$.

## Definition

Let $n \ge 1$. Given a [[partition]] $\{\lambda_{1},...,\lambda_{k}\}$ of the integer $n$, we have the Young subgroup:

$\mathfrak{S}_{\lambda_{1}} \times ... \times \mathfrak{S}_{\lambda_{k}} \le \mathfrak{S}_{n}$

associated to this partition.
