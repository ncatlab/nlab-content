## Idea

The Radon monad is a [[measure monad]] on the [[category]] of [[compact Hausdorff spaces]]. 
Its functor assigns to each space $X$ the space of [[Radon measure|Radon]] [[probability measure|probability]] or subprobability [[measures]] (equivalently, normalized or subnormalized [[continuous valuations]]), equipped with the [[weak topology]].

There is also an [[order theory|ordered]] version of the Radon monad on the category of [[compact ordered spaces]] (see [below](#the_ordered_case)).
