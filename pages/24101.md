## Definition ##
In measure theory, a __content__ on a [[distributive lattice]] $(L, \leq, \bot, \vee, \top, \wedge)$ is a [[valuation (measure theory)|valuation]] $\mu:L \to [0, \infty]$ such that pairs of elements are mutually disjoint and the valuation is finitely additive.
$$\forall a\in L. \forall b \in L. a \wedge b = \bot$$

$$\forall a\in L. \forall b \in L. \mu(\bot) = 0$$
$$\forall a\in L. \forall b \in L. \mu(a \vee b) = \mu(a) + \mu(b)$$
