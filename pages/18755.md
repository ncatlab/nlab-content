## Definition

For a [[particle]]/[[field (physics)|field]] of [[mass]] $m$, its _Compton wavelength_ is the [[length]]

$$
  \frac{\hbar}{m c}
$$

where $c$ denotes the [[speed of light]] and $\hbar$ denotes [[Planck's constant]].
