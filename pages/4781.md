## Definition

Let $\pi : P \to X$ be a [[bundle]] in the [[category]] [[Diff]] of [[smooth manifold]]s. A [[vector field]] $v \in \Gamma(P)$ is _vertical_ with respect to this bundle if it is in the [[kernel]] of the [[differential]] $d \pi : T P \to T X$.
