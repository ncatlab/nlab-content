## Idea

The _heat equation_ is a [[partial differential equation]]:

$$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t}(t,x)=\frac{\alpha}{2}\Delta u(t,x)$$

where $\Delta$ is a [[Laplace operator]].

Its [[fundamental solution]] is called the _[[heat kernel]]_.
