## Idea

The _slope_ of a line in the [[Cartesian space|Cartesian]] [[plane]] $\mathbb{R}^2$ given, in the canonical [[coordinates]] $(x,y \;\colon\; \mathbb{R}^2 \to \mathbb{R})$ by the [[equation]]

$$
  y = y_0 + s \cdot x 
$$

is the factor $s$.
