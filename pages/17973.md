## Idea

In [[string phenomenology]] for [[type IIA string theory]] quasi-realistic [[model (physics)|models]] are obtained for [[KK-compactifications]] with configurations of [[D6-branes]] which fill all of 4d spacetime and intersect in a certain way in the 6-dimensional fiber space. The [[Chan-Paton gauge field]] on the [[D6-branes]] yields the [[gauge fields]] in 4d, and the precise intersection pattern determines the effective [[fundamental particle]] content in 4d. 

See also at [[string phenomenology]] the section _[Models in type II with intersecting branes](string+phenomenology#ModelsInTypeIIWithIntersectingBranes)_.
