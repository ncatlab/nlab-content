## Idea

By a _knot complement_ one means the [[complement]] of a [[knot]]. More precisely:  The [[complement]] in the [[3-sphere]] $S^{3}$ or in [[Euclidean space]]/[[Cartesian space]] $\mathbb{R}^{3}$ of a [[tubular neighbourhood]] of a [[knot]].

## Properties

### Homology

The [[ordinary homology]] of the [[complement]] of a [[knot]] $K \colon S^1 \hookrightarrow S^3$ in the [[3-sphere]] is concentrated in degrees 0 and 1:

$$
  H_n\big( S^3\setminus K, \mathbb{Z}\big)
  \;=\;
  \left\{
  \array{
    \mathbb{Z} &\vert& n \in \{0,1\}
    \\
    0 &\vert& n \geq 2
  }
  \right.
$$

(see for instance [Greene 13, Prop. 3.1](#Greene13), [Haney 16, Cor. 1.6](#Haney16))
