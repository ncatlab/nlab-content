#Idea#

The notion of [[limit]] generalizes to [[quasi-category|quasi-categories]] straightforwardly using the generalization of [[over-category]] to [[over-category in quasi-categories|over-categories in quasi-categories]].


#Definition#

For $K$ and $C$ two [[quasi-category|quasi-categories]] and $F : K \to C$ a morphism of [[quasi-category|quasi-categories]], the **limit** over $F$ is, if it exists, the [[terminal object in a quasi-category|terminal object]] in the [[over-category in quasi-categories|over-categories in quasi-categories]] $C_{/F}$:

$$
  lim F := TerminalObj(C_{/F})
  \,.
$$
