## Idea

A _proton_ is a [[bound state]] of one [[up quarks]] and two [[down quark]], hence a [[baryon]]. 

The [[bound states]] of [[protons]] and [[neutrons]] (jointly: [[nucleons]]) in turn are the [[nucleus (physics)|nuclei]], the cores of [[atoms]].
