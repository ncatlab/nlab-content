## Idea

The _reflection principle_ is principle in [[set theory]] that states that it is possible to find [[sets]] that resemble the [[class]] of all sets that are constructed in some ways.

In [[type theory]] this principle is embodied by a [[type of types]] ([Martin-Löf 74, p. 6](#MartinLoef74)).
