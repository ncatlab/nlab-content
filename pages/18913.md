## Definition

The [[complex numbers]] $+i$ and $-i$, i.e. the two [[solutions]] $x$ to the [[equation]] $x^2 = -1$, are the _imaginary units_.

A [[complex number]] of the form $y i$ with $y \in \mathbb{R}$ a [[real number]] is called a _[[purely imaginary number]]_.
