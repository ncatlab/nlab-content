Let $(X,\tau)$ be a [[normal topological space]] and let $A, B \subset X$ be two [[closed subsets]] which are disjoint

$$
  A \cap B = \emptyset
  \,.
$$

Set

$$
  C_1 \coloneqq A
  \phantom{AAA}
  U_0 \coloneqq X \backslash B
  \,.
$$
