Georges Maltsiniotis is a Greek mathematician, who works at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu, Université Paris 7 Denis Diderot.

He has worked for many years now editing,  extending and completing the work of [[Grothendieck]] on [[Pursuing Stacks]], and areas such as [[Les Dérivateurs]] and [[homotopy theory of Grothendieck|Grothendieck's homotopy theory]]. Most materials available at his 

* [homepage](http://people.math.jussieu.fr/~maltsin/)

* _La K-théorie d'un dérivateur triangulé_ (suivi d'un appendice par B. Keller) dans "Categories in Algebra, Geometry and Mathematical Physics", Contemp. Math. __431__, pp. 341-368, (2007); Version preprint: [ps](http://people.math.jussieu.fr/~maltsin/ps/streetfest.ps) (2005)

* _Catégories triangulées supérieures_, Pré-preprint [ps](http://people.math.jussieu.fr/~maltsin/ps/triansup.ps) (2005)

* _Grothendieck $\infty$-groupoids, and still another definition of $\infty$-categories_, [arXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/1009.2331) -- see [[Grothendieck ∞-groupoid]]
