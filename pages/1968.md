Georges Maltsiniotis is a French mathematician, who works at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu, Université Paris 7 Denis Diderot.

He has worked for many years now editing,  extending and completing the work of [[Grothendieck]] on [[Pursuing Stacks]], and areas such as [[Les Dérivateurs]] and [[homotopy theory of Grothendieck|Grothendieck's homotopy theory]].
