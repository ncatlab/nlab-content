## Defintion

For $\mathcal{C}$ an [[(∞,1)-category]], its **arrow $(\infty,1)$-category** is the [[(∞,1)-category of (∞,1)-functors]] 

$$
  \mathcal{C}^{\Delta^1}
  \coloneqq
  Func_\infty(\Delta^1, \mathcal{C})
  \,.
$$
