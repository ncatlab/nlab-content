## Idea

For $(A, \ast)$ a [[star-algebra]] and $H$ a [[Hilbert space]], a _star-representation_ of $(A,\ast)$ on $H$ is a [[homomorphism]] of star algebras from $A$ to [[linear operators]] on $H$.
