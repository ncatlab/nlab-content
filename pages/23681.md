## Idea ##

An elementarily topical dagger 2-poset is a dagger 2-poset whose [[category of maps]] is an [[elementary topos]].

## Definition ##

An **elementarily topical dagger 2-poset** $C$ is a [[unital dagger 2-poset|unital]] [[tabular dagger 2-poset|tabular]] [[power dagger 2-poset|power]] [[dagger 2-poset]].

## Examples ##

The dagger 2-poset [[Rel]] of [[sets]] and [[relations]] is an elementarily topical dagger 2-poset.
