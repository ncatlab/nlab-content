__Alain Verschoren__ was an [[algebraist]] at Antwerp. With [[Fred van Oystaeyen]] he coauthored the first monograph on noncommutative algebraic geometry

_Non-commutative algebraic geometry_ (Springer LNM 887, 1981).

* [webpage](http://www.ua.ac.be/main.aspx?c=alain.verschoren)
