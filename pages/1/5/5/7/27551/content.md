## Idea

(...)

$$
  k \in 2\mathbb{N} + 1
  ,\,\;
  a \in \mathbb{Z}
  \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;
  \vdash
  \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;
  (a \vert k) \,\in\, \{-1, 0, + 1\}
$$

## Properties

$$
  (a \vert k) 
    \,=\,
  \left\{
  \begin{array}{ccc}
    0 &if& gcd(a,k) \neq 1
    \\
    \pm 1 &if& gcd(a,k) = 1
  \end{array}
  \right.
$$
