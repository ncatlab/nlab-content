## Idea

The *2-sphere* $S^2$ is the ordinary [[sphere]] of [[dimension]] $2$, hence the [[n-sphere|$n$-sphere]] for $n = 2$. 

Equipped with its canonical [[complex structure]] this is known as the *[[Riemann sphere]]*.
