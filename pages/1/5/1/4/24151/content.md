## Definition 

Let $R$ be a [[unique factorization domain]] with [[decidable equality]]. The **content** of a [[polynomial]] $p \in R[x]$ is the [[greatest common divisor]] of the coefficients 
$$\gcd_{i \leq d(p)} p_i$$ 
where $d(p)$ is the [[degree]] of the polynomial.
