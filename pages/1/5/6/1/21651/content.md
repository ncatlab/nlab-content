## Properties

### Exceptional isomorphism with $SL(2,\mathbb{H})$
 {#ExceptionalIsomorphism}

  The canonical [[subgroup]] inclusion of [[Spin(5)]] into $Spin(1,5)$ is eqjuivalent to the inclusion of the [[quaternionic unitary group]] [[Sp(2)]] $= U(2,\mathbb{H})$ into [[SL(2,H)|$SL(2,\mathbb{H})$]]:

\begin{tikzcd}
  \mathrm{Spin}(5) 
    \ar[r, hook] 
    \ar[d, "\sim"{sloped}]
  & 
  \mathrm{Spin}(1,5)
    \ar[d, "\sim"{sloped}]
  \\
  \mathrm{U}(2,\mathbb{H}) 
    \ar[r, hook] 
  & 
  \mathrm{SL}(2,\mathbb{H})
\end{tikzcd}

See at *[[Spin(5)]]* the section *[Exceptional isomorphism](Spin5#ExceptionalIsomorphism)* and add  *[[SL(2,H)]]*, the sections *[Unitary subgroup](SL2H#UnitarySubgroup)* and *[Relation to Spin(1,5)](SL2H#RelationToSpin51)*.
