## Definition


Some authors take *pre-additive category* to mean *[[Ab-enriched category|$Ab$-enriched category]]*, for example:

* [[Nicolae Popescu]], _[[Abelian categories with applications to rings and modules]]_, London Math. Soc. Monographs __3__, Academic Press (1973) &lbrack;[MR0340375](http://www.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=0340375)&rbrack;

* [[Charles Weibel]], *[[An Introduction to Homological Algebra]]*, Cambridge University Press (1994) &lbrack;[doi:10.1017/CBO9781139644136](https://doi.org/10.1017/CBO9781139644136), [pdf](https://web.math.rochester.edu/people/faculty/doug//otherpapers/weibel-hom.pdf)&rbrack;

* [[Masaki Kashiwara]], [[Pierre Schapira]]: Section 8 in: *[[Categories and Sheaves]]*, Grundlehren der Mathematischen Wissenschaften __332__, Springer (2006) &lbrack;[doi:10.1007/3-540-27950-4](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/3-540-27950-4), [pdf](https://www.maths.ed.ac.uk/~v1ranick/papers/kashiwara2.pdf)&rbrack;

* [[The Stacks Project]], *12.3 Preadditive and additive categories* &lbrack;[09SE](https://stacks.math.columbia.edu/tag/09SE)&rbrack;

Other authors require in addition the existence of a [[zero object]] (which, this being the [[empty diagram|empty]] [[coproduct]], is the first step towards requiring more general [[finite coproducts]] as for an [[additive category]], whence the terminology):

* Sandro M. Roch, Def. 1.0.4 in :*A brief introduction to abelian categories* (2020) &lbrack;[pdf](https://page.math.tu-berlin.de/~roch/files/abelian_categories.pdf)&rbrack;
