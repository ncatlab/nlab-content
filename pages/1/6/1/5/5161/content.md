Jean Leray (1906-1998) was a mathematician at Collège de France. He obtained his PhD degree in 1933 from École Normale Supérieure de Lyon, supervised by Henri Villat.

* [Wikipedia entry](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean_Leray)
