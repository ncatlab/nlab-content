## Idea

The *moduli stack of L-parameters* is an [[algebraic stack|algebraic]] [[moduli stack]] that parametrizes [[L-parameters]], which generalize the [[Weil-Deligne representations]] for the [[local Langlands correspondence]] when the [[reductive group]] is not the [[general linear group]] $GL_n$.

In the "classical" $\ell$-adic case of the local Langlands correspondence, this moduli stack was constructed independently by Dat-Helm-Kurinczuk-Moss ([DHKM20](#DHKM20)), Fargues-Scholze ([FarguesScholze21](#FarguesScholze21)), and Zhu ([Zhu20](#Zhu20)). For the p-adic local Langlands correspondence, the relevant stack has to be the moduli stack of etale $(\varphi,\Gamma)$-modules (also known as the [[Emerton-Gee stack]]), constructed by Emerton-Gee ([EmertonGee19](#EmertonGee19)).

The corresponding notion in the [[geometric Langlands correspondence]] is the [[moduli stack]] of [[local systems]] (recall -- e.g. from [Deligne 1970](#Deligne70) -- that local systems on a curve (or any space) give [[linear representations]] of its [[fundamental group]], hence are akin to [[Galois representations]]).
