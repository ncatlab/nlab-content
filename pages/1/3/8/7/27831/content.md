## Statement

\begin{theorem}
  Every [[compact subspace|compact]] [[convex set|convex]] [[subset]] of a [[Hausdorff topological space|Hausdorff]] [[locally convex topological vector space]] is the [[closed subset|closed]] [[convex hull]] of its [[extreme points]].
\end{theorem}
