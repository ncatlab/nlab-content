## Idea

A _Cisinski model structure_ is the structure of a [[model category]] on a [[topos]] such that the cofibrations are precisely the [[monomorphism]]s.

The archetyical and motivating example is the standard [[model structure on simplicial sets]], which is a Cisinski model structure on the [[presheaf topos]] on the [[simplex category]].

## Definition

A [[cofibrantly generated model category]] structure on a [[topos]]  is a **Cisinski model structure** if the cofibrations are precisely the [[monomorphism]]s. 

A class $W$ of [[morphism]] in a [[Grothendieck topos]] $E$ is a [[localizer]] if it is the class of weak equivalences of  a Cisinski model structure on $E$. 


## Properties

Every set of [[morphism]]s $\Sigma$ in a topos is contained in a smallest [[localiser]] $W(\Sigma)$. We say that $W(\Sigma)$ is the 
localiser _generated_ by $\Sigma$.
