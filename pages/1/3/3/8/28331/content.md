## Idea

The *Kirby-Siebenmann invariant* controls the existence of [[PL manifold|PL structures]] on [[topological manifolds]]. The analogue concept controlling the existence of [[smooth structures]] on [[topological manifold|topological]] and [[PL manifold|piecewise linear (PL) manifolds]] are the [[Kervaire-Milnor groups]].
