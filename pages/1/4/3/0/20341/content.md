## Statement

The [[coset space]] of [[Spin(5)]] by its [[subgroup]] [[SU(2)]] is [[diffeomorphism|diffeomorphic]] to the standard [[7-sphere]]:

\[
  \label{Spin5BySU2}
  Spin(5)/SU(2)
  \;\simeq_{diff}\;
  S^7
  \,.
\]

This is however not an [[isometry]] to the standard [[Riemannian manifold]]-[[structure]] ("[[round n-sphere]]"), whence one speaks of a [[squashed n-sphere]].

The identification (eq:Spin5BySU2) follows via the exceptional [[isomorphisms]]

[[Spin(5)]] $\simeq$ [[Sp(2)]]

and

[[SU(2)]] $\simeq$ [[Sp(1)]] 

as a special case of the general statement

$$
  S^{4n-1} \simeq_{diff} Sp(n)/Sp(n-1) 
$$

(see [this Prop.](sphere#SphereAsSymplecticUnitaryCoset)).

In particular, there is a [[principal SU(2)-bundle]] $Spin(5)\twoheadrightarrow S^7$. Such principal bundles are classified by $\pi_7 B SU(2)\cong\pi_6 SU(2)\cong\pi_6 S^3    \cong\mathbb{Z}_{12}$.
