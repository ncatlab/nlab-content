## Idea

_D = 6 Seiberg-Witten theory_ (short _D = 6 SW_) is a transfer of [[Seiberg-Witten theory]] from [[Riemannian manifold|Riemannian]] [[4-manifolds]] to [[Riemannian manifold|Riemannian]] 6-manifolds.

## Description

While usual [[Seiberg-Witten theory]] is based on the [[Lie group]] $Spin^\mathrm{c}(4)=U(2)\times_{U(1)}U(2)$, quaternionic Seiberg-Witten theory is based on the [[Lie group]]:
$$
Spin^\mathrm{c}(6)
\coloneqq\big(Spin(6)\times U(1)\big)/\mathbb{Z}_2
\cong\big(SU(4)\times U(1)\big)/\mathbb{Z}_2.
$$
With $U(4)\cong\big(SU(4)\times U(1)\big)/\mathbb{Z}_4$ one has a [[double cover]] $Spin^\mathrm{c}(6)\twoheadrightarrow U(4)$.
