## Idea

A [[Fredholm operator]] $F \,\colon\, \mathcal{H} \longrightarrow \mathcal{H}$ by definition has [[finite-dimensional vector space|finite-dimensional]] [[kernel]] $ker(F)$ and [[cokernel]] $coker(F)$. Its *[[index]]* is the difference between these [[dimension of a vector space|dimensions]]:

$$
  ind(F)
  \;\coloneqq\;
  dim\big(ker(F)\big)
  \,-\,
  dim\big(coker(F)\big)
  \,.
$$
