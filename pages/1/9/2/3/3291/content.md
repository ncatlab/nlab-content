**Valery Lunts** is a professor of mathematics at the Indiana University at Bloomington ([faculty profile](http://www.math.indiana.edu/people/profile.phtml?id=vlunts)). His interests include [[algebraic geometry]] (including [[noncommutative algebraic geometry|noncommutative]]), [[representation theory]], [[D-modules]], [[deformation theory]], [[sheaf theory]] and [[homological algebra]].
