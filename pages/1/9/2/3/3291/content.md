**Valery A. Lunts** is a professor of mathematics at the Indiana University at Bloomington ([faculty profile](https://math.indiana.edu/about/faculty/lunts-valery.html)). His interests include [[algebraic geometry]] (including [[noncommutative algebraic geometry|noncommutative]]), [[representation theory]], [[D-modules]], [[deformation theory]], [[sheaf theory]] and [[homological algebra]].
