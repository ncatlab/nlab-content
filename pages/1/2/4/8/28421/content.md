## Idea

*Einstein-Cartan-Dirac theory* is a [[theory (physics)]] or [[model (in theoretical physics)|model]] in [[physics]], which combines [[Einstein-Cartan theory]] (Einstein-Cartan-Sciama-Kibble theory) with the [[Dirac equation]] ("Dirac theory"). It describes [[gravity]] coupled to [[spin]] and [[fermion|fermionic]] [[matter]].
