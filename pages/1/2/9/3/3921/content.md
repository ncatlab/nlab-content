## Idea


The _Korteweg de Vries equation_ is a [[differential equation]], a particular nonlinear variant of a [[wave equation]]:

$$
  u_t = (3u^2 + u_{xx})_x
$$

This is one of the main examples in the study of [[integrable systems]].
