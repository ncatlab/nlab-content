## Idea

Invertible counital *bialgebra 2-cocycles* are the basic datum in [[Drinfel'd twist]] theory for twisting [[bialgebras]] and [[Hopf algebras]]. 

A generalization to [[associative bialgebroids]] was introduced in [Xu 1999](#PingXu99):

using an invertible bialgebroid 2-cocycle $F$ and a, say, left $R$-algebroid $H$, one obtains a new base algebra $R_F$ and a left $R_F$-algebroid $H_F$ whose underlying associative algebra structure is unchanged but whose coproduct is appropriately twisted. 
The corresponding cocycles and the twisting procedure are now also referred to as *Drinfeld--Xu twists*. 

There is also a dual theory of cotwists.
