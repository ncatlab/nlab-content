## Idea

Invertible counital bialgebra 2-cocycle is a basic datum of the [[Drinfel'd twist]] theory of twisting bialgebras and [[Hopf algebra]]s. A generalization for [[associative bialgebroid]]s is introduced in [Ping Xu 1999](#PingXu99);
using an invertible bialgebroid 2-cocycle $F$ and a say left $R$-algebroid $H$, one obtains a new base algebra $R_F$ and a left $R_F$-algebroid $H_F$ whose underlying associative algebra structure is unchanged and the coproduct is appropriately twisted. 
The corresponding cocycles and the twisting procedure are sometimes referred to as Drinfeld--Xu twists. 

There is also a dual theory of cotwists.
