## Idea

What are called _matrix product states_ (MPS) in [[quantum physics]] (specifically in [[solid state physics]] and in [[AdS/CFT]]) are those [[tensor network states]] of the form of a ring of [[tensors]] all of rank 3.

For example, given a [[metric Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{g}$ (with [[string diagram]]-notation as discussed [there](metric+Lie+algebra#Definition)) its [[Lie bracket]]-[[tensor]] $f \coloneqq [-,-] \in \mathfrak{g}^\ast \otimes \mathfrak{g}^\ast \otimes \mathfrak{g}$ gives rise to matrix product states of the following form:
