## Idea

Given a [[symmetry group]] $G$ equipped with a [[homomorphism]] $G \to O(V)$ to an [[orthogonal group]] of some [[inner product space]] $V$ -- for instance a [[Pin group]] -- then its _pseudovector representation_ is the [[linear representation]] (over the given [[ground field]] $k$)

$$
  V_{{}_{sgn}}
  \;\in\;
  Rep_k(G)
$$

which is the [[tensor product of representations]] of the [[vector representation]] $V$ with the [[pseudoscalar representation]] ([[determinant]]-representation) $\mathbf{1}_{{}_{sgn}}$:

$$
  V_{{}_{sgn}}
  \;\coloneqq\;
  V \otimes \mathbf{1}_{{}_{sgn}}
$$
