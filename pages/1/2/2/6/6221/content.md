## Definition

A __linear algebraic group__ $G$ is any (Zariski closed) [[algebraic group|algebraic]] [[subgroup]] of [[general linear group|$GL(n,k)$]] where $k$ is a [[field]] and $n$ a [[natural number]].

An algebraic group over a field is linear iff it is [[affine scheme|affine]] 
as a $k$-[[scheme]]. 

An algebraic group scheme is *affine* if the underlying scheme is [[affine scheme|affine]].

The category of [[affine group scheme]]s over a field $k$ is the [[opposite category|opposite]] of the category of commutative [[Hopf algebras]] over $k$.
