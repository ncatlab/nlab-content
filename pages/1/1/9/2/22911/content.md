## Definition

In [[analysis]], given a [[series]] 

$$
  \underoverset
    {k = 0}
    {\color{blue}\infty}
    {\sum} 
  a_k
$$ 

its $n$th *partial sum* (for $n \in \mathbb{N}$ any [[natural number]]) is the actual [[sum]] 

$$
  \underoverset
    {k = 0}
    {\color{blue}n}
    {\sum} 
    a_k
$$ 
of the first $n+1$ summands.
