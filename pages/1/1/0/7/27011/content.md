## Definition

Let $k$ be a field and $\overline{k}$ its [[algebraic closure]].
A [[reductive algebraic group]] $G$ over $k$ is **split** if it contains a split torus $\mathbb{G}_m^{\times n} \subset G$ whose base change is a maximal torus in $G_{\overline{k}}$.
