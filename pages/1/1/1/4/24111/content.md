## Idea 

A [[neural network]] which relies on a physical medium, such as a [[nonlinear system|nonlinear]] [[optics|optical system]], a mechanical plate oscillator, or an [[electronics|electronic]] [[circuit]], to emulate neurons, rather than [[software]] [[code]].
