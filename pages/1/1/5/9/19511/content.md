## Idea

For a charged and/or rotating [[black hole]] ([[Kerr spacetime]], [[Reissner-Nordstrom spacetime]], [[Kerr-Newman spacetime]]) there is a [[minimum]] possible [[mass]] for given [[charge]] and [[angular momentum]]. When this minimum is attained, one hence speaks of an _extremal black hole_.

## Properties

### Near horizon geometry

The [[near horizon geometry]] of extremal black holes in $d$ dimensional spacetime  is [[anti de Sitter spacetime]] $AdS_{2}$ ([KLR 07](#KLR07), [AGJST 06](#AGJST06), [AY 07](#AY07), [ANT 07](#ANT07)).
