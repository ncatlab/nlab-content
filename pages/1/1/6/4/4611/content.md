## Definition

A [[category]] $D$ is called __sifted__ if [[colimits]] of [[diagrams]] of shape $D$ (called [[sifted colimits]]) commute with finite [[products]] in [[Set]]. 

## Properties

Gabriel and Ulmer proved that a category is sifted if and only if the [[diagonal functor|diagonal]] $D \to D\times D$ is a [[final functor]].
