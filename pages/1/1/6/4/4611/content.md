A [[category]] $D$ is called _sifted_ if [[colimits]] of [[diagrams]] of shape $D$ commute with finite products in $Set$. Gabriel and Ulmer proved that a category is sifted if and only if the diagonal $D \to D\times D$ is a [[final functor]].
