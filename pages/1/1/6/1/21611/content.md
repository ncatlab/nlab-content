## Idea

Orbifold differential K-theory is a generalization of [[differential K-theory]] from [[smooth manifolds]] to [[orbifolds]], hence enhancement of [[orbifold K-theory]] to [[differential cohomology]]. On [[global quotient orbifolds]] this is typically supposed to reduce to [[equivariant differential K-theory]].
