## Idea

Let $\mathscr{A}$ and $\mathscr{B}$ be [[bicategories]]. 

A **local adjunction** between $\mathscr{A}$ and $\mathscr{B}$ comprises [[lax functors]] $L \colon \mathscr{A} \to \mathscr{B}$ and $R \colon \mathscr{B} \to \mathscr{A}$ and a [[family]] of [[adjunctions]] 
$$
  \mathscr{B}(L A, B) 
  \rightleftarrows 
  \mathscr{A}(A, R B)
$$ 

between [[hom-categories]], [[natural transformation|natural]] in $A$ and $B$.
