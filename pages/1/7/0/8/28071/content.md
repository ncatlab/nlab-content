## Idea

*Principal O(2)-bundles* are special [[principal bundles]] with the second [[orthogonal group]] [[O(2)]] as [[structure group]]([[gauge group]]).

## Characteristic classes

\begin{proposition}
A principal O(2)-bundle $P$ fulfills:
$$
w_2^2(P)
\equiv p_1(P) mod 2.
$$
(In general, a principal $O(n)$-bundle $P$ fulfills $w_{2k}^2(P)\equiv p_k(P) mod 2$ for $2k\leq n$.)
\end{proposition}

([Hatcher 17, Prop. 3.15 a](#Hatcher17))

## Examples

* One has $S^n\cong O(n+1)/O(n)$, hence there is a principal O(2)-bundle $O(3)\twoheadrightarrow S^2$. Such [[principal bundles]] are classified by:
$$
\pi_2B O(2)
\cong\pi_1 O(2)
\cong\mathbb{Z}.
$$
