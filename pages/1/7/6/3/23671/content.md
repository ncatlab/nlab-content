## Definition ##

A **unital dagger 2-poset** is a [[dagger 2-poset]] $C$ with an object $\mathbb{1} \in Ob(C)$ such that for every morphism $f \in Hom(\mathbb{1}, \mathbb{1})$, $f \leq 1_\mathbb{1}$, and for every object $A \in Ob(C)$, there is an [[onto morphism in a dagger 2-poset|onto morphism]] $u_A \in Hom(A,\mathbb{1})$. 

## Examples ##

* The dagger 2-poset [[Rel]] of sets and relations is a unital dagger 2-poset.
