## Idea ##

A differential ring is an $\mathbb{Z}$-[[differential algebra]]. 

## Definition ##

A **differential ring** is a [[ring]] $R$ with an [[abelian group]] [[homomorphism]] $d:R \to R$ such that $d(a \cdot b) = a \cdot d(b) + d(a) \cdot b$.
