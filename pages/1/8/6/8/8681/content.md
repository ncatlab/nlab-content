## Statement

Let $R$ be a [[commutative ring]]. (or maybe any ring?)

+-- {: .num_prop}
###### Proposition
**(Lazard's criterion)**

An $R$-[[module]] is a [[flat module]] precisely if it is a [[filtered colimit]] of [[free modules]]. 

=--

This is due to ([Lazard (1964)](#Lazard)). See at _[[flat module]]_ for more.
