## Idea

The [[representation theory]] of the [[general linear groups]].

## Properties

### Polynomial irreps

The polynomial [[irreps]] of $GL(n,\mathbb{C})$ are labeled by [[Young diagrams]] $\lambda \in Part(n)$ (e.g. [Fulton 97, Thm. 2 on p. 114](#Fulton97)). 

These are equivalently the irreps of the [[special linear group]] (e.g. [Sternberg 94, Sec. 5.8](#Sternberg94)).

And for $rows(\lambda) \leq n-1$ these are also the irreps of the [[special unitary group]] (e.g. [Peluse 14, p. 2](representation+theory+of+the+special+unitary+group#Peluse14)).
