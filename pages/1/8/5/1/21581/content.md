## Idea

A _Lorentzian orbifold_  is an [[orbifold]] with [[Lorentzian structure]], hence like a [[Lorentzian manifold]] but possibly with [[singularities]] modeled on [[fixed points]] of local [[actions]] of [[finite groups]] by [[Lorentz transformations]].
