## Idea

The _Monster vertex algebra_ is a [[super vertex operator algebra]] whose [[automorphism group|group of]] [[automorphisms of a super VOA]] is the [[monster group]]. ([Frenkel-Lepowski-Meurman 89](#FrenkelLepowskiMeurman89), [Griess-Lam 11](#GriessLam11)).
