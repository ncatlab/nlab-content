## Idea

*Hirzebruch's signature theorem* relates the [[signature]] of [[oriented]] [[smooth manifold|smooth]] $4n$-manifolds with its [[Pontrjagin numbers]].

## Statement

\begin{proposition}
**(Hirzebruch signature theorem for [[4-manifolds]])**
For an [[oriented]] [[smooth manifold|smooth]] [[4-manifold]] $M$ with [[fundamental class]] $[M]\in H^4(M,\mathbb{Z})\cong\mathbb{Z}$, its [[signature]] is given by [[Pontrjagin numbers]] as:
$$
\sigma(M)
=\frac{1}{3}\langle p_1(M),[M]\rangle
\in\mathbb{Z}.
$$
\end{proposition}

\begin{proposition}
**(Hirzebruch signature theorem for [[8-manifolds]])**
For an [[oriented]] [[smooth manifold|smooth]] [[8-manifold]] $M$ with [[fundamental class]] $[M]\in H^8(M,\mathbb{Z})\cong\mathbb{Z}$, its [[signature]] is given by [[Pontrjagin numbers]] as:
$$
\sigma(M)
=\frac{1}{45}\langle(7p_2-2p_1^2)(M),[M]\rangle
\in\mathbb{Z}.
$$
\end{proposition}

\begin{proposition}
**(Hirzebruch signature theorem for [[12-manifolds]])**
For an [[oriented]] [[smooth manifold|smooth]] [[12-manifold]] $M$ with [[fundamental class]] $[M]\in H^12(M,\mathbb{Z})\cong\mathbb{Z}$, its [[signature]] is given by [[Pontrjagin numbers]] as:
$$
\sigma(M)
=\frac{1}{945}\langle(62p_3-13p_1p_2+2p_1^3)(M),[M]\rangle
\in\mathbb{Z}.
$$
\end{proposition}

\begin{proposition}
**(Hirzebruch signature theorem for [[16-manifolds]])**
For an [[oriented]] [[smooth manifold|smooth]] [[16-manifold]] $M$ with [[fundamental class]] $[M]\in H^16(M,\mathbb{Z})\cong\mathbb{Z}$, its [[signature]] is given by [[Pontrjagin numbers]] as:
$$
\sigma(M)
=\frac{1}{14175}\langle(381 p_4-71p_1p_3-19p_2^2+22p_1^2p_2-3p_1^4)(M),[M]\rangle
\in\mathbb{Z}.
$$
\end{proposition}
