## Idea

The _Pontrjagin character_ is the analog for [[KO]]-[[topological K-theory|-theory]] of what the [[Chern character]] is for [[KU]]-[[topological K-theory|-theory]]. Like the latter may be expressed as a [[series]] of [[Chern classes]], so the Pontrjagin character is expressed as a series of [[Pontrjagin classes]].

Equivalently, the Pontrjagin character is the [[Chern-Dold character]] on [[KO]].
