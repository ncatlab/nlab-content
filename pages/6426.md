## Idea

The analog of [[unitary representation of the Poincaré group]] for the [[super Poincaré group]]: [[supersymmetry]].

An [[irreducible representation]] of the [[super Poincaré Lie algebra]] is called  **super multiplet**. It is in general itself a [[super vector space]] which contains an ordinary irreducible [[unitary representation of the Poincaré group]] -- which may be identified with a [[boson|bosonic]] [[relativistic particle]] of some [[mass]] -- together with the images of these "bosonic" elements under the odd generators: the **superpartners** of the bosonic particles.
