__Viktor A. Vassiliev__, a Russian geometer, a pupil of [[Vladimir Arnold]]. He is also active in the questions of education and the well-being of the academic community in contemporary Russia. 

* [webpage](http://www.hse.ru/en/org/persons/1297545) at Higher School of Economics, Moscow
