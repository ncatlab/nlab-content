## Idea

For $G$ a [[compact space|compact]] [[Lie group]] there is a way to equip the topological [[classifying space]] $B G$ with [[smooth structure]] such that the corresponding smooth [[universal principal bundle]] $E G \to B G$ carries a [[connection on a bundle|smooth connection]] $\nabla_{univ}$ with the property that for every $G$-[[principal bundle]] $P \to X$ with [[connection on a bundle|connection]] $\nabla$ there is a smooth representative $f : X \to B G$ of the classifying map, such that $(P, \nabla) \simeq (P, f^* \nabla_{univ})$. This $\nabla_{univ}$ is called the **universal $G$-connection**.
