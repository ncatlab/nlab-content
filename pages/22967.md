To given $n$ particles in a 3-dimensional Euclidean space, Atiyah defines certain energy. This energy functional is conjectured to have certain positivity property, what appeared to be a very difficult problem in metric geometry even for very small $n$. The conjecture has later been refined in terms of a certain determinant by Atiyah and Sutcliffe.
