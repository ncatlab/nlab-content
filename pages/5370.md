## Idea

Many general results discussed at [[model structure on operads]] are based on the existence of a _cocommutative Hopf interval object_ . In the [[category of chain complexes]] -- the case for [[dg-operad]]s -- the Hopf interval is not cocommutative, so this case requires special discussion.
