Crossed modules of groups, [[differential crossed modules]], and crossed modules of crossed modules, are a special case of an (internal) crossed module in a [[semi-abelian category]] which is along with precrossed modules introduced in 

* G. Janelidze, _Internal crossed modules_, Georgian Math. J. 10 (2003) 99114 ([journal](http://www.heldermann.de/GMJ/GMJ10/GMJ101/gmj1008.htm)).

In Mal'cev categories there is an article on precrossed modules

* T. Everaerta, M. Gran, _Precrossed modules and Galois theory_, J. Algebra __297__, no. 1 (2006) 292--309 [doi:10.1016/j.jalgebra.2005.06.034](http://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.jalgebra.2005.06.034).
