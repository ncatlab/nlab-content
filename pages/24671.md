__Heun equation__ is a second order linear [[ordinary differential equation]] of Fuchsian type with 4 regular singular points at $0,1,a,\infty$. Its normalized holomorphic solution is the Heun function. If some of the singular points coalesce we talk about the confluent Heun equation.
