
# Nowhere dense sets
* table of contents
{: toc}

## Definition

A [[subspace|subset]] $A\subseteq X$ of a [[topological space]] $X$ is called **nowhere dense** if the [[interior]] of its [[closure]] is [[empty subspace|empty]]:
$$ Int(Cl(A)) = \emptyset .$$
