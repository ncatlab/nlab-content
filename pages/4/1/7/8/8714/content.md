Ernie Manes wrote one of the well-known books on [[algebraic theory|algebraic theories]].  He was a long term collaborator of [[Michael Arbib]].

* [MathematicsGenealogy entry](http://www.genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/id.php?id=38925)
