## Idea

A _nonabelian bundle 2-gerbe_ is a model for a [[principal infinity-bundle|principal 3-bundle]].

The notion is

* the next step in [[higher category theory]] of the notion of [[nonabelian bundle gerbe]];

* a generalization of the notion of [[bundle 2-gerbe]] from abelian to nonabelian structure [[infinity-group|3-groups]].
