## Idea

The [[G-symmetric monoidal envelope]] is to [[G-∞-operads]]/[[G-symmetric monoidal ∞-categories]] as the [[symmetric monoidal envelope]] is to [[∞-operads]]/[[symmetric monoidal ∞-categories]].
In particular, it is the [[left adjoint|left]] [[adjoint (infinity,1)-functor|adjoint]] to the canonical inclusion of [[G-symmetric monoidal ∞-categories]] into [[G-∞-operads]].
