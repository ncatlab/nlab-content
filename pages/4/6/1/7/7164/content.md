## Works



* [[Richard Dedekind]], _Essays on the Theory of Numbers_ (trans. W. Beman), 1901 ([Project Gutenberg](http://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/21016))

containing _Continuity and irrational numbers_ (Stetigkeit und irrationale Zahlen) and _The nature and meaning of numbers_ (Was sind und was sollen die Zahlen). These two essays contain the definitions of [[Dedekind cuts]] and infinite sets (this definition is now taken to define _Dedekind_ infinite sets) an axiomatization of the [[natural numbers]] adapted by Peano into [[Peano arithmetic]].
