[[Andrés Villaveces]] is a mathematician at Universidad Nacional de Colombia sede Bogotá ([web](https://bogota.unal.edu.co)) specialized in [[model theory]]. He is also interested in philosophy and history of mathematics. 

* personal [homepage](https://avillavecesn.net)
