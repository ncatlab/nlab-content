

## Idea

A _smooth structure on a topos_ is a kind of [[internalization|internal]] version of a [[smooth structure]] on its [[Dedekind real number object]] $\mathbb{R}_{Dedekind}$.

[Fourman 75, def. 4.1](#Fourman75)
