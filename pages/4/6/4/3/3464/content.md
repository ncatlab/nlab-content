This entry is about the proceedings volume for the 1990 conference on [[category theory]] in Como:

* A. Carboni, M. Pedicchio, G. Rosolini, _Category theory_ , Proceedings of the International Conference held in Como 1990, Lecture Notes in Math. __1488__, Springer (1991).

The preface is

* [[Bill Lawvere|F. W. Lawvere]], _[[Some Thoughts on the Future of Category Theory]]_, 1–13

  (on [[cohesive toposes]]).

Contents:

* [[J. Adámek]], [[J. Rosický]], _What are locally generated categories?_, 14–19

* [[J. Bénabou]], _Some remarks on free monoids in a topos_, 20–29

* [[F. Borceux]], G. van den Bossche, _A generic sheaf representation for rings_, 30–42

* [[D. Bourn]], _Normalization equivalence, kernel equivalence and affine categories_, 43–62

* S. Carmody, R. F. C. Walters, _Computing quotients of actions of a free category_, 63–78

* [[A. M. Cegarra]], A. R. Garzon, _A long exact sequence in non-abelian cohomology_, 79–94

* [[P. J. Freyd]], _Algebraically complete categories_, 95–104

* [[J. W. Gray]], _Order-enriched sketches for typed lambda calculi_, 105–130

* [[J. M. E. Hyland]], _First steps in synthetic domain theory_, 131–156

* [[G. Janelidze]], _Precategories and Galois theory_, 157–173

* [[G. Janelidze]], [[W. Tholen]], _How algebraic is the change-of-base functor?_, 174–186

* C. B. Jay, _Fixpoint and loop constructions as colimits_, 187–192

* [[P. Johnstone]], [[S. Vickers]], _Preframe presentations present_, 193–212

* [[André Joyal|A. Joyal]], [[Myles Tierney|M. Tierney]], _Strong stacks and classifying spaces_, 213–236 ([pdf](http://www.pps.jussieu.fr/~mellies/semantics-operads-categories/joyal-tierney-strong-stacks.pdf))

   (on [[(2,1)-sheaves]] and [[2-sheaves]]).

* S. Kasangian, S. Vigna, _Trees in distributive categories_,  237–248

* [[G. M. Kelly]], _A note on relations relative to a factorization system_, 249–261

* [[Anders Kock|A. Kock]], _Algebras for the partial map classifier monad_,  262–278

* [[Bill Lawvere|F. W. Lawvere]], _Intrinsic co-Heyting boundaries and the Leibniz rule in certain toposes_,  279–281

  (see also at _[[co-Heyting algebra]]_)

* J. L. Macdonald, _Concretely functorial programming_, 282–297

* S. B. Niefield, _Weak products over a locally Hausdorff locale_, 298–305

* [[Dusko Pavlovic|Duško Pavlović]], _Categorical interpolation: descent and the [[Beck-Chevalley condition]] without direct images_, 306–325

* J. Power, _An $n$-categorical pasting theorem_,  326–358

  (on [[pasting diagrams|pasting]] in [[n-categories]]).

* [[Gonzalo Reyes|G. E. Reyes]], H. Zolfaghari, _Topos-theoretic approaches to modality_,  359–378

  (on [[modal logic]] in [[topos theory]]).

* [[Stephen Schanuel|S. H. Schanuel]], _Negative sets have Euler characteristic and dimension_, 379–385

* M. Thiébaud, _Modular categories_,  386–400

* J. J. C. Vermeulen, _Some constructive results related to compactness and the (strong) Hausdorff property for locales_,  401–409

* [[Andre Joyal|A. Joyal]], [[Ross Street|R. Street]], _An introduction to [[Tannaka duality]] and [[quantum groups]]_, 413–492

About the geographic place see Wikipedia: [Como](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Como) and the conference centre: [Villa Olmo](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Villa_Olmo).

In 2018, a [school and conference](http://tcsc.lakecomoschool.org/) on topos theory was hosted in Como ([video playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLh_3Q6ZRqWs0LBptMGClJ8OArR0fBT6Pp)).
