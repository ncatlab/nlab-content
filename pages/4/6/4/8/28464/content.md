## Idea

The [[tensor product]] of [[presentable (infinity,1)-categories|presentable]] [[stable (infinity,1)-categories|stable $\infty$-categories]] ("Lurie tensor product") is the [[symmetric monoidal (infinity,1)-category|symmetric monoidal operation]] which universally co-represents [[bifunctor|bi]]-[[(infinity,1)-functors|$\infty$-functors]] that are exact and continuous in each variable (hence which induce [[triangulated bifunctors]] on [[triangulated category|triangulated]] [homotopy categories](stable+infinity1-category#TheTriangulatedHomotopyCategory)).

On [[underlying]] [[presentable (infinity,1)-categories|presentable $\infty$-categories]] this is just the [[tensor product of presentable (infinity,1)-categories|tensor product of presentable $\infty$-categories]].
