## Definition 

For $n \in \mathbb{Z}$, $-2 \leq n \lt \infty$,
an _$(n,1)$-sheaf_ is an [[n-truncated]] [[(∞,1)-sheaf]]: an [[(∞,1)-sheaf]] with values in [[n-groupoids]]. 

This is also sometimes called an _$(n-1)$-stack_.
