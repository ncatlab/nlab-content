## Idea

The [[representation theory]] of the [[special unitary group]].

## Properties

### Irreps

The [[irreps]] of $SU(n)$ are those polynomial [[irreps]] of [[representation theory of the general linear group|GL(n,C)]], hence those irreps of $SL(n,\mathbb{C})$, which are labeled by [[partitions]]/[[Young diagrams]] $\lambda \in Part(n)$ with $rows(\lambda) \leq n - 1$.

(e.g. [Peluse 14, p. 1](#Peluse 14))
