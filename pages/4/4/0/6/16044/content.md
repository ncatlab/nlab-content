## Idea

Am *Iwasawa decomposition* is an [[analytic function|analytic]] [[diffeomorphism]] $G \,\simeq\, K \times A \times N$ between 

1. a [[connected topological space|connected]] real [[semisimple Lie group]] $G$ 

1. a [[product manifold]] $K \times A \times N$, 

   where $K$, $A$ and $N$ are 

   * [[analytic function|analytic]]$\;$[[subgroups]] of $G$, 
  
   * with $A$ and $N$ [[simply connected topological space|simply connected]], 
 
   * [[Lie integration|integrating]] [[Lie algebras]] $k,a,n$, respectively, where 

   * $k\oplus p$ is the [[Cartan decomposition]] of the Lie algebra of $G$, 

   * $a$ is the maximal Abelian subalgebra of $p$ 

   * $n$ is a certain [[nilpotent Lie algebra|nilpotent]] Lie subalgebra of $p$ with $p \simeq a\oplus n$ as [[vector spaces]]. 

The Iwasawa decomposition generalizes a statement expressing the [[QR decomposition]] of [[matrices]].
