## Idea

Iwasawa decomposition is an analytic diffeomorphism between a connected semisimple real Lie group $G$ and a product manifold $K\times A\times N$ where $K$, $A$ and
$N$ are analytic subgroups of $G$ integrating Lie algebras $k,a,n$ where $k\oplus p$ is the [[Cartan decomposition]] of the Lie algebra of $G$, $a$ the maximal Abelian subalgebra of $p$ and $n$ certain nilpotent Lie subalgebra of $p$ with $p = a\oplus n$ as a vector space. $A$ and $N$ are simply connected.

Iwasawa decomposition describes a generalization of a situation which is implied by the [[QR decomposition]] of matrices.
