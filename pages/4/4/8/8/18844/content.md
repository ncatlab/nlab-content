## Idea 

_Generalized contact geometry_ is the odd dimensional analogue of [[generalized complex geometry]]. One proposal for an odd dimensional analogue of a generalized complex manifold is called a **generalized contact bundle** ([VitaAis15]({#VitaAis15)). This concept encompasses both not necessarily coorientable contact manifolds and line bundles equipped with an integrable [[complex structure]] on their [[Atiyah algebroid]].
