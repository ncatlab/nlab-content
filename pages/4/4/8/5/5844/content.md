__Joachim Cuntz__ is a mathematician at M&#252;nster, Germany. His central interests include [[functional analysis]], [[operator algebras]], [[homological algebra]] (especially [[cyclic homology]]) and [[noncommutative geometry]]. He also studied regularity and smoothness conditions in ring theory.
