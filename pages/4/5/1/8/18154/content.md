Walter Rudin was an Austrian-American mathematician, professor at University of Wisconsin at Madison. His work focused on mathematical analysis, especially complex analysis. Apart from research he is well known for several highly appreciated monographs and advanced textbooks.

* [Wikipedia entry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Walter_Rudin)

## Selected writings

Monographs:

* Principles of mathematical analysis

* Functional analysis, [archive](https://archive.org/details/functionalanalys00rudi)

* Real and complex analysis

* Function theory in polydiscs (1969)

* Function theory in the unit ball of $\mathbb{C}^{n}$ (1980)
