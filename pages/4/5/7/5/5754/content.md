## Idea

Given an (local) [[action functional]] the _Peierls bracket_ is a canonical [[Poisson structure]] on its [[covariant phase space]].


## References

* R. Peierls, _The commutation laws of relativistic field theory_ (1952) ([jstor](http://www.jstor.org/pss/99080))

In this article the Peierls bracket on the [[covariant phase space]] of a [[gauge theory|non-gauge]] system is defined and equivalence with the Hamiltonian phase space [[symplectic structure]] is proven. 

See also exercise 17.12 in 

* [[Marc Henneaux]], [[Claudio Teitelboim]], _[[Quantization of Gauge Systems]]_
