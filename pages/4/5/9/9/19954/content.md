##Idea
As with the ordinary [[chain rule]], in [[Goodwillie calculus]] the **Goodwillie chain rule** is a formula for describing the [[Goodwillie derivatives]] of a composite functor $F G$ in terms of the derivatives of $F$ and $G$.
