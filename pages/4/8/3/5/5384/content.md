#### $(\infty,1)$-Category theory
+--{: .hide}

Entries on the [[HomePage|nLab]] that use the **implicit $\infty$-category theory convention** leave the "$\infty$"-symbol notationally implicit when working in [[(∞,1)-category theory]].

In this convention

* _category_ means _[[(∞,1)-category]]_, and so $Cat$ means _[[(∞,1)Cat]]_

* _topos_ means _[[(∞,1)-topos]]_, and so $Topos$ means _[[(∞,1)Topos]]_

* _groupoid_ means _[[∞-groupoid]]_, and so $Gpd$ means _[[?Gpd]]_

* _$n$-category_ means _[[(∞,n)-category]]_

* _functor_ means _[[(∞,1)-functor]]_ or _[[(∞,n)-functor]]_ for some $n \in \mathbb{N}$, depending on the codomain;

* _presheaf_ means _[[(∞,1)-presheaf]]_, 

* _sheaf_ means _[[(∞,1)-sheaf]]_ , a.k.a. _[[∞-stack]]_   

* _limit_ means _[[(∞,1)-limit]]_

* _algebraic theory_ means _[[(∞,1)-algebraic theory]]_

* _algebra over an algebraic theory_ means _[[∞-algebra over an (∞,1)-algebraic theory]]_ .

Etc.
