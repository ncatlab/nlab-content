The term **left Bousfield localization** can refer to

* [[left Bousfield localization of model categories]] (most common meaning)

* More generally, left Bousfield localization of [[relative categories]].

* In Cisinski's [[Higher Categories and Homotopical Algebra]], “left Bousfield localization” refers to a [[reflective localization]] of a [[quasicategory]], i.e., [[reflective localization of an (∞,1)-category]].
