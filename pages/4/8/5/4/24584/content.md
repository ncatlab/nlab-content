## Definition

In [[cubical type theory]], it is not the case that the sequent 
$$r:I \vdash r = 0 \vee r = 1 \;\mathrm{true}$$ 
holds. 
The **boundary** is thus defined as the [[formula]]
$$\delta(r) \coloneqq r = 0 \vee r = 1$$
