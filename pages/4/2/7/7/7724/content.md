## Definition

If $A = (a_{i j})$ is a [[matrix]], then its _transpose_ $A^T$ is the matrix $A^T \coloneqq (a_{j i})$.

A square matrix which is its onw transpose, $A =A^T$, is called a [[symmetric matrix]].
