Category mistake is assigning an action or an attribute to wrong ontological category.

As per mainstream science, it is proven without reasonable doubt that there is no separatable “ghost in the machine” i.e. “substance dualism” is false. Not accepting it is a "Category mistake", as per mainstream science.

As per Bigelow Institute, it is proven without reasonable doubt that there is separatable “ghost in the machine” i.e. “substance dualism” is true. Not accepting it is a "Category mistake", as per people who agree with the findings of Bigelow Institute.
