## Definition

A _Lie 3-group_ of _3-Lie group_ is a 3-[[truncated]] [[∞-group]] in [[?LieGrpd]].


## From Lie 2-crossed module

A smooth [[2-crossed module]] is a model for semistrict Lie 3-groups, Lie [[Gray group]]s.
