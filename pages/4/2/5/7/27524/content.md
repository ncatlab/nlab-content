A Hawkes process $(N_t)$ is a self-exciting point process with stochastic intensity

$$ 
\lambda_t = \mu + \int_0^t \varphi (t-s) \mathrm{d} N_s,
$$

where $\mu \geq 0$, and $\varphi \colon R_+ \to \R_+$.
