## Idea

The _sigma-meson_ is the lightest [[Lorentz group|Lorentz]]-[[scalar]] and [[isospin]]-[[scalar]] [[meson]], now also called the _$f_0(500)$-resonance_. Together with the [[omega-meson]] the sigma is responsible for the long-range [[interaction]] between [[baryons]], exhibiting the residual [[strong nuclear force]] between them.
