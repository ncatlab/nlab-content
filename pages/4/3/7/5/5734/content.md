Alexander Mikhailovich Vinogradov (Александр Михайлович Виноградов, 1938-2019) was a Russian mathematician working in the geometric theory of [[differential equations]], developing the theory of [[diffieties]] with applications to [[variational calculus]]. 

In co-authorship with members of his seminar he published also under the pseudonym *[[Jet Nestruev]]* (cf. [Nestruev 2002](#Nestruev02)).
