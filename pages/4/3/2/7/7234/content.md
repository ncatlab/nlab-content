## Idea

One of the [[exceptional Lie groups]].

## Properties

### As automorphisms of the exceptional Jordan algebra

The [[group]] of [[determinant]]-preserving [[linear map|linear]] [[isomorphisms]] of the vector space underlying the [[octonionic Albert algebra]] is  ${E}_{6(-26)}$. (see e.g. ([Manogue-Dray 09](#ManogueDray09))).

This may be written as $SL(3,\mathbb{O})$. ([Dray-Manogue 09a (16)](#DrayManogue09a))


<img src="https://ncatlab.org/nlab/files/MapOfE6.png" width="500">

### Outer automorphism

The [[Dynkin diagram]] of $E_6$ has 2-fold symmetry, corresponding to a nontrivial [[outer automorphism]] of order 2. $E_6$ acts on the [[octonionic projective plane]] and its outer automorphism gives rise to a projective duality in its geometry.
