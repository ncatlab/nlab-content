## Idea

A _difunctional relation_ is a [[relation]] that is the union of a collection of "completely disjoint rectangles".

Originally defined by [[Jacques Riguet]] in [1948](#Riguet48), nowadays they occur in [[computing]] and program specification. Their definition is reminiscent of the equations defining an [[inverse semigroup]].
