## Idea

*Simplicial type theory* ([Riehl & Shulman 2017, §3](#RiehlShulman17)) is a [[homotopy type theory]] which introduces additional non-fibrant layers on top of [[Martin-Löf type theory]] to provide a logical calculus for a [[directed homotopy theory|directed]] [[interval type]] and with it for  [[geometric shape for higher structures|geometric shapes]], consisting of cube judgments, tope judgments, inequality topes, extension types, [[Segal space]]-types, [[Rezk completion|Rezk complete]]-types, and covariant fibrations.
