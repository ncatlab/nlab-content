## Idea

The application of mathematics in the theory of music goes at least back to Pythagoras. Recently, **mathematical music theory** has gained impetus through the Neo-Riemannian theory of harmony. David Lewin's research on transformational theory and Guerino Mazzola's work on a topos-theoretic approach have greatly enhanced the variety of mathematical concepts employed in the study of music that today include tools from [[group]] and [[topos|topos theory]].
