## Idea

In [[trigonometry]], the _tangent function_ is one of the basic [[trigonometric functions]]. 

## Definition

The tangent function is the [[ratio]] of the [[sine]] and the [[cosine]]:


$$
  \tan(x) = \frac{\sin(x)}{\cos(x)}
$$
