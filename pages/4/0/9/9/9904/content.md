
A Soviet and Russian mathematical physicist. Discovered
[[Maslov index]], [[Fourier integral operator]]s (in terms of "canonical operator"), [[Maslov-Feynman calculus]], systematic approach to [[WKB method]] to all orders, corresponding "Maslov" quantization conditions and the complex WKB method. He also works on idempotent analysis.

* [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victor_Pavlovich_Maslov)

* _[Fiftieth anniversary of research and teaching by Victor Pavlovich Maslov]_, Theoretical and Mathematical Physics, 155(2): 674--677 (2008), transl. from Teoreticheskaya i Matematicheskaya Fizika __155__:2, pp. 197--201 (2008) ([pdf](http://www.maths.ed.ac.uk/~aar/papers/maslovbio.pdf))
