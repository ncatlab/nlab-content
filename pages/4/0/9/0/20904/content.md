## Idea

A _square matrix_ is an $n \times m$-[[matrix]] for $n = m$.

Under [[matrix multiplication]] the $n \times n$ square matrices for fixed $n \in \mathbb{N}$ with values in some [[commutative ring]] $R$ form the _[[matrix algebra]]_ $Mat_n(R)$, inside which one finds [[matrix groups]] etc.
