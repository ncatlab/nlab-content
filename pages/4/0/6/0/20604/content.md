# Totality spaces

## Definition

A **totality space** is an [[arity space]] for $\kappa=\{1\}$.  At least some references add the additional condition that $X = \bigcup \mathcal{U} = \bigcup \mathcal{U}^\perp$.

## References

* Loader, Ralph. *Linear logic, totality and full completeness.* Proceedings Ninth Annual IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science (1994): 292-298.
