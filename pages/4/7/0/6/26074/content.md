__George I. Kac__ (also spelled Kats) was an algebraist with contributions to noncommutative algebra, group rings, Hopf algebras, extensions of Tannaka-Krein duality etc. So called Kac algebras (especially suitable for [[Tannaka duality]] in certain generality) are named after him.

The standard monograph on [[Kac algebra]]s is

* M. Enock, J. M. Schwartz, _Kac algebras and duality of locally compact groups_,  Springer-Verlag, 1992, , x+257 pp. [gBooks](http://books.google.com/books/about/Kac_algebras_and_duality_of_locally_comp.html?id=U6e6aD1gj3oC), [MR94e:46001](https://mathscinet.ams.org/mathscinet-getitem?mr=1215933)

## Selected publications
