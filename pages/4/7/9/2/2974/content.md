###### Str&#248;m's theorem
Let $(X,A)$ and $(Y,B)$ be [[NDR-pairs]]. Their product in the category of topological pairs

$$
(X\times Y,X\times B\cup A\times Y)
$$ 

is also an NDR-pair.
