The *Category Theory Zulip server* 

* [categorytheory.zulipchat.com](https://categorytheory.zulipchat.com/)

is a [Zulip server](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zulip) on the topic of *[[category theory]]*. 

The server was set up in 2020 and is currently active. Messages can be read without an account, but an account is necessary to post. There is an archive of messages available at:

* [Category Theory Zulip archive](https://mattecapu.github.io/ct-zulip-archive/)
