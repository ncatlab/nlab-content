

For $\{U_i \to X\}$ and $\{V_j \to X\}$ covering [[sieve]]s in a [[site]], $V$ is a **refinement** of $U$ if $V$ factors through $U$, i.e. if there is a map $f : J \to I$ of indices and diagrams

$$
  \array{ 
    V_j &&\to&& U_{f(j)}
    \\
    & \searrow && \swarrow
    \\
    && X
  }
$$

for each $j$.
