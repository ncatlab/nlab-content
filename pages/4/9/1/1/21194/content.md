## Idea

In [[nuclear physics]] ([[quantum chromodynamics]]) the _bag model_ is an _ad hoc_ [[effective field theory|effective]] [[model (in theoretical physics)|model]] of [[quark]] [[confinement]] in [[hadrons]], assuming literally that the [[quarks]] are constrained within an impenetrable membrane, the "bag".

The _[[Cheshire cat principle]]_ is the observation that this "bag" is not actually [[observable]].
