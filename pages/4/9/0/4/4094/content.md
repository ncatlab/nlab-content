_Lev Landau_ (&#1051;&#1077;&#1074; &#1044;&#1072;&#1074;&#1080;&#1076;&#1086;&#1074;&#1080;&#1095; &#1051;&#1072;&#1085;&#1076;&#1072;&#1091;) was one of the leading theoretical physicists of 20th century; leading an original school of theoretical physics in Harkov, Soviet Union. He died early as a  consequence of injuries in a traffic accident.
