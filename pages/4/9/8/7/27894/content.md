##Left  and right Fox derivatives

A $K$-linear map $\partial : A \to A$ on an augmented $K$-algebra, $A$, is a *left (resp. a right) Fox derivative* if for all
$a, b \in A$, we have 

$\partial(ab) = \partial(a) aug(b) + a\partial(b)$

 (resp. $\partial(ab) = \partial(a)b + aug(a)\partial(b))$.
