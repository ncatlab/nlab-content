__Paolo Aschieri__ is a mathematical physicist currently at Alessandria, Italy. His interests include noncommutative geometry, field theories (classical and quantum, gauge and gravity, magnetic-electric duality, noncommutative field theories), quantum groups, gerbes and deformation quantization. His coauthors include Castellani, Wess, Jurčo, Schupp, Dimitrijević...
