Set theoretic Yang-Baxter equation is the quantum Yang-Baxter equation in the Cartesian monoidal category of sets. Thus we talk about set-theoretic solutions of (quantum) Yang-Baxter equation.  

Pioneering work

* T. Gateva-Ivanova, M. Van den Bergh, _Semigroups of I-type_,
J. Algebra __206__ (1998) 97-112 

based on some observations from 

* Tatiana Gateva-Ivanova, _Noetherian properties of skew polynomial rings with binomial relations_, Trans. Amer. Math. Soc., 343 (1994) 203-219, _Skew polynomial rings with binomial relations_, J. Algebra __185__ (1996) 710-753

eventually lead to modern theory of [[brace]]s and skew-braces.
