Set theoretic Yang-Baxter equation is the [[quantum Yang-Baxter equation]] in the Cartesian monoidal category of sets. Thus we talk about set-theoretic solutions of (quantum) Yang-Baxter equation.  

Pioneering work

* T. Gateva-Ivanova, [[M. Van den Bergh]], _Semigroups of I-type_,
J. Algebra __206__ (1998) 97-112 

based on some observations from 

* Tatiana Gateva-Ivanova, _Noetherian properties of skew polynomial rings with binomial relations_, Trans. Amer. Math. Soc., 343 (1994) 203-219, _Skew polynomial rings with binomial relations_, J. Algebra __185__ (1996) 710-753

eventually lead to modern theory of [[brace]]s and skew-braces.

* [[Pavel Etingof]], Travis Schedler, Alexandre Soloviev, _Set-theoretical solutions to the quantum Yang-Baxter equation_,  Duke Math. J. 100(2) (1999) 169-209 [doi](10.1215/S0012-7094-99-10007-X) 
* W. Rump, _Braces, radical rings, and the quantum Yang-Baxter equation_, J. Algebra __307__ (2007), no. 1, 153-170 [doi](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jalgebra.2006.03.040)
* Tatiana Gateva-Ivanova, [[Shahn Majid]], _Matched pairs approach to set theoretic solutions of the Yang–Baxter equation_, J. Algebra __319__:4, 15 (2008) 1462-1529 [doi](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jalgebra.2007.10.035)
* F. Cedó, E. Jespers, J. Okniński, _Braces and the Yang–Baxter equation_, Commun. Math. Phys. __327__, 101–116 (2014). [doi](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00220-014-1935-y)
* Aryan Ghobadi, _Skew braces as remnants of co-quasitriangular Hopf algebras in SupLat_, J. Algebra __586__ (2021) 607-642 [doi](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jalgebra.2021.07.006)
