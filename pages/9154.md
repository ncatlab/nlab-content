## Definition

For $V$ a [[vector space]] and $0 \leq r lt dim V$, then the **Grassmannian** $Gr(r,V)$ is the space of all linear [[subspaces]] of $V$ of [[dimension]] $r$.

For $r = 1$ this is the [[projective space]] of $V$.
