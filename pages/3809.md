**Terence Tao** is an Australian-American mathematician, Fields medalist, a Professor at UCLA. His major interests are in analysis (especially PDE-s, [[functional analysis]] and [[harmonic analysis]]), dynamical systems, [[number theory]] (especially the study of *integer sequences*), probability theory, ergodic theory.
