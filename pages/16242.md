## Idea

One way to define [[homotopy colimits]] and [[homotopy limits]] in a [[dg-category]] is to compute them in the [[(infinity,1)-category]] of [[dg-modules]].

## References

See section 2.1 in

* [[B. Toen]], _Derived Azumaya algebras and generators for twisted derived categories_, [arXiv:1002.2599](http://arxiv.org/abs/1002.2599).
