
## Definition

The _logarithmic integral function_ is the [[integral]]

$$
  x\mapsto li(x) \coloneqq \underoverset{0}{x}{\int}\frac{d t}{ln t}
$$ 

over the inverse of the [[logarithm]] of the argument.
