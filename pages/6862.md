## Idea

A _current algebra_ of a [[Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{g}$ is a [[vertex operator algebra]] describing (one chiral piece of) a [[string]] propagating on a [[Lie group]] corresponding to $\mathfrak{g}$.

This appears in the [[WZW-model]] 2d [[sigma-model]] [[CFT]] on $G$.
