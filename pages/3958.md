

A [[manifold]] is a **smooth manifold** (resp. differentiable manifold of class $C^k$) if its transition functions are [[smooth function]]s (resp. of class $C^k$), $\mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}^n$. See [[manifold]] for details.
