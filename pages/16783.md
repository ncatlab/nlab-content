The _regular icosahedron_ is the unique [[regular polyhedron|regular]] [[convex set|convex]] [[polyhedron]] with 20 ([[triangle|triangular]]) faces. One of the [[Platonic solids]].

Its [[symmetry]] [[group]] is called the [[icosahedral group]].
