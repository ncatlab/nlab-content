## Idea

<div style="float:right;margin:0 10px 10px 0;"><img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/eb/Icosahedron.jpg/621px-Icosahedron.jpg" width="100" alt="regular icosahedron" /> <br/> (<a href="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Icosahedron.jpg">image source</a>)</div>

The _regular icosahedron_ is the unique [[regular polyhedron|regular]] [[convex set|convex]] [[polyhedron]] with 20 ([[triangle|triangular]]) faces. One of the [[Platonic solids]].



Its [[symmetry]] [[group]] is called the [[icosahedral group]].
