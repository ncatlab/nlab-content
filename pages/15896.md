## Statement

If a [[3-manifold]] $\Sigma$ (or more generally a [[smooth manifold]] of [[dimension]] $\geq 3$) admits the [[mathematical structure|structure]] of a [[hyperbolic manifold]] of [[finite number|finite]] [[volume]], then this structure is unique up to [[isometry]], and in fact it is uniquely determined already by the [[fundamental group]] $\pi_1(\Sigma)$. 

([Mostow 68](#Mostow68)).
