## Idea

According to the [classification of superconformal symmetry](supersymmetry#ClassificationSuperconformal), there should exists [[superconformal field theories]] in 3 dimensions

[[!include superconformal symmetry -- table]]

Such superconformal [[gauge field theories]] involving [[Chern-Simons theory]] coupled to matter exist and are thought to describe the [[worldvolume]] theory of [[black brane|black]] [[M2-branes]] located at [[ADE-singularities]]:

[[!include 7d spherical space forms -- table]]

The corresponding story for the [[M5-brane]] is the [[6d superconformal gauge field theory]].
