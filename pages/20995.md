## Idea 

A _$T \bar T$-deformation_ of a [[conformal field theory]] is a deformation induced by the term $T \bar T$ quadratic in the [[energy-momentum tensor]]

## References

### General

The original discussion for [[2d CFT]]:

* [[Alexander Zamolodchikov]], _Expectation value of composite field $T \bar T$ in two-dimensional quantum field theory_ ([arXiv:hep-th/0401146](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0401146))

Generalization to [[CFT]] of arbitary dimension

* Marika Taylor, _TT deformations in general dimensions_ ([arXiv:1805.10287](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.10287))

Review:

* [[John Cardy]], _The $T \bar T$ deformation of quantum field theory_, 2018 ([pdf](https://icmp2018.org/sites/icmp/files/slides/0900_cardy_symposia_thursday.pdf), [[CardyTTDeformation.pdf:file]])

* Yunfeng Jiang, _Lectures on solvable irrelevant deformations of 2d quantum field theory_ ([arXiv:1904.13376](https://arxiv.org/abs/1904.13376))

See also

* George Jorjadze, [[Stefan Theisen]], _Canonical maps and integrability in $T \bar T$ deformed 2d CFTs_ ([arXiv:2001.03563](https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.03563))

* [[John Cardy]], Benjamin Doyon, _$T \bar T$deformations and the width of fundamental particles_ ([arXiv:2010.15733](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.15733))



### In $AdS_3/CFT_2$

The interpretation of the $T \bar T$-deformation in [[AdS3-CFT2 duality]] is due to 

* Lauren McGough, Márk Mezei, [[Herman Verlinde]], _Moving the CFT into the bulk with $T \bar T$_, J. High Energ. Phys. (2018) 2018: 10 ([arXiv:1611.03470](https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.03470))

### Of the DBI action

Proposal of a non-abelian [[DBI-action]] via $T \bar T$-deformation:

* T. Daniel Brennan, Christian Ferko, [[Savdeep Sethi]], _A Non-Abelian Analogue of DBI from $T \bar T$ ([arXiv:1912.12389](https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.12389))
