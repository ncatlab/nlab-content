## Idea


Given an [[n-sphere]] $S^n$, regarded under its standard [[embedding]] into [[Cartesian space]] $\mathbb{R}^{n+1}$, then its _equator_ is the [[intersection]] with $\mathbb{R}^n \subset \mathbb{R}^{n+1}$. This devides the $n$-sphere into its to [[hemi-n-spheres]].
