## Idea

A square [[matrix]] $(A_{x,y})\in Mat_{n \times n}(R)$ whose values for $x\neq y$ are all [[zero]] is called a _diagonal matrix_.

## Properties

Diagonal matrices form the [[center]] of the ring of matrices under [[matrix multiplication]].
