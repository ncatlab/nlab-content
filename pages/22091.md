## Idea

By an _oriented submanifold_ one may mean 

1. a [[submanifold]] with [[orientation structure]] on the [[tangent bundle]] of its underlying abstract [[differentiable manifold]];

1. a [[submanifold]] of a [[differentiable manifold]] with [[orientation structure]] on its [[normal bundle]].
