## Idea

In the context of square [[matrices]] an _upper triangular matrix_ is one all whose entries below the diagonal vanish. Analogously for a _lower triangular matrix_.

## Examples

* The [[table of marks]] of a [[finite group]] is a triangular matrix over the [[integers]] ([this Prop.](table+of+marks#TableOfMarksIsInvertibleUpperTriangular)).
