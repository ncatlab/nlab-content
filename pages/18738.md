## Idea

In [[harmonic analysis]], _Parseval' theorem_ states that [[Fourier transform]] is a [[unitary operator]].

For Fourier transform over [[Cartesian spaces]] see e.g. [Hörmander 90, theorem 7.1.6](#Hoermander90), [this prop.](BasicPropertiesOfFourierTransformOverCartesianSpaces).
