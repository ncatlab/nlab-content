## Idea

The _photon_ is the [[gauge boson]] of the [[electromagnetic field]], hence of _[[electromagnetic radiation]]_ and of _[[light]]_.

In [[quantum electrodynamics]] the photon [[interaction|interacts]] with the [[electron]] via the [[electron-photon interaction]].
