## Properties

### Exceptional isomorphisms

+-- {: .num_prop}
###### Proposition

The [[exceptional isomorphism]] [[Spin(5)]] $\simeq$ [[Sp(2)]] ([this Prop.](Spin5#ExceptionalIsoToSp2)) generalizes to

$$
  Pin^\pm(5)
  \;\simeq\;
  Sp(2) 
    \sqcup 
  \omega Sp(2)
  \phantom{AAA}
  \omega^2 = \pm e
$$

where $\omega \in Z\big( Pin^+(5)\big)$ is an element in the [[center of a group|center]] which, for $Pin^+(5)$, squares to the the [[neutral element]] $e$ (corresponding to the [[Clifford algebra]] element $+1$) or, for $Pin^-(5)$, to $-e$ (the Clifford algebra element $-1$).

=---
 
(e.g. [Varlamov 99, Theorem 5](#Varlamov99))
