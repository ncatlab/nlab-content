## Idea

An [[internal groupoid]] in the [[category]] of [[Lie algebroids]].

## Examples

For $\mathcal{G}_\bullet$ a [[Lie groupoid]], forming degreewise the [[tangent Lie algebroid]] yields the tangent Lie algebroid groupoid

$$
  \array{
    T \mathcal{G}_1 &\to& \mathcal{G}_1
    \\
    {}^{\mathllap{d s}}\downarrow \downarrow^{\mathrlap{d t}}
    &&
    {}^{\mathllap{s}}\downarrow \downarrow^{\mathrlap{t}}
    \\
    T \mathcal{G}_0 &\to& \mathcal{G}_0
  }
  \,.
$$
