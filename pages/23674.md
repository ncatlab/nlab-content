## Idea ##

A morphism similar to how injective relations are defined in [[Rel]]

## Definition ##

A morphism $f:hom_A(a,b)$ of a [[dagger 2-poset]] $A$ is an **injective morphism** if $f^\dagger \circ f \leq 1_a$.
