\section{Idea} 

In [[acoustics]] and [[signal processing]], given a [[periodic function|periodic]] [[waveform]] $P$, a **harmonic** is a periodic waveform whose [[frequency]] is an [[integer]] [[multiple]] of the [[fundamental frequency]] of $P$.
