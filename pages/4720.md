_Jakob Nielsen_ was a Danish mathematician and one of the pioneers of the [[combinatorial group theory]]. [[Nielsen-Schreier theorem]], [[HNN-extension]]s, Nielsen–Thurston classification, Nielsen fixed point theorem, [[Nielsen invariant]], [[Fenchel-Nielson coordinates]] on Teichmüller space and Nielsen's method of small cancellations were named after him.
