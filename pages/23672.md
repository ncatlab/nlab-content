## Idea ##

A morphism similar to how functional relations are defined in [[Rel]]

## Definition ##

A morphism $f \in hom_A(a,b)$ of a [[dagger 2-poset]] $A$ is a **functional morphism** or a **partial map** if $f \circ f^\dagger \leq 1_b$.
