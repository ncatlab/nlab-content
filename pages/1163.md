Standard $n$-th homology of CW complex $X$ is isomorphic to the $n$-th homotopy group of free topological commutative monoid on space $X$, which is an infinite symmetric product, that is the colimit $\mathrm{Sym}^\infty X$ (denoted also $SP^\infty$) of the symmetric powers $\mathrm{Sym}^N X = X*X*...*X = (X\times X\times ...\times X)/\Sigma_N$ of $X$:
$$
H_i(X) = \pi_i (\mathrm{colim}_N\, \mathrm{Sym}^N X)
$$
studied in the article

A. Dold, R. Thom, Quasifaserungen und unendliche symmetrische Produkte, Ann. Math. (2) 69 (1959), 239--281.
