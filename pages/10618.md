## Idea

A _fraction_ (or _quotient_) $\frac{x}{y}$ is the expression for the [[product]] of a quantity $x$ with the multiplicative [[inverse]] of a quantity $y$.

Here $x$ is called the _numerator_ and $y$ is called the _denominator_.
