Lipót Fejér was a Hungarian mathematician.
He defended his PhD thesis in 1902 at the University of Budapest, advised by [[Hermann Schwarz]], where he proved his theorem on summability of [[Fourier series]].

His PhD students include [[John von Neumann]].
