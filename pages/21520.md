## Idea

The _Chen-Ruan cohomology_ ([Chen-Ruan 00](#ChenRuan00)) of an [[orbifold]] $\mathcal{X}$ is the traditional [[orbifold cohomology]] with [[real numbers|real]] or [[complex numbers|complex]] [[coefficients]] of its [[inertia orbifold]] $I \mathcal{X}$, hence is the [[rational cohomology]]/[[real cohomology]]/[[complex cohomology]] of the underlying [[topological quotient space]] of $I \mathcal{X}$.

Chen-Ruan cohomology is motivated from the role that [[orbifolds]] play as [[target spaces]] in [[perturbative string theory]], as in the algebraic operation of orbifolds [[2d CFTs]].
