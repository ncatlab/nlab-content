## Idea

A _symplectic orbifold_  is an [[orbifold]] with [[symplectic structure]], hence like a [[symplectic manifold]] but possibly with [[singularities]] modeled on [[fixed points]] of local [[actions]] of [[finite groups]] of [[symplectomorphisms]].
