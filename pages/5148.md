David Abraham Blanc is an algebraic topologist at the Department of Mathematics, University of Haifa.

He got his PhD degree in 1988 from MIT, advised by [[Daniel M. Kan]].

His interests include homotopy operations and [[Pi-algebras]] and  higher-order homotopy operations.
