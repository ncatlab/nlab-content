## Idea

The generalization of the [[Adams spectral sequence]] ([[Adams-Novikov spectral sequence]]) from [[stable homotopy theory]] to [[equivariant stable homotopy theory]].


> one may set up an equivariant analogue of the Adams spectral sequence $[...]$ We prefer not write $[$this$]$ out because this method needs time to mature. ([Adams-Gunawardena-Miller 85](#AdamsGunawardenaMiller85))
