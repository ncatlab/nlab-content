## Idea

$FinCat$ is the [[2-category]] of [[finite categories]] (that is, categories with finitely many objects and finitely many morphisms). $FinCat$ is a [[2-pretopos]] which does not have [[coinserters]], [[coinverters]], or [[coequifiers]]. Also, it does not admit groupoid reflections.
