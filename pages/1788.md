$$

$(E \to X) \in \mathbf{H}_{/X}$ maps to 

$$
  X \stackrel{\vdash E}{\to} Type
$$

defined by [[(∞,1)-pullback|∞-pullback]]

$$
  \array{
     E &\to& \widehat{Type}
     \\
     \downarrow && \downarrow
     \\
     X &\underset{\vdash E}{\to}& Type
  }
$$

an equivalent notion for this is 

$$
  x : X \vdash E(x) : Type
$$
