
Let 

* $B \in kHaus \hookrightarrow kTop$ be

  * a [[compactly generated topological space|compactly generated]] [[Hausdorff space]]

  * a [[homotopy 1-type]]

    \[
      \label{AssumptionThatBaseIsOneTruncated}
      \underset{b \in B}{\forall}\;\;\; \pi_{n \geq 2}(B,b) \;\simeq\; 0
    \]

* $(X \xrightarrow{p_X} B) \in kTop_{/B}$ be 

  * a [[compactly generated topological space]] (i.e. a [[k-space]]) 

  * in the [[slice category]]  over $B$, via a [[Hurewicz fibration]] $p_X \,\in\, hFib$.

\begin{example}
  For $n,N \,\in\, \mathbb{N}$, the map of [[configuration spaces of ordered points]] in the [[plane]] 
$$
  X 
    \;=\; 
  \underset{
    \{ 1, \cdots, n+N \}
  }{Conf}
  \big(
    \mathbb{R}^2
  \big)
  \xrightarrow{\;\;\;}
  \underset{
    \{1, \cdots, N\}
  }{Conf}
  \big(
    \mathbb{R}^2
  \big)
  \;=\;
  B
  \,
$$
(which forgets the position of the first $n$ among $n + N$ points)
satisfies the above assumption, by [this Prop.](configuration+space+of+points#ForgettingPointsIsAFibration) and [this Prop.](configuration+space+of+points#CnfSpaceOfPointsInPlaneIsEMSpace). 
\end{example}

For any [[element]] $b \in B$ write

$$
  \array{
    &&
    X
    \\
    \downarrow
    \\
    B
  }
$$
