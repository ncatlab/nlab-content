## Statement

The isomorphism type of the [[local ring]] of an isolated complex [[hypersurface singularity]] is determined by its Tjurina algebra. In positive characteristic there is an analogue where the local ring is determined by a higher Tjurina algebra instead.

## Literature

* John N. Mather, Stephen S.-T. Yau, _Criterion for biholomorphic equivalence of isolated hypersurface singularities_, Proc. Nat. Acad. Sci. U.S.A. 78 (1981), no. 10, Phys.
