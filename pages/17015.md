## Idea

The limit in which [[F-theory]] reduces to the [[perturbative string theory]] of [[type IIB string theory|type IIB]] is called the _Sen limit_.

Essentially this is the limit where one approaches the [[nodal curve]] [[singularities]] of the F-theory [[elliptic fibration]]. A detailed analysis is in ([Clingher-Donagi-Wijnholt 12](#ClingherDonagiWijnholt12)).
