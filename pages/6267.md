###### Definition


An __umbrella category__ is an [[inhabited set|inhabited]] [[category]] $C$ such that for every [[object]] $X$ in $C$, there is at least one [[subterminal object]] $T$ such that the [[hom-set]] $C(X,T)$ is [[inhabited set|inhabited]] (hence being a singleton).
