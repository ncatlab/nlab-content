## Definition

An element $a \in A$ in a [[C-star algebra]] is called _normal_ of its commutes with its star-adjoint:

$$
  a a^* = a^* a
  \,.
$$ 


## Properties

The [[spectral theorem]] asserts, roughly, that (bounded) normal operators can be diagonalized
