

A **simplicial $C^\infty$-ring** is a [[simplicial object]] in the category of [[generalized smooth algebra|C∞-rings]].

The way $C^\infty$-rings serve as funcion algebras on [[smooth loci]], simplicial $C^\infty$-rings serve as function rings on [[derived smooth manifold]]s.
