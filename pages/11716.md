## Definition

A **tensor triangulated category** is a [[category]] $Ho$ equipped with the structure of 

1. a [[symmetric monoidal category]] $(Ho, \otimes, 1, \tau)$;

1. a [[triangulated category]] $(Ho, \Sigma, CofSeq)$

such that for each object $X \in Ho$ the functors $X \otimes (-) \simeq (-)\otimes X$

1. are [[exact functors]];

1. preserve [[coproducts]] 

(hence are exact [[additive functors]]).

(e.g. [Balmer 05, def. 1.1](#Balmer05), [Stevenson 11](#Stevenson11))
