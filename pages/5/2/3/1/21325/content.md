## Idea 

A _Kerr-Schild spacetime_ $(\mathbb{R}^{d,1}, g)$, is a [[spacetime]] which is [[deformation]] of [[Minkowski spacetime]] $(\mathbb{R}^{d,1}, \eta)$ whose [[pseudo-Riemannian metric]] is of the form

$$
  g_{\mu \nu} =
  \eta_{\mu \nu}
  +
  H \cdot k_\mu k_\nu
$$

where $k$ is a [[lightlike]] [[vector field]] with respect to both metrics, and $H$ is any [[scalar]] [[function]].
