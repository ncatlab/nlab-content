##Definition
The **reverse category** of a [[monoidal category]], $\mathcal{C}$, has the same underlying category and unit as $\mathcal{C}$ but reversed monoidal product, $X \bigotimes^{rev} Y = Y \bigotimes AX$, and similarly for morphisms. The associator in the reverse category is $\alpha^{rev}_{X,Y,Z} = \alpha^{-1}_{Z,Y,X}$.
