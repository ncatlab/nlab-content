__Dennis Gaitsgory__ is a mathematician at Harvard. His research is in [[representation theory]] and [[algebraic geometry]], especially [[D-modules]], [[chiral algebras]] and [[geometric Langlands]].

* [webpage](http://www.math.harvard.edu/~gaitsgde)
