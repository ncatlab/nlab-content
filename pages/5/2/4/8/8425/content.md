
Lou Kauffman is an American [[topology|topologist]] working in [[knot theory]] and its relationships with [[algebra]], [[combinatorics]] and [[physics]], specifically [[statistical mechanics]], [[quantum theory]].

He is based at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

* [homepage](http://homepages.math.uic.edu/~kauffman/)
