## Idea

An [[endofunctor]] on a [[category]] $\mathcal{A}$ is _copointed_ if it is equipped with a [[natural transformation]] to the [[identity functor]].


## Definition
 {#Definition}


\begin{definition}\label{CopointedEndofunctor}
An [[endofunctor]] $S \colon \mathcal{A}\to \mathcal{A}$ is called **copointed** if it is equipped with a [[natural transformation]] $\sigma \colon S \to Id_\mathcal{A}$ to the [[identity functor]] on $\mathcal{A}$.  
\end{definition}
