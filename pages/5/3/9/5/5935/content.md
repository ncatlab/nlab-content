## Definition

Let $\mathbf{H}$ be a [[cohesive (∞,1)-topos]].

We may regard $\mathbf{H}$ as the [[classifying topos]] for a [[theory]] $\mathbb{T}$ of [[local algebra]]s. 

For $\mathcal{X}$ any [[(∞,1)-topos]] a [[geometric morphism]]

$$
  (\mathcal{O}_{\mathcal{X}} \dashv R ) : \mathcal{X} \to \mathbf{H}
$$

therefore exhibits on $\mathcal{X}$ the structure of a [[locally algebra-ed topos|locally T-algebraed topos]]: a [[structured (∞,1)-topos]] ([Lurie](#Lurie)).

Let in addition $i : \mathbf{H} \hookrightarrow \mathbf{H}_{th}$ be an [[cohesive (∞,1)-topos -- infinitesimal cohesion|infinitesimal cohesive neighbourhood]]. This induces a notion of [[formally étale morphism]]s in $\mathbf{H}$.

We say a morphism $\mathcal{O}_{\mathcal{X}} \to \mathcal{O}'_{\mathcal{X}}$ of $\mathbb{T}$-structures is **local**, if its components on [[formally étale morphism]]s are [[(∞,1)-pullback]] squares.
