**Under Construction**

#Definition#

A causal set, or **causet** is a locally finite [[poset]].

A **causal subset** in a poset $X$ is an "interval", namely for two objects $x,y$ in the poset the [[under category|under-over category]]

$$x\darr X\darr y$$

of all objects in the past of $x$ and in the future of $y$.
