## Properties

### Representation

#### $\mathbf{56}$ -- The smallest fundamental representation
 {#SmallestFundamentalRepresentation}

The smallest [[fundamental representation]] of $E_7$ is of [[dimension]] $56$. Under the [[special linear group|special linear]] [[subgroup]] $SL(8,\mathbb{R}) \hookrightarrow E_7$ this decomposes as (e.g. [Cacciatori et al. 10, section 4](#CacciatoriEtAl10))

$$
  \mathbf{56} \simeq \mathbf{28} \oplus \mathbf{28}^\ast \simeq \wedge^2 \mathbb{R}^8 \oplus \wedge^2 (\mathbb{R}^8)^\ast
  \,.
$$

Under the further subgroup inclusion $SL(7,\mathbb{R}) \hookrightarrow SL(8,\mathbb{R}) \hookrightarrow E_7$ this decomposes further as

$$
  \mathbf{56}
  \simeq
  \mathbb{R}^7 \oplus \wedge^2 (\mathbb{R}^7)^\ast \oplus \wedge^5 (\mathbb{R}^7)^\ast \oplus \wedge^6 \mathbb{R}^7
  \,.
$$


### As U-Duality group of 4d SuGra
 {#AsUDualityGroup}

$E_{7(7)}$ is the [[U-duality]] group (see there) of [[11-dimensional supergravity]] [[KK-compactification|compactified]] on a 7-dimensional fiber to [[4-dimensional supergravity]] (e.g. [[M-theory on G2-manifolds]]).

Specifically, ([Hull 07, section 4.4](#Hull07)) identifies the [[vector space]] underlying the $SL(7,\mathbb{R})$-decomposition of the [smallest fundamental representation](#SmallestFundamentalRepresentation) 

$$
  \mathbf{56}
  \simeq
  \mathbb{R}^7 \oplus \wedge^2 (\mathbb{R}^7)^\ast \oplus \wedge^5 (\mathbb{R}^7)^\ast \oplus \wedge^6 \mathbb{R}^7
  \,.
$$

as the [[generalized tangent bundle]]-structure to the 7-dimensional fiber space which one obtains as discussed at _[M-theory supersymmetry algebra -- As an 11-dimensional boundary condition](M-theory+supersymmetry+algebra#AsAn11DimensionalBoundaryCondition)_. Here $\mathbb{R}^7$ is the ordinary [[tangent space]] itself, $\wedge^2 (\mathbb{R}^\ast)^7$ is interpreted as the local incarnation of the possible [[M2-brane]] [[charges]], $\wedge^5 (\mathbb{R}^\ast)^7$ the [[M5-brane]] charges and $\wedge^6 \mathbb{R}^7$ as the charges of [[Kaluza-Klein monopoles]].
