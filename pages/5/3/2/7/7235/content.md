## Idea

One of the [[exceptional Lie groups]].

## Properties

### As U-Duality group of 4d SuGra

$E_7$ is the [[U-duality]] group (see there) of [[11-dimensional supergravity]] [[KK-compactification|compactified]] on a 7-dimensional fiber to 4 dimensions.

According to ([Hull 07, section 4.4](#Hull07)) the $\mathbf{56}$-[[representation]] of $E_7$ decomposes under the subgroup $SL(7,\mathbb{R}) \hookrightarrow E_7$ as

$$
  \mathbf{56}
  \simeq
  \mathbb{R}^7 \oplus \wedge^2 (\mathbb{R}^\ast)^7 \oplus \wedge^5 (\mathbb{R}^\ast)^7 \wedge \wedge^6 (\mathbb{R})^7
  \,.
$$

This is just the [[generalized tangent bundle]]-structure to the 7-dimensional fiber space which one obtains as discussed at _[M-theory supersymmetry algebra -- As an 11-dimensional boundary condition](M-theory+supersymmetry+algebra#AsAn11DimensionalBoundaryCondition)_.
