## Idea

An _10-manifold_ is a [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] 10. (Generally a [[topological manifold]], but it can be specified to a [[PL manifold]] or a [[smooth manifold]].)

## Examples

* 10-[[sphere]]
* O(5)
* [[SO(5)]]
* [[Spin(5)]]$\cong$[[Sp(2)]]
