## Definition

The *totally nonnegative Grassmanian* (informally called the *positive Grassmannian*) $G_+(n,k)$ is the part of the real [[Grassmannian]] $G(n,k)(\mathbf{R}^n)$ which consists of points for which all maximal [[minors]] are nonnegative.
