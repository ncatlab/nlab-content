##Subtitle: a sketch of a programme

This was a document produced by [[Alexander Grothendieck]] that outline a series of interrelated research projects, (see the [Wikipedia page](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esquisse_d%27un_Programme)).

###Summary  of Grothendieck's programme

(English translation with original in brackets.)

("Sommaire")

1. The Proposal and enterpise ("Envoi").

1.     "Teichm&#252;ller's Lego-game and the [[Galois group]] of Q over Q" ("Un jeu de "Lego-Teichm&#252;ller" et le groupe de Galois de Q sur Q").

       (on the relation between the [[absolute Galois group]] and the [[Grothendieck-Teichmüller group]])

1.    Number fields associated with "[[children's drawings]]". ("Corps de nombres associ&#233;s &#224; un [[dessin d'enfant]]").

1.     Regular polyhedra over finite fields ("Poly&#232;dres r&#233;guliers sur les corps finis").

1.    Denunciation of so-called "general" topology, and heuristic reflections 
towards a so-called "tame" topology. 
 ("Haro sur la topologie dite 'g&#233;n&#233;rale', et r&#233;flexions heuristiques vers une topologie dite 'mod&#233;r&#233;e").

1.     Differentiable theories and moderated theories ("Th&#233;ories diff&#233;rentiables" (&#224; la Nash) et "th&#233;ories mod&#233;r&#233;es").

1.    [[Pursuing Stacks]] ("&#192; la Poursuite des Champs"}.

1.     Two-dimensional geometry ("Digressions de g&#233;om&#233;trie bidimensionnelle".

1.   Summary of proposed studies ("Bilan d'une activit&#233; enseignante").

1.    Epilogue.
  
   Notes
