## Idea 

The notion of **model site** is a [[model category]] model for the notion of [[(∞,1)-site]].

An [[(∞,1)-category]] equipped with the structure of a [[site]] on its [[homotopy category of an (∞,1)-category|homotopy category]] serves as a domain for [[∞-stack]]s. It may be modeled by a [[model category]].

A [[model category]] with a [[site]] structure on its [[homotopy category]] is called a pseudo **model site** ([def 4.1.1](http://arxiv.org/PS_cache/math/pdf/0207/0207028v4.pdf#page=43) in [ToVeI](http://arxiv.org/abs/math/0404373)).
