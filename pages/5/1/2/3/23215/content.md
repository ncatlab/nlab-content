## Idea
 {#Idea}

__$Surj$__ (or __$Set_{surj}$__) is the [[wide subcategory]] of the [[topos]] [[Set]] with its [[morphisms]] restricted to [[epimorphisms]] ([[surjective functions]]).

It is the [[free]] [[symmetric monoidal category]] generated by a [[commutative monoid]].
