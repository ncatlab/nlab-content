## Idea

The double [[power set]] construction $\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{P}(X))$ on a set $X$. The double power set represents the set of all subsets of a set $X$, and is important in numerous concepts in [[topology]], such as in the definitions of a [[filter]], a [[topological space]], a [[continuous function]], and a [[complete space]].
