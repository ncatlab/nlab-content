## Idea

$G$-$d$-operads are to [[G-∞-operads]] as [[d-operads]] are to [[∞-operads]].

## Definition

\begin{definition}
  Fix $d \in \mathbb{N}$ a [[natural number]]
  A [[G-∞-operad|$G$-$\infty$-operad]] $\mathcal{O}^{\otimes}$ is **essentially $d$** (or a **$G$-$d$-operad**) if, for all colors $X_1,\dots,X_n,Y \in U\mathcal{O}$, the space $\mathrm{Mul}_{\mathcal{O}}(X_1,\dots,X_n;Y)$ is [[homotopy n-type|$(d-1)$-truncated]] 
\end{definition}
