## Definition

A _$(2,1)$-site_ is an [[(∞,1)-site]]. whose underlying [[(∞,1)-category]] is a [[(2,1)-category]].

An [[(∞,1)-sheaf]] on a $(2,1)$-site is a [[(2,1)-sheaf]]/[[stack]].
