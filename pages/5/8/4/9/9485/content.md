## Idea

The [[distance]] between two [[crests]] of a [[wave]].

## Definition

For $n \in \mathbb{N}$ and

$$
  \vec x \mapsto e^{2 \pi i \vec k \cdot \vec x}
$$

a [[plane wave]] with [[wave vector]] $\vec k \in \mathbb{R}^n$, then its _wavelength_ is the inverse of the [[norm]] of $k$:

$$
  \lambda \coloneqq 1/{\vert k\vert}
  \,.
$$

The quotient $k/{\vert k \vert} \in S(\mathbb{R}^n)$ is the _[[direction of a vector|direction]]_ of the [[plane wave]].
