## Definition ##

A morphism $f \in Hom_A(a,b)$ of a [[dagger 2-poset]] $A$ is an **epic map** if it is an [[onto morphism in a dagger 2-poset|onto]] [[map in a dagger 2-poset|map]]. 

The type of all epic maps in $Hom_A(a,b)$ is defined as 

$$EpiMap(a, b) \coloneqq \sum_{f:Hom_A(a,b)} isOnto(f) \times isMap(f)$$
