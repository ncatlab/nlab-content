#### $\infty$-Lie theory
+--{: .hide}
[[!include infinity-Lie theory - contents]]

=--

The classification of [[simple Lie group]]s consists contains four infinite series of "classical" Lie groups:


* $A_n = SU(n+1)$, the [[special unitary group]] 

* $B_n = SO(2n+1)$, the [[special orthogonal group]]

* $C_n = Sp(n)$, the [[symplectic group]]

* $D_n = SO(2n)$, the [[special orthogonal group]] 


Apart from this there are fixe [[exceptional Lie groups]].
