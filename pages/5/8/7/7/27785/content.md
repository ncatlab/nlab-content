##  Idea 

Just as a [[multicategory]] generalises a [[monoidal category]], a **bimulticategory** generalises a [[monoidal bicategory]].

A **2-multicategory** is a [[Cat]]-[[enriched category|enriched]] multicategory, which is a strict bimulticategory.

(Note that the term **[[multibicategory]]** is used in the literature for a different concept: namely a [[virtual bicategory]].)

## References

Bimulticategories (and their pseudofunctors, transformations, and modifications) are introduced in Definition 4.2.29 of the following, where it is also proven that every [[monoidal bicategory]] induces a bimulticategory:

* Philip Saville, *Cartesian closed bicategories: type theory and coherence*. PhD thesis, 2020. [pdf](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2007.00624.pdf).

A definition of [[representable multicategory|representable]] bimulticategory is proposed therein, but the relationship with [[monoidal bicategories]] is not established.
