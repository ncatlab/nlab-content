## Idea

*Sub-Riemannian geoemtry* is a generalization of [[Riemannian geometry]] where the [[metric tensor]] is not necessarily defined on the whole [[tangent bundle]] but on a smooth [[sub-bundle]] (a *[[distribution of subspaces]]*), then called the subbundle of *horizontal* [[vector fields]].

Typically authors require further conditions on the horizontal subbundle, notably the *bracket-generating condition* (also called the *HÃ¶rmander condition* or *Chow-Rashevskii condition*) which states that the operation of forming iterated [[Lie brackets]] of [[germs]] of horizontal [[vector fields]] yields all germs of all [[vector fields]].
