## Idea

The _homotopy 2-category_ of an [[(∞,n)-category]] $\mathcal{C}$ is the [[2-category]] $Ho_2(\mathcal{C})$ with the same [[objects]] and [[1-morphisms]] as $\mathcal{C}$ and with the [[2-morphisms]] being the [[equivalence classes]] of 2-morphisms of $\mathcal{C}$.

In other words, for every [[pair]] $X,Y$ of [[objects]] in $\mathcal{C}$, the [[hom-category]] $Ho_2(\mathcal{C})(X,Y)$ is the ordinary [[homotopy category]] of the $(\infty,n-1)$-category $\mathcal{C}(X,Y)$.
