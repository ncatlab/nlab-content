## Idea 

Given a [[manifold]] $\Sigma$, such as the [[3-sphere]], its _space of knots_ is a [[topological space]] parametrizing [[knots]] in $\Sigma$, hence something like the [[topological subspace]] 

$$
  Emd(S^1, \Sigma)
  \;\subset\;
  Maps(S^1,\Sigma)
$$

of the [[space of maps]] $S^1 \to \Sigma$ on those maps which are [[embedding of smooth manifolds|embeddings]]. This space is often denoted just $\mathcal{K}$.

A [[knot invariant]] is then equivalently a [[locally constant function]] on the space of knots.
