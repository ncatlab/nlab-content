## Idea

Composite Higgs Models are speculative [[models (in theoretical physics)]] which explore the possibility that the [[Higgs boson]] is an excitation of a new [[non-perturbative effect|strongly interacting sector]] giving rise to [[electro-weak symmetry breaking]].
