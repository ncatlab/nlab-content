## Idea 

A _$T \overline{T}$-deformation_ of a [[conformal field theory]] is a deformation induced by the term $T \bar T$ quadratic in the [[energy-momentum tensor]]

## References

### General

The original discussion for [[2d CFT]]:

* [[Alexander Zamolodchikov]], _Expectation value of composite field $T \bar T$ in two-dimensional quantum field theory_ ([arXiv:hep-th/0401146](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0401146))

Generalization to [[CFT]] of arbitary dimension

* Marika Taylor, _TT deformations in general dimensions_ ([arXiv:1805.10287](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.10287))

Review:
