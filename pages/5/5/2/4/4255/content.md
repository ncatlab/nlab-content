**Dusa McDuff** is a English mathematician, who worked in the USA. She has proved profound results in [[operator algebra]] and [[differential geometry]]. In recent years she has concentrated on [[symplectic geometry|symplectic]], [[contact geometry]] and [[symplectic topology]].
