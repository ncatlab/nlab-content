## Idea

In [[quantum information theory]], by *quantum state tomography* one means [[quantum measurement]]-protocols which, when applied successively to copies of one and the same [[quantum state]] ([[quantum state collapse|collapsing]] one copy at a time), allow to determine that quantum state, to prescribed accuracy.
