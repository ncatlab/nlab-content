## Idea

The concept of _adelic integration_ is a concept of [[integration]] in [[arithmetic geometry]] over [[rings of adeles]] ([Fesenko 08, chapter 3](#Fesenko08)).

## Examples

* The [completed Riemann zeta function](Riemann+zeta+function#TheCompletedZetaFunction) (see there) is naturally an adelic integral.
