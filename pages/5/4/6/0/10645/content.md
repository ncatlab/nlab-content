## Idea

A [[higher chromatic Chern character]] for [[Morava E-theory]].

The construction in ([Torii 03](#Torii03)) produces a map from [[Morava E-theory]] at [[chromatic level]] $(n+1)$ to [[Morava K-theory]] of [[chromatic level]] $n$, tensored over something.  This is induced by degenration of the corresponding [[formal group laws]].
