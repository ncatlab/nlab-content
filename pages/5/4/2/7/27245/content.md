## Idea

A **lift** or **lifting** of a morphism $f: Y\to B$ through an epimorphism (or more general map) $p:X\to B$, is a morphism $\tilde{f}: Y\to X$ such that $f = p\circ\tilde{f}$.

This is the [[duality|dual]] concept to [[extension]].
