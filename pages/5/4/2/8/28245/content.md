## Idea

*D=6 [[Yang-Mills theory]]* (D=6 YM theory) studies the [[Yang-Mills equations]] over a base [[manifold]] of [[dimension]] $D=6$, hence a smooth [[6-manifold]]. A special case is over [[Calabi-Yau manifold|Calabi-Yau 3-manifolds]].
