## Definition

The _primary homotopy operations_ are the operations generated by

* the [[Whitehead products]];

and

* the [[composition operations]].

## Properties

The primary homotopy operations encode via [[Pi-algebras]] (see there for more) into an algebraic structure akin to [[models]] for a [[Lawvere theory]].
