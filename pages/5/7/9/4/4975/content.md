Professor Glynn Winskel is a professor in the Computer Laboratory,
University of Cambridge.  He was formerly Professor at the Computer Science Department, University of Aarhus and
Director of [[BRICS]] (centre for Basic Research in Computer Science).

* [home page](http://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~gw104/)
