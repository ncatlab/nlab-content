### Jones polynomial as Wilson loop observables
  {#ReferencesJonesPolynomialAsWilsonLoopObservables}


The identification of the [[Jones polynomial]] with [[Wilson loop observables]] in [[Chern-Simons theory]] is due to

* {#Witten89} [[Edward Witten]], _Quantum field theory and the Jones polynomial_, Commun. Math. Phys. 121,351-399 (1989) ([euclid.cmp/1104178138](https://projecteuclid.org/euclid.cmp/1104178138))
  
see also

* [[Jürg Fröhlich]], C. King, _The Chern-Simons theory and knot polynomials_, Comm. Math. Phys.
Volume 126, Number 1 (1989), 167-199 ([euclid.cmp/1104179728](https://projecteuclid.org/euclid.cmp/1104179728))

Lecture notes:

* {#Witten13a} [[Edward Witten]], _A New Look At The Jones Polynomial of a Knot_, Clay Conference, Oxford, October 1, 2013 ([[WittenOnJonesPolynomial2013.pdf:file]])
