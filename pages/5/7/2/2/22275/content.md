## Idea

An analogue of the [[Gelfand duality]] for [[Riesz spaces]].

## Statement

The category of [[compact Hausdorff topological spaces]]
and [[continuous maps]] is contravariantly [[equivalent]]
to the category of uniformly complete Archimedean unital [[Riesz spaces]].
