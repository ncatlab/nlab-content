## Idea

The notion of _surface knots_ is the next higher [[dimension|dimensional]] analog of that of _[[knots]]_: Where a [[knot]] is a 1-dimensional [[submanifold]] of the [[Euclidean space|3d Euclidean space]]/[[3-sphere]], so a _surface knot_ is a 2-dimensional submanifold (hence a [[surface]]) of the [[Euclidean space|4d Euclidean space]]/[[4-sphere]].
