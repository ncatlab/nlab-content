## Idea

In the [[standard model of particle physics]] the _top quark_ is the down-type [[quark]] in the third [[generation of fermions]], a [[heavy quark]].

The [[bound states]] of two (anti-)bottom quarks are the _[[B mesons]]_. 

Hints of [[flavour anomalies]] indicate that something peculiar may be going on with bottom quarks.
