## Idea

*Super-modular tensor categories* are to [[modular tensor categories]] as [[spin Chern-Simons theory]] is to [[Chern-Simons theory]]. Concretely, these are pre-modular tensor categories whose [[MÃ¼ger center]] is equivalent as a [[symmetric monoidal category]] to that of [[super vector spaces]].
