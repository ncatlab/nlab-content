## Idea

A [[partial differential equation]] is said to be _formally integrable_ (e.g. [Goldschmidt 67, def. 7.2](#Goldschmidt67)) if it is [[integrable PDE|integrable]] at least over [[infinitesimal neighbourhoods]] (aka "formal neighbourhoods", whence the name).
