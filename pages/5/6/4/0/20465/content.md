## Idea

A [[duality in string theory]]: relation between the 3d [[superconformal geometry|superconformal]] [[worldvolume]] [[quantum field theory]] on [[M5-brane]] [[wrapped brane|wrapped]] on a ([[hyperbolic 3-manifold|hyperbolic]]) [[3-manifold]] and 3d [[Chern-Simons theory]]/[[analytically continued Chern-Simons theory]]. 

Possibly the conceptual reason behind the truth of the [[volume conjecture]] ([Gang-Kim-Lee 14, 3.2](#GangKimLee14))

## Related concepts

[[wrapped brane|Wrapping]] the [[M5-brane]] on a [[torsion subgroup|torsion]] 3-cycle yields: [[fractional M2-brane]].

[[wrapped brane|Wrapping]] the [[M5-brane]] instead on a 2-manifold yields: [[AGT correspondence]].
