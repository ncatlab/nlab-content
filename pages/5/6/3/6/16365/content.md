## In continuum mechanics / solid state physics

In [[elasticity theory]] (in the context of [[continuum mechanics]]/[[solid state physics]]) given _[[stress tensor]]_ expressing [[forces]] acting inside a [[solid body]], then the corresponding _strain tensor_ expresses the deformation of the body in reaction to these forces.
