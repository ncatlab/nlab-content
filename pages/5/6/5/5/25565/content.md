Pjotr Sergeevič Novikov also spelled Pyotr Sergeyevich Novikov was a mathematicial logician from Soviet Union. 
He has shown that the word problem in some abstract groups is undecidable. Topologist [[Sergei Novikov]], Fields medalist, is his son.
