## Idea

A _modular operad_ is an [[operad]] that has the kind of extra structure which is exhibited by the operad whose $n$-ary operations are [[Riemann surface]]s with $(n+1)$-punctures, and whose composition is sewing of such surfaces. Every modular operad has a structure of a [[cyclic operad]] and also some additional structure. 

## Motivations

* [[2d TQFT]]
* constructing [[graph complex]]es of Kontsevich as Feynman transforms of suitable [[twisted modular operad]]s
