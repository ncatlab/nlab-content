## Idea

A _modular operad_ ([Getzler & Kapranov 1998](#GetzlerKapranov1998)) is an [[operad]] that has the kind of extra structure which is exhibited by the operad whose $n$-ary operations are [[Riemann surface]]s with $(n+1)$-punctures, and whose composition is sewing of such surfaces. Every modular operad has a structure of a [[cyclic operad]] and further additional structure. 

## Motivations

* [[2d TQFT]]

* constructing [[graph complexes]] of Kontsevich as [[Feynman transforms]] of suitable *twisted modular operads*
