## Idea

Given a [[field]] $F$, let $\overline{F}$ denote its [[algebraic closure]]. A *square-free polynomial* is a univariate [[polynomial]] $q \in \overline{F}[x]$ such that for all [[elements]] $a \in \overline{F}$, $(x - a)^2$ does not divide $q$.
