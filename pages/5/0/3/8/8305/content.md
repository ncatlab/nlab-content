## Definition

For $g,h \in G$ a [[pair]] of [[elements]] of a [[group]], their **group commutator** is the element

$$
  [g,h] 
    \coloneqq 
  g^{-1} \cdot h^{-1} \cdot g \cdot h
  \,.
$$

This definition clearly generalizes to  [[invertible semigroups]].
