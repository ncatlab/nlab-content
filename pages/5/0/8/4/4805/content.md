For an [[object]] $X$ in a [[category]] with [[coproduct]]s, the **codiagonal** of $X$ is the canonical [[morphism]]

$$
  \nabla : X \coprod X \stackrel{(Id,Id)}{\to} X
  \,.
$$

The dual concept is _[[diagonal]]_ .
