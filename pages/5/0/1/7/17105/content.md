The Fano plane is the [[projective plane]] over the [[finite field]] $\mathbf{F}_2$ with two elements. It is denoted either $\mathbb{F}_2\mathbb{P}^2$, $\mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{F}_2}$ (in [[algebraic geometry]]), or $PG(2,2)$ (in finite geometry). It is often pictured as at right, but there are many different representations.

## Properties

### Automorphisms

The [[automorphism group]] $PGL(3,\mathbb{F}_2)$ is isomorphic to the [[projective special linear group]] $PSL(2,\mathbb{F}_7)$.
