## Idea

An [[algebraic definition of higher categories|algebraic definition]] of [[∞-groupoid]]s and [[∞-categories]] similar to (but older than) the definition [[Batanin ω-categories]].

## References

The original definition is indicated in words in sections 1-13 of

* [[Alexander Grothendieck]], _[[Pursuing Stacks]]_

This has been extracted and formalized in 

* [[Georges Maltsiniotis]], _Infini groupo&iuml;des d'apr&egrave;s Grothendieck_ ([web](http://www.math.jussieu.fr/~maltsin/textes.html))

A comprehensive account of a simplified version of the definition and its generalization from $\infty$-groupoids to $\infty$-categories is given in

* [[Georges Maltsiniotis]], _Grothendieck $\infty$-groupoids and still another definition of $\infty$-categories_ ([arXiv:1009.2331](http://arxiv.org/abs/1009.2331))

A definition as [[models]] of a [[dependent type theory]] is given in

* [[Eric Finster]], [[Samuel Mimram]], _A Type-Theoretical Definition of Weak ω-Categories_ ([arxiv:1706.02866](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.02866))

The relation to [[Batanin ω-categories]] is discussed in 

* {#Ara10} [[Dimitri Ara]], _Sur les $\infty$-groupo&iuml;des de Grothendieck_ Thesis, under the supervision of G. Maltsiniotis, (2010) ([pdf](http://www.normalesup.org/~ara/files/these.pdf))

A brief survey is provided in

* [[Mike Shulman]], ([blog entry](http://golem.ph.utexas.edu/category/2010/09/grothendieckmaltsiniotis_categ.html))
