## Idea

The concept of **Borcherds algebra** or **Borcherds-Kac-Moody algebra** is a generalization of that of [[Kac-Moody algebra]] (hence also called **generalized Kac-Moody algebra**) obtained by allowing imaginary [[root (in representation theory)|simple roots]].
