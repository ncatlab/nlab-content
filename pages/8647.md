## Idea

The _femotmeter_ is a [[physical unit]] of [[length]].

## Definition

A **femtometer** (abbreviated $fm$) is one $10^{15}$th of a [[meter]]:

$$
  fm \coloneqq 10^{-15} m
  \,.
$$

The [[radius]] of the [[proton]] is about $0.8 \, fm$.
