##Idea 

In the context of [[Schur-Weyl duality]], given a word of length $n$ over the alphabet $[1, N]$, one can associate to it two tableaux of size $n$ and the same shape by using the **Robinson-Schensted-Knuth algorithm** or **correspondence**, the first of these is a [[semistandard Young tableau]] with entries in $[1,N]$ and the second a [[standard Young tableau]] ([Meliot 10, p. 3](#Meliot10)).
