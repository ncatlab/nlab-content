## Idea

A generalization of the notion of [[C*-algebras]]. Used in ([Joachim-Johnson 07](#JoachimJohnson07)) order to have a [[model category]] presentation of [[KK-theory]] instead of just a [[category of fibrant objects]]-structure (as described at [[homotopical structure on C*-algebras]]).
