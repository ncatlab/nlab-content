## Idea

Considering a class of diffeomorphisms which are not isometries but change metrics a tiny bit, with some asymptotic boundary conditions. 

This is useful to obtain conformal symmetry algebras in some physical theories of gravity, esp. in 3D. 

* J. Brown, M. Henneaux, _Central charges in the canonical realization of asymptotic symmetries: an example from three-dimensional gravity_, Comm. Math. Phys. __104__ (1986) 207 (key paper!!) [euclid](http://projecteuclid.org/euclid.cmp/1104114999)

* Coussaert, M. Henneaux, P. van Driel, Class. Quant. Grav. __12__ (1995) 2961

...Brown-Henneaux boundary condition

* Marc Henneaux, Claudio Teitelboim, _Asymptotically anti-de Sitter spaces_,  Comm. Math. Phys. __98__, Number 3 (1985), 391-424, [euclid](http://projecteuclid.org/euclid.cmp/1103942446)
