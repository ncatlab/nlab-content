**Max Karoubi** is a French topologist, with main interests in algebraic and topological K-theory, cyclic (co)homology and [[noncommutative geometry]] a la Connes. He introduced the noncommutative differential forms to generalize the de Rham complex to [[noncommutative geometry]]. See also [[Karoubian envelope]].

* _$K$-theory. An introduction_, Grundlehren der Mathematischen Wissenschaften, Band __226__, Springer 1978. xviii+308 pp. 

* _Lectures on K-theory_, Proc. of the Trieste Conference in 2002, Contemporary Developments in Algebraic K-theory, ICTP Lecture notes __15__ (2003).

* _Algebraic Topology via Differential Geometry_ (with Christian Leruste). Cambridge Univ. Press (1987), based on _Méthodes de géométrie différentielle en topologie algébrique_ (1982, Paris 7)

* _Homologie cyclique et K-théorie_, Astérisque __149__. Société Mathématique de France (1987).
