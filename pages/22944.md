Daniel Bennequin is a French mathematician who gave the first example of an exotic [[contact structure]] on $\mathbb{R}^3$ in his dissertation. He was a [member](https://books.google.com.au/books?id=-CXn6y_1nJ8C&pg=PA17) of [[Bourbaki]].

* [Wikipedia entry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_Bennequin)
