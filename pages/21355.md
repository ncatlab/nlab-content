## Idea

In [[quantum hadrodynamics]], _charmonium_ $J/\psi$ is the [[charm quark|charm]]-form of _[[quarkonium]]_: a [[meson]] which is a [[bound state]] of a [[charm quark]] and its [[antiparticle]].
