## Definition

Let $\mathcal{M}$ be a moduli space parametrizing objects $P$ that come with $\mathbb{C}$ vector spaces $H^{i}(P)$ for $i\in \mathbb{Z}$ (e.g. the objects can be studied through a cohomology theory).

The **cohomology jump loci** of $\mathcal{M}$ are defined as

$$
  \mathcal{V}^{i}_{k} = \{P\in \mathcal{M} | \dim H^{i}(P) \geq k\}
  \,.
$$

And for every $i$ there is a chain of inclusions


$$
  \mathcal{M}=\mathcal{V}^{i}_{0}\subset\mathcal{V}^{i}_{1}\subset\mathcal{V}^{i}_{2}\subset
  \,...
$$
