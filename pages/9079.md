## Definition

For $G$ a [[compact Lie group]] with [[Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{g}$, a **Cartan subalgebra** of $\mathfrak{g}$ is a sub-Lie algebra 

$$
  \mathfrak{t} \hookrightarrow \mathfrak{g}
$$

that is the Lie algebra of a [[maximal torus]] 

$$
  T \hookrightarrow G
  \,.
$$
