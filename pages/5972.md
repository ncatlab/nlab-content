[[Madhav Nori]] invented an original approach to motives, in the spirit of Tannakian theory, but based on a specific [[Nori's Tannakian theorem]]. Unfortuntaley his original work, which has been lectured on some summer schools and conferences is not widely available in written form. Kontsevich based his approach to periods and their appearance in deformation quantization partly on Nori's insight.
