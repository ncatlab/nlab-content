## Idea

A kind of [[character]] for [[loop group representations]] (e.g. [Gordon 08, def 5.11](#Gordon08)).

The _[[Weyl-Kac character formula]]_ expresses it for integrable highest weigth representations in terms of the weights (e.g. [Gordon 08, theorem 18.16](#Gordon08)).

## Properties

### Jacobi form and relation to Witten genus

On an integral highest weight representation the KW character is a [[Jacobi form]], closely related to the [[Witten genus]] ([KL 95](#KL95)). See also at _[[equivariant elliptic cohomology]]_.
