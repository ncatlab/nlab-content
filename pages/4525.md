A **Gray-group** is a semistrict version of a [[3-group]]: it is a 3-group whose [[delooping]] is a one-object [[Gray groupoid]].

Gray groups are modeled by [[2-crossed module]] in analogy to how [[strict 2-group]]s are modeled by [[crossed module]]s.
