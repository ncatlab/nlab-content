## Idea

In [[quantum field theory]] the _time slice axiom_ is an additional axiom often considered on top of the basic [[Haag-Kastler axioms]]: it encodes the property that the time evolution of the classical field theory underlying a [[QFT]] is governed by well-defined initial value problems.

## Definition

A [[local net of observables]] $A : Op(X) \to C^\ast Alg$ satisfies the **time slice axiom** if whenever a causal inclusion of [[Lorentzian space]]s $O_1 \hookrightarrow O_2$ is such that $O_1$ contains a [[Cauchy surface]] of $O_2$ then $A(O_1 \hookrightarrow O_2)$ is an [[isomorphism]].
