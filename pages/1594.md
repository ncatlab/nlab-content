An _orbifold_ is essentially a certain kind of [[Lie groupoid]]. People say "orbifold" when they want to think of the Lie groupoid as a generalization of the quotient of a [[manifold]] by a group action (and that's also how the original definition of orbifold is phrased).


* Ieke Moerdijk, _Orbifolds as Groupoids: an Introduction_ ([arXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/math.DG/0203100) [blog](http://golem.ph.utexas.edu/string/archives/000733.html))

* Eugene Lerman, _Orbifolds as stacks?_ ([arXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/0806.4160))
