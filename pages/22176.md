## Idea

The _Thompson group_ is a [[sporadic finite simple group]] in the third generation of the [[Happy Family]]. It is of order $2^{15}\cdot 3^{10}\cdot 5^3 \cdot 7^2 \cdot 13 \cdot 19 \cdot 31 = 90745943887872000$. 

(Note that this group is different from the three [[Thompson groups]].)
