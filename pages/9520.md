## Idea

A _dense subalgebra_ of a [[topological algebra]] is a [[subalgebra]] which is also a [[dense subspace]].

For instance where a [[C-star-algebra]] behaves like the algebra oc [[continuous functions]] on a suitable [[topological space]], there ma be certain dense subalgebras that behave like the subalgebra of [[smooth functions]] for some [[smooth structure]].
