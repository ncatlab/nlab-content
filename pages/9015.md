## Definition

For $(\mathcal{C}, \otimes)$ a [[monoidal (∞,1)-category]], is **Picard $\infty$-group** is the [[∞-group]] induced on the full sub-[[∞-groupoid]] $PIC(\mathcal{C}, \otimes)$ on the objects that are invertible under the tensor product.
