## Idea

Every [[complex oriented cohomology theory]] induces a [[formal group]]. The _Landweber exact functor theorem_ show that conversely forming the [[tensor product]] of [[complex cobordism cohomology theory]] ([[MU]]) with a [[ring]] via some [[formal group law]] yields a [[cohomology theory]].
