[[!redirects J. Giraud]]

Jean Giraud (1936 &#8211; March 28, 2007) was a French mathematician, who worked on the theory of toposes and also, extensively, on non-abelian cohomology.

* [Wikipedia entry](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean_Giraud_%28mathematician%29)
