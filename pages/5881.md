## Idea

The _gluon_ is the [[gauge boson]] of the $SU(3)$-[[Yang-Mills field]] in [[QCD]].

A [[vector boson]] in the [[standard model of particle physics]].

The [[bound states]] of gluons are [[glueballs]].
