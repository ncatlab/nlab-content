Hyperfinite constructions of [[nonstandard analysis]] were used in [[homology]] and [[cohomology]] theory by M. C. McCord, Rade Živaljević and Jerome Keisler ([wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Howard_Jerome_Keisler), [homepage](http://math.wisc.edu/~keisler)).

See

* Rade T. Živaljević, _On a cohomology theory based on hyperfinite sums of microsimplexes_, Pacific J. Math. Volume 128, Number 1 (1987), 201-208; ([project euclid](http://projecteuclid.org/euclid.pjm/1102699443))

* M. C. McCord, _Non-standard analysis and homology_, Fund. Math., 74 (1972), 21-28; ([pdf](http://matwbn.icm.edu.pl/ksiazki/fm/fm74/fm7412.pdf))
