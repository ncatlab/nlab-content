## Idea

The [[homotopy category of an (∞,1)-category]] of [[∞Grpd]]. [[equivalence of categories|equivalent]] to [[Ho(Top)]]. Obtained as the [[localization]] of [[sSet]] at the [[weak homotopy equivalences]]. This is the classical [[homotopy category]].

## Properties

### H-Monoids

A [[monoid object]] in $Ho(\infty Grpd)$ is an _[[H-monoid]]_.
