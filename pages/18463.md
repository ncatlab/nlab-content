
The page collects material related to

* [[Henri Epstein]] and [[Vladimir Glaser]], 

  _The Role of locality in perturbation theory_, Annales Poincaré Phys. Theor. A 19 (1973) 211.

on [[renormalization]] of [[quantum field theory]] compatible with [[causal locality]]. This is the basis for [[causal perturbation theory]] and [[locally covariant perturbative quantum field theory]].

This is based on earlier work 

* Stückelberg, E. C. G., and Peterman, A., _La normalisation des constants dans la theorie des quanta_ Helv. Phys. Acta 26, 499 (1953); and earlier references therein

* Bogoliubov, N. N., and Shirkov, D. V., _Introduction to the Theory of Quantized Fiels_, New York: John Wiley and Sons, 1976, 3rd edition

Hence one speaks of the _Stückelberg-Bogoliubov-Epstein-Glaser method
of [[renormalization]]_, for instance in

* {#BrunettiFredenhagen00} [[Romeo Brunetti]], [[Klaus Fredenhagen]], _Microlocal Analysis and Interacting Quantum Field Theories: Renormalization on Physical Backgrounds_, Commun. Math. Phys. 208 : 623-661,2000 ([math-ph/9903028](https://arxiv.org/abs/math-ph/9903028))
