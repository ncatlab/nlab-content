## Idea

A $(2,1)$-sheaf is a [[sheaf]] with values in [[groupoid]]s. This is traditionally called a [[stack]].

## Definition

Let $C$ be a [[(2,1)-site]]. Write [[Grpd]] for the [[(2,1)-category]] of [[groupoid]]s, [[functor]]s and [[natural isomorphism]]s.

A **$(2,1)$-sheaf** on $C$ is equivalently

* a [[2-functor]] $C^{op} \to Grpd$ that satisfies [[descent]];

* a [[2-sheaf]] with values in [[groupoid]]s;

* a [[1-truncated]] [[(∞,1)-sheaf]] on $C$.


## The $(2,1)$-category of $(2,1)$-sheaves

The [[(2,1)-category]] of a $(2,1)$-sheaves on a [[(2,1)-site]] forms a [[(2,1)-topos]].

There are [[model category]] [[presentable (infinity,1)-category|presentations]] of this $(2,1)$-topos. See [[model structure for (2,1)-sheaves]].
