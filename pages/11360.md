## Idea

A [[sheaf of vertex operator algebras]] which in degree-0 coincides with the [[structure sheaf]] of the base space is a kind of "[[horizontal categorification]]" of [[vertex operator algebra]], hence also called a _vertex operator algebroid_ or _vertex algebroid_ for short.
