For $p \geq 2$ a [[prime number]] , then [[quotient]] $\mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z}$ of the [[ring]] of [[integers]] by $p$ is a [[finite field]] denoted $\mathbb{F}_p$, a _prime field_.

Prime fields have [[positive characteristic]].
