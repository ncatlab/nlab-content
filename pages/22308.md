## Definition

A __Malcev group__ is a [[filtered group]] $G=F_1 G\supset F_2 G\supset\cdots$
such that

* the [[associated graded]] [[Lie algebra]] $gr(G)$ over [[integers]]
is uniquely divisible, hence a rational [[Lie algebra]];

* the graded rational [[Lie algebra]] $gr(G)$ is generated as a [[Lie algebra]] by its degree 1 component;

* the [[filtered object]] $G$ is complete: the canonical map $G\to lim_n G/F_n G$
is an [[isomorphism]].

Morphisms of Malcev groups are morphisms of [[filtered groups]].

## Properties

The category of Malcev groups is equivalent to the category of rational [[complete Hopf algebras]].  See there for more information.
