##Barycenter of a simplex
+--{: .un_defn}
######Definition
If $\sigma = \{ v_0, \ldots, v_q\} \in K_q$, the set of $q$-simplices of a [[simplicial complex]], $K$, then its **barycentre**, $b(\sigma)$, is the point 

$$b(\sigma) = \sum_{0\leq i \leq q}\frac{1}{q + 1} v_i \in |K|.$$
=--
