## Idea

A _subgroup_ of a [[group]] $G$ is a "smaller" group $K$ sitting inside $G$.

## Definition

A _subgroup_ is a [[subobject]] in the [[category]] [[Grp]] of [[group]]s: a [[monomorphism]] of groups

$$
  K \hookrightarrow G
  \,.
$$

Here $K$ is _a subgroup of $G$_.
