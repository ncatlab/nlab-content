## Definition

The _empty diagram_ in some [[category]] is the [[diagram]] indexed by the [[empty category]]. 

## Properties

The [[limit]] over the empty diagram is, if it exists, the [[terminal object]].

The [[colimit]] over the empty diagram is, if it exists, the [[initial object]].
