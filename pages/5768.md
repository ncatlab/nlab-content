Tropical geometry is often thought as an [[algebraic geometry]] over the [[tropical semiring]]. Good part of it is combinatorial in nature, with relations to the (geometry and combinatorics of) polyhedra and toric geometry. Recently it found applications in explaining [[mirror symmetry]] at more fundamental level.
