## Idea

The _Teichmüller space_ $\mathcal{T}_{\Sigma}$ of a  ([[closed manifold|closed]] ) 2-dimenional [[manifold]] $\Sigma$  is the [[moduli stack of complex structures]] on $\Sigma$ (in fact an [[orbifold]]). Closely related to the [[moduli space of conformal structures]] hence of  [[Riemann surfaces]].

Since the [[diffeomorphism]] class of  a [[closed manifold]] of dimension 2 is given by its [[genus]] $g\in \mathbb{Z}$, typically one speaks of the Teichmüller space $\mathcal{T}_g$ for each $g$. More generally one considers $\Sigma$s equipped with $n$ punctures/[[boundary]] circle and then writes $\mathcal{T}_{g,n}$. Hence $\mathcal{T}_{1,1}$ is the [[moduli stack of elliptic curves]] over the complex numbers.

## Properties

### Complex structure on Teichmüller space
 {#ComplexStructureOnTeichmüllerSpace}

Teichmüller space itself carries a [[complex structure]]. 

This was envisioned in ([Teichmüller 44](#Teichmuller44)) and proven in ([Ahlfors 60](#Ahlford60), [Bers 60](#Bers60)).
