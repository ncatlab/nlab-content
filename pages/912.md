# Frechet--Urysohn spaces
* tic
{: toc}


## Idea

A Frechet--Urysohn (or Frechet--Uryson) space is a [[topological space]] in which the [[closed subspace|closure]] of a [[subspace]] may be described using only [[sequences]].


## Definition

Recall that, given any subset $A$ of *any* topological space, a point $x$ belongs to the closure of $A$ if and only if $x$ is a limit point of at least one [[net]] whose elements belong to $A$.

A topological space is __Frechet--Uryson__ (or __Frechet--Urysohn__) if a point $x$ of the closure of any given subset $A$ of $X$ is a limit point of at least one [[sequence]] whose elements belong to $A$.
