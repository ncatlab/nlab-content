## Idea

A _tower_ is a [[diagram]] of the shape of the [[poset]] of [[natural numbers]]

$$
  \cdots \to X_2 \to X_1 \to X_0 
  \,.
$$


A [[limit]] over a diagram of this form is called a [[sequential limit]]/[[directed limit]].
