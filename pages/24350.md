## Idea

_Arithmetic gauge theory_ is a theory proposed by [[Minhyong Kim]] to look at [[Galois representations]] with an action of another group (the [[gauge group]]) as being analogous to [[gauge fields]]. It is an approach to Diophantine problems such as the effective [[Mordell conjecture]].
