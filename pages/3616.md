**Marius Dadarlat** is a Professor of Mathematics at [Purdue University](http://www.math.purdue.edu), working in [[operator algebras]] and [[noncommutative geometry]], including the methods of KK-theory, E-theory and homotopy and [[shape theory]] for operator algebras.
