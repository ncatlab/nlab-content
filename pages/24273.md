## Idea


In *topological machine learning* one tries to combine methods from both [[topological data analysis]] and [[machine learning]] for application to [[data analysis]].

A popular approach (originating around [RHBK15](#RHBK15)) is to use [[persistent homology]] as a pre-processing stage and train [[neural networks]] not on the full data set, but on just its [[persistence diagram]]/[[barcode]] (which may  still be a large amount of data).
