## Idea

In [[chiral perturbation theory]]/[[quantum hadrodynamics]] the _chiral partner_ of a [[meson]] is that with the same [[Wigner classification]] except for parity: The chiral partner of a [[Lorentz group|Lorentz]] [[scalar]] is a [[pseudoscalar]] and that of a [[vector representation]] a [[pseudovector representation]].
