
The **analytic torsion** is an invariant of Riemannian manifolds which is an analytic analogue of the [[Reidemeister torsion]] and it has been proved to agree with it for the compact Riemannian manifolds. 

* D. Ray, I. Singer, _Analytic torsion_, Ann. Math. __98__, 1 (1973), 154--177.
