## Idea

The _Kantor-Koecher-Tits construction_ produces a [[Lie algebra]] from a [[Jordan algebra]].

Applied to the [[exceptional Jordan algebra]] this construction yields the Lie algebra of the [[exceptional Lie group]] [[E7]].
