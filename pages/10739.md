
## Idea

The Adams-Quillen theorem describes the [[Hopf algebroid]] structure on the dual [[BP]]-[[Steenrod algebra]] $BP_\bullet(BP)$.

This is the analog for [[BP]] of what the [[Landweber-Novikov theorem]] is for [[MU]].
