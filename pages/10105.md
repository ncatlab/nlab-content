## Idea

The [[2-category]]-analog of the notion of _[[compact closed category]]_.

## Examples

For $C$ a [[2-category]] with weak [[finite limits]], the [[(infinity,n)-category of spans|2-category of spans]] in $C$ is compact closed. ([Stay 13](#Stay13))
