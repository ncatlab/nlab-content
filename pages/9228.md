## Definition

For $\mu$ a [[probability measure]] on the [[real line]] $\mathbb{R}$, its **$n$th moment** for $n \in \mathbb{N}$ is the [[expectation value]] of $x^n$.

## Properties

* Moments of [[Gaussian probability distributions]] have a combinatorial expression by [[Wick's lemma]].
