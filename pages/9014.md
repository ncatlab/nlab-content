## Definition

An _abelian 3-group_ is an [[abelian infinity-group]] which is a [[3-group]].

## Examples

If $(\mathcal{C}, \otimes)$ is a [[symmetric monoidal 2-category]] then its [[Picard 3-group]] is an abelian 3-group.
