## Idea

The _string orientation of tmf_ is the universal [[orientation in generalized cohomology]] for [[tmf]]-cohomology ("universal [[elliptic cohomology]]), given by a hmomorphism

$$  
  \sigma \;\colon\; M String \longrightarrow tmf
$$

of [[E-∞ rings]], from the [[String structure]]-[[Thom spectrum]] to [[tmf]].

This is refinement of the [[Witten genus]] to a morphism of the integral [[Witten genus]] 


$$
  Z_{Witten} \;\colon\; \Omega^{string}_\bullet \longrightarrow MF_\bullet
$$

(with values in the ring of [[modular forms]]) which it reproduces on [[homotopy groups]]

$$
  Z_{Witten} \simeq \pi_\bullet(\sigma)
  \,.
$$

All this is due to ([Ando-Hopkins-Rezk 10](#AndoHopkinsRezk10)).
