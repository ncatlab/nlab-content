## Idea

The notion of _Kleisli 2-category_ over a [[2-monad]] is the generalization of the notion of [[Kleisli category]] over a [[monad]] from [[category theory]] to [[2-category]] theory (or from 2- to [[3-category]] theory, depending on perspective).
