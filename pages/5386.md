## Idea

The (large) $(\infty,n+1)$-category $(\infty,n)Cat$ is the collection of all (small) [[(∞,n)-categories]]:

* [[object]]s are [[(∞,n)-categories]];

* [[morphisms]] are [[(∞,n)-functor]]s;

* [[k-morphism]]s for $k \geq 2$ are $(k-1)$-[[transfor]]s.
