## Statement

Let $A$ be a [[Hopf algebra]] over a [[field]] $k$, and let $B$ be a normal su Hopf algebra of $A$. Then

$$
  Cotor_A(B,k) \simeq Cotor_{A//B}(k,k)
  \,.
$$

This is used in computations with the [[Adams spectral sequence]].
