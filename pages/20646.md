## Idea

The [[dihedral group]] of [[group order|order]] 6 -- $D_6$

and

the [[binary dihedral group]] of [[order of a group|order]] 12 -- $2 D_{12}$

correspond to the Dynkin label [[D5]] in the [[ADE-classification]].

## Properties

$D_6$ is [[isomorphism|isomorphic]] to the [[symmetric group]] on 3 elements

$$
  D_6 \simeq S_3
  \,.
$$

These are the smallest [[non-abelian groups]].
