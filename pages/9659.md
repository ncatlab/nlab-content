__Wallman compactification__ is a particular compactification of $T_1$-topological spaces introduced in

* Henry Wallman, _Lattices and topological spaces_, Annals of Math. __39__:1 (Jan., 1938), pp. 112-126 [jstor](http://www.jstor.org/stable/1968717)

There are recent applications related to topoi and noncommutative geometry

* [[Olivia Caramello]], _Gelfand spectra and Wallman compactifications_, [arxiv/1204.3244](http://arxiv.org/abs/1204.3244); _Dualit√© de Gelfand et bases de Wallman_, [seminar talk videos](https://sites.google.com/site/logiquecategorique/autres-seminaires/20130404-caramello-gelfand-wallman)

A __Wallman base__ $B$ for a topological space $X$ is a sublattice of of the [[frame]] $Open(X)$ of open sets of $X$ which is a [[base of a topology|base for the topology]] and satisfies the property that for any $U\in B$ and
$x\in U$ there exists $V\in B$ such that $U\cup V = X$ and $x\notin V$.
