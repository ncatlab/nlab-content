__International Mathematical Union__ (abbreviation: IMU) is the principal international organization of mathematicians; it includes many national mathematical societies as collective members. IMU organizes the International Congress of Mathematicians ([[ICM]]) every four years and awards the Fields Medal
([wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fields_Medal), [F.M. at IMU](http://www.mathunion.org/general/prizes/fields/details)).
