I am a $14$-year old student aspiring to be a mathematician. I love studying higher category theory. Right now, I'm interested in a version of "$\mathcal{K}$-theory" (it's a construction analagous to ordinary algebraic $\mathcal{K}$-theory from Quillen's $Q$-construction) of $\mathcal{M}\mathrm{od}^\mathcal{O}_A(\mathcal{C}^\otimes)^\otimes$, where $\mathcal{C}^\otimes$ is a unital $\infty$-operad. [I have taken some notes on the same at my blog.](http://categorymath.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/algebra_in_infinity_n_operads.pdf) See Lurie's [Higher Algebra](http://www.math.harvard.edu/~lurie/papers/HigherAlgebra.pdf) for terminology.
