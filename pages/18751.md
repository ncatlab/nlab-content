## Idea

The _amplitude_ of a [[wave]] is a measure for its maximal change with [[space]] and [[time]].

## Definition

For $n \in \mathbb{N}$ and $\mathbb{R}^n$ the corresponding [[Cartesian space]], a [[plane wave]] is a function $\mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{C}$ of the form

$$
  \vec x \mapsto  A e^{-2 \pi i \vec k \cdot \vec x}
  \,.
$$

Here $a \in \mathbb{C}$ is the _amplitude_ of the [[plane wave]] (while $\vec k$ s its [[wave vector]]).
