__Nicolae Popescu__ was a Romanian mathematician with focus on algebra, especially study of rings and modules, [[abelian categories]] and localization theory. Cf. also [[Gabriel-Popescu theorem]].

* Nicolae Popescu, Liliana Popescu, _Theory of categories_. Martinus Nijhoff Publishers, The Hague; Sijthoff & Noordhoff International Publishers, Alphen aan den Rijn, 1979.
