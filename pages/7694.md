Bondal and Kapranov introduced the following definition

Given an algebraically closed field $k$, and a $k$-linear additive category $C$ with finite-dimensional morphism spaces an additive endofunctor, $S : C\to C$,
is called a __Serre functor__ if $S$ is an auto-equivalence and there
are isomorphisms
$$
Hom_C(X, S Y ) = Hom_C(Y,X)^*,
$$
natural in $X$ and $Y$.

Of course, formally the definition could be used in categories enriched over a symmetric monoidal category with a sufficiently nice involution.
